A total of 52 combat clashes were recorded during the day.

About thisreported on the evening of March 24General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In total, the enemy inflicted 32 missile and 47 aviation strikes, carried out 48 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

Unfortunately, Russian terrorist attacks are killed and injured among civilians. Multi -storey and private homes were destroyed and damaged.

In Volyn and Polissya directions, the operational situation without significant changes. No signs of formation of offensive groups of the enemy were detected.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directions, the enemy retains military presence in the border areas, conducts sabotage and intelligence activities, carries out shelling of settlements from the territory of the Russian Federation, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border of Ukraine.

The enemy struck in the areas of settlements Victory and Popivka of Sumy region.

The enemy's artillery and mortar shellings have suffered more than 15 settlements, including spinners of Chernihiv region; Sorokine, Bachivsk, Starykovo, Volfine, Popivka of Sumy region; Veterinary, Borysivka, Ambarne, Sagittarius of Kharkiv region.

In the Kupyansk direction, the enemy of offensive (assault) did not take action. Over 10 settlements, including Sinkivka, Ivanivka, and Berestovo, Kharkiv region, were under artillery and mortar fire.

In the Liman direction, our soldiers repelled 5 enemy attacks in the area of the settlement of Bigorivka of Luhansk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops.

The enemy struck in the areas of Serebryansk forestry of Luhansk region and Lake Donetsk region.

More than 10 settlements were injured from artillery and mortar shelling, including Makeevka, Bigorivka, Luhansk region and Tern, Serebryanka, controversial, Donetsk region.

In the Bakhmut direction, our soldiers repelled 5 attacks of the enemy in the areas of settlements Bogdanivka, Kingshchiivka, Andreevka of Donetsk region. The enemy struck an aviation strike in the area of the settlement of Iron Donetsk region.

Under artillery and mortar fire were about 10 settlements, including Bogdanivka, Vasyukivka and the time of the Yar of Donetsk region.

In the Avdeevsky direction, our defenders reflected 8 enemy attacks in the areas of Berdychi settlements, Eastern Uman, Pervomaisk Donetsk region.

The enemy struck aviation in areas of settlements of the reeds and Novokalinovo Donetsk region. About 20 settlements, among them Alexandropil, Ceramik, Novobakhmutivka, Semenivka, Donetsk region, have experienced artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Novopavlovsky direction, the forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of settlements of Krasnogorivka, St. George, Novomikhailivka and Urozhayne, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried 17 times to break through the defense of our troops.

Under the artillery and mortar fire of the invaders were about 10 settlements, among them Krasnogorivka, St. George, Paraskovivka, coal of Donetsk region.

In the Orikhiv direction, the enemy 2 times, with the support of aviation, attacked the positions of our defenders in the area of the Robician Zaporizhzhya region.

Also struck aviation in areas of settlements Makarovka, Staromayorsk Donetsk region; Malynivka of Zaporizhzhya region. About 10 settlements, among them Poltava, Rivnoil, Magic, Orikhiv, five -way of Zaporizhzhya region, have experienced artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Kherson direction during the day the enemy of the offensive (assault) did not take action. The enemy fired from the jet fire systems of such settlements as Cossack, Poniativka of the Kherson region.

Asreported the armyinform, last day, the loss of the enemy amounted to 1000 invaders, 44 artsystems and 28 armored vehicles.
