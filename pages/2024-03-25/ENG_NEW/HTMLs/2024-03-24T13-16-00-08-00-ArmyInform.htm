The OSCE Malta President is concerned about the escalation of violence against Ukraine and condemns Russian shelling of the critical infrastructure of our country.

This is stated in the statement,PostedIn Twitter.

"Purposeful shelling of civilians and critical infrastructure objects are unacceptable and flashy violations of international law," the statement said.

The statement stresses that all OSCE member states are obliged to protect innocent civilians and respect the rule of international law.

«The OSCE presidency expresses solidarity with innocent civilians who have suffered from this meaningless violence, ”the OSCE Maltese presidency said.

We will remind, from January 1, 2024 Malta began the annual presidency in the organization for safety and cooperation in Europe (OSCE).
