During the week from March 17 to March 24, 2024, 6010 personnel of the enemy were destroyed by the Defense forces of Ukraine.

About thisreportedCommander of the Land Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk.

Significant losses, he said, suffered weapons and military equipment of Russian troops, namely:

  • 86 tanks,
  • 161 Battle armored car,
  • 221 Artillery System,
  • 1 RSZV,
  • 3 air defense settings,
  • 334 units of auto -engineering,
  • 55 units of special equipment.

"In addition, our defenders destroyed 70 enemy missiles and 238 UAVs," said Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk, commander of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

As reportedArmyinform, last day, the enemy minus almost 1000 invaders, 44 artsystems and 28 BBM.
