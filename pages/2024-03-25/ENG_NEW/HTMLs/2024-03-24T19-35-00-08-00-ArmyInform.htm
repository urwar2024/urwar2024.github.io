The sowing area for the crop this year will be 337 thousand hectares.

About thisreported March 24Head of Donetsk Regional Military Administration Vadim Filashkin.

According to him, winter crops last fall were sown on an area of 120 thousand hectares, the rest of the fields from this week began to sow with spring crops.

Asreported the armyinform, over one and a half thousand beats have struck the enemy in the past 24 hours.
