The President recalled the positive decisions of international organizations and individual countries on financial assistance to Ukraine.

About this Volodymyr Zelenskyydeclared on the evening of March 24.

The President attributed the President to the EU decision on the Ukrainian Assistance Fund with a volume of 5 billion euros, as well as a new tranche of macro -financial assistance from the European Union by 4.5 billion euros. The IMF has decided to provide a new tranche for almost $ 900 million, Canada will provide $ 2 billion in direct budget support.

"There are good results for defense support packages - there are both packages and new ones have been announced," said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

According to him, they relate to artillery, drones and armored vehicles.

"The main thing is not to get tired, not to lose our activity, not to lose faith in Ukraine, to our people and to our opportunity to convey to the world that the enemy of human life has no right to win Putin has no right to win," said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

BeforeThe Armyinform reportedthat the President discussed with the Spanish prime minister strengthening the Ukrainian air defense.
