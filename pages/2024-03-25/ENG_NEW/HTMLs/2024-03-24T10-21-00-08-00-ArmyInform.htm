Last day, the occupiers carried out two assaults in the area of the Ukrainian bridgehead on the left bank of the Dnieper in the Krynkov area and four in the area of the robot in the Zaporozhye direction.

All the assaults were unsuccessful, in the area of the Krynkov the enemy lost up to 60% of personnel.

Colonel Natalia Humeniuk told this during a live broadcast of the Unified News Telelorphone.

A characteristic feature of hostile assault, she said, is that they occur without the use of armored vehicles.

The spokeswoman noted that the enemy was trying to storm Ukrainian positions daily, but has significant problems with motivation.

-The enemy has significant problems with the collection of assault units, since the moral and psychological spirit of the occupation troops is extremely low,-she said.

AsreportedArmyinform, last day of defense forces of southern Ukraine shot down 22 enemy drones.
