In Dnipropetrovsk, almost 7 thousand fortifications of reinforced concrete structures "Dragon teeth" are installed, from which traps for armored vehicles are laid on the terrain.

About thisreportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Regional State Administration Sergey Lisak.

“Fortification construction in Dnipropetrovsk is ongoing, large -scale works. Many specialists and equipment, involved enterprises from Dnipropetrovsk and Zaporizhzhya region are also involved, ”Sergey Lisak said.

He noted that all services operate at full capacity.

“Reinforced concrete structures are made for fortifications. They create the same "dragon teeth" from which traps for armored vehicles are taught on the terrain, "he said.

The head of the ODA noted that such concrete pyramids have already been installed almost 7 thousand pieces and the process is ongoing.

“We are acting in harmony and in conjunction with the defenders. All in the terms set by President Vladimir Zelensky, ”he summarized.
