As a result of the morning hostile attack in the Lviv region, there was an entry into the ground infrastructure of one of the underground gas storage facilities.

About thisHe wrote on March 24Alexey Chernyshov, Chairman of the Board of Naftogaz of Ukraine.

According to him, technological equipment was damaged.

Localization and elimination of consequences are currently underway.

“There are no critical consequences to ensure the operation of the storage, because gas is at considerable depth. The damaged part of the ground infrastructure will require recovery, but we have enough reserve capacity, ”Alexei Chernyshov assured.

In his opinion, the attack will not affect the supply of blue fuel to Ukrainian consumers.

The Armyinform reportedthat during the night attack the air forces of the Armed Forces destroyed 43 enemy targets
