Concrete pyramids are part of the fortifications built in the region.

About thisreported March 24Head of the Odessa Regional Military Administration Oleg Kiper.

According to him, the fortifications under construction provide a system of trenches, armor and other shelters, both for personnel and equipment.

"Construction in Odesa region goes in accordance with the military expediency, planning of the Ministry of Defense and engineering standards," Oleg Kiper wrote.

According to him, the trenches are protected from waterproofing, fortified wood and embankment.

Asreported the armyinform, nearly 7,000 Dragon teeth are installed in Dnipropetrovsk.
