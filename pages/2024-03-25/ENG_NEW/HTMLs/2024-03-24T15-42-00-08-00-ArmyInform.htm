Military doctors of stabilization points and advanced surgical groups urge their civilian colleagues to mobilize.

About thiswroteMarch 24 Command of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.

Medical services that provide the stages of medical evacuation of wounded soldiers require specialists such as:

  • anesthesiologists;
  • surgeons;
  • traumatologists;
  • Secondary medical staff.

Doctors of stabilization points work day and night, as even then the medical evacuation of the wounded defenders continues. Despite active fighting and loads, doctors on the advanced stable and confidently save the lives and health of our fighters, according to the command of the medical forces.
