Today, March 24, during a rocket attack of Russian troops on Ukraine, Poland involved military aviation for the safety of its airspace.

About thisinformsOperational Command of the Armed Forces of Poland.

"The Armed Forces Operational Command is watching the intensive activity of longitudinal aviation of the Russian Federation, related to aviation and missile strikes on objects in Ukraine," the statement reads.

The military department noted that all the necessary procedures for ensuring the safety of Poland's airspace have been launched, the situation will be monitored.

"We warn that Polish and allied aviation is involved, which can lead to an increase in noise, especially in the southeastern part of the country," the Polish military warned.
