According to the schedule of hourly power outages of electricity, each of the city districts 6 hours a day will be without light.

Schedulepublished in the evening of March 24Head of Kharkiv Regional State Administration Oleg Sinegubov.

According to him, in the period from March 25 to March 31, electricity will be missing:

  • Shevchenkivskyi and Kyiv districts: from 9:00 to 15:00;
  • Slobidsky, Nameyansky districts: from 15:00 to 21:00;
  • Saltovsky, Nemyshlyansky, industrial districts: from 21:00 to 03:00;
  • Kholodnogorsky, Novobavar districts: from 03:00 to 9:00.

There will be 2 hours for the shutdown area:

  • Merefian TG, Lyubotin TG: from 9:00 to 11:00;
  • Zmiiv TG, Balakliyiv TG: from 11:00 to 13:00;
  • Chuguev TG, Izium TG: from 13:00 to 15:00;
  • Bohodukhiv TG, Dergachiv TG: from 15:00 to 17:00.

“I emphasize that this is a forced temporary step. It is needed to ensure that as many inhabitants of the region there are light and businesses could work, ”Oleg Sinegubov wrote.

According to him, experts continue to eliminate the effects of arrivals, to repair the infrastructure after the enemy shelling around the clock.

Asreported the armyinform, in Kryvyi Rih, schedules of emergency power outages were introduced.
