The cities of Kharkiv, Kupyansk and Vovchansk, the villages of Novoosinovo Kupyansky district, Vovchansky farms of Chuguev district and Slobozhansk Kharkiv district were fired by Russian invaders last day.

As a result of shelling, a 72-year-old man was killed in Vovchansky farms, a 43-year-old man in Kupyansk was injured.

About thisinformsHead of Kharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov.

In the Slobid district of Kharkiv, a private enterprise was found under hostile fire - 4 premises and 2 cars were affected.

“Currently, up to 200,000 city subscribers have been disabled. Energy continue to work 24/7 to eliminate the consequences of shelling, ”the message reads.

AsreportedArmyinform, yesterday in Kharkiv region as a result of the shelling of the village of Vovchansky farms of the Volchansk community, a 72-year-old man was killed.
