The forces of Ukraine's defense successfully struck large landing ships of Russian invaders "Yamal" and "Azov".

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

The Armed Forces also struck the center of communication and several objects of infrastructure of the Black Sea Fleet of the invaders in the temporarily occupied Sevastopol, in Crimea.

As reportedArmyinform, last day, the enemy minus almost 1000 invaders, 44 artsystems and 28 BBM.
