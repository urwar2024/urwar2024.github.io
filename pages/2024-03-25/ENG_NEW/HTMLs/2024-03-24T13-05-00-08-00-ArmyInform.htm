The Officer of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine with the call sign "Vito" told the details of the preparation of military intelligence officers for the defense of Kharkiv.

About thisreportedMarch 24 Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

"I am with my friend, he is also a colonel, together they came to Kharkiv even in a large -scale invasion - to prepare for the defense of the city and the region," Vito said.

To protect Kharkiv and the region from the Russians to the city before the invasion, they decided to transport goods with weapons.

“Two to three weeks before the fighting, our officers were sent parties of small arms together with grenade launchers, of course, explosives, etc. to the city of Kharkiv to create from local, let's say, militia. It helped, ”said Lieutenant General Kirill Budanov.

Vito is one of the officers who implemented the preparation for the defense of Kharkiv.

“The commander called me and my colleague. We gathered - and in Kharkiv. They downloaded two weapons, ammunition. They came to the city, made a secret bookmark. On February 23, I gave the first weapons to people, ”Vito said.

According to him, the number of volunteers who stood in defense of their city from Russian invaders exceeded all expectations - there was not enough weapon.

«There were not enough ammunition, grenades, RPG. What we brought was designed for one number of volunteers, people who would take their weapons. But the Kharkiv people proved to be completely different. They had to be armed, ”Vito added.
