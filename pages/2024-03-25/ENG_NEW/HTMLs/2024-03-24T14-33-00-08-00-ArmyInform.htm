The Kyiv Regional State Administration and the American Philanthropist Foundation Della Lay Hansen began jointly developing the concept of establishing a modern rehabilitation hospital for military personnel in the Kyiv region.

AsreportedIn the KOVA press service, a detailed analysis of the entire documentation will begin next week.

“We involve the international coalition of architects and engineers of medical buildings. The institution will be the standard for future rehabilitation centers both in Ukraine and in other countries of the world affected by the war, ”the department said.

The Kyiv Regional Military Administration noted that this modern center will satisfy both the physical and psychological needs of the military.

“The project needs considerable investment. Therefore, it requires the involvement of a circle of philanthropists, government, non -governmental organizations under the direction of the Mission of Hansen in Ukraine. The Foundation and its founder Del Loi Hansen are reliable and financially responsible partners, ”the military administration summarized.

As reportedArmyinform,The rehabilitation center was opened for the military in Khmelnitsky.
