In the morning of March 24, during an air attack on Ukraine, one Russian winged rocket flew into Poland and was in the sky above the country for 39 seconds.

About thisreportedOperational Command of the Armed Forces of Poland.

The department claims that the targets were located in the west of Ukraine.

“The object flew into the Polish space at the height of the village of Oserdov (Lublin Voivodeship) and was there for 39 seconds. Throughout the flight, he was observed by military radar systems, ”the message reads.

The command added that at night all the necessary procedures were activated to ensure the safety of Polish airspace. The Polish aircraft and allies of allies were raised.

"The Polish army continuously monitors the situation in the Ukrainian territory and is in constant combat readiness to ensure the safety of the Polish airspace," - said in the Armed Forces of Poland.

As reportedArmyinform, today, March 24, during a rocket attack of Russian troops on Ukraine, Poland involved military aviation for the safety of its airspace.
