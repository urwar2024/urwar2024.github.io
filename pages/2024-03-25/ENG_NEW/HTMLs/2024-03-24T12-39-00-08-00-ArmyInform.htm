A hotline for veterans support has been launched in Ukraine. Consultations on urgent issues to veterans and veterans, their families and families of fallen soldiers will be provided by qualified specialists.

About thisinformsMinistry of Reintegration of Temporarily Occupied Territories of Ukraine.

You can call a single veteran line with questions about:

  • obtaining psychological support, treatment and rehabilitation, confirmation of injury;
  • social benefits to the participants of the fighting and families of the dead, as well as people who have acquired a disability as a result of war;
  • receiving financial assistance and compensation;
  • housing and legal advice, etc.

The service was launched by the Ministry of Justice together with the Ukrainian Red Cross. The introduction of a telephone unified veteran line is part of a new veteran policy in support of Ukrainian defenders and defenders and their relatives.

Currently, a single veteran line works in test mode. You can get a consultation dailyfrom 08:00 to 8:00 pmby calling the number:0 800 505 217.
