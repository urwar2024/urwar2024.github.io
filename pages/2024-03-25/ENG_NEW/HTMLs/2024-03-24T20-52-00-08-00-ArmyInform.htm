Volodymyr Zelensky had a telephone conversation with the Prime Minister of Spain Pedro Sanchez on March 24.

The president was about itreportedon your page.

“He thanked Spain for his strong support, in particular for a recent considerable military assistance package. The need of Ukraine to strengthen air defense was discussed, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

According to him, bilateral cooperation and cooperation at the EU level were also discussed.

"They agreed to accelerate the work on the preparation of a bilateral security agreement between Ukraine and Spain," said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

The parties also paid special attention to the future of the first global peace summit in Switzerland and agreed to work together to encourage more global countries to participate in it.

Asreported the armyinform, Volodymyr Zelenskyy stated that Russia should lose in this war. This can only protect life reliably.
