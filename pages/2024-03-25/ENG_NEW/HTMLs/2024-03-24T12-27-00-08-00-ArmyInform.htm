Before supplying the Armed Forces of Ukraine, clothing and ammunition are tested for quality in three stages - laboratory control of materials, checking the control sample of the goods and quality control of the whole batch.

About thistoldDirector of Delivery Management of the State Operator of the rear of which Kovalenko.

The quality control of real property consists of three stages:

  1. Laboratory control of materialsthat will be used to produce a lot of goods for compliance with technical requirements. Conducts the manufacturer in accordance with the requirements of the tender. The results of the control are transmitted to the Central Quality Control Department of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.
  2. Control sample.The manufacturer produces a control sample of the goods, gives it for inspection to the laboratory, and then transmits the Central Department of Quality Control to another inspection and approval with the conclusions. The production of the whole party begins only after positive conclusions at this stage.
  3. Party quality control.When the party is ready, the Central Quality Control Office, along with DOT, selects from the party to check in the laboratory. After receiving the results of the tests indicating the compliance of the goods with all quality requirements, the supplier takes the party to the place of delivery.
