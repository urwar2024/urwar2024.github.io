In the south of Ukraine, Russia's troops returned to the tactics of use of managed aircraft. In particular, in the Kherson region, the most of the shelling is affected by Mykilsk, Zmiivtsi and Red Lighthouse.

About thisinformsHead of the Regional Military Administration Alexander Proudin.

“Last day, the enemy was fired at Berislav, Novoberislav, Dniprovske, Burguna, Kizomis, Giant, Antonivka, Alexandrovka, Gavrilovka, Golden Beam, Tyaginka and Kherson. According to Mykilsky, Zniivka and Red Lighthouse, the enemy was directed by managed aircraft, ”the message reads.

According to Alexander Proudin, the Russian military kissed the residential quarters of the settlements of the region, resulting in a high -rise building and 10 private homes.

“Admi -building, school, culture, shop, driving school and cars were also recorded. There are no casualties and victims among civilians, ”said the head of the regional military administration.

As reportedArmyinform, the army of the Russian Federation on March 25 attacked Kherson region, and destruction was recorded.
