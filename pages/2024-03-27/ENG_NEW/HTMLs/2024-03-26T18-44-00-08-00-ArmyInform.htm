The russian military dropped a one-and-a-half-ton aerial bomb on Krasnohorivka in the Donetsk region.

This was reported by the National Police of Ukraine.

It is noted that the Russian troops dropped the CAB-1500 on a five-storey building.

In the first hours after the shelling, the White Angel crew worked on the spot. The police evacuated three victims and two women who asked to pick them up.

On the way out of the city, the group almost came under enemy attack.
