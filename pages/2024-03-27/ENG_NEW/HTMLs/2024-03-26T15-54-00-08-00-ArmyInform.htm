President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky signed a decree on the release of Alexander Litvinenko from the post of head of the Foreign Intelligence Service.

AppropriatedecreeIt is located on the site of the head of state.

"To release Alexander Litvinenko from the post of head of the Foreign Intelligence Service of Ukraine," the document reads.

Oleksandr Litvinenko has headed the external intelligence service of Ukraine since July 2021.

To othersby decreeThe President appointed the new head of the department Oleg Ivashchenko.

"To appoint Ivashchenko Oleg Ivanovich as head of the Foreign Intelligence Service of Ukraine", - the text of the decree reads.

Oleg Ivashchenko earlier held the position of Deputy Chief of the Main Department of Intelligence of MOU.

As reported by the Armyinform, President of Ukraine Volodymyr ZelenskyyreleasedAlexei Danilov from the position of NSDC Secretary and appointed to his place the head of the foreign intelligence service Alexander Litvinenko.
