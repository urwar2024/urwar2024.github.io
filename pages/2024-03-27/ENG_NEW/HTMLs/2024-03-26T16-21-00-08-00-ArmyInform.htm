In the defense forces of Ukraine, they told about the unique possibility of a light armored car Spartan.

This is stated inmessageForces of Defense of South of Ukraine.

It is noted that Spartan gives a unique opportunity to choose a way to travel.

Some details allow him to overcome large water obstacles. It is distinguished by a very small size and low silhouette.

It is possible to effectively transport specialized groups to the right points.

In conditions of increased danger, Spartan is useful for scouts, anti-aircraft gunners, sappers and even mortar operators.

He is able to protect from fire from small arms, pomegranate and artillery. With a frontal hit, this APC can withstand armor -piercing balls of 14.5 from a distance of 200 meters.

As reported by the Armyinform, Iveco, which joined the Ukrainian Alliance of Defense Industries,presented a new version of the APC.
