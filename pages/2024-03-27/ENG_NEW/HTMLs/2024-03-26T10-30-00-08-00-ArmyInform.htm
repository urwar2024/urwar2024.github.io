Viceremier-Minister for Innovation, Development of Education, Science and Technology-Minister of Digital Transformation of Ukraine Mikhail Fedorov told about the profits of Ukrainian citizens received through the purchase of military bonds.

This is stated inmessageMikhail Fedorov.

“94+ million hryvnias have earned the bonds that they bought through action. Since October 2022, Ukrainians have purchased more than 4 million military bonds because of its action, which they invest in the state and also made a profit, ”the minister said.

According to him, it works very simply.

“Military bonds are securities. Ukrainians borrow money temporarily, which then work for the army and economy. Bonded bonds are ammunition, medicines, provisions, salaries of military and doctors. Then the funds are returned with a percentage, ”Mikhail Fedorov said.

He urged Ukrainians to buy military bonds.

“Make the state, and a pleasant bonus will be a profit. We bring the victory and the de -occupation of cities together, ”the minister summarized.

As reported by the Armyinform, in March 2024 the Ministry of Finance of UkraineplacedAt the auction, military bonds for another 16.3 billion hryvnias.
