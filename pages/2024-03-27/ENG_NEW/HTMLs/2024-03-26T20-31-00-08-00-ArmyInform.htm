The instructors and instructors of the Norwegian National Guard Heimevernet are continuously training Ukrainian military in the United Kingdom within the framework of the Multinational Interflex Educational Operation.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its Facebook page.

Norwegian instructors conduct sessions on conducting positional (trench) hostilities, in particular the preparation, seizure and maintenance of the trench, trench, position, first aid on the battlefield.

Under the expert guidance of the Norwegian National Guard instructors, Ukrainian recruits receive and trade valuable experiences and skills that will help them protect Ukraine.

This preparation allows you to master the combat skills so that volunteer soldiers who have virtually no or minimal military experience can protect Ukraine.

Large -scale training is aimed at improving the safety and capacity of the Armed Forces of Ukraine in the frontier.

The Kingdom of Norway continues to support the people of Ukraine and provides continuous support in response to ongoing violations of international law.
