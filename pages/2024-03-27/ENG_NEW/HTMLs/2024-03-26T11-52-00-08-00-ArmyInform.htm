On Sunday, March 31, the declaration campaign for the past year is coming to an end.

About thisThey remindedat the National Agency for Corruption Prevention.

Yes, the NAPC emphasized that there was little time left to file declarations and urged civil servants to rush to perform this duty.

«In order to accelerate the process of filling in the NAPC Declaration, the service of obtaining personal data of the declarant's family data in the Action application was implemented.Thus, when forming a "Declaration" certificate, the declarant receives not only the attitude towards himself, but also to his family members», - said in the department.

As reportedArmyinform, IUU implements the practice of filing a medical declaration through action.
