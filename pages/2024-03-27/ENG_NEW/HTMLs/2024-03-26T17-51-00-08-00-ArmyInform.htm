Prime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal and Speaker of Rygicogu (Parliament) of Estonia Lauri Hussar discussed defensive assistance and prosecution of Russia for crimes against Ukraine.

The head of the Ukrainian government was about itreportedIn telegrams.

“They discussed defense assistance and holding Russia. In particular, the confiscation of frozen Russian assets. Estonia is an active promoter of this issue, and we appreciate it, ”the message reads.

Denis Shmigal thanked Estonia for the support of the Presidential Presidential Formula, in particular, attachment to the implementation of such points as "restoration of the territorial integrity of Ukraine" and "restoration of justice."

Recall EstoniaannouncedA new military assistance package to Ukraine, which will include artillery ammunition, anti -tank weapons, explosives, sniper equipment and other weapons. Estonia also transmits 155 mm caliber shells, which is an additional contribution to the European Union initiative.

Earlier the Ministers of Defense of Ukraine and EstoniaAgreedto deepen security and defense cooperation.
