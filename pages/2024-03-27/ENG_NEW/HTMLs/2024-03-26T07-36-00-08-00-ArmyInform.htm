On the night of March 26, the Russians attacked Ukraine with 12 shock drones such as Shahd, all enemy drones were destroyed.

About thisreportedAir Force Commander Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk.

"The enemy attacked 12 shaped UAVs from Chauda-Crimea and Kursk region-the Russian Federation, as well as two anti-aircraft guided missiles C-300 towards Donetsk region," he said.

According to the commander, the air attack reflects the mobile fire groups of defense forces of Ukraine in the Nikolaev and Kharkiv regions.

"All 12" Shahmed "was shot. Thank you for your successful combat work! Together to victory!" -said Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk.

As reportedArmyinform, over the last day, the air defense has destroyed 8 shock UAVs in the south.
