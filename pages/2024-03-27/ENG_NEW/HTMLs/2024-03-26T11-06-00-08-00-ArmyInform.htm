President Volodymyr Zelensky awarded the Honorary Honorary Honorary Honor "For Courage and Courage" Department of Military Counterintelligence of the Security Service of Ukraine.

Appropriate Decree No. 170/2024 dated March 25publishedon the site of the Head of State.

According to the decree, the department is marked with the purpose of decent commemoration of courage and heroism, discovered in the protection of state sovereignty, independence, territorial integrity of Ukraine.

As reported by Armyinform, SBU Chairmanreactedto the decision of the Russian court about his arrest.
