Today, March 26, the chairman of the commission of Rygicogu (Parliament of Estonia) in foreign affairs of Marco Mikhkelson arrived in Ukraine.

About this Estonian politicianreportedIn Twitter.

"Good morning, Kiev!" - wrote Marco Mihkelson, publishing a photo at the Kiev railway station.

Recall EstoniaannouncedA new military assistance package to Ukraine, which will include artillery ammunition, anti -tank weapons, explosives, sniper equipment and other weapons. Estonia also transmits 155 mm caliber shells, which is an additional contribution to the European Union initiative.
