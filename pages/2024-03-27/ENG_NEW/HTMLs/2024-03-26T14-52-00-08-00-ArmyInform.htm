A spokesman for the Air Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Major Ilya Yevlash told what weapons are currently needed by the Defense Forces of Ukraine to strengthen the air defense system.

Elijah Yevlash provided his comments duringlive etherOn March 26, 2024.

In particular, according to him, self-propelled anti-aircraft missile systems such as Avenger, Gepard and Skynex anti-aircraft self-propelled guns are needed.

“We need different systems: both anti-aircraft missile and different types of Avenger, Gepard, Skynex, which can work on Shahmed. Because of course, it is unprofitable to run a multimillion-dollar rocket on fairly inexpensive enemy-unmanned-and-old-fashioned apparatus, ”said the AFS spokesman of the Armed Forces.

He recalled that the collision line with the enemy along the entire border with Russia, Belarus, Transnistria and the sea zone is more than 2 thousand kilometers.

"Of course, this requires enormous effort, tremendous resources, including the cover of our infrastructure objects of critical infrastructure, military facilities," Ilya Yevlash said.

At the same time, he noted that defense forces improve air defense at the expense of mobile fire groups that work quite successfully.

"But we need more systems, more radars that will detect these systems on the grounds and destroy them with the sighting fire of our mobile groups and various air defense systems," - summed up a spokesman for the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.
