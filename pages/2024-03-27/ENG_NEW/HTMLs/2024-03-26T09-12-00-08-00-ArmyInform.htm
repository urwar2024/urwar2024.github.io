The Kharkiv region per day was numerous attacks by Russian troops, including a blow to the city of Kharkiv. Under artillery and mortar shelling there were 15 settlements, which led to the death of one and injury to four people.

About thisreportedThe head of Kharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov.

“In the evening, a hostile shelling was carried out. Moscow Kupyansky district. Hit on the territory. Without the victims. Also, Vovchansk came under enemy shelling. Damaged one private home. Without the victims, ”Oleg Synigubov said.

In the afternoon of March 25, 20 private homes, 1 outbuilding, 2 cars were damaged in Dergachi as a result of firing.

"4 women were injured: two of them have an explosive injury, others have an acute response to stress," said the chairman of the Kharkiv Ova.

Also yesterday, according to Oleg Sinogubova, 2 private homes were burning in Volchansk in Volchansk. A 65-year-old man was killed on the site of the debris.

“In the village. The Pokrovskoye Izyum district 53-year-old tractor driver exploded in a field on an explosive device. Without hospitalization, ”he said.
