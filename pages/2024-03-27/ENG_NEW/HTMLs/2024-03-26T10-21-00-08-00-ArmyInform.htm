The Head of the Security Service of Ukraine Vasyl Malyuk commented on the decision of the Basman Court of Moscow, who decided to arrest Vasyl Malyuk in absentia on charges of terrorism.

The comment of Vasyl Malyuk was provided by the SBU press service for the Armyinform.

“The decision to arrest from the Kremlin's pocket judges does not deserve any attention. I, like all Ukrainians, are interested in only one court - over their master Putler. We do everything on us to see this military criminal on the Hague court bench as soon as possible, ”the SBU chairman said.

According to the media, the Moscow Basmann Court granted the request of the investigation and arrested the head of the SBU Vasyl Malyuk in absentia on charges of organizing a truck explosion on the Crimean bridge in 2022.
