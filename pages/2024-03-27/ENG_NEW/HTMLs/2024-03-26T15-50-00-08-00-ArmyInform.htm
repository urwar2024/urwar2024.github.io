President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky dismissed Alexei Danilov from the position of NSDC Secretary and appointed the head of the Foreign Intelligence Service Alexander Litvinenko.

This is stated in the appropriateDecree, published on the site of the Head of State.

"To release Danilov Alexei Myacheslavovich from the post of Secretary of the National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine," - stated in the decree.

At the same time the new Secretary of the NSDC PresidentappointedAlexander Litvinenko whom before thatreleasedfrom the post of head of the Foreign Intelligence Service.

We will remind that Alexei Danilov was the Secretary of the National Security and Defense Council since October 2019.
