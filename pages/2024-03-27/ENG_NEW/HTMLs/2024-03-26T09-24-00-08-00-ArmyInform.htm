President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky congratulated the staff and veterans on the 10th anniversary of the National Guard of Ukraine.

The head of statewroteIn the telegram.

“Ten years ago, our National Guard of Ukraine was established. And each of these years is a time of unchanged courage for the sake of Ukraine, unchanged force for the necessary results of Ukraine and unchanged devotion to Ukraine, ”the President of Ukraine said.

Volodymyr Zelensky emphasized that Ukraine is proud of every unit of the National Guard of Ukraine.

“I thank all the soldiers of the National Guard, who have been combined with the fate of Ukraine's independence from the fate of Ukraine from 14 February. And we remember all the warriors of the Guards who gave their lives in the battles for the sake of the state. Glory to Ukraine!" Said the Head of State.

As reportedArmyinform, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces Lieutenant General Alexander Sirsky also congratulated the defenders on the National Guard Day of Ukraine.
