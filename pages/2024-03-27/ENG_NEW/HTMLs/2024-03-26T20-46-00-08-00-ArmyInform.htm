Volodymyr Zelensky met with a delegation led by the speaker of the Estonian Parliament Lauri Hussar.

“This visit is an important signal of support for our country. We appreciate the support of Estonia from the first day of a full -scale war, ” -statedVolodymyr Zelenskyy followed by the meeting of March 26.

The parties discussed further assistance to Ukraine by Estonia and other partners, progress in the preparation of a bilateral security agreement. Separately talked about the use of Russian frozen assets in favor of protecting and restoring life in Ukraine.

Asreported the armyinform, Defense Ministers of Ukraine and Estonia have agreed to deepen security and defense cooperation.
