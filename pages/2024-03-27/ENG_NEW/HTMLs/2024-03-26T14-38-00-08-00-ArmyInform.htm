In the capital, another 626 servicemen who were mobilized in the Armed Forces of Ukraine after December 1, 2023 received financial assistance in the amount of 30 thousand UAH.

About thisinformsKyiv City State Administration.

One -time financial assistance to the mobilized is accrued in non -cash form on social (salary) account automatically.

In order to receive payment, the mobilized must provide a copy of the identification code, a passport of a citizen of Ukraine or ID card, a certificate/extract from a bank about opening a social (salary) account to the territorial center of acquisition and social support.

The KSCA reminded that it is a one -time payment. It is envisaged for the citizens of Ukraine who are called for military service during the mobilization, for a special period, for military service under the contract of ordinary, sergeant, officers and officers and are in military registration in the district in the capital of the Tax Code and JV. In early March such assistancegotThe first 473 servicemen.
