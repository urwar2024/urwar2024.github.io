President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky took part in the celebrations on the occasion of the 10th anniversary of the creation of the National Guard of Ukraine and the issue of officers of the National Academy of NSU and the Kiev Institute of the National Guard.

About thisreported March 26Office of the President

"Ukraine will always be grateful to the soldiers of the National Guard, in particular the soldiers of the Azov unit, who defended Mariupol and Azovstal. We remember all Ukrainians, all the defenders of Mariupol - all the Azov fighters who are now in Russian captivity," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

He recalled heroic pages written by warriors of the National Guard units during the protection of Ukraine from the 14th year and from the Russian invasion in the 22nd year, including the protection of Kharkiv and the defense of the Kyiv region.

“Our state independence is held precisely on such a large -scale courage of the Ukrainian people - our people, our forces of defense and security. Ukraine will always remember all its people who have fought and gave their lives for the sake of independence and future for the Ukrainian people! ”, - said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

Asreported the armyinform, Volodymyr Zelenskyy celebrated 19 servicemen with state awards.
