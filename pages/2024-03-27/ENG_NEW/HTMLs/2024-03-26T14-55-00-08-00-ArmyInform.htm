UAV operators, doctors, equipment repair specialists, drivers, cooks, accountants, clerks and chaplains are not a complete list of professionals needed by the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Thousands of vacancies are available in the Armed Forces to contract or officially mobilize by the end of martial law. You can learn about them in many ways.

Recruiting platforms

The most convenient way to find vacancies in the Armed Forces is to contact the platforms on the Internet, with which the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine cooperates:

  • Recruitment to protect Ukrainefrom robota.ua
  • The only vacancy portalState Employment Service
  • Lobby X
  • Work.ua
  • OLX.ua

These resources help those who want to join the Defense Forces of Ukraine by choosing the desired position, and to find the military command to find motivated people to fill the vacancies in the relevant units.

You can choose positions in them both in military specialty and native troops, as well as a specific brigade.

Recruitment centers to the Armed Forces of Ukraine

Not only can you learn about vacancies, but you can get detailed explanations and consultations at the Armed Forces recruitment centers on the basis of administrative services (CNAPs). Network of such recruiting centers Ministry of DefenseCreates nowThroughout Ukraine, starting from the regional centers as the largest cities, there are also smaller cities of Ukraine with the plan to cover the centers.

- These are the first entry points, in fact, to the Ukrainian army to understand what is, what vacancies are, how I can join, what algorithm, what process of all this, - told about recruiting centers authorized by the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine on recruitment Alexei Alexei Beige.

Centers on the basis of CNAPs in the following cities have already been opened:

  • Dnipro (Slobozhansky Avenue, 8)
  • Zaporozhye (Central Boulevard, 27)
  • Lviv (67 Kostya Levitsky Street)
  • Poltava (45a Soborna Street)

By the end of the first half of 2024, the opening of 27 centers of recruitment of the Armed Forces was planned in the country.

Recruiting to individual units and subdivisions

You can also find out the vacancies on the resources of individual crews, units and military units. Some of them have powerful recruiting programs, platforms and their own centers.

Among the most famous areThe third separate storm brigadeandDa Vinci Wolves Battalion. The recruiting center recently openedSeparate assault brigade named after the cat chieftain Ivan Sirkoin Kharkiv.

By contacting the resources of these and other units and units, you can not only find out the current information about the available vacancies, but also interview, get all the necessary consultations and clarification. In some cases, such as in the third assault, you can even pass a short-term intensive training before making the final decision.

An important aspect in the independent search of vacancies is to obtain from the subdivision of written consent (it is also called agreement or recommendation letter) for replacement of the chosen position.

According to recentexplanationsThe Ministry of Defense on the new recruitment algorithm to the Armed Forces, the Labor Code and the training centers are obliged to send recruits for service to military units of priority staffing strictly in accordance with the letters provided.
