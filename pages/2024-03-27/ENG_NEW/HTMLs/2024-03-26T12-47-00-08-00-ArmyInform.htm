The chairman of the Ovidiopol Military Medical Commission of the Military Medical Commission in Kyiv prosecutor's office raised suspicion of assisting the illegal transportation of persons across the state border of Ukraine, by prior conspiracy by a group of persons, for selfish reasons (Part 3 of Article 332 of the Criminal Code).

This is said inmessagepress services of the Kyiv City Prosecutor's Office.

According to the investigation, the suspect, together with the accomplices, searched the citizens of Ukraine who wanted to illegally cross the state border of Ukraine and produced certificates of the Military Medical Commission with fictional diseases, which subsequently provided the possibility of crossing the state border.

The cost of such a service cost US $ 5500 for help.

Thus, in December 2023, the suspect, together with the doctor "at the request of the client" for $ 5,500, illegally produced a certificate from the Military Medical Commission and a temporary certificate of the military, which allowed him to cross the state border of Ukraine without interruption.

At that time, the said criminal proceedings were informed of the suspicion of one of the hospitals of Odesa region in assisting the illegally crossing of persons across the state border of Ukraine (Part 3 of Article 332 to Ukraine).

Currently, the same criminal proceedings have been informed of the suspicion of the head of the Ovidiopol freelance military medical commission.

The investigators documented the facts of transferring money to the persons and making counterfeit documents.
