On Thursday, air defense forces destroyed the enemy X-59 rocket in the Dnipropetrovsk region.

About thisreportedEast Air Command on its Facebook page.

The controlled aviation missile X-59 was shot down around 16.05 in the Kryvyi Rih district of Dnipropetrovsk region.

Earlier, the air forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine were warned of the launch of a managed aviation missile in the direction of Kryvyi Rih.
