In absentia, the serviceman of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation was found guilty of violation of the laws and customs of war (Part 1 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

About thisreported March 26Office of the Prosecutor General.

The court proved that on March 5, 2022, the military, while in the household of one of the villages of Chernihiv district, illegally imprisoned a family of two adults and two young children, closing them in the cellar.

The victims for more than 8 hours were forcibly kept indoors with an area of 9.2 square meters. m without access to fresh air, at low temperature, without any lighting in complete darkness, without food and in unhappy conditions.

Asreported the Armyinform,The suspicion of the Russian, who shot a civilian of Chernihiv region, was reported.
