The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine at a meeting on Thursday decided to appoint two Deputy Minister of Veterans.

About thisreportedPermanent representative of the CMU in the Verkhovna Rada Taras Melnychuk.

The government appointed Ruslan Prykhodko and Farhad Farhadov as deputy ministers.

Currently, Alexander Pryhun is temporarily acting by the Minister of Veterans of Ukraine.

As reported by ArmyInform, Oleksandr Porhunstatedthat veteran policy needs dramatic changes and voiced a strategic vision of restarting the Ministry of Veterans.
