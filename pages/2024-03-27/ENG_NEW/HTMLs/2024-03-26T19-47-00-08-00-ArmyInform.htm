The Kiev police explosive equipment seized the combat part of the rocket, which fell on March 25 in the private sector of the Solomenskiy district.

The National Police of Ukraine reported this on the evening of March 26.

Specialists inspected the homeownership and a few hundred meters from private homes found a fragment of a rocket with an explosive and detonators weighing more than 20 kg.

Law enforcement officers disconnected the detonators from the balance of the combat unit and removed them for further destruction in the landfill.

Asreported the armyinform, emergency rescue work after a rocket attack has been completed in Kiev.
