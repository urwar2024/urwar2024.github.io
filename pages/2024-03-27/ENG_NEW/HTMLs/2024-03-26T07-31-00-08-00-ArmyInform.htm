Lithuania President Hitanas Nauseda has appointed the Minister of Defense of the former chairman of the Parliamentary Committee on National Security and Defense Laurinas Kashchuna.

About thisinformsPresident Litvi press service.

“The President, guided by 7th paragraph 84. The Constitution of the LR, taking into account the statement of Prime Minister Ingrida Shimonite, signed decrees on the dismissal of the head of the Ministry of Defense Arvisas Anushauskas and appointment to this position of Laurinas Kashchynasn, ”the message reads.

Laurinas Kashchuasn is a Lithuanian politician and political scientist. Member of the Seimas of the Republic of Lithuania from the party of the Conservatives "Union of the Fatherland - Lithuanian Christian Democrats", Chairman of the Committee of the Seimas on National Security and Defense. Foreign Policy Advisor to the Seimas Chairman (2009–2012), Director of the Center for Eastern European Studies (2012-2016), member of the Ronald Reagan Center for Freedom Ronald and Young Christian Democrats.
