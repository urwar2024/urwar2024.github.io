As a result of the night shelling of the city of Novogrodivka in Donetsk region, one person was killed and another wounded.

This was reported by the head of Donetsk Ova Vadim Filashkin.

The Russians hit the city with two rockets about 3:40-killed a 23-year-old woman. Another woman has been injured and does not need hospitalization.

Rockets were destroyed by administrative building and hangar, damaged a hostel, two private homes and two non -residential premises.

“Staying in Donetsk is dangerous! I urge all civilians to evacuate the regions distant from the front, ”Vadim Filashkin emphasized.

Earlier the Air Force of Ukraine reported that the Russians at nightThey hittwo anti-aircraft controlled C-300 missiles towards Donetsk region.
