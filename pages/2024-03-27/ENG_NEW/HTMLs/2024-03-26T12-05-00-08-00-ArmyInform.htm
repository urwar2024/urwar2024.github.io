The third rehabilitation center for the wounded military national network Recovery was opened in the Dnieper.

About thisinformsMinistry of Health.

“The state -of -the -art rehabilitation center for the wounded military started work in the Dnieper. This is the 11th Center of the National Recovery. Every year, more than 700 defenders and defenders of Ukraine will be able to receive quality, evidence and free rehabilitation services, ”the message reads.

To restore the wounded defenders will be a multidisciplinary team of doctors, which includes specialists of physical rehabilitation medicine, physical therapists, ergotherapists, massage therapists, language therapists and speech, psychologists and others.

"According to the needs of the wounded, such a team can be replenished with other specialists," the Ministry of Health added.

All rehabilitation services are free of charge for all patients and are completely covered by the NSAU as part of a medical guarantee program.

The Center has a physical therapy, psychological support, ergotherapy, medical staff and other office space.

Recovery project focuses on such directions: creation of a national network of rehabilitation centers for wounded military; rehabilitation of military at partnership private medical clinics; sending seriously wounded military for complex prosthetics abroad; International level educational programs for medical professionals.

As reportedArmyinform, The Queen of the Netherlands visited Ukrainian soldiers at the Rehabilitation Center.
