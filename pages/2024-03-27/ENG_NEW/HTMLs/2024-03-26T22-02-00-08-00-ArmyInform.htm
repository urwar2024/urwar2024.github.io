The Ukrainian hospitals, primary health care centers, outpatients, perinatal centers and oncological dispensaries will receive 73 generators with a capacity of 10.8-240 kW for uninterrupted operation under the conditions of possible power outages.

About thisreported March 26Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.

The equipment is purchased within the framework of a joint project of the Ministry of Health of Ukraine and the World Bank "Emergency Response to the Covid-19 and Vaccination in Ukraine".

The generators are divided into Ternopil, Rivne, Chernihiv, Kharkiv, Khmelnytsky, Volyn, Ivano-Frankivsk, Vinnytsia, Poltava, Kirovograd, Dnipropetrovsk, Odessa, Sumy, Zhytomyr, Lviv regions and the city of Kyiv.

Given the constant attacks and firing of Russia's energy infrastructure of Ukraine, providing health care institutions with generators will allow hospitals to work smoothly under any critical conditions.

11139 generators who will work automatically in the event of an emergency are already available for the smooth work of Ukraine's medical institutions.

Asreported the armyinform, there will be no electricity in Kharkiv.
