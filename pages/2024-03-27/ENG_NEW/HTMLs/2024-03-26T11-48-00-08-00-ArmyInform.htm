Prime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal commented on the receipt of $ 880 million from the International Monetary Fund by Ukraine.

This is stated instatementPrime Minister of Ukraine.

According to him, it is the third tranche of financial support within the Extended EFF financing program.

"The funds will help to cover priority budget expenditures and maintain macro -financial stability," Denis Shmigal said.

Ukraine, as he emphasized, continues to successfully fulfill its obligations to partners.

“We are already getting ready for the next viewing of the program. I am grateful to the IMF for support and cooperation! ” - said the Prime Minister of Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, on March 24, 2024, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyystatedthat the IMF has decided to provide a new tranche for almost $ 900 million.
