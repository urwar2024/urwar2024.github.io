A pre -trial investigation in criminal proceedings was initiated on violation of the laws and customs of war (Part 1 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

About thisreported March 26Office of the Prosecutor General of Ukraine.

According to the investigation, representatives of the aggressor state destroyed the ancient historical monument of the Grandfather mound, dated to the V millennium BC, with excavators.

Its modern height reached 6 m and the diameter was 70 m.

Such actions are a violation of the Hague Convention on the protection of cultural values and provisions to the IV Convention on the Laws and Customs of War on Land.

Asreported by ArmyInform, Ukraine and the US will develop a strategy for cooperation in investigating Russian crimes against cultural heritage.
