In the Zhytomyr region, the construction of additional fortifications is underway at the Ukrainian-Belarusian border.

This was reported by the head of the Zhytomyr Regional Military Administration Vitaliy Bunchko.

According to him, in cooperation with the military command, long -term firing positions of reinforced concrete have already been built and engineering barriers were installed: anti -tank ditches, mine fields, dragon teeth.

The erection of additional fortifications is now underway to protect our border.

Despite the changing weather, builders do not stop digging moats and joints and install concrete structures to protect the soldiers of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Asreported the armyinform, nearly 7,000 Dragon teeth are installed in Dnipropetrovsk. AlsoConstruction continuesfortifications in the Odessa region.
