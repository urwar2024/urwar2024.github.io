Operators of the 3rd SCO Regiment during intelligence in the Zaporozhye direction found the enemy's anti-aircraft missile system.

About thisreported March 26Forces of special operations of the Armed Forces.

The SCO operators set the Himars fire on the hostile target of the rocket-artillery unit of the Defense Forces. As a result of the fire damage to the BUK, the BUK was destroyed.

Asreported the armyinform, SCO warriors with surgical accuracy eliminated 5 invaders, destroyed the Station of the IEC and the Alientech antenna.
