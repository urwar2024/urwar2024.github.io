Treaties for the supply of water for the Armed Forces of Ukraine are concluded with the Private Joint Stock Company Obolon and Aquaplast LLC.

About thisinformsState rear operator (DOT).

The contracts were concluded for a total amount of UAH 410 million.

Purchase took place in the Prozorro system.

“Thanks to competition managed to buy water on averagefor 3,46 UAH per liter,what's on39% cheaperthan in contracts before DOT starts. For the quarter, the savings will reach more than UAH 233 million, ”the department reported.

The state operator of the rear stressed that the term of the contract is 3 months.

Supply will start from April 1.
