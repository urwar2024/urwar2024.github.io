In Odessa region, law enforcement officers exposed the attacker who made false documents to the military.

About thisinformsState Border Service of Ukraine.

As it is noted, the border guards, together with law enforcement officers, exposed a man who helped the military to avoid mobilization and go abroad illegally.

“The organizer of the criminal scheme was a local resident of Odesa region. He offered conscripts forged temporary certificates of the military and certificate of the Military Medical Commission on unfitness in military service, ”the State Tax Service of Ukraine said.

The department also said that such a service cost "customers" $ 4,500. The man was looking for buyers because of his personal connections among acquaintances, mostly with the help of "sundress radio". At first, the man received a deposit from a client in the amount of $ 1,000. And already during the transfer of the promised documents he received the second part of the money. Law enforcement officers detained a dilus on hot, during the transfer of the second tranche for the promised documents.

In addition, law enforcement officers found that the attacker offered potential conscripts not only forged military accounting documents, but also forged passport documents of other countries.

Thanks to the coordinated actions of law enforcement officers, the criminal scheme is revealed.

“Currently, the man has been detained in the order of Art. 208 of the Criminal Code and suspected of Part 3 of Art. 332 of the Criminal Code "Illegal Transportation of Persons across the State Border of Ukraine", - the message reads.

Measures to expose criminal actions were carried out by employees of internal and own safety of the State Border Guard "South" of the State Border Guard together with representatives of the National Police under the procedural leadership of the regional prosecutor's office.
