For the evacuated population of Sumy region, financial assistance in the amount of 3600 UAH per person per month is provided.

Assistance is paid within three months at the expense of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees.

About thisreported March 26Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.

You can apply for assistance at the collection points of the BF "Right to Protection".

The humanitarian mission "Proliska" also contributes to the departure of the civilian population from dangerous regions. Including organizes a special medical evacuation of low -mobility, seriously ill people and people with disabilities in specialized transport, accompanied by medical staff.

It was also about supporting the temporary stay of internally displaced persons from Sumy region. International partners have expressed their readiness to provide them with food. In particular, representatives of the humanitarian organization "Man in trouble" assured that they will help drinking water reserves and capacity to store it.

Asreported the armyinform, in three settlements of Sumy region, a forced evacuation of children was announced due to enemy shelling.
