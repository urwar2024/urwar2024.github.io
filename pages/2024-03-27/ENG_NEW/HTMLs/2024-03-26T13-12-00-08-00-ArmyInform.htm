In cooperation with the National Health Service of Ukraine, the Ministry of Veterans Affairs explains to veterans and veterans what rehabilitation services they can receive on a free basis.

This is stated in a message onSiteGovernment portal.

In particular, it is about medical institutions that have a contract with the NSAU, where only evidence -based rehabilitation methods are used.

Restoration of functionality of the patient is engaged in rehabilitation professionals in the field of health care: physicians of physical and rehabilitation medicine, physical therapists, ergotherapists, language therapists and speech, orthotist prosthetics, psychotherapists, etc.

Rehabilitation requires a referral from a family or attending physician.

Veterans can get the following services for free:

  • rehabilitation examination;
  • individual rehabilitation plan and therapy program;
  • neurorehabilitation, orthopedic, cardio-respiratory rehabilitation and others;
  • anesthesia in the hospital;
  • Psychological help.

In addition, rehabilitation is included in a number of medical packages as a component of service. In particular, it is rehabilitation after surgery, in the treatment of oncology, heart attack, stroke.

It is not necessary to pay extra for any components of rehabilitation.

NSAU covers rehab services on two packages:

  • "Rehabilitation assistance to adults and children in hospital";
  • "Rehabilitation assistance to adults and children in outpatient conditions."

Check if the Hospital has a contract with the NSSU on the necessary package can be done using informationdashboardOr by phone contact center of the NSAU-16-77.
