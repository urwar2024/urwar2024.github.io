The punishment was received by a resident of Izium, who after the capture of the city by the Russians voluntarily joined the occupying "branch of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Russian Federation".

About thisShe reported March 26Security Service of Ukraine.

According to the department, the man was initially appointed "district" and later "inspector of the road patrol service" of the Russian pseudo-institution.

After enrollment in the occupying body of the collaborator, they issued uniforms of Russian sample, weapons and a "certificate", with which he moved freely in the captured district center at that time.

While in "positions", the collaborator helped the invaders to fabricate "cases" against the participants of the resistance movement in the region.

To do this, he "handed over" to the Russian invaders the addresses of residence of Ukrainian patriots who remained in the city and resisted the Kremlin regime.

The attacker also traveled the community territory and followed the prohibition from the local occupation administration of the Russian Federation to hold mass meetings.

During such "raids", the racists imprisoned the participants of the pro -Ukrainian actions directly in the middle of the street and taken them to Russian tortures.

After Izum's release, the collaborator tried to "lie down to the bottom" to avoid punishment.

However, he failed. SBU staff exposed the criminal acts of the person and detained it during stabilization measures.

During the searches, the detainee was removed from the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Russian Federation and other evidence of illegal activity.

In view of the cooperation with the investigation, the court found the attacker guilty under Part 7 of Art. 111-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (collaboration) and appointed him 12 years in prison with confiscation of property.

Asreported the armyinform, two traitors were detained, who guarded the Russian torture during the occupation of Kherson region.
