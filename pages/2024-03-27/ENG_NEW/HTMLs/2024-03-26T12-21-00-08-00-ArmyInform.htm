President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky celebrated 19 defenders of Ukraine state awards.

The President decided to award defenders for personal courage and dedication, revealed in the protection of state sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine, conscientious and impeccable service to the Ukrainian people.

The Head of State awarded the Order of Bohdan Khmelnitsky III degree and "for courage" of the third degree, awarded the medals "for military service of Ukraine" and "Defender of the Fatherland".
