Since the beginning of a large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation, Ukrainian air defense has shot over 2000 Russian winged and ballistic missiles.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

"From the beginning of a large -scale invasion, more than 2000 Russian winged and ballistic missiles have been shot down," the statement said.

The Defense Ministry noted that it was the result of titanic work of Ukrainian air defenders. Thousands of lives have been saved thanks to modern air defense systems provided by our partners.

“However, Ukraine still needs more air defense systems to protect our people from Russian terror. After all, civil infrastructure remains the main goal of Russian blows, ”the defense department added.

As reportedArmyinform, on the night of March 26, the Russians attacked Ukraine with 12 shock drones like Shahd, all the enemy drones were destroyed.
