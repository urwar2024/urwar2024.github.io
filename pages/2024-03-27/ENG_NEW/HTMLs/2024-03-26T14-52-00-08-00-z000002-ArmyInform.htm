Over the past 24 hours, 125 Russian soldiers were eliminated in the Kherson and Orikhiv directions of defense forces.

About thisReportThe forces of the South Defense.

It was also confirmed that during the day the enemy destroyed 37 units of weapons and military equipment, including:

  • 12 artillery systems;
  • 19 units of autobroetens;
  • 6 units of special equipment.

As reportedArmyinform, Last week, more than 6 thousand invaders were eliminated, 1169 units of Russian weapons and military equipment were destroyed.
