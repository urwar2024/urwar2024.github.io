The Russian military does not actually use warships after the loss of several vessels for several past weeks.

A spokesman for the Naval Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine told this, the captain of the 3rd rank Dmitry Pletenchuk on the air of the Televahorphone "Unified News".

“The enemy is actually in defense, hid on its bases. For the most part, it is a base on the Naval Base Novorossiysk. There they even closed the entrance to the Bonov barriers, ”Dmitry Pletenchuk said.

According to him, after the destruction of the patrolmanShip "Serggy Kotov"The situation in the Black Sea is more or less stable.

“You can actually fix the absence into the sea. There were several very short stocks, but they were more demonstration. Because it is difficult to call a full -fledged way out near the shore, ”the spokesman added.

Dmitry Pletenchuk noted that this situation will change once and hostile ships will return to the sea, but as of now it is.

"There are now only two small maritime patrols in the sea," he added.
