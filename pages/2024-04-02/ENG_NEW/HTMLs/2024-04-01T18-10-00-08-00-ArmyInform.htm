In the last days of March in Donetsk, the Russians have suffered an epic defeat, trying to attack the positions of defense forces - the destruction of a large column of hostile equipment is confirmed by the 25th separate airborne Sicheslav Brigade.

The success of our boys did not leave Russian propagandists indifferent: military telegram channels of the invaders recognize large-scale losses and demand to punish the perpetrators, accusing the inadequacy of command.

On Monday, April 1,25-a OPDBrandAir Assault Forces CommandThey spread a video that recorded the destruction of a column of enemy equipment, which can count about ten pieces of equipment.

“There is no impossible for us! Sicheslav paratroopers destroyed a column of enemy equipment! Four invaders and two BMPs turned into scrap from the filigree work of paratroopers. No matter how much you are, wherever you hide-we will still find and destroy, ”-described the events in the East in the 25th Brigade.

Information about the large -scale destruction of Russian technology quickly flew through social networks, some journalists write about a slightly more burned enemy tanks and BMPs.

Thus, the invaders attempted perhaps the largest mechanized attack over the last six months. The video of this failure of the Russians is reminiscent of another epic battle near the carbon, when the frames of burnt equipment of the invaders flew the whole world.

Western media have already managedto nameThis battle is potentially the largest "tank massacre" during the large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation into Ukraine. Forbes notes the professional work of the Ukrainian military for having broken such an expensive attack by the Russians.

The stunning fiasco caused a great "swamp". Thus, in one of the so-called "Z-channels", several hundred thousand subscribers publish atypical for propagandists the sharp criticism of the military command of the Russian Federation in this direction and complain of significant losses in live power and equipment.

In addition, supporters of the invasion of Ukraine write that "it is impossible to exchange units" and require Russian military commanders to be reconfigured.

Due to the scale of Z-channel hysterics, the number of burned units of equipment can be much larger than the officially voiced by our defenders.
