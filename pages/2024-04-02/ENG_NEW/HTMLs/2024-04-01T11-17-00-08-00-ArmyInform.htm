The speaker of the House of Representatives of the United States, Mike Johnson, has accumulated that after the Easter vacation, he plans to submit to the draft law on additional assistance to Ukraine, which will include "some important innovations".

About thisinformsThe Hill.

It is noted that in an interview with Sunday Night in America with Trey Gowdy Gowdy, Johnson emphasized the difficult situation in which he found himself, having a historically narrow majority in the House of Representatives. However, he noted that he worked on a package of measures and plans to submit it for consideration when the House of Representatives will be gathered after the holidays.

“We talked to all members, especially now, while working in the district. When we return after this period of work, we will promote the bill on additional assistance to Ukraine, but I think it will have some important innovations, ”he said.

Mike Johnson cited as an example of the possibility of granting a loan to Ukraine, and also mentioned the law on the confiscation of frozen assets of the Russian Federation, which gives the President the right to arrest Russian sovereign assets frozen in the United States and to transfer them to Ukraine for use against Russia.

The Speaker of the House of Congress representatives is also possible to establish exports by the United States of natural gas, which will reduce Russia's income from its sale abroad.
