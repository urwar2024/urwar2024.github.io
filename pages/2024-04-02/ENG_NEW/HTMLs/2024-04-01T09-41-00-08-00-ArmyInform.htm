The occupiers on March 31 at about 22:15 hit Kharkov with two UMPB D-30 (unified interspecific planning ammunition, 30 cm caliber). Ammunition of this type is released from the aircraft.

This is stated inmessageThe Prosecutor General's Office.

In the Shevchenkivskyi district of the city, windows were knocked out in high -rise buildings, a car was damaged.

Under the procedural guidance of the Shevchenko District Prosecutor's Office of Kharkiv, a pre -trial investigation was launched on violation of the laws and customs of war (Part 1 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

Prosecutors and investigators take all possible and proper measures to document war crimes committed by representatives of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation.

The head of Kharkiv Ova Oleg SynigubovReportedTwo buses, a truck and a car were burning as a result of shelling.

The building of an educational institution was partially destroyed, the roof of the dormitory and a residential building were damaged. There were no casualties.

Previously in the Air Force of the Armed Forces of the countrytoldthat the military of the Russian Federation began to use upgraded fugas aviation bombs for strikes in the frontal regions of Ukraine, in particular in Kharkiv.

As ArmyInform reported, on March 27, the enemy for the first time in two yearshit with aerial bombsin Kharkov. As a result of the russian attack on residential areas, 16 residents of the city were injured.
