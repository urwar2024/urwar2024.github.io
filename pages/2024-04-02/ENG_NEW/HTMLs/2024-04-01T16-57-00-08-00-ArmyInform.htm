The suspect supplied the occupiers products from which the dragon's teeth were made for the enemy fortifications.

About thisreported on April 1Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, in 2022, after the temporary occupation of part of the territories of Zaporizhzhya region, the suspect re-registered his company under the legislation of the aggressor state.

He moved to the capital and remotely managed economic activity.

During March-September 2022, his enterprise supplied the contractors with sand, rubble and cement.

From these materials, another society produced reinforced concrete structures "Dragon Teeth", which were transferred to the units of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation for the construction of fortifications.

The entrepreneur has now been informed of the suspicion of collaboration (Part 4 of Article 111-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

At the request of the prosecutor's office, he was remanded in custody.

Asreported the armyinform, the police officer of the occupied bare pier received 15 years in prison.
