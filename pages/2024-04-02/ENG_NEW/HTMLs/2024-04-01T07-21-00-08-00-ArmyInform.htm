During the last day, there were 53 combat clashes at the front. In total, the enemy caused 22 rocket launchers and 79 aviation strikes, making 97 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on the Facebook page.

In Volyn and Polissya directionsThe operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent the transfer of our troops to threatening directions, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy of the offensive (assault) did not take action.

In the estuary directionOur warriors repelled 3 enemy attacks in the area of the settlement of Terny of Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionOur warriors repelled 8 attacks in areas of settlements of Bigorivka of Luhansk region; Verkamyanskoye, Ivanivske, Klyshchiyivka, Andreevka, Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to improve the tactical position.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 13 attacks in the areas of settlements of Berdychi, Semenivka, Uman, Pervomaisko and Nevelskoye Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to knock out our units from the occupied borders.

In the Novopavlovsky directionDefense forces continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of settlements of St. George, Novomikhailivka and Easier of the Vodyany Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops 22 times.

In the Orikhiv directionThe invaders made 2 attacks on the positions of our defenders in the areas of Staromayorsky Donetsk region and a working Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson directionThe enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. Over the past day, he attacked the positions of our troops 2 times.
