In the Lviv region, a border officer was exposed, who promised to ship the conscript age across the border.

About thisreported on April 1State Bureau of Investigation.

According to the investigation, a citizen of Ukraine, who permanently resides and works in Poland, but recently returned to Ukraine to visit relatives, turned to the official for advice.

The man wanted to find out how legally you could get back to Poland, because he did not think about it in advance.

The woman said that he would no longer be able to legally leave Ukraine, but for $9000 she would be able to "help" organize his transfer.

She also suggested that her husband find 3-4 more people who would like to get to Europe with him.

The border guard assured that she would send a "group" by a car to the border with Moldova or Poland, and will contribute to the border crossing.

The official appointed a meeting for the transfer of money in the premises of the real estate agency. After receiving the bribe, the person was detained by law enforcement. The funds have been withdrawn.

Asreported the armyinform, an employee of the Kyiv TCC and SP, who promised a deferment from conscription for $300, was detained.
