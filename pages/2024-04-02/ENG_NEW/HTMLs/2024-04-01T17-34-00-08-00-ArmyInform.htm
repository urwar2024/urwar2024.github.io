The fuel and lubricants are tested for chemical composition before sending the military units.

About thisinformsState operator of the rear.

According to Dot DOT Director Yakim Kovalenko, fuel and lubricants are controlled in two stages.

"The first is quality control at the delivery site. When a batch of fuel and lubricants arrives at the delivery site, the military takes samples and checks the necessary chemical properties," he said.

If the check is successful at this stage, the military is sent to a specialized laboratory.

“The second is the quality confirmation in the specialized laboratory. The laboratory checks the samples of each batch of fuel and lubricants. The chemical composition of delivery is investigated. If the conclusion is positive at this stage, the military can accept this party, ”Kovalenko said.

Therefore, the military receive supply only in case of positive results.
