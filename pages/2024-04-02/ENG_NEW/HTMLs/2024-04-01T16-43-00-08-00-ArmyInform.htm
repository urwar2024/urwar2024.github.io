The meeting with the Netherlands Parliament was held on April 1.

About thisreportedThe site of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine.

“The first foreign visit of the Dutch Parliament delegation after the election is Ukraine. We thank the Netherlands for all the assistance provided from a full -scale invasion and important solutions for Ukraine! ” - said the chairman of the Committee on National Security, Defense and Intelligence Alexander Zaitnevich.

The participants of the meeting discussed the situation on the situation on the front lines, about Ukrainian opportunities in active actions and in defense, about trends in the situation, because the enemy is not considered with losses and is trying to continue to capture new territories with a further target for Western countries.

According to Alexander Zaitnevich, it was once again stated that, despite the sanctions, Russia is able to increase the release of ammunition, replenish its reserves from Iran and North Korea, and even individual components receive from Western companies.

“The key is that Russia must lose. And our country, our partners - to restore security, ”Alexander Zaitnevich wrote.

During the meeting, special attention was paid to the provision of our defenders with artillery and UAV.

"I am grateful to our Dutch colleagues for their support, we rely on their assurances that they will make efforts to help Ukraine deter the enemy and win in every possible way. And we are waiting for the F-16," said Oleksandr Zavitnevych.

Asreported the armyinform, Ukraine and the Netherlands have signed documents on cooperation in the field of defense industry.
