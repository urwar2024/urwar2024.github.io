Crazy attempts have increased recently, disguised as financial assistance from international donors.

About thisreported on April 1Ministry of Reintegration of the occupied territories.

In particular, fraudsters are spreading fake references to cash registration from the International UN Migration Organization (IOM).

In this regard, IOM stressed that it never uses Google Forms to collect any personal data for the provision of multi-purpose cash assistance.

There is no need to enter payment card details on unfamiliar and suspicious websites and follow links from unreliable sources.

If you have questions or suggestions on the work of the IMO, you can call the hotline (from Monday to Friday from 09:00 to 17:00) by numbers:

  • 0 800 215 510 (free for all operators);
  • (095) 106-55-55 (free for Vodafone);
  • (096) 106-55-55 (free for kyivstar).
