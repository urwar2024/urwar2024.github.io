Drones in this war will say one of the decisive words to win, and it should be a Ukrainian word, Ukrainian drones, Ukrainian victory, and therefore more and more effective Ukrainian drones are needed.

On thisHe stressedPresident of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy during a meeting thatSpent1 April. In particular, it concerned the production of drones and all the necessary species of drones - from FPV to shock drones, which show excellent results in deep blows at Russia's military potential.

“Today, they also talked about the nuances of production, support and financing, contracts, and the necessary flexibility in determining priorities. It is exactly what the war requires to be produced by our defense-industrial complex-as much as it is needed, as it is necessary, ”the President said.

He also stated that Ukraine can provide everything necessary for the production of drones.

Recall that on April 1, the Armed Forcesknocked downRussian drone worth almost $ 7 million.
