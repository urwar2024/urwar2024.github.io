Another Recovery Innovation Center was opened in Odessa, which provides free assistance to the wounded military since April 2023. From now on, more than 1,500 defenders and defenders of Ukraine will be able to recover every year.

The new branch of the Odessa Center Recovery is equipped with the world standards of inclusiveness and manufacturability. Due to its opening, the number of beds is increased from 30 to 60. The wards are provided with multifunctional beds with electric drive and functional furniture for comfortable and convenient stay of patients. The whole space is barrier to patients: wide doorways, lack of thresholds, anti -slip coating on the floor, handrails in corridors, inclusive toilet rooms with an area sufficient for persons on wheel chairs.

It also opened additional physical rehabilitation hall, with virtual reality technologies and feedback from the patient, as well as offices of individual and group classes, assimilant technologies, electroneuromiography, ergotherapy, manipulation and horde. The Center is also equipped with additional rehabilitation equipment, including stairs with a sloping frame and a ceiling lift to restore the skills of gait, a stabilloplatform for correction of balance and coordination, a system of rehabilitation of upper extremities with virtual reality technology and feedback from the patient.

"When we developed plans for 2024 at the end of 2023, we planned to open new centers as close as possible to the places of residence of the heroes, to their families. But we also agreed on the development of some RECOVERY centers that are already operating. It is important to invest where there are the best professionals whose eyes are shining. From the first time, I remember the Odessa team, very professional. If you ask the heroes, everyone says that the Odesa center has a great reputation," says the founder of the RECOVERY project.

The complex of simulators and apparatus for rehabilitation of the Odessa Center covers the full range of possible functional disorders, in particular obtained as a result of mine-explosive injuries, and at a high technological level helps to restore the wounded. As with other Recovery Network centers, an experienced multidisciplinary rehabilitation team is working with patients of the Odessa Center, which includes specialists of physical rehabilitation medicine, physical therapists, ergotherapists, massage therapists, speech and speech, psychologists and others.

“To be part of Recovery-first, it is an opportunity to provide quality, evidence-based rehabilitation assistance in advanced conditions for our defenders and defenders. And for our team these are prospects of continuous training, because within the network on a regular basis educational seminars, workshops and trainings are held. Studying and exchanging experiences between teams, we make the whole field in Ukraine, ”says Olena Tarakanova, Head of Re -Tarakanova Rehabilitation Center in Odessa.

To date, 11 Recovery rehabilitation centers provide free services in Kyiv, Lviv, Rivne, Vinnytsia, Cherkasy, Khmelnitsky, Odessa (two institutions) and in the Dnieper (three institutions). In total, nearly 8,000 patients have received rehabilitation assistance from the national network centers.

The plans of the project founders by the end of 2024 to expand the network to at least 16 innovative rehabilitation centers, where about 20,000 soldiers can be restored every year.
