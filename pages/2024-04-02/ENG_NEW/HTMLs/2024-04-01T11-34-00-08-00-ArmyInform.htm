By strikes in Ukraine with hypersonic rockets "Zircon", Russian troops actually carry out their testing in combat.

A spokesman for the Air Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Major Elijah Yevlash said about it on the air"The only news."

“We know a little about this weapon. The enemy is kept secret TTH, our experts now study what features it has. These weapons are used by the enemy and how to test it in combat, to test our air defense systems, ”said Elijah Yevlash.

According to him, only the Patriot anti-aircraft missile can now be knocked down, which has repeatedly proved its high efficiency not only with "zirconic" but also with dagger missiles.

“It is extremely difficult to impress, it quickly gets any point in our country. Therefore, when there is a report of air alarm, we recommend that you go down immediately to the shelter, ”the spokesman added.

Elijah Yevlash also commented on a pause in the hostile shocks of "Shahaneda". According to him, the Russians analyze the information of previous days about the use of fire damage.

“They conduct analysis, find routes where our air defense stations and radar stations. This break is, in particular, work and planning and accumulation, since the Russian Federation cannot constantly perform massive blows, no matter how it wanted it, ”he said.
