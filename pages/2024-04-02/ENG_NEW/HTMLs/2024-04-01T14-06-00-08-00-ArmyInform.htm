The editor -in -chief of the Russian TV channel was charged with encroachment on the territorial integrity and integrity of Ukraine, the promotion of war, justification, the recognition of legitimate, the denial of the armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine, the glorification of its participants and genocide.

About thisreported April 1Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, the suspect, using his media popularity on Russian television, organizes and participates in television socio-political shows with the participation of so-called political experts.

During a speech on one of these shows on December 23, 2023, she publicly called for the physical destruction of the patriotic part of the Ukrainian people - residents of the western regions of Ukraine.

The pro -Kremliv propagandist justifies the armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine, started in 2014, glorifies the persons who carry it out and calls for the genocide of part of the Ukrainian nation.

During an interview with journalists, she made public calls for an aggressive war and change the boundaries of the territory and state border of Ukraine for violation of the order established by the Constitution of Ukraine.

Asreported the armyinform, in the occupied territories of Ukraine, the Russians have established more than 2000 of their towers.
