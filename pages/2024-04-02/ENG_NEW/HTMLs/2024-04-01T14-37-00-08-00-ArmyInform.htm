During 2023-2024 the title of "Honorary Citizen of Kherson" was deprived of four collaborators.

About thisreportedHead of Kherson Military Administration Roman Mrochko.

"With the support of Russia, justification of its aggression against the people of Ukraine, cooperation with the occupying power of the title" Honorary citizen of the city of Kherson "during 2023-2024 were deprived of Sergey Garmash, Larisa Latinin, Yuri Romaskevich and Vyacheslav Gromikhin," he said.

In addition, Roman Mrochko noted that the process of depriving the honorary title of persons who discredited themselves before the community was started last year, and now this work is ongoing.
