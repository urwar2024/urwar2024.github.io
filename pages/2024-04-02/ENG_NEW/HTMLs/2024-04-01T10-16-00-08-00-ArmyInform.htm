Cyber specialists of the Security Service of Ukraine (SSU) have detained another russian intelligence agent who was adjusting missile and drone strikes on Kharkiv.

About thisInformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

It is noted that the offender is an employee of a local kindergarten, who in her free time helped the ruscists to target the bases of the Defense Forces in the regional center.

According to the SBU, for the enemy fire the persons received from the occupiers of the geolocation of potential goals, and then conducted there. Being near the object, she watched it to establish an indicative number of personnel and military equipment there.

"Then the agent sent the received information to her Russian handler via messenger in the form of text messages with coordinates on Google maps. Based on the intelligence 'report', the occupiers were preparing air attacks on the city using missile weapons and Shahed kamikaze drones," the message reads.

Due to the SSU's prompt response, the enemy accomplice was exposed at the initial stage of her intelligence activity, and then everything was documented step by step.

Also at this time, comprehensive measures were taken to secure the bases of the defenders of Kharkiv. Later, the SSU detained the offender in his own home.

According to the investigation, the woman was remotely recruited by Russian intelligence in the fall of 2023. She came to the attention of the occupiers through her acquaintance from the city of Kupiansk, who fled to russia during the seizure of the district center and joined the enemy troops.

On the basis of the evidence collected, the security service investigators informed her about suspicion under Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (State betrayal committed in the conditions of martial law).

The malefactor is currently in custody. She is threatened with life imprisonment with confiscation of property.
