This night, the enemy struck the settlement that borders the aggressor country.

About thisreportIn the SES of Ukraine, reports the Armyinform.

One of the hits came directly to the building of the fire and rescue unit. As a result of the shelling, a third of the fire depot was destroyed.

In addition, the windows and gates of the facade, the interior partitions and the door of the building are damaged. There were 6 rescuers on duty, fortunately none of them were injured.
