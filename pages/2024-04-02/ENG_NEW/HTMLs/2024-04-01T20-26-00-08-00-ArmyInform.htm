Instead of the units of the 4th Army of the Russian Federation, the 20th occupying army is assisted.

About it in the army of TV armyHe told April 1Commander of the platoon of the first battalion of the 95th separate landing brigade, junior lieutenant Stanislav Krasnov.

“The invaders continue to storm the thorns. They do not reduce their efforts, despite the great losses that they bear, ”said Stanislav Krasnov.

According to him, the enemy tries to storm under the guise of aviation, which dumps controlled aviation bombs from aircraft and strikes uncontrollable helicopter aircraft missiles.

“Under this cover, the invaders try to move forward. But they do not work because they are destroyed on approaches. And our artillery and percussion UAVs do not allow them to move forward, ”said Stanislav Krasnov.

According to him, the 25th Army of the Russian Federation operated in the Liman direction, but because of huge losses it is almost completely bleeding, so the 20th occupying army is rotated.

“Different private military companies are also involved, which I conditionally call the Storm Z. They go on the assault, because great losses do not regret them. And they are already following the more prepared consolidation groups, ”said Stanislav Krasnov.

Asreported the armyinform, in the last day in the estuary direction our soldiers repelled 8 attacks of the enemy in the area of the settlement of Terny of Donetsk region.
