President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky presented the Order of the Golden Star to the defenders who were awarded the title of Hero of Ukraine and families of fallen soldiers who were awarded this title posthumously.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

The President expressed his gratitude to every Ukrainian soldier and every commander who fight for Ukraine and everyone who works for a common Ukrainian victory.

“Thank you everyone. I thank moms and dads of Ukrainian heroes for such sons. I thank all our people, our soldiers for the independence of Ukraine. Today, the Orders of the Golden Star, which we celebrate the best Ukrainian soldiers, handed over today, ”the Head of State said.

The attendees paid a moment of silence to the memory of all those killed for the freedom and independence of Ukraine.

Volodymyr Zelenskyy presented the "Golden Star" Hero of UkraineCaptain Alexander Nadenkowhose unit performed offensive actions in the Donetsk region. During the fighting in November 2023, the captain was the first to reach the enemy's positions and destroyed the enemy. Thanks to the skillful actions of Alexander Nastenko, his unit took enemy positions.

The title of Hero of Ukraine with the presentation of the order was awarded toкапітан Едуард Сабанін. During March - October 2023, the unit under his command destroyed and disposed of 52 tanks and other armored vehicles. During the battles in Luhansk region, the captain was seriously injured, but despite this ensured the evacuation of his brothers from the shelling and provided first aid to the wounded soldier.

The "Golden Star" of Hero of Ukraine from the hands of the president receivedSergeant Oleg Fedorov, which in the armored group participated in battles in Kherson region. As a result of the coordinated actions, Russian invaders from the village of Sadka were knocked out. In November 2022, the combat vehicle under the command of Oleg Fedorov occupied a line on the Dnieper bank near the village of Kozatsky, cutting off the occupants of safe departure.

The President also passed the Order of the Golden Star to the father of the fallen Hero of UkraineLeutenanta Oleg Paint, who defended Ukraine in the Donetsk region. Thanks to the competent and decisive actions of Oleh Buyal, the village of Novodonetske was liberated from the occupiers. On June 13, 2023, the enemy launched a massive artillery strike on the positions of his unit, as a result of which Oleh Buyalo was killed.

The Golden Star got the relativesJunior Sergeant Vitaliy Derech, awarded the title of Hero of Ukraine posthumously. In May 2022, the calculation of the Scythian PTRC ("Stugna-P") under the command of the junior sergeant destroyed 15 units of Russian occupiers. As a result of a hostile blow on May 28, 2022, Vitaliy Derech was killed in a battle position.

The head of state passed the "golden star" of the Hero of Ukraine to his nativeCaptain Bohdan Dobbabenko. In 2022, the unit under his command took the leading positions of the enemy in Kherson region and ensured the promotion of other units. Thanks to his skillful actions, the settlements of Ivanivka and Trifonivka were de -Kocoupled. In October 2022, during the liberation of the five -tangibles in Kherson region, the captain destroyed the enemy armored car and a group of Russian infantry, but in the battle the captain came under a massive shelling and died heroically.

The Order of the Head of State received the Native Hero of UkraineJunior Sergeant Alexander Waterun, who participated in the liberation of Kupyansk in Kharkiv region, as well as Stelmakhivka and Novoselivsky in Luhansk region. On May 13, 2023, during a massive storm by the enemy, the junior sergeant arrived on the aid of the units that fought, and despite the dense shelling helped to clear the blockages and provide first aid to the wounded. Oleksandr Kavun suffered injuries and fragile wounds, but refused evacuation and continued the combat mission. As a result of the artillery mines, the Sergeant was injured, incompatible with life.

The President passed the "golden star" to the family of the fallen Hero of Ukraine KApitan Bohdan Kalagursky,who has been fighting for Ukraine since the beginning of russia's full-scale invasion. In August 2022, the commander of a mechanized company held back an enemy assault on Ukrainian positions in the Mykolaiv region, where he was mortally wounded.

The Golden Star also got a familyCaptain Sergei King, to which the highest title posthumously. In 2023, the Rifle Company commander participated in the assault in Kharkiv region. In a battle that lasted almost six hours, the captain was injured and died heroically.

Volodymyr Zelenskyy handed over the Order to the Fallen familyHero of Ukraine Captain Ivan Krymskywhich has been defending the state since 2020. In May 2022, in Donetsk region, the captain, together with the head of the unit, entered the battle from the DRG of the enemy, who surrounded the Ukrainian position, pushed the enemy and was able to evacuate the wounded. Unfortunately, on the same day, the assault group came across an enemy mortar shelling, resulting in Ivan Crimean injured.

The President also handed over the order to the family of the deceasedHero of Ukraine Michael Maksiyan's headquarters,which has repeatedly participated in the reflection of the assault of the enemy, as well as in the assistance of Mariupol's defenders and the liberation of captured Ukrainian territories. The headquarters died in January 2023, while performing a combat mission in Zaporozhye.

The Golden Star Order received a familyHero of Ukraine Junior Sergeant Alexei Mirchavto whom this title is awarded posthumously. In November 2022, during the offensive actions in Luhansk region, he was injured, but continued to fight and ensured the departure of the Ukrainian group. The junior sergeant fired on Russian invaders until he ended the ammunition, and at the cost of his own life did not allow the enemy's breakthrough.

Volodymyr Zelenskyy presented the highest state award to the family of the fallenHero of Ukraine Captain Vitaliy Nevinsky,who has defended Ukraine since 2017. The captain repeatedly destroyed the manpower and equipment of the Russian occupiers, but in December 2022, during the fighting, he was mortally wounded.

The President also handed the Golden Star Order to the Tactical Aviation Pilot's familyColonel Vladislav Rikov, which is awarded the highest title posthumously. Since the beginning of the armed aggression of the Russian Federation, he made 385 combat departments, destroyed more than 80 Russian tanks and 65 armored vehicles, 18 management points and more than 1300 Russian military. The colonel was killed in February 2024 in Donetsk region as a result of the defeat of his aircraft with an enemy rocket.

The "golden star" from the hands of the head of state received the familyJunior Sergeant Alexander Romanov. In 2022, in Kharkiv region, the group under his command ensured the deblock of one of the units of the National Guard of Ukraine, but could not leave in time due to the prevailing forces of the enemy. The junior sergeant was injured, but remained covering the group's departure to the last nail and breath.

The Order was also received from the hands of the President by the family of the deceasedHero of Ukraine Lieutenant Denis Samfal, which since the mid -March 2022 participated in the fighting in the Donbass and in Kherson region, successfully destroyed the living power and equipment of the invaders. In April, during the defense of Kreminna, he was injured in the leg, but already in October was involved in a successful offensive in the Kherson direction, managed the unit during the storming of hostile positions. He died on October 15, 2022.

The head of state presented the "Golden Star" to members of the fallen family in the battles for UkraineSenior Soldier Dmitry Shpak, which in June 2022 took an active part in the defense of Severodonetsk and Lysychansk Luhansk region. Subsequently, he courageously performed special combat missions in the Zaporozhye region. Repeatedly, with the risk to life, he participated in firing raids on boats in the water area of the Kakhovka reservoir on the coastal positions of the enemy. In November 2022, during one of these operations, the group entered the battle with the prevailing enemy forces. Providing a fire cover of the group's departure, Dmitry Shpak suffered a deadly bullet wound.

The Golden Star was also received by parentsJunior Sergeant Artem Sauh, which in the fall of 2022 in the assault unit successfully performed combat missions in the Mayorsky Donetsk region, and since December he courageously participated in the defense of Bakhmut. In March 2023, rescuing the seriously wounded brothers under a dense mortar fire of the enemy, he suffered a fatal fragile wound.

The Golden Star also received the native Lieutenant Colonel of the National Guard Igor Demchuk and Colonel of the National Guard Andriy Priymachenko.
