Today, on April 1, the Ukrainian air defense forces over the Black Sea were shot down by a Russian Frost. This is, by the way, one of the most expensive UAVs of the Russians.

About thisStatingA spokesman for the Armed Forces of Ukraine Major Ilya Yevlash on the air of the Telernado "One News".

“Air Force has worked a good enough goal today, we have such goals infrequently, but we still managed to knock down one of the most expensive unmanned aerial vehicles of the forpost. It is Russian UAV, its cost is about seven million dollars. He was knocked down by the Odesa anti-aircraft missile brigade over the Black Sea, ”Ilya Yevlash said.

According to him, the UAV "Forpost" is a rather "rare bird", is an analogue of Turkish Bayraktar, which can carry additional combat load in the form of two missiles or other weapons.

Elijah Yevlash said that the drone can perform combat missions along the collision line for a long time. He can also carry out intelligence.

As reported byArmyinform, in the Air Force, they explained that the most effective way to reduce the number of hostile blows with managed airbams is to fight the carriers of these ammunition.
