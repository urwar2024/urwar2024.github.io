According to the results of the fifth in 2024, the wave of applications for veterans grants identified 47 winners. They will receive 21 million UAH from the state to start or develop their business.

About thisreportedDeputy Minister of Economy of Ukraine Tatiana Berezhna.

"47 veterans and other spouses were recognized as winners of this year's fifth wave of the veteran component of the Own Business program. The state invests UAH 21 million in starting or scaling their business through grants. In total, 454 veterans and their family members have already received a positive decision to provide a grant from the start of this direction of the program in the total amount of UAH 200 million. A third of these entrepreneurs will open new companies, while others will develop existing businesses. During the implementation of their business projects, they will create a thousand new jobs," said Tetyana Berezhna.

It is also noted that among the 47 winners of the fifth this year the wave of applications for grants 27 participants of hostilities and 20 members of veterans families. 37 winners received a grant up to 500 thousand UAH, 10 - up to 250 thousand UAH.

Most of the granted granuers from Ivano-Frankivsk, Sumy, Ternopil, Cherkasy regions. The most popular areas in which they plan to develop their business granting are wholesale and retail, temporary placement and food organization, processing industry, agriculture, providing other services.

According to the terms of the program, combatants or persons with disabilities as a result of war, as well as their spouse, can receive the following grants:

  • up to UAH 250 thousand, up to UAH 500 thousand and up to UAH 1 million for the veteran, subject to the creation of one, two and four jobs respectively;
  • up to UAH 250 thousand and up to UAH 500 thousand - for the second of the veteran spouse, subject to the creation of one and two jobs, respectively.

Upon receipt of a grant from UAH 500 thousand to UAH 1 million, the veteran must be registered as a FOP for at least 3 years. In addition, such a grant is provided with co -financing with the recipient in the ratio of 70/30, that is, the grant recipient should invest 30% of their own funds, 70% of the funds will be provided by the state in the form of a grant.

Grant applications are submitted through the portal with the business plan.
