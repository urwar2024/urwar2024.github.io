During the day in the eastern direction, the Russian army lost 533 people, 15 tanks, 15 BBM and UAV management.

About thisinformsKhortytsky wind.

As it is noted, the forces of defense continue to inflict on the enemy losses in living strength and equipment.

In particular, in this area of loss of Russia per day are 533 invaders, 15 tanks, 15 combat armored vehicles, 19 guns and mortars, 1 anti -tank agent, 1 air defense agent, 19 units of automotive equipment, 6 remedies of HRS, 348 UAVs, 1 point of management of UAV.

In addition, our defenders managed to destroy 36 shelters, 10 ammunition depots and three PMMs of the invaders.

We will remind, for the day of defense forces of the SouthEliminatedMore than one and a half hundred Russians.

As reportedArmyinform, the total combat loss of the enemy as of April 1 is approximately 442 880 people.
