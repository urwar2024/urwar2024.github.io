Poisonous substances try to force the armor, and dummies are used for intelligence.

About this on the Army TV streamShe told April 1Colonel Natalia Humeniuk, Head of the Joint Coordination Press Center of the South Defense Forces.

“The enemy drops ammunition that secrete a poisonous substance in order to smoke our people from the dugout and then cover with a gap. Ours know about it and respond accordingly: they can throw away a grenade from the armor in time, ”said Natalia Humeniuk.

The enemy also resorts to the use of dummies, which he launches in boats to our shore to check our defense.

“We do not do it, we do not spend ammunition, because we can distinguish between living soldiers and dolls. But still launch boats with dummies to find out if we are waiting. Let them not doubt, we wait, ”Natalia Humeniuk assured.

BeforeThe Armyinform reportedon increasing the use of ammunition with toxic substances by the Russian occupation army in the Zaporizhzhia direction.
