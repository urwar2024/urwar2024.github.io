Ukraine can become the world's first country to use robot-assistant surgery for surgery in the event of a combat injury.

About thistoldChief Surgeon of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Colonel Konstantin Humeniuk, who participated in the second congress of robotic surgeons of Ukraine.

The Armed Forces command of the Armed Forces noted that more than 7 thousand robots of surgeons are already working in 70 countries. One of the most modern is the DA Vinci robot that has already been made in Ukraine overThree HundredOperations in several private and public clinics in the country. -The prospects for robot-assistant surgery are extremely important, since the operation itself is used faster, as well as postoperative recovery of the patient,-said Konstantin Humeniuk.

At the same time, the military surgeon says that he often stands at the operating table for 5-6 hours continuously. He adds that with the help of work he will be able to work sitting, managing surgery with the help of robotic manipulators and 3D visualization. Recently military doctorsspentTwo unique operations.
