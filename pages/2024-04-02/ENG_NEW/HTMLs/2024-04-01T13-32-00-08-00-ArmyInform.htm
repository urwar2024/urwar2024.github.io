Counterintelligence of the Security Service has disrupted the Russian Federation to inflict fire damage to the strategic objects of Ukrzaliznytsia in Chernihiv.

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

It is noted that as a result of the special operation in the regional center, a Russian agent was detained, which prepared a rocket strike on a railway line connecting the northern and eastern regions of Ukraine.

“Another potential goal of the enemy was the platform for loading of moving warehouses. In order to accomplish a hostile task, the person involved in check the intelligence of the occupiers about the possible use of the railway line to move the military equipment of the Armed Forces, ”the department said.

The investigation found that he was hidden to monitor the movement of echelons, and also recorded the directions of their routes and stops.

“The data obtained had to transfer to its Russian curator remotely in the form of coordinates of the railway branch and the loading points of the moving warehouses. This information was planned to be confirmed by photographs of Ukrainian objects, ”the message reads.

The invaders planned to use this information for a missile impact on Ukrzaliznytsia infrastructure to disrupt the Armed Forces logistics.

“However, the SBU staff prevented the transfer of an agent“ report ”to Russian intelligence. This was made possible by timely exposing the traitor and his delay during the enemy task, ”the SBU noted.

According to the investigation, the detainee was an employee of the local unit of Ukrzaliznytsia, an ideological supporter of Rashism.

Since last year, he became an active participant in Russian telegrams, and because of this he got into the field of view of the invaders.

During a search, a mobile phone and a tablet that he used to communicate with an aggressor through a messenger were removed in the detainee's house.

Currently, investigators of the Security Service have informed him about suspicion under Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (State betrayal committed in the conditions of martial law).

The attacker is in custody. He is threatened with life imprisonment.

The special operation was conducted by SBU staff in the Chernihiv region under the procedural guidance of the regional prosecutor's office.
