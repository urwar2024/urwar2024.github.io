As a result of daily bombardment and shelling of civil infrastructure, Russia has damaged or destroyed from the beginning of a large -scale war of 1772 the facilities of medical institutions in Ukraine.

About thisInformsMinistry of Health of Ukraine.

In particular, the department noted that 1564 medical facilities were damaged and another 208 were completely destroyed.

“These are hospitals, outpatient clinics, maternity hospitals and more. The greatest losses were suffered by medical institutions in Kharkiv, Donetsk, Mykolaiv, Kyiv, Chernihiv, Dnipropetrovsk, Kherson and Zaporozhye. It is impossible to obtain comprehensive information about the degree of destruction of medical institutions in the temporarily occupied territories, ”the Ministry of Health said.

Also during this time, the Russian army destroyed 260 emergency medical care, 149 damaged, and 125 another captured.

At the same time, despite the constant attacks and articles from Russia, Ukraine continues to restore medical infrastructure in order to ensure that each patient is able to access quality and necessary medical care. Thus, the Ministry of Health, together with partners, managed to completely restore 512 facilities of medical institutions, 358 more - partially rebuilt or in the recovery process.

“It is about medical facilities in the de -enrolled territories, as well as those that have undergone small damage, such as window knocking out, roof destruction, facade damage and more. In particular, the most restored medical institutions - in Mykolaiv region, Dniprovschyna, Kyiv, Kharkiv and Chernihiv region, ”the department said.

The Ministry of Health emphasized that work on the restoration of Ukrainian medical infrastructure will continue in the future.

In addition, the department reminded that the Ministry of Health recently reported on the restoration of units of the Nikopol District CPMSD, which in the Dnipropetrovsk region, which were injured during another shelling of the region in the first year of the large -scale war.
