The military of the Russian Federation actively use reconnaissance drones in the southern direction, trying to compensate for the absence of aircraft of long-range radar intelligence A-50.

Colonel Natalia Humeniuk said this on a broadcast“Only news”.

According to her, if there were more than 200 intelligence drones last day in the area of responsibility of the Defense Forces of South of Ukraine from Odessa to Zaporizhzhya, then there was less this day.

"But the enemy continues intelligence, and it is obvious that he has the need to compensate for the A-50 air in this way," Natalia Humeniuk explained.

She noted that the enemy gathers information for future strokes of both point and massive attacks.

“Obviously, drone attacks will not stop, because the accumulation of the enemy is. In addition, there was a flight from Tehran, which suggests that the finished products of the ShahED type and accessories in active work, ”the spokeswoman added.

Natalia Humeniuk noted that the wreckage of "Shahmed", which have been found recently, managed to establish that many drones are produced by the enemy in its territory because it larses them with the letters of the Russian alphabet.
