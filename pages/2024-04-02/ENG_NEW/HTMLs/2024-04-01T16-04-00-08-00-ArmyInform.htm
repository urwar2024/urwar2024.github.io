The national flag was raised between Kurdyumivka and Ozorianivka, where the invaders are still staying.

About thisreported on April 1State Border Service of Ukraine.

The flag was installed by aero -intelligence officers from the Phoenix unit of the Guard Brigade of the offensive. To do this, they used a large copter who installed a flag on the water tower.

Asreported the Armyinform,Ukrainian flags were raised over the settlements of Shabelne, Pishchane and Degtyarne.
