Today, April 1, the multinational exercise JCET-2024 began in Moldova, in which military personnel from Romania and the United States of America take part.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defense of Moldova.

Training takes place in the National Army Training Centers. Their goal is the joint preparation and exchange of experience of Moldovan, Romanian and American special forces, as well as increasing the level of operational compatibility between the participants.

According to the exercise scenario, servicemen will perform parachute jumps, day and night, training in the field with combat missions.

JCET-2024 (Joint Combined Exchange Training) is in accordance with the plan of preparation of the National Army of Moldova for 2024.

As reportedArmyinform, the Interflex commander spoke about the training of the Armed Forces.
