In March 2024, the Defense of Ukraine eliminated 28 200 invaders and destroyed 5039 units of weapons and military equipment.

About thisreportedCommander of the Ground Forces, Lieutenant General Oleksandr Pavliuk.

Also, according to him, our defenders destroyed a significant amount of weapons and military equipment of Russian troops, in particular:

  • 376 tanks,
  • 739 combat armored vehicles,
  • 976 artillery systems,
  • 23 RSZV,
  • 53 PPO units,
  • 1 ship,
  • 1546 units of auto -engineering,
  • 215 units of special equipment.

In addition, the military from the defense forces knocked down 2 aircraft, as well as 963 UAVs and 145 enemy rockets.

As reportedArmyinform, the total combat loss of the enemy as of April 1 is approximately 442 880 people.
