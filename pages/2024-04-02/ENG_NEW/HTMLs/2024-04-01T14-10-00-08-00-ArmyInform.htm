The forces of defense for the last day have eliminated more than one and a half hundred Russian invaders and enemy equipment in the south of Ukraine.

About thisinformsThe forces of the South Defense.

According to confirmed data, the enemy on the left bank was less than 156 invaders.

42 units of weapons and military equipment were also destroyed, of which: 11 guns; 1 easel anti -tank grenade launcher; 26 units of autobroetens; 2 Intelligence UAV.

In addition, the Armed Forces destroyed 2 UAV management points.

As reportedArmyinform, the total combat loss of the enemy as of April 1 is approximately 442 880 people.
