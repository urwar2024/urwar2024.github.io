Today, the government has allocated UAH 5 billion for security and defense forces. The funds will be directed to the purchase of drones needed for our front line defenders.

About thisreportedPrime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal.

“We continue to support the Ukrainian army in the fight against the Russian aggressor. Every hryvnia, which comes to the state budget in the form of taxes, will go to strengthen our defense capability, ”the head of government said.

As reportedArmyinform, The President stated that Ukraine will always have its own shock force in the sky.
