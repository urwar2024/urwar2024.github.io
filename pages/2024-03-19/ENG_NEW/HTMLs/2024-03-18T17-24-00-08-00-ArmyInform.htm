Lithuanian Foreign Minister Gabrielus Landsbergis stated that a procedure that mimics voting for the Russian President, which took place in the conditions of unprecedented restrictions on civil and political rights, cannot be considered and called elections.

About thisIt is saidOn the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry website.

“The law cannot arise from unlawful acts, and coercion, oppression and falsification cannot generate respectful international legitimacy. That is why we do not consider or call this fake procedure elections, because, unfortunately, it is more like a tragic farce, ”he said.

According to the head of Lithuanian diplomacy, the procedure was neither fair nor free, nor compliance with any generally accepted standards of democracy and the rule of law.

It should be reminded that the so -called elections were held in the Russian Federation, the victory of which was attributed to Vladimir Putin. BeforeThe Armyinform reportedthat more than 50 UN countries have condemned Russian pseudo -elections in the temporarily occupied territories of Ukraine.

The head of the UK's defense departmentstatedthat Putin stole another "election", but he will not steal Ukraine. Foreign Minister of Italystatedthat pseudo -elections in Russia are "not free or honest."

In the EUmade a statementAgainst the backdrop of "elections" in Russia, noting that the results are void and are not valid.
