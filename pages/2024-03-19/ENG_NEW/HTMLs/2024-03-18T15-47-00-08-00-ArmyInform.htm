The Defense Procurement Agency of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine and the Estonian Center for Defense Investments have signed a memorandum of cooperation.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

As it is noted, the event was attended by Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Dmitry Klimenkov, Director of the Defense Procurement Agency, Maryna Bezrukov, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Estonian to Ukraine Anneli Kolk, ECDI CEO of Magnus-Valdemaar.

“The development of bilateral defense cooperation with Estonia is one of our main priorities. I am sure that further effective interaction between agencies will facilitate the purchase of weapons and military equipment, ” - said Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Dmitry Klimenkov.

The department noted that the parties agreed on the beginning of active work on the implementation of the provisions of the memorandum on cooperation and jointly processing new ways of supplying weapons to Ukraine. Experience will also be exchanged in the field of providing the needs of the Armed Forces of Ukraine and Estonia.

“We are counting on active interaction with the Estonian Defense Investment Center for purchases and joint projects. This will contribute to the modernization of the Armed Forces of Ukraine and to meet their needs, ”said Marina Bezrukov, director of the Defense Procurement Agency.

ECDI Director General Magnus-Waldemar Saar said that the intensification of defense cooperation is a logical continuation of Estonia's current efforts to assist Ukraine.

“Estonia was one of the first assistants to Ukraine, delivering materials before and during the war. The agreement is now even more intensifying cooperation between the two countries, ”Magnus-Waldemar Saar added.

As reported by the Armyinform, the procurement agency of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine“State Tyle Operator”(DOT) improved the quality of goods of three products of products.
