NATO Secretary General Stoltenberg said that the situation on the battlefield remains difficult in Ukraine. Therefore, it is important to strengthen Ukraine's support.

He is about itstatedAt a press conference with Georgia Prime Minister Iraklius Kobakhidze.

«R.The axis continues its pursuit of imperial ambitions.And in Ukraine, the situation on the battlefield remains difficult.Therefore, it is vital that we continue to increase our support.NATO members provide 99 percent of all military assistance to Ukraine.And our partners play a key role in this, ”Jens Stoltenberg said.

NATO Secretary General also greeted the factthat Georgia accepts thousands of Ukrainian refugees and provides important humanitarian and financial assistance.

Also parties while meetingThey discussed the safety in the Black Sea, which is important for both Georgia and NATO.

“The Russian war has threatened the freedom of navigation in the Black Sea and threatened global food supply.But with our support, Ukraine has repelled - destroyed or damaged a large part of the Black Sea Fleet of Russia, ”Jens Stoltenberg stressed.

As reportedArmyinform, Dmytro Kuleba and Jens Stoltenberg coordinated the positions on the eve of the meeting of the Council of Ukraine - NATO.
