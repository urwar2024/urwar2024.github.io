Andriy Yusov made a relevant statement on a liveTelelorphoneThe only news.

The Legion of Freedom of Russia, the RDC just announced the loss of the enemy during this period. We can confirm them. These are very serious figures. More than 600 dead, killed, liquidated occupiers. In addition, more than 800 wounded, also destroyed 20 armored vehicles, APC, BMP and 7 tanks. That is, it is a serious loss on the site, which has long been declared an enemy closed, protected, ” - said GUR.
