The European Union has criticized the presidential elections in Russia, stating that they took place in the face of severe restrictions and complicated by Russia's illegal aggressive war against Ukraine.

This is stated instatementHigh EU Representative on Foreign Affairs and Security Policy of Josepa Borrel.

The EU stressed that they strongly condemn the illegal holding of the so -called elections on the territories of Ukraine temporarily occupied by Russia.

“The so -called elections in these territories are another obvious violation of RSI of international law, including the UN Charter, as well as the independence, sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine. The European Union once again states that it does not recognize and never recognizes neither these so -called elections in the territory of Ukraine nor their results. They are void and cannot have any legal force. The political leadership of Russia and those involved in their organization will be responsible for the consequences of these illegal actions, ”the EU's official position stated.

It should be reminded that the so -called elections were held in the Russian Federation, the victory of which was attributed to Vladimir Putin. BeforeThe Armyinform reportedthat more than 50 UN countries have condemned Russian pseudo -elections in the temporarily occupied territories of Ukraine.

The head of the UK's defense departmentstatedthat Putin stole another "election", but he will not steal Ukraine. Foreign Minister of Italystatedthat pseudo -elections in Russia are "not free or honest."
