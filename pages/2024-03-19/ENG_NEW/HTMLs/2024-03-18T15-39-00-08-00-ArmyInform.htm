33 countries have already joined the international coalition for the return of Ukrainian children, headed by Ukraine and Canada.

About thisreportedCommissioner of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine for Human Rights Dmitry Lubinets.

According to him, the coalition has already been joined: Albania, Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Canada, Costa Rica, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Georgia, Germany, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuan , Moldova, Montenegro, the Netherlands, Northern Macedonia, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Spain, Sweden, Ukraine, the United States of the Kingdom, the United States.

"All countries are making joint efforts to reunite Ukrainian children with families and ensure their reintegration into the family and the community," Ombudsman said.

He recalled that the coalition was started in Kiev on February 2, 2024.

“This is a joint initiative of Ukraine and Canada. It works within the fourth paragraph of the President of Ukraine peace formula, ”Dmitry Lubinets added.

As reportedArmyinform, in Donetsk region, more than 700 people were evacuated from the front line, of whom 68 are children.
