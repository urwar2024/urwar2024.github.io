Since the beginning of the large-scale invasion, the Defense Forces have eliminated more than 13K Russian armored combat vehicles.

About thisReportsMinistry of Defense of Ukraine on Twitter.

“This is an average of more than 500 units of armored vehicles per month. Ukrainian soldiers destroy Russian weapons at incredible speed. Bravo!" - emphasized in the defense department.

As reported by the Armyinform, in February the Defense MinistryreportedAbout 12,000 destroyed Russian combat armored vehicles. In January - about 12 thousandliquidatedRussian vehicles.
