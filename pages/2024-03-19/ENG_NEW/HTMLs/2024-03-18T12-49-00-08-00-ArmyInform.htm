On March 18, the projectile entered a boiler room, which was near the church building in the village of Novoosinovo Kurilov community of Kupyansk region.

About thisreportedState Emergency Service.

The boiler room caught fire. The wood was also engaged. The total area of the fire was 60 square meters.

Rescuers prevented the fire to turn to the church.

The explosions also damaged the village council building and 2 private homes, 1 person was injured.

The village is located near the line of combat collision, so it is now in the area of lesions of Russian barrel and jet artillery.

AsArmyInform reported earlier, almost 600 explosions rang out on the border of Sumy, Chernihiv and Kharkiv regions.
