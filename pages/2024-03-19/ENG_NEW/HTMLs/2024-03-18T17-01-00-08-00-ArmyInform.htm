Prime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal discussed the confiscation of frozen Russian assets and the need for the front at a meeting with US Senator Lindsey Grame.

About this head of governmentreportedTelegram.

“The key issue remains the confiscation of frozen Russian assets. We need to accelerate the work of the required mechanism. Russian assets will become a significant resource to strengthen our country, ”the message reads.

Denis Shmigal stressed that support from the United States is adamant.

“He was convinced of this while meeting with US senator. We discussed our urgent needs for the economy and the front. He thanked the US Senate for supporting the package assistance for Ukraine. Our partners have a clear understanding that we need more support and more weapons, ”he wrote.

The parties also discussed the help of the United States in the implementation of reforms.
