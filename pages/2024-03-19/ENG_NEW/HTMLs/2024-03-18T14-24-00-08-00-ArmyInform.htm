President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a meeting of the Staff of the Supreme Commander-in-Chief. In particular, he heard a report on the situation on the battlefield, the purchase and production of equipment and shells, and the construction of defensive lines.

About thisIt is saidin his telegram channel.

“He received detailed sum from the front. The report of the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Alexander Sirsky in the main areas of active action, ”he wrote.

The Head of State heard reports on the expenses and ammunition, intelligence data. They also went on the state of execution of contracts and contracts for future periods: purchase and production of equipment, weapons, shells.

In addition, he heard reports on the implementation of previous decisions. In particular, on the protection of critical infrastructure: energy, water and gas supply. Prime Minister Denis Shmigal, Viceremery Alexander Kubrakov, Naftogaz Chairman Alexei Chernyshov and Ukrenergo Volodymyr Kudrytsky reported.

The Prime Minister and the Head of the Special Transport Service of the Ministry of Defense Bogdan Bondar were also reported to the continuation of the construction of defense borders.

As reportedАрміяInform, Volodymyr Zelenskyy noted the battle units that best proved to be on the battlefield.
