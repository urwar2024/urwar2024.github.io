Exactly half of them took place in the Novopavlovsky (former Mariinsky) direction.

About it in the morningMarch 18 reportedGeneral Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In total, the enemy caused 14 missile and 75 aviation strikes, and made 95 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements. Unfortunately, Russian terrorist attacks are killed and injured among civilians.

Last day, Russian invaders attacked Ukraine by applying 22 Shahaned UAVs, 17 of which were destroyed.

The settlements of Lukashivka of Chernihiv region suffered during the last day of aviation strikes; Velyka Pisarivka, Popivka, Oleksandrivka, Lugovka of Sumy Oblast and Regional Center; Vovchansk, Gptivka, Basova of Kharkiv region; Netaylovo, Novoselivka First, Shakhtarske, Makarivka, Staromayorsk Donetsk region, robots of Zaporizhzhya region.

Under the artillery fire were about 140 settlements of Chernihiv, Sumy, Kharkiv, Lugansk, Donetsk, Zaporizhia, Dnipropetrovsk, Kherson and Mykolaiv regions.

In Volyn and Polissya directions, the operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directions, the enemy retains military presence in the border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent the transfer of our troops to threatening directions, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border in Belgorod region.

In the Kupyansk direction, the enemy carried out 2 unsuccessful assaults of the positions of defense forces in the area of the settlement of Sinkivka of Kharkiv region.

In the Liman direction, our soldiers repelled 3 attacks of the enemy in the area of the settlement of Rozdolivka of Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut direction, our defenders reflected 8 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Ivanivske and Klyshchiyivka of Donetsk region.

In the Avdeevsky direction, our defenders reflected 13 enemy attacks in the areas of Berdichi settlements, Tonenke, Pervomaisk, Orlovka, Oleksandropol and Nevelsky Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovskaya direction, the forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the districts of Krasnogorivka, Georgiivka, Novomykhailivka and Victory of Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops 36 times.

In the Orikhiv direction, the enemy attacked the positions of our defenders 7 times in the areas of the robot and northwestern Verbov Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson direction, the enemy does not give up the intention of knocking out our units from the bridgeheads on the Left Bank of the Dnieper. So, during the last day, 3 storms of the positions of our troops have been carried out.

At the same time, our warriors continue to be active in the occupation troops of losses in living strength and technology, depleting the enemy along the entire front line.

Aviation of Defense Forces struck 6 areas of personnel, weapons and enemy military equipment.

The missile troops were affected by 3 areas of the enemy's personnel.

Earlier the Armyinform, with reference to the General Staff,reportedthat the forces of defense were neutralized by two Russian DRGs in Sumy region.
