During the day, February 18, the Russian army attacked Nikopol region several times from heavy artillery, fired at the community and three times - the district center.

About thisreportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration Sergey Lisak on Telegram.

“From heavy artillery, the aggressor fired at the communities of Nikopol district three times a day - Mirovsk, Chervono -Grigorivsk and Manganese. And three drones-Kamikadze directed Nikopol himself, ”the message reads.

As a result of the attack, a gas station was damaged. A fire broke out there, which was extinguished by rescuers. A mutilated infrastructure facility.

In addition, 11 private homes, 2 outbuildings and the same car are beaten. And yet - almost six dozen solar panels, transmission lines and gas pipelines. People were not injured.
