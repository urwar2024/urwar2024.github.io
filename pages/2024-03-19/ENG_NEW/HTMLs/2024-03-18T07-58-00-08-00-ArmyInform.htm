The General Staff of the Armed Forces reported the loss of Russians in the last day, as well as their total losses from the beginning of a large -scale invasion.

Data were published by the General Staff of the Armed ForcesMarch 18th.

The losses of the Russians make up:

  • personnel - 431550 (+810) persons liquidated
  • tanks - 6809 (+19)
  • combat armored vehicles - 13014 (+17)
  • artillery systems - 10668 (+34)
  • RSWW - 1017 (0)
  • air defense facilities - 720 (0)
  • aircraft - 347 (0)
  • helicopters - 325 (0)
  • UAV Operative-tactical level-8308 (+36)
  • Winged missiles - 1922 (0)
  • ships / boats - 26 (0)
  • submarines - 1 (0)
  • automobile equipment and tanks - 14141 (+68)
  • Special equipment - 1731 (+8).

AsThe ArmyInform reported earlier, 72 combat clashes took place during the day.
