In the village of Lviv in Beryslav region, three locals were killed who tried to disassemble the FPV-Dron.

About thisreportedHead of Kherson Regional Military Administration Alexander Proudin.

“Men 45, 55 and 67 have brought home previously found in the courtyard of FPV drone and began to disassemble it. As a result, the ammunition was detonated, ”he said.

All three were seriously injured and died on the way to the hospital.

The head of the Kherson Ova expressed their condolences to the relatives and relatives of the dead and urged to be extremely cautious and not to pick up suspicious objects.

As reportedArmyinform, last day, March 17, the enemy fired at Kherson region again, as a result of the attack, one person was injured.
