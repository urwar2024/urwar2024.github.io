The Defense Ministry introduces a new form of training and the procedure for obtaining officers for mobilized physicians.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

According to Deputy Minister of Defense Natalia Kalmykova, from now on the title of junior lieutenant and return to a more appropriate position can only be in 40 days, which include: online courses and 18 days of practical training within the walls of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.

The next course starts on April 8 at the Military Medical Academy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

"Upon completion of the educational process, mobilized medical professionals will not only receive an officer rank, but also an opportunity to return to the unit to work in a more appropriate position for themselves," she explained.

According to Natalia Kalmykova, a new and more flexible form of education was introduced at the order of the Commander of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces - for health workers who did not graduate from the military department and have no officers' rank, but serve in the army in different non -core positions.

Eye courses at the moment have passed 118 mobilized doctors.

Applications to write for courses for mobilized doctors are accepted to an email addressdoz@mod.gov.ua

“At the same time, civilian doctors who are suitable for military service but do not have military education may apply to military departments of medical higher education institutions. In four months of study, they can obtain an officer rank for further service in the Armed Forces in the profile positions for themselves, ”the Defense Ministry said.

As reportedArmyinform, Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova, at a meeting with members of the Kiev Association of Military Attache, spoke about changes in the system of military medicine.
