From March 4 to March 18, 2024, drones of the Army of Drones eliminated 88 tanks, 174 armored personnel carriers, 133 guns and 183 occupiers.

Such dataPublicatedViceremier-Minister for Innovation, Development of Education, Science and Technology-Minister of Digital Transformation of Ukraine Mikhail Fedorov, reports the Armyinform.

According to him, Russian technology is getting smaller. Ukrainian drones on the front successfully continue to disarm the Russian army.

In particular, during this time it was possible to destroy 88 tanks, 174 combat armored vehicles, 180 trucks and 133 guns. More - on graphics.

“There will be more drones and technologies on the front. The invaders and their equipment, respectively, are less, ”Mikhail Fedorov emphasized.

As reported by ArmyInform, the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed two boats and anotherseveral units of Russian equipment in the south of Ukraine.
