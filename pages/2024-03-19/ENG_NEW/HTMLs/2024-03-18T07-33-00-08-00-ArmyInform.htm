On the morning of March 18, hostile military ships were absent in the Black and Azov Seas.

About thisreportedThe Navy of the Armed Forces.

There are 2 enemy ships in the Mediterranean.

During the day, in the interests of the Russian Federation, the passage of the Kerch Strait was made:

18 vessels to the Azov Sea, 8 of them moved from the Bosphorus Strait;

21 vessels to the Black Sea, 5 of them continued in the direction of the Bosphorus Strait.

The Russian Federation continues to violate the International Convention on the Protection of Human Life at the Sea of 1974 (SOLAS), switching off automatic identification systems (AIS).

Asreported the armyinform, in the Navy of the Armed Forces, the Russian Tanker "Mechanic Pogodin" was destroyed in the area of Kinburn.
