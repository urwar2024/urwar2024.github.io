Counterintelligence thwarted the aggressor's attempts to obtain geolocations of air defense systems of the Armed Forces of Ukraine near Avdiivka, Donetsk oblast.

About thisShe reported March 18SBU.

As a result of a special operation, the SSU detained an FSB agent who was reconnoitering the combat positions of Ukrainian air defense in the Avdiivka direction.

The figure was a local resident who was wanted for fraud and theft. In order to avoid punishment for these crimes, in the summer of 2023 a woman escaped to the Zhytomyr region.

Hiding in a rented house, she actively communicated in the chats of Russian Telegram channels, where she tried to find earnings.

Therefore, the malefactor got into the field of view of the Russian special service. Subsequently, she was remotely recruited by the FSB personnel employee.

On his instructions, the agent resumed her communication with former villagers in Donetsk region in order to "secretly" receive the "necessary" information from them.

It was found that a traitor in the guise of household conversations on the phone tried to find out about the facts of the Ukrainian air defense during the racist air attacks.

In the case of "confirmation", she further asked about the approximate base of firing positions of the anti-aircraft missile complexes of the Armed Forces.

Intelligence information was needed by occupiers to "bypass" Ukrainian air defense on the Eastern Front.

However, the Security Service thwarted the enemy's plans. Having exposed the russian agent in advance, the SSU documented her criminal actions and took measures to secure air defence.

At the final stage of the special operation, the defendant was detained in temporary premises. During the search, she found a mobile phone, which she used in intelligence and subversive activities.

The SSU investigators have notified the detainee of suspicion under Part 2 Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (high treason committed under martial law).

The malefactor is in custody. She is threatened with life imprisonment.

Asreported the armyinform, detained a father and son from Brovary, who were adjusting rocket attacks and collecting the remains of the Russians.
