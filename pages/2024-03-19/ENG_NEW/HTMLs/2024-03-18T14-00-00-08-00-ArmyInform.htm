At their meeting, EU foreign ministers will consider increasing the European Peace Facility by €5 billion to support Ukraine and using the profits from Russian frozen assets to support Kyiv.

About thisstatedEU High Representative Josep Borrell.

“I hope we will finally make important decisions today. First, the Ukrainian Assistance Fund with a European Peace Fund of 5 billion euros has been discussed for several weeks. I hope that today ministers will definitely approve it to increase our military support for Ukraine, which is very needed, ”the message reads.

He also informed that today a political agreement would be reached to use the income from frozen assets of the Russian Federation in support of Ukraine.

"I hope that a political decision will finally be made to pick up this unexpected profit, not capital, but income to increase our ability to finance Ukraine's defense," he said.

It is noted that the meeting will be remotely attended by Foreign Minister of Ukraine Dmitry Kuleb and US Secretary of State Anthony Blinken.
