Annual competitions in military-sports all-around, dedicated to the memory of Major GUR of Ukraine Artem Prisyazhnyuk, took place in Zaporozhye.

About thisinformsMain Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

As noted, Artem is from Zaporizhzhia, served in law enforcement agencies and was professionally involved in sports.

"After the start of Russia's large-scale invasion, he volunteered to defend Ukraine, and later joined the ranks of the Kabul 9 special military intelligence unit," the Main Directorate of Intelligence said.

The department also stressed that the GRU officer of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Artem Prysyazhnyuk was killed for the will of the Ukrainian nation while performing a combat mission in December 2022.

"The tournament organized in his honor is being held for the second time," the message says.

According to GUR, professional athletes and young people took part in the championship. The winners received honors and prizes from the GRU of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine and the All-Ukrainian Federation of Military Sports Aupport in Zaporizhzhia region.
