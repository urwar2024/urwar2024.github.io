The band "Mound & amp; Agregat ”together with the organizers of the charity concert tour of Ukraine“ Sivak. Concert Agency »purchased and handed over to the special unit of the GRU 175 drones-Kamikadze.

About thisreported March 18Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

Drones for Ukrainian scouts were raised during the charitable tour of the band Ukraine. The tour, within which concerts in 22 cities of Ukraine are planned to be given. Currently, the artists have already visited 19 cities and raised 12 million hryvnias.

According to the organizers, the speeches were completely charitable: 100% of the raised funds will be directed to the needs of the warriors of defense forces.

Currently, some of the money raised has already gone for the purchase of Dronov-Kamikadze for Timur GUR special unit. Another part will be spent on the necessary equipment for soldiers from the special unit "Kraken", as well as transfer to the strengthening of other units of the security and defense sector.

AsThe ArmyInform reported earlier, GUR is recruiting people to their units of active action.
