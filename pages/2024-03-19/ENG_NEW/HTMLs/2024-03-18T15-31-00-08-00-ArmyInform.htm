The procurement agency of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine "State Tale Operator" (DOT) improved the requirements for the quality of goods of three categories of products.

About thisinformspress service.

"DOT analyzed the technical requirements for three categories of goods and suggested changes that will significantly improve the quality of food," the statement said.

In particular, changes relate to the following categories:

  • Dairy products (cheese, sour cream, butter)-an indicator of lack of vegetable fat is added, which makes it impossible to supply substitutes.
  • Meat (fresh, frozen) - clear requirements for packaging to ensure sanitary standards.
  • Meat and storage products-information on the composition of raw materials in accordance with the State Standard of Ukraine has been added.

It is noted that the relevant changes, after approval by the Armed Forces, were made in the updated technical requirements. Currently, the agency is also preparing projects for changes in the technical requirements for the group "Vegetables/Fruits" and conservation.

As reported by the Armyinform, DotHe concluded contractswith food suppliers for the Armed Forces.
