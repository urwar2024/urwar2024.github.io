The unified electronic register of conscripts, servicemen and reservists "Oberig" is a tool for military accounting of citizens. It aims to reduce the number of errors as a result of the human factor and save resources of the state and human being through digitalization.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

One of the advantages of the Oberig Register is to prevent corruption.

"Thanks to the registry, every step of data change is tracked and confirmed by an electronic signature," the defense department said.

Currently, the Ministry of Defense, together with partners, is working to create an electronic military accounting document.

"This is an excerpt from a registry, which will testify to the accounting status of a citizen - where he is registered, or is suitable for service, etc.", - said at MOU.

The only electronic register "Oberig" was modernized with the support of the European Union within the Eu4digitalua project, which is implemented by the Estonian Academy of Electronic Management.

As reportedArmyinform, the register of conscripts "Oberig" has already been connected to the largest registers.
