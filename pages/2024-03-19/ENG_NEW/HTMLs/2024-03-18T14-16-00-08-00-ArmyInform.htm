In February, representatives of the Central Quality Control Department checked food at warehouses of suppliers before shipment to the units.

About thisreportedMinistry of Defense of Ukraine.

“We systematically inspect the warehouses of companies for supply to the units. If inspectors record the spoiled products, the supplier must replace the party, ”said Deputy Minister of Defense Vitaliy Polovenko.

Another problem is the failure to fulfill its contractual obligations in full.

“For February troops, they did not pass the products for almost UAH 36.479 million. Supplies are disrupted by companies that systematically do not perform their responsibilities. We will openly tell the society about all food supply issues. In all circumstances, our military should not have any problems with food quality, ”Vitaliy Polovenko assured.
