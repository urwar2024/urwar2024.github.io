The border guards of the Guard Brigade of the offensive "Steel Border" broke the armor and the observation center of the Russian invaders.

About thisinformsState Border Service of Ukraine.

"In the Kharkiv region, a hostile armor and the observation post of the invaders were in the lens of border guards," the statement said.

It is noted that as a result, from the shock drones of the border guards the positions of the invaders suffered fire.

The enemy's losses are currently clarified.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Drone Army has destroyed in two weeks88 tanks, 174 BBM, 133 guns and 183 occupiers.

We will remind, the Armed Forces destroyed two boats and stillseveral units of Russian equipment in the south of Ukraine.
