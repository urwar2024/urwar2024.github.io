President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky met with a member of the US Congress Senate Lindsey Grame, who is visiting our country.

About thisReportsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

Volodymyr Zelenskyy informed the American senator about the situation on the forefront and priority needs of the Armed Forces. The President emphasized on a critical sense of further provision of military assistance by partners, including air defense systems and missiles.

Particular attention was paid to the topic of Euro -Atlantic integration of our country.

“Ukraine is counting on further support from the United States of our Euro -Atlantic aspirations. It is also important for strengthening all transatlantic security, ”said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

At the meeting, the parties exchanged views on further strengthening of sanction pressure on the aggressor state and a significant role of congress in this matter. In particular, the importance of completing the formation of a legal framework for the use of frozen Russian assets for the urgent needs of Ukraine was emphasized.

As reported by ArmyInform, the Prime Minister of Ukraine at a meeting with U.S. Senator Lindsey GrahamHe notedWhat should accelerate the creation of a mechanism for confiscation of assets of the Russian Federation.
