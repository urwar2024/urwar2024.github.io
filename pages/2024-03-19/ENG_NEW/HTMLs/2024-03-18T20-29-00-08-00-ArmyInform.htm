President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky thanked the leadership of the European Union and Member States for creating a new fund of assistance to Ukraine within the European Peace Fund.

The head of statereportedDuring the evening appeal to the people of Ukraine.

Volodymyr Zelensky noted that the new assistance fund for Ukraine with a volume of 5 billion euros will support our defensive actions this year.

"We also expect that this Fund will become a component of the general security agreement between Ukraine and the European Union," the Head of State summarized.
