Italy Foreign Minister Antonio Tajani said he did not consider Vladimir Putin free and honest "re -election" in Russia and condemned the organization of voting in the temporarily occupied territories of Ukraine.

He is relevantPublishedIn his Twitter.

“The elections in Russia were neither free nor honest and were conducted in particular in the unlawfully occupied territories of Ukraine. We will continue to work on achieving a just peace, for which Russia will cease an aggressive war against Ukraine in accordance with the rules of international law, ”said the Foreign Minister of Italy.

It should be reminded that the so -called elections were held in the Russian Federation, the victory of which was attributed to Vladimir Putin. BeforeThe Armyinform reportedthat more than 50 UN countries have condemned Russian pseudo-elections in the temporarily occupied territories of Ukraine.

The head of the UK's defense departmentstatedthat Putin stole another "election", but he will not steal Ukraine.
