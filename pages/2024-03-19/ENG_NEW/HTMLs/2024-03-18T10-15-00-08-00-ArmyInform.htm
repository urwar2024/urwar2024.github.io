Two residents of Brovary transmitted data for rocket attacks, as well as collected and arranged reburials for the remains of Russian soldiers who died in the battles near Kyiv.

About thisShe reported March 18SBU.

According to the department, the father and son were preparing rocket strikes of the Russian Federation in the capital region.

In particular, they "drained" the geolocation of the defense forces, including military personnel of the Special Operations of the Armed Forces. The attackers also transferred to the FSB coordinates of industrial shops, where the production of Ukrainian drones was established.

On the instructions of the suspects, the invaders planned to carry out targeted missile strikes on Ukrainian targets.

As a result of the special operation in the Kyiv region, both agents were exposed, their criminal acts were documented and detained.

Synchronous measures were carried out to protect the units of the Armed Forces and production capacity of the defense and industrial complex of Ukraine.

Among other things, these FSB agents performed the specific task of the Russian intelligence service - searched for the remains of the racists who died during the fighting for Kiev.

The persons collected fragments of the invaders' bodies in containers and then taken to the forestry of the capital region and buried.

After that, they sent a 'photo report' to the FSB with reference to the area and coordinates of the burial place of the enemy remains.

According to the investigation, the detainees had previously served in law enforcement agencies.

After their release, they were remotely recruited by an FSB officer, who 'drew attention' to the traitors after their anti-Ukrainian comments on YouTube.

In the initial stages of cooperation with the invaders, the son of the person involved even conducted online training in tactical medicine for the militants of the Russian group "Akhmat".

For the completed tasks, the traitors received a monetary 'reward' from the invaders on their own bank cards.

Currently, both defendants have been notified of suspicion of high treason under Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine.

They are threatened with life imprisonment.

AsArmyInform reported earlier, Ukrainian cyberfactors blocked the supply of components for thousands of Shahaneda and the winged missiles.
