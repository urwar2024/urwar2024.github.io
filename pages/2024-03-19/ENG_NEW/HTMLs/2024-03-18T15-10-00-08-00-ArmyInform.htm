The EU Foreign Council meeting has made the final decision to allocate an additional 5 billion euros for Ukraine's military support within the European Peace Fund.

About thisreportedForeign Minister of Ukraine Dmytro Kuleba.

The minister called on European partners to make all the necessary decisions as soon as possible to increase and accelerate the supply of weapons and ammunition for the Ukrainian military, as well as to develop the Defense Industrials of the European Union and Ukraine.

Dmytro Kuleba also thanked the Czech Republic for the timely initiative to purchase artillery ammunition for Ukraine and all EU member states that have already made their contributions to its implementation.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine stressed that Ukrainian soldiers are now the only line of European defense against aggressive Putin Russia, which seeks to destroy Europe and the normal lives of millions of Europeans.

“The Ukrainian soldier is the only hope of Europe for peace. There should be no restrictions or delays in providing the Ukrainian military with everyone they need to break the spine with Russian occupation troops on Ukrainian soil, ”he said.

As reported by the Armyinform, the EUwill increaseProduction of shells up to 2 million per year. For this purpose, 500 million euros were allocated.
