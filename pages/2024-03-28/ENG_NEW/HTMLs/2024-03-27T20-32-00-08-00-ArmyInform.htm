One person was killed as a result of a hostile shelling of the village of Raygorodok of the Nikolaev community of Donetsk region.

About thisreportedHead of Donetsk Ova Vadim Filashkin.

The Russians fired the village this morning - according to preliminary information, used cassette ammunition.

A 66-year-old man died of shrapnel wounds. 12 private homes are damaged.

Also today is hostile shellingUkrainianSelidov community - 2 people were injured, 29 high -rise buildings were damaged.

“Every day, Russians carry out new and new war crimes on our earth. Staying here is dangerous! Keep yourselves! Evature in a timely manner, ”Vadim Filashkin stressed.
