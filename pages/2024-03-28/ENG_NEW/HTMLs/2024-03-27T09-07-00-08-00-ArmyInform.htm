On the night of March 27, the Russians attacked Ukraine with 13 shaped percussion drones, 10 of whom were destroyed.

This was reported by the Commander of the Air Force Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk.

“The enemy attacked the Shahd UAV from Kursk region - the Russian Federation. A total of 13 enemy drones were used. It was shot - 10 in Kharkiv, Sumy and Kyiv regions, ”he said.

According to Mykola Oleshchuk, anti -aircraft missile units, mobile firing groups, radio electronic struggle and defense forces of Ukraine were involved in the reflection of the air attack.

In addition, the commander thanked for the work done.
