In the Bakhmut direction, the movement of the Russian military on motorcycles was recorded.

About it on airArmy TVThe head of the Public Relations Service of 80 separate airborne assault brigade was told by Major Ulyana Sozanskaya.

“The fact of movement was recently recorded even with hostile motor groups, on motorcycles. But it did not last long in them, ”the spokeswoman said.

Ulyana Sozanskaya explained that the occupiers use motorcycles, as well as Bagi, for faster movement, but our UAV units quickly and effectively reacted and eliminated them.

In general, the situation in the Bakhmut direction, according to the spokeswoman, remains difficult, but controlled.

"The tactics of the enemy have not changed significantly, they continue to promote small groups, but with the intense support of drones, artillery, cabin, facilities, etc.," she said.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Zaporizhzhya direction the military of the Russian FederationusedAt night, vehicles with headlights on the line of combat collision.
