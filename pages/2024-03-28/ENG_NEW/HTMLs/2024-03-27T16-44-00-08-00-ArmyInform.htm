The Security Service has assembled an evidence of three more Russian high -ranking officials who are involved in the aggressive war against Ukraine.

This is stated in a message onSiteSBU.

According to the investigation, the defendants used the civilian vessels of the Mort for the transfer of Russian troops to the temporarily occupied regions of southern Ukraine.

It is about:

  • Alexander Poshivaya - Deputy Minister of Transport of the Russian Federation;
  • Zakhariya Jioeva - Head of the Federal Agency of Maritime and River Transport;
  • Sergey Safonichev is the head of the Federal Institution "Administration of Sea Ports of the Azov Sea" of the aggressor country.

After the capture of Berdyansk, they organized the use of a local maritime trade port for the needs of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation.

This became especially relevant to the Kremlin after the explosion on the Kerch Bridge, when the logistics paths of the invaders were broken and their provision through the temporarily occupied peninsula.

In addition, the defendants organized the erection of the Berdyansk Sea and its waters of Russian fortifications and barriers.

On the basis of the evidence collected, the security service investigators informed in absentia, Jioev and Safonichev about suspicion under Part 5 of Art. 27, Part 2 of Art. 28, Part 2 of Art. 437 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (aiding and absence in the conduct of an aggressive war, committed by prior conspiracy by a group of persons).

As the persons involved in the territory of the Russian Federation, complex measures are underway to hold them accountable for crimes against our country.
