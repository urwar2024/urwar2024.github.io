The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov, together with the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Colonel-General Alexander Syrsky conducted a video call with the new Defense Minister Lithuania Laurinas Kashchynas.

This is stated in a message onSiteDefense Office.

Rustem Umerov and Alexander Syrsky congratulated Laurinas Kashchunas with recentpurposeto the post.

The video call was also attended by the commander of the Armed Forces of Lithuania Valdemaras Rupshis.

Alexander Sirsky informed his colleagues about the operational security situation at the front.

“We discussed ways of expanding our bilateral defense cooperation and thanked Lithuania for their unwavering support. I am waiting for a visit to the Minister Kashchunassus to Kiev in the near future, ”Rustem Umerov said.
