The head of the Department of Press and Information of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Illarion Pavliuk commented on the Russian blasting campaign against Ukraine under the code name "Maidan-3".

Illarion Pavliuk made his statement on March 27, 2024 during a speech at the Kyiv Stratcom Forum 2024: How to Communicate New Challenges to Ukraine and the World (Future Strategy), ",passesMedia detector.

According to the MOU representative, hostile calculations for the third Maidan in Ukraine are absolutely unfounded.

“We can argue how good or bad Ukraine manifests itself in this information war. I would also like us to be much stronger and more present and more influenced. But it seems to me that all Ukrainians are obvious that Russian calculations for the third Maidan are absolutely unfounded. The Russians simply do not realize what Ukrainian society is, they measure us with their measure, ”Illarion Pavliuk said.
