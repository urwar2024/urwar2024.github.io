The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine has explained that it is envisaged by Bill No. 10449 on mobilization and military training.

Explanationwas published in the evening of March 27.

The Law provides for:

  • mobilization from 25 to 60 years;
  • introduction since 2025 in higher educational establishments of basic military training;
  • Some categories of citizens are exempted from undergraduation at universities;
  • Instead of conscript, basic military service for a period of 5 months in peacetime and 3 months in military.
  • Citizens from the age of 18 can choose the time of undergoing military service;
  • Citizens from the age of 18, regardless of undergoing basic military training or service, will receive a deferral until they reach the mobilization age.

Asreported the armyinform, The Defense Ministry responded so to changes in the mobilization process: Ukrainians understand the need to defend the country.
