The army of the Russian Federation on the night of March 27 struck an air strike on raisins of Kharkiv region. There is a destruction, a 64-year-old man.

About thisinformsOffice of the Prosecutor General.

“According to the investigation, on March 27, at about 3:15, the enemy was attacking drones on Izum. High school and adjacent residential buildings were damaged. A 62-year-old man-a security guard of an educational institution, ”was injured,” the message reads.

After the shelling, law enforcement officers seized the fragments of three UAVs.

"Under the procedural leadership of the Izium District Prosecutor's Office of Kharkiv region, a pre -trial investigation was launched into violation of the laws and customs of war," the Prosecutor General's Office said.

As reportedArmyinform, Rosarmia fired at Krasnopil and Znob-Novgorod communities of Sumy region.
