Operational employees of the Chop Border Detachment were detained in the Uzhgorod district of the 60-year-old Transcarpathian, who sent a citizen of Ukraine to Slovakia from Ukraine to Slovakia.

About thisinformsState Border Service of Ukraine.

Law enforcement officers found that a resident of the border village of Suurte met in Uzhgorod with a 30-year-old Lviv resident and offered him assistance in the illegal crossing of the Ukrainian-Slovak border. For this service, the latter paid $ 2000.

The investigation found that at a certain time the men met at the gas station, from where they advanced towards the state border. During their movement, they were stopped by border operatives. The perpolector left his ward and resorted to escape, but in a few hours the search for the offender was found on the outskirts of Uzhgorod.

"Transcarpathian was notified of suspicion of committing a criminal offense under Part 3 of Article 332 of the Criminal Code" Illegal Trail of Persons Against the State Border of Ukraine ". The decision of the Uzhgorod City Court was chosen by a preventive measure in the form of night arrest, ”the message reads.

Investigative actions are currently underway.
