As a result of Russian attacks, on March 27, Kharkiv was killed and 12 were injured.

This was reported by the mayor of Kharkiv Igor Terekhov.

“Two in serious condition. The five -story buildings where people lived are severely damaged. The institute of emergency surgery was also injured, ”Igor Terekhov wrote.

"There are four children among the wounded," said Oleg Synigubov, the head of the Kharkiv Regional State Administration.

How the aggressor struck the civilians of Kharkiv is now being established.

Asreported the armyinform, in Nikolaev there are 6 victims as a result of a Russian rocket strike.
