One person was killed and 18 was injured during a Russian blow to Kharkiv.

About thisreported on the evening of March 27Office of the Prosecutor General.

A pre -trial investigation into the violation of the laws and customs of war combined with premeditated murder was started (Part 2 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

A number of high -rise buildings, medical institutions, cars, school, kindergarten and other civil infrastructure objects have been damaged.

According to preliminary data, the enemy hit two UMPB D-30 (unified interspecific planning ammunition, 30 cm caliber).

This is the first time that the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation hit this type of ammunition around the city. The ammunition was released from the plane.

Asreported the armyinform, ammunition, which the Russians hit Kharkov can fly up to 90 km.
