The court was sent to the court with respect to the deputy commandant of the Russian troops, who ordered the imprisonment of pro -Ukrainian residents of Kherson region.

About thisinformsKherson Regional Prosecutor's Office.

"Under the procedural leadership of the Kherson Regional Prosecutor's Office, an indictment was sent to court on a resident of Donetsk region upon issuing an order for ill -treatment of a civilian population (Part 2 of Article 28, Part 1 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine)," - the message reads.

The investigation found that the 39-year-old accused during the temporary occupation of the region was appointed to the position of "deputy" of the so-called commandant-the chief of the garrison of Russian troops. The military unit was stationed in the territory of one of the sanatoriums in the village of Lazurne, Skadovsky district.

“The accused ordered the subordinate to search the apartment and imprison the patriotic citizens of Ukraine. This was done with the use of physical violence and threat of weapons, ”the department said.

As it is noted, the detainees were imprisoned in the basement that is not suitable for people there. At the behest of the accused, they were subjected to torture and abuse without providing medical care.

Special pre -trial investigation was carried out by SUNP in the Kherson region.
