The minibus was immediately detained in Bukovina with eight men of conscript age, each of which paid 8 thousand euros.

About thisShe reported March 27State Border Service of Ukraine.

According to the department, the passenger minibus of the brand "Renault" in the middle of the night was heading to the Ukrainian-Moldovan border. Men from Kharkiv, Donetsk, Poltava and Kyiv regions were in the minibus.

As it was previously established, the driver for a monetary remuneration, in the amount of 8 thousand euros per person, had to provide "pseudotourists" illegal transfer outside the checkpoints to Moldova.

With regard to those who want to illegally get abroad drawn up protocols under Art. 204-1 of the Code of Administrative Offenses "Illegal Crossing or attempting to illegally cross the state border of Ukraine".

The driver, as an organizer, threatens the criminal liability, provided for in Art. 332 of the Criminal Code. The sanction of the article provides for imprisonment for up to 9 years.

Asreported the armyinform, in Odesa region, a trucker was exposed, who took money from the evasion depending on the amenities.
