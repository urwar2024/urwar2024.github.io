On Wednesday, the Air Force units of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed a managed aviation missile in the Zaporozhye region.

About thisreportedEast Air Command on its Facebook page.

The rocket was destroyed between 4:22 and 17:15, it was at this time that air alarm was declared in the Zaporozhye region.

The East Air Command also reported that at about 18.00 in the Dniprovsky district of Dnipropetrovsk region the enemy UAV SUPERCAM was destroyed.

As reported by the Armyinform, earlier on Wednesday the warriors of PEC "East"knocked downWinged rocket in the sky above the Dnipropetrovsk region.
