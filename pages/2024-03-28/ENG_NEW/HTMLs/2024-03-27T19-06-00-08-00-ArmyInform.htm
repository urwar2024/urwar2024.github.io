The head of state visited the wounded defenders in Sumy region and celebrated them with state awards.

The president was about itreportedduring his or her evening address.

Volodymyr Zelensky thanked all medical professionals and everyone who helps the military recover from injuries.

“The second one today is, of course, all issues of protection of Sumy region, every community, every city. It is especially difficult in the border: there is a permanent Russian terror. Constant shelling, aviation blows, counteraction to the sabotage groups of the enemy. I am grateful to all soldiers, all commanders and armed forces, and the soldiers of the border service, and our police, the National Guard, the Security Service of Ukraine - to all those who are involved, to all who protect, ”the President stressed.

Earlier the Armyinformreportedthat Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited the TRI brigade in Sumy and checked the construction of fortifications.
