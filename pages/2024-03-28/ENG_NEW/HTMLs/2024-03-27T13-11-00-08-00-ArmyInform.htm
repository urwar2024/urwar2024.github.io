In the Khmelnytsky region, an employee of the Tax Code and its accomplice was exposed, who for $ 10,000 promised to "resolve the issue" about evasion of mobilization.

About thisreportedSpecial Prosecutor's Office of the Western Region.

According to the investigation, an employee of the regional shopping center and a local dentist for money promised to give the military a conclusion that he is not suitable for military service for health reasons, with further exclusion from military accounting.

The dentist offered this service to his patient during treatment. For $ 10,000, he and his girlfriend from the CCC had to influence members of the Military Medical Commission and military officials for making a positive decision.

Both of the suspect has now selected a measure of restraint in the form of detention with the right to make bail.

The TCC serviceman was removed from office. The involvement in the crime of other persons is checked.

As reported by the Armyinform, operative employees of the Chop Border SquadThey were detainedIn the Uzhgorod district of the 60-year-old Transcarpathian, who transported a citizen of Ukraine to Slovakia from Ukraine to Slovakia.
