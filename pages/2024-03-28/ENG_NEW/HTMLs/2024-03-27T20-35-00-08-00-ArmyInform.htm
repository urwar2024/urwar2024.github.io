In the WhatsApp messenger is spreading a new fraudulent scheme, the purpose of which is to access user accounts.

About thisShe reported March 27Cyber Police

According to her, users receive a message with reference to the fake page of "Electronic Petitions" about a shortage of a few votes in order to gain the required number of signatures to assign the title of Hero of Ukraine to an unnamed relative.

In fact, a petition link requires additional login in WhatsApp, after which fraudsters get access to a personal WhatsApp-Account.

The Center calls for such messages to block the number from which the SMS was sent, and in no case to go on unknown links.

BeforeArmyinform reported,that in the cyber police told how to reach the defense forces, not fraudsters.
