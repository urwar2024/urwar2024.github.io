Two servicemen of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation, in the manner of special trial, were found guilty of violating the laws and customs of war committed by a group of persons by prior conspiracy, combined with premeditated murder.

About thisreported March 27Office of the Prosecutor General.

They were sentenced to life imprisonment by the sentence of the Ichniansky District Court.

According to the investigation, in March 2022 the commander of the Tank Platoon of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation and his subordinate - the tank commander, while in the village of Olshany of the Pryluky district, two civilian cars were shot dead by automatic weapons, which did not pose any threat to them.

In one of them, two men carried bread and food to the residents of the Ichnian OTG, and in the other - two residents went to the hospital in the city of Pryluky. Drivers and passengers were killed on the spot.

In addition, Russian servicemen took over the mobile phones of dead people.

Asreported the armyinform, reported suspicion to two Russian generals who bombed Borodyanka.
