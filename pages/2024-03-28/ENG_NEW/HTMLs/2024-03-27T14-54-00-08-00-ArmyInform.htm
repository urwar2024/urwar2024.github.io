The enemy remotely changes our land with ball shaped air bombs Shoab-0,5. Their diameter is almost seven centimeters. Such aviation is used by aviation.

About thisinformsSES of Ukraine.

The SHAB-0,5 Air-Bombs of Russia are used in the border areas of Ukraine. One RBC-500 cassette contains 565 SHAB-0,5 ball aircraft weighing 400 grams each. Burning from them with a diameter of about 70 cm, deep - almost 10 cm.

“Outwardly, they look like a small ball of silver. Children can attract their appearance. So warn about the danger of loved ones, ”rescuers warn.

The department emphasizes that these ball aircraft have a large area of lesion (6–10 meters): When they fly and fall to the ground, some of them do not detonate.

"So there may be no touch sensor from any touch, although they do not have a seismic sensor," the SES of Ukraine noted.

SHAB-0,5 is a sub-bar, which consists of two aluminum hemispheres, fastened with a mowing ring. It has about 304 steel shrapnel balls.

As reportedArmyinform, in the 118th brigade recorded the use of ball-shaped balloons.
