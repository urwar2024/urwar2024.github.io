On the competitive selection "It is worth starting your own business" of the Ukrainian Veterans Fund of the Ministry of Veterans received 219 projects. Currently, each of them is carefully worked out by a team of specialists.

The Ministry of Veterans of Ukraine informs about it.

In particular, the completeness of the submitted documents and compliance with the established requirements, including anti -corruption verification of the applicants, are checked.

“Please note that the date of receipt of the application package is the date when all the documents were delivered to the fund through the SRM system. Each applicant will receive information on the date of registration of his or her application and its number through the email specified in the application form, ”the department said.

The Ministry of Veterans also noted that the responsible structural unit of the Ukrainian Veterans Fund of Ministry of Ministry of Ministers accepts documents and within 5 working days from the date of receipt, in chronological order, performs technical selection for compliance with all established requirements.

“We strive to provide a fair and transparent selection of each project. If you have a question or need to clarify the information on the submitted application package, please send your appeals to uvfprojects@veteranfund.mva.gov.ua with the letter "Competition of Projects" You should start your own case "and your application number. This will effectively process your appeal and give you a comprehensive answer, ”the message reads.
