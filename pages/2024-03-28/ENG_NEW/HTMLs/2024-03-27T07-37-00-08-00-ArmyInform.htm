The participants of the Swedish government delegation visited the Kyiv City Clinical Hospital of War Veterans and talked with medical staff and patients of the institution.

About thisreportedFirst Deputy Head of the Kyiv City State Administration Mykola Povoroznik.

Sweden's Deputy Prime Minister-Minister for Energy, Business and Industry of Sweden, Ebba Bush, assured her country's ready to cooperate with Kiev in the field of health care, in particular in the rehabilitation of Ukrainian servicemen.

According to Nicholas Povoroznyk, the conditions of stay of our unbreakable warriors and the level of equipment of the branches where they are rehabilitated, made a great impression on foreign guests.

“The level of preparation of rehabilitologists and the results of their work deserve special attention. It is thanks to them that our defenders and defenders after rehab in the hospital have a chance for a better life after serious injuries received in the battles for our Ukraine, ”he said.

The participants of the Swedish government delegation assured the doctors who would continue to cooperate with the city in the health care field, in particular in the rehabilitation of Ukrainian fighters.

As reportedArmyinformThe Ministry of Defense has returned the health care facility where the wounded rehabilitation center will be created.
