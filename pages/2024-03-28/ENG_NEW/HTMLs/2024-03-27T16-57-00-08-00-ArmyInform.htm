Volodymyr Zelenskyy made a visit to Sumy on March 27.

About thisnotifiedon his official page.

The head of state met with servicemen undergoing treatment after injuries in the Kupyansk, Bakhmut directions, near the village of Krynka in Kherson region.

The President was told about the course of treatment and restoration of Ukrainian soldiers, including seriously wounded. He asked the well -being of the military and wished them a speedy recovery.

"Get well, guys. All you are the best. Thank you for your service, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

The President presented state awards to the wounded defenders of Ukraine: Orders for Courage of the 3rd degree, the medal "For military service of Ukraine", a medal "Defender of the Fatherland".

The President also examined the rehabilitation equipment, the ergography office and talked with doctors.
