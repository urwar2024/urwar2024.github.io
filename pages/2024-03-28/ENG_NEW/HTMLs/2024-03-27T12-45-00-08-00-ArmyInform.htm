Law enforcement officers exposed the administrator of the telegram channel, who is suspected of appropriation of almost 1 million UAH of charitable contributions for the Armed Forces of Ukraine in the Dnipropetrovsk region.

This is stated inmessageOffice of the Prosecutor General of Ukraine.

Residents of Kyiv reported suspicion of unlawful use of charitable donations during martial law (Part 3 of Article 201-2 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

According to the investigation, the suspect-the administrator of the famous telegram-channel Nikopol-has repeatedly organized the fundraising allegedly for assistance to the Ukrainian military.

To transfer money for military equipment, cars, quadcopters of the pseudo -mortar indicated the details of her bank cards. Then she assigned voluntary donations and spent her own needs.

So, in half a year, the woman received almost UAH 1 million from citizens.

During the searches at the place of residence, law enforcement officers seized computer equipment, mobile phone, cash.
