The defense forces of the South of Ukraine have eliminated more than a hundred invaders during the day, as well as destroyed or damaged 31 units of weapons and military equipment of the invaders.

About thisreportDefense forces of South of Ukraine.

“The forces of the South further inflict fire damage to the locations of the enemy, fire positions and rear. During the day, they received confirmation that the enemy in the Orikhiv and Kherson directions became less than 116 invaders, ”the message reads.

It was also confirmed that during the day the enemy destroyed 31 units of weapons and military equipment, including:

  • 2 BUK anti-aircraft missile complexes;
  • 5 artillery systems;
  • 1 mortar;
  • 2 stations of radio electronic struggle;
  • 1 CCTV complex "Mur-M";
  • 18 units of autobroetens;
  • 1 aerial photo fixation station;
  • 1 boat.

The destruction of 3 felds of ammunition was also recorded.

As reportedArmyinform, in the last day in the Kherson and Orikhiv directions of defense force 125 Russian military.
