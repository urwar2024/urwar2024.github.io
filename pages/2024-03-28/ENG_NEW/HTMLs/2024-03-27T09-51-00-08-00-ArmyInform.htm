President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky congratulated the actors on the occasion of the International Theater Day and thanked those theater figures who mobilized in the defense forces of Ukraine.

This is stated inGreetingsPresident of Ukraine.

“Many actors, the workers of the scene took their arms and defend our land. And they fight, like all Ukrainians, not only with the occupier and his terror, but also with the dominance of the "commanding Russian culture", whose representatives cynically destroy, plunder and destroy our cultural heritage, "Volodymyr Zelenskyy emphasized.

He noted that contrary to the war, theatrical figures are actively developing, enriching the repertoire and collecting full halls.

“It is nice how the viewer responds to your plots. Incredibly, as a siren and anxiety theatrical art inhales life and strengthens faith, ”the President of Ukraine said.

In free Ukrainian cities, he said, theaters that have fled from the hell of the fire are being restored, and new bright projects are born here.

“Thank you for the strength and sustainability, for the theaters and performances in bomb shelves, for volunteer staffs, for charity performances for the Armed Forces. Your talents and perseverance support the spirit of resistance, add faith to our inevitable victory, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy emphasized.

As reported by the Armyinform, in Donetsk regionHe spokeIn front of the children, the clown from Italy.
