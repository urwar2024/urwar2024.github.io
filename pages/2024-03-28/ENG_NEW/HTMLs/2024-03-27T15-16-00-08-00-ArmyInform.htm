Since April 2, the International Register of Losses caused by Ukraine to the Russian war will be opened for applications from the victims.

About thisreportedin the register.

«April 2 2024 RD4U (register of losses from Russian aggression)Officially opens the process of filing claims for damages, losses or injuries caused by Russian aggression against Ukraine, ”the message reads.

The opening and presentation of the Register will be held in the Hague within the framework of the Ministry of Official Conference "Restoration of Justice for Ukraine". It will be attended by the Secretary General of the Council of Europe Maria Burich, heads of international organizations, ministers and high-ranking officials of 60 countries.

“The destruction of houses during the war has a huge impact on people's lives. Expected to be in this category will be from300 000to600 000claims. The form of the statement of claim for submitting this category of requirements was also approved, ”the department said.

It is noted that claims will be filed with RD4U through the Ukrainian digital system, the mobile application and a wicked portal, which are widely used and trusted by 20 million Ukrainians. In addition, the integration of action into the extensive infrastructure of digital services and state registers in Ukraine will facilitate the applicants for the process of collecting evidence to support their requirements.

As reportedArmyinform, Ukrainians will be able to apply to the International Register for damages caused by Russian aggression to apply to the International Register.
