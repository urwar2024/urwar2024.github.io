The Commander of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Oleksandr Pavlyuk spoke about the restoration of the combat capability of military units, withdrawn from the area of combat missions.

Video Recovery from one of the brigadesPostedOn the Facebook page.

The first stage is vacations, treatment, and preventive health care measures.

Further, the warriors are waiting for training in training centers. Both basic - for newcomers and professional - for future tasks.

After the coordination, the restored units will return to the front to give the enemy's battle with renewed vigor.
