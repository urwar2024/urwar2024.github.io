During the last day, on March 26, pyrotechnic units of the SES of Ukraine surveyed the territory of 236.33 hectares and neutralized 533 explosive objects.

About thisinformsState Service of Ukraine for Emergency Situations.

In total, during the day pyrotechnic units of the SES were involved 133 times.

“Most often pyrotechnic units worked: in the Kharkiv region 28 thousand 085, Kyiv region - 9 thousand 840, Donetsk region - 11 thousand 052, Mykolaiv region - 7 thousand 373, Kherson - 11 thousand 188, Chernihiv - 5 thousand 335, Sumy region, Sumy region. - 3 thousand 238, Cherkasy region - 1 thousand 791, " - said in the department.

As it is noted, from the beginning of a large -scale military invasion of the Russian Federation in the territory of Ukraine 477 thousand 796 units have been disposed of. explosive objects and 2 thousand 948 kg of explosive, including 3 thousand 271 units. aviation bombs. The territory of more than 122 thousand 725 ha was surveyed.
