The state of the rear operator (DOT) received the delivery of tactical headphones for the Armed Forces. Before supplying to the troops, they undergo quality control.

About thisinformsThe state operator of the rear.

DOT and the Central Quality Control Department of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine selected control 10 samples from a party of tactical headphones. They will be sent to an independent laboratory for quality check.

“Our task is to work out a step -by -step quality check. It includes selection of quality samples, and the next stage - acceptance control of the goods by quality, ”said Colonel Valery Tokar, the head of the territorial center of quality of real property control of the quality property of the central region.

After the positive results of the laboratory control, the whole batch will be delivered to the warehouse of the military unit.

Another check will take place during acceptance. Only then will the goods receive the goods.

Before supplying the Armed Forces of Ukraine, clothing and ammunition are tested for quality in three stages as it happens - in informationArmyinform.
