The evidence was collected and the material was transferred to the court for five more traitors who fought against the defense forces in eastern Ukraine.

About thisShe reported March 27Security Service of Ukraine.

According to the investigation, at the beginning of a large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation they lived in a temporarily occupied part of Donetsk and Luhansk regions.

From there, they voluntarily "mobilized" to the occupying groups of the Southern Military District of the aggressor country.

In the ranks of the Russian troops, the persons were stormed by Ukrainian cities on the Eastern Front and erected fortifications, including "Dragon Teeth".

In February this year, the attackers were captured by Ukrainian defenders during fierce battles in the areas of Avdiivka, Marinka and Bakhmut.

Among the detained military counterintelligence of the SBU - a sniper of a hostile unit, which in June 2022 fired at the positions of defense forces in Severodonetsk, and later fought in Donetsk region.

Another militant - was originally a sanator in the evacuation unit of a medical platoon of the invaders, but soon passed "courses" in the tactics of hostilities in the conditions of urban development.

After that, the traitor was appointed commander of the assault department and transferred to the village of Bigorovka in the Kramatorsk direction.

The other three detained - participated in the "meat" storms of Avdiivka and Marinka, and also captured the city of Rubizhne in Luhansk region.

Currently, investigators of the security services have been informed by the persons suspected of two articles of the Criminal Code of Ukraine:

  • h. 1, 2 tbsp. 111 (treason committed in martial law);
  • Part 7 of Art. 111-1 (collaborative activity).

The attackers are threatened with life imprisonment.

Asreported the armyinformThe agent group of the FSB was detained, which was preparing rocket strikes on the Kiev TV tower and units of the General Staff of the Armed Forces.
