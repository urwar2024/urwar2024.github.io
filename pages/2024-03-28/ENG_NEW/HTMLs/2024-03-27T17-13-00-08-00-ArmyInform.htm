Within the working trip to Sumy region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky visited the location of the 117th separate territorial defense brigade.

About it reportsplaceHeads of State.

The president heard the reports of the commander of the operational-tactical group "Siversk" Major-General Dmitry Krasilnikov and Kombrig-Colonel Maxim Aksamitovsky on the operational situation in the area of responsibility of the brigade and cooperation with neighboring units.

The commander of the 117th Separate Brigade TEP reported on the ratio of forces, equipment and weapons, informed about the features and dynamics of use by the enemy of managed airbots.

During the meeting, the President discussed the need to combat this type of weapon with the commander.

Volodymyr Zelensky was told about the organization of defense on three lines of borders. The current needs for weapons and equipment were discussed.

The head of state also inspected the team point of the brigade, in particular the department of support forces, the department of work of air defense, the point of adoption of decisions.

The President spoke with the defenders of Ukraine and thanked them for their daily service during the war.

“Thank you for protecting Ukraine, our sovereignty and territorial integrity. I wish you health, your families and, of course, victory. It depends on our future, ”the Head of State said.

Volodymyr Zelensky also expressed gratitude to his relatives and close servicemen who support and inspire our defenders, and wished the soldiers strength.

The President awarded servicemen of the territorial defense forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the State Border Service and the National Guard with the orders of Bohdan Khmelnitsky III degree, "for courage" of the third degree, medals "for military service of Ukraine" and "Defender of the Fatherland".

The President also inspected the construction of fortifications in Sumy region.

“I looked at trenches, armor, fire and command and surgery. We strengthen our protection. Thank you to everyone who is involved in this work, ”he said.

As reported by the Armyinform, Head of StatemetIn Sumy with military personnel who are treated after injuries in Kupyansk, Bakhmut directions, near the village of Krynka in Kherson region.
