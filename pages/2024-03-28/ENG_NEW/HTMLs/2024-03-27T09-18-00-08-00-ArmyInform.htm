At night and morning, the Russians carried out 2 shelling of border areas and settlements of Sumy region. 16 explosions were recorded.

About thisinformsSumy Regional Military Administration.

The shelling was suffered by Krasnopil and Znob-Novgorod communities.

In the Krasnopil community, the enemy beat mortars (10 explosions). Under the shelling there was also a ZNOB-Novgorod community, it recorded 6 explosions.

As reportedArmyinform, the enemy per day made 386 beats in the Zaporozhye region, there is a destruction of housing and infrastructure.

Last day, March 26, enemy againfiredKherson region, as a result of the attack, three people were injured.
