The declaration for medical practice for military units is now available through the portal.

She reporteddeputyDefense Minister Natalia Kalmykov on her Facebook page.

“In cooperation with the Ministry of Health of Ukraine and the Ministry of Digital Transformation of Ukraine, we have simplified the process of obtaining permission for medical practice: conveniently, quickly, without unnecessary documents and queues. Now this procedure takes only a few minutes, ”Natalia Kalmykova said.

The filing of electronic declarations through the portal simplifies the process, making it prompt and effective.

It is a step towards digitalization in the army and avoiding corruption risks.

The first declarations have already been submitted, allowing you to provide medical assistance at the forefront.

Important: The declaration is valid until the end of martial law plus 3 months after its completion and remains valid when the commander of the military unit is changed.
