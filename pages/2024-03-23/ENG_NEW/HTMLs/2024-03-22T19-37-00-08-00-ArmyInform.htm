The state operator of the rear presented the concept of reform of procurement in the field of defense and the first results of the delegation from the Dalo Agency for the Procurement and Logistics Support of the Danish Ministry of Defense.

About thisIt is saidon the website of the defense department.

In particular, DT CEO Arsen Zhumadilov spoke about the concept of procurement reform and the results of 100 days of work.

The teams of DOT and DALO also discussed the prospects for cooperation of purchasing agencies in the future.

“I am impressed with the results of the DOT in such a short time of work of the agency. We support the steps of DOT in the construction of a transparent, accountable and effective system of providing in accordance with NATO standards and plan to work out a joint vision of future cooperation, ”said Dalo Commander Lieutenant General Kim Jesper Jerogensen.
