The Security Service has collected a evidence of another Russian occupier, who is involved in the massacres of civilian population during the fighting for Kiev.

This is stated in a message onSiteSBU.

It is about Timur Seferov-a 32-year-old contractor of the 15th separate motorized rifle brigade of the Central Military District of the Armed Forces of Russia.

In the occupied village of Mokrets of the Brovarsky district, Rashist, together with other invaders, fired private apartments, sniffed at them and selected property from people.

It is documented that during one of these "raids", Seferov wanted to pick up a mobile phone from a 53-year-old resident.

Feeling resistance from the man, the Russian made several shots from the machine gun directly in the courtyard. As a result of severe gunshot wounds, the victim died on the spot.

On the basis of the information collected, investigators of the Security Service informed Seferov of suspicion under Part 2 of Art. 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (violation of laws and customs of war).

Currently, materials are being prepared to announce the suspect in the international wanted list.

Complex measures are also underway to establish the location of a military offender from the Russian Federation and his punishment for the murder of a citizen of Ukraine.
