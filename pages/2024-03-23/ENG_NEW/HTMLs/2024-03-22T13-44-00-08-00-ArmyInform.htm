At the border with Slovakia stopped the pseudonygers, who was traveling by the bus to leave Ukraine through the checkpoint "Uzhgorod".

About thisinformsState Border Service of Ukraine.

As it is noted, he provided a copy of the foreign passport of a citizen of Nigeria for passport control. The man told the border guards that he lives in Odessa, and lost his passport during the latest shelling in the city.

“When the inspector compared the photo with the bearer, he noticed that the photo shows another person. In addition, law enforcement officers found that the owner of a foreign passport left the territory of Ukraine at the beginning of a large -scale invasion, ”the department said.

The SSSU noted that during the conversation he confessed that he was in fact a citizen of Ukraine and lives in Kiev. Given the similarity with a foreigner, he decided to use a photo of his passport and invented a legend.

“The Ukrainians were refused passing across the border. For him, administrative and procedural documents under Part 1 of Article 204-1 of the Code of Administrative Offenses "Illegal Crossing or Attempted Crossing of the State Border of Ukraine" was drawn up. The case will be transferred to court, ”the message reads.
