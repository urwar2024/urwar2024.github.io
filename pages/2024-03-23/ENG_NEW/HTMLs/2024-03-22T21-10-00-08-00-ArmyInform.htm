Special forces of the Security Service of Ukraine "White Wolf" have already destroyed the third tank regiment of the Russians.

About thisInformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

The agency said that since the beginning of the large-scale invasion, fighters of this unit alone, as part of the Special Operations Center "A" of the Security Service of Ukraine, have destroyed 296 tanks.

"Given that the staffing of the Russian Army Tank Regiment is 94 units, our boys" opened "a separate account for the fourth regiment," the SBU emphasized.

The department also added that in total, the "wolves" hit more than 800 units of enemy equipment at the front.
