President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky celebrated 364 defenders of Ukraine state awards, 268 of them - posthumously.

The corresponding decree№166/2024Posted on the website of the Head of State.

The President decided to award defenders for personal courage, revealed in the protection of state sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine, as well as for selfless military duty.

The Head of State gave Bohdan Khmelnytsky's servicemen and "for courage", awarded the medals "For Military Service of Ukraine", "Defender of the Fatherland" and "For Saved Life".
