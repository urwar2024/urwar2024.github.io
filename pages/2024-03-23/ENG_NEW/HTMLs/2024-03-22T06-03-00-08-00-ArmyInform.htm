Another photo story from the Armyinform is the story of snipers of a separate tank brigade named after Konstantin Pestushko.

The history of jewelers who accurately and meticulously eliminate the enemy.

Meet Vladimir with the call sign "Best". And it was he who eliminated the Russian officer from the distance of 1800 meters. The bullet went straight into the heart.

He learned the Best about his work, when he monitored Russian television - there was a story about the funeral of one commander, to whom the "hostile" (that is, our Ukrainian) sniper kissed the heart. The place of action pointed to those positions on which Best worked.

Everything happened in February 2022 in Luhansk region, four days before the large -scale invasion of Russia. The commander of a hostile intelligence unit received a bullet from the Armed Forces when he started the Russian military in the trench.

For over a year, the army of this military unit performed tasks in one of the most difficult areas of the front - Bakhmut. Now are other problems and challenges, but our snipers remain unchanged.

Therefore, training and honing skills are required for these military specialists. For example, on a landfill in Donetsk, snipers work out archery.

With the help of a special device, the sniper measures humidity, wind, pressure - not key performance for their work.

But most of the results of their work, combat outputs and risky operations will be known in a while - but will definitely be included in history textbooks.
