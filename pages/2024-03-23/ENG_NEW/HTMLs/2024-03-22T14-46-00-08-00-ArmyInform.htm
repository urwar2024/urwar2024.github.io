Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Lieutenant General Ivan Havrylyuk said that in Ukraine the production of ammunition has increased several times how to compare with the beginning of 2022.

Ivan Havrylyuk made his statement during a live broadcast in TV.“Single news”March 22, 2024.

“I do not want to talk about numbers, I do not have the right because it is a limited information. But I tell you with a clear responsibility that the production of ammunition, mortar mines in Ukraine has increased at times compared to 22, ”he said.

According to Ivan Havrylyuk, Ukraine today produces the entire spectrum of artillery ammunition.

“In the state of development there is an ammunition 155 mm. We produce the rest of the ammunition, ”the Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine emphasized.

According to him, the growth and scaling of Ukrainian production is taking good steps.

"I think, by the end of the year, we will reach the plans that we have determined by the President of our country," Ivan Havrylyuk said.

As reported by the Armyinform, in February 2024 Ivan Havrylyukstatedthat Ukraine increases its own production of ammunition of different nomenclatures.
