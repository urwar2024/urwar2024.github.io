The Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk, the Commander of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces, visited the heroes who undergo treatment and rehabilitation during a business trip to Visbaden (Germany).

This is stated in a message in the television channel of the commander.

“Many of our soldiers were seriously injured. But here they can undergo a full recovery and care of the highest level, ”said Alexander Pavlyuk.

He added that this is the invaluable help that our partners provide today.

“After all, Ukrainian doctors have been intensively working for more than two years to save the lives of wounded soldiers and a victim of civilian population in Ukraine. Often - in very difficult conditions, ”the commander said.

Oleksandr Pavlyuk presented departmental awards and honors while communicating with our warriors.

“We are waiting for a full recovery and return to everyone and everyone! Thank you to partners for caring about our heroes! I will never forget this support in the most difficult time, ”the commander emphasized.
