The Netherlands Prime Minister Mark Rutte said that a large number of ammunition would be sent to Ukraine thanks to the Czech Republic's initiative.

About thisIt is saidon his Twitter page.

"It was a pleasure to talk to the Czech Prime Minister Peter Fiala and Denmark Prime Minister Mette Frederixen about the supply of ammunition to Ukraine," he wrote.

Mark Rutte added that in the coming weeks and months, the Netherlands will continue to do everything possible to support Ukraine by giving priority to artillery and air defense.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Defense MinistrytoldAbout significant growth of ammunition production in Ukraine.
