“The situation is under control. Energy are already working to restore power supply. We unfold in the area of unbreakable ones to work, people can charge gadgets and be constantly communicated, ”the message reads.

“In fact, these are preventive shutdowns to balance the power system. We keep calm. Experts carry out restorative work, ”Philip Pronin added.
