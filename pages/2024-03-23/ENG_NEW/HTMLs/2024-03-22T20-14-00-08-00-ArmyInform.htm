The US Ambassador to Ukraine Bridget Brinka responded to the Russian rocket attack across Ukraine on the night of March 22 and stressed that Ukraine needs urgent assistance from the United States.

About it reports Bridget Brinka wrote on Twitter.

“Today's brutal blows - 165 missiles and drones - have been the biggest attack on Ukraine's energy system since the beginning of the war of Russia. More than 2.5 million Ukrainians are faced with serious power supply. It is critical that Congress urgently assists Ukraine, ”the message reads.

As reported by ArmyInform, United States Ambassador Bridget Brink once againcalledTo provide Ukraine with more assistance against the backdrop of massive missile attacks of Russia.
