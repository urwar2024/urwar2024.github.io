The countries of the North Atlantic Alliance can and should do more to support Ukraine, otherwise in 2-3 years it will be too late.

About thisstatedDenmark Minister Trols Lund Pulzin during the 16th Annual Kyiv Security Forum.

"Why am I here today? To communicate with the people of Ukraine and declare that we will be here as long as it takes," he said.

The Minister stressed that NATO countries should work for the long run, since Russia's aggression applies not only to Ukraine.

“Russia will not stop and go on. If we do not act today, it will be late tomorrow. Today, a key factor is a factor of time. In 2-3 years it will be late to "wake up" to all other countries, "Trols Lund Pulzen emphasized.

In particular, he noted the insufficient expenditures of some NATO members on defense and problems of the European military-defense complex, in particular with the production of shells. Pulseen also urged the West countries to accelerate security investments.

"We have to show Russia that we are ready to defend ourselves," the Minister of Defense said.
