The first Ukrainian pilots, which have undergone intensive training under the guidance of instructors of the British Royal Air Force, graduated from a flight school.

This is stated in a message onSiteBritish government.

"10 pilots have undergone basic flight, ground and language training in the United Kingdom and one day will become the first line of defense in defense of the sky of Ukraine from Putin forces," the statement said.

Now they will move to in-depth flight training provided by the French Air Force before learning to control F-16 fighters.

In the framework of the preparation of pilots, they taught the general control of plane, flights on aircraft devices, low -level navigation and pilot in complex formations under the guidance of experienced instructors of the British Air Force.

They have also been prepared in English, which will help them in learning and enhance the operational compatibility with NATO's military-air forces, as English is a standardized international language of flight.

Closely working with the French Air Force over the reconciliation of our curricula, the pilots will continue their studies in France, where they will undergo in-depth flight training before they go to study on the F-16 aircraft themselves.

"I would like to congratulate these brave pilots on completing their initial training here in the UK. Thanks to the RAF's world-renowned skills, these pilots have received the best training and are now one step closer to joining the fight against Putin's illegal invasion," said British Defense Secretary Grant Shapps.

Over the past two years, the United Kingdom has played a leading role in the development of the Air Force of Ukraine, equipping its aircraft with additional equipment, and has become the first country to provide Ukraine with a distant radius of action.

As reported by the Armyinform, NATO Military Committee Chairman Admiral Rob Bauerpredictedthat F-16 fighter jets will have an impact on the situation on the battlefield in Ukraine.
