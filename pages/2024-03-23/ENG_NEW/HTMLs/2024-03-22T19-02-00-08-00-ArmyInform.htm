The command of the Land Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine decided to check the state of affairs at the National Academy of Land Forces named after Hetman Peter Sahaidachny.

About thisIt is saidOn the Telegram Command page.

“Cases that have been published by the Verkhovna Rada's temporary investigative commission will be checked for the real state of affairs. In case of confirmation of these cases, all necessary measures will be taken, and the responsible officials will be applied to the penalties in accordance with the current legislation of Ukraine, ”the department said.

The Land Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine noted that the command is interested in a comprehensive inspection of a higher military educational institution in order to provide an objective assessment of all cases. The results of the audit will be brought to the public.

“At the same time, we emphasize that the war is ongoing and the discredit of servicemen only plays the enemy's hand. Currently, any issues concerning the military are sensitive. We recommend that officials, regardless of status, are responsible for communicating issues related to the performance of their duties by military personnel. Especially - about the accusation of certain officials and teams, ”the statement reads.

At the same time, the command emphasized that emotionally colored accusations and attempts by populist cliches are unacceptable. It is necessary to check the information carefully and only after confirmation of real cases make any statements.
