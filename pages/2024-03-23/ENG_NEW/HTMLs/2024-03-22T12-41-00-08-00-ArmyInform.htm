The death toll from the enemy rocket shelling of Zaporozhye increased to three.

The head of Zaporizhzhya Ova Ivan Fedorov informed about it.

The number of wounded also increased to 14 inhabitants. Two more people are considered missing.

Two rocket strokes in the private sector of 7 private homes have been destroyed and dozens were damaged.

Two entrances and dozens of apartments are damaged by a rocket stroke near a high -rise building.

The traffic was stopped by Dniproges dam, which was also fired by the invaders. There are no interruptions in the city at this time.
