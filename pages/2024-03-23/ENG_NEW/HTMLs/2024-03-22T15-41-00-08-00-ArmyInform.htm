The number of wounded from a hostile rocket firing of Zaporozhye increased to 20 people on the night of March 22.

About thisreportedHead of Zaporizhzhya Ova Ivan Fedorov.

As a result of the impact of the Russian Federation, the girl and her father, as well as the trolley driver, were killed in Zaporozhye.

“An 8-year-old girl and a 35-year-old man are daughter and father. The Russian rocket was killed in the private sector of Zaporozhye. The morning hostile attack and the life of a 62-year-old man-a driver of a trolley, moved by a dam of a hydroelectric power station. There were no passengers in transport, ”he added.

As reported by the Armyinform, as a result oftwo rocket strokesIn the private sector, 7 private homes are destroyed and dozens are damaged. Two entrances and dozens of apartments are damaged by a rocket stroke near a high -rise building. The traffic was stopped by Dniproges dam, which was also fired by the invaders.
