On the night of March 22, the enemy struck a combined missile and audience strike on the objects of critical infrastructure of Ukraine.

About thisreportedCommander of the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk.

The enemy used a 151 air attack:

  • 63 SHAHED-136/131 UAVs (Primorsko-Akhtarsk-RF);
  • 12 Iskander-M ballistic missiles (Belgorod region-Russian Federation, Crimea);
  • 40 winged rockets X-101/X-555 (out of 13 strategic Tu-95MC bombers-Caspian Sea water area);
  • 5 winged X-22 missiles (from five TU-22m3 bombers-Rostov region-Russian Federation);
  • 7 aerobalist missiles X-47M2 "Dagger" (out of ten MiG-31k-Tambov region-Russian Federation);
  • 2 managed aviation missiles X-59 (from two SU-30 (SU-34)-TT of Zaporizhzhya region);
  • 22 anti-aircraft managed missiles C-300/C-400 (Belgorod, Kursk region-Russian Federation).

Air Force and Land Forces, mobile fire groups, radio electronic struggle of the Defense Forces of Ukraine were involved in the reflection of rocket and aviation strike.

As a result of airfill, 92 air targets were destroyed:

  • 55 SHAHED-136/131 shock UAVs;
  • 35 winged missiles X-101/X-555;
  • 2 guided aviation missiles X-59.
