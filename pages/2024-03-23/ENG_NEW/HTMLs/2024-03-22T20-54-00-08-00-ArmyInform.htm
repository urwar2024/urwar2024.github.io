Due to the SSU's evidence, another russian military intelligence agent operating in Zhytomyr region was sentenced to a real prison term.

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

At the beginning of a large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation, the attacker tracked the places of base and moving the defense forces involved in the protection of the capital.

He also handed over to the aggressor of geolocation of several enterprises of the Defense and Industrial Complex of Ukraine.

The SSU Counterintelligence detained him in July 2022 as a result of a multi-stage special operation in the region.

The court appointed the attacker for 15 years of prison with confiscation of property.

As the investigation established, a former employee of one of the local defense plants was a hostile associate.

"During the battles for Kyiv, he was remotely recruited by an officer of the Main Directorate of the General Staff of the Russian Armed Forces (known as the GRU). He was involved in cooperation through the banned social network VKontakte, where the traitor published his anti-Ukrainian comments," the department said.

During the search of the suspect's home, the SSU seized computer equipment and mobile phones, which he used in reconnaissance and subversive activities in favor of russia.

“On the basis of the evidence collected, the court found it guilty under Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (State betrayal committed in martial law), ”the message reads.

The investigation was conducted by SBU staff in the Zhytomyr region under the procedural guidance of the regional prosecutor's office.
