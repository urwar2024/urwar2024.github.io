In Zaporizhzhia, work continues to eliminate the consequences of russian missile strikes. 10 people were injured, 3 more are missing.

About thisReportsMinistry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.

Under the procedural leadership of the Zaporizhzhya Regional Prosecutor's Office, an investigation was initiated in criminal proceedings on violation of the laws and customs of war, combined with premeditated murder (Part 2 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

According to the investigation, on the morning of March 22, 2024, the russian military preliminarily launched 20 missile strikes on the territory of Zaporizhzhia.

The city's infrastructure was damaged and destroyed, including the Dnipro Hydroelectric Power Plant, private and apartment buildings, and civilian vehicles.

Currently known aboutone deadas a result of the shelling of the Dnieper Hydroelectric Power Plant.

Emergency services and law enforcement officers are working at the scene, recording another crime of the russian federation. The number of victims is being established.

As ArmyInform reported, Russia todayShe hiton hydraulic structures and a dam at the Dnieper Hydroelectric Power Plant in Zaporizhzhia, but there is no threat of a dam break.

The air defense forces destroyed 92 air targets during a massive attack of the Russian Federation in Ukraine.
