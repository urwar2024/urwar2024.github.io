According to the decisions made, the optimization of military organizational structures is ongoing, which are directly involved in the reflection of large -scale armed aggression of the Russian Federation.

About thisreportedCommander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, Colonel-General Alexander Sirsky.

"Over the ten years of the war, temporary military administration and areas of responsibility of troops were formed and changed in accordance with the operational situation and current challenges," he said.

According to the Committee, organizational measures are being taken into account by the experience of hostilities. The main purpose of these measures is to simplify and maximize the quality and efficiency of management.

“Some troops (forces) will be reformatted. First of all, it is about managerial staffing potential, its competent and rational redistribution, ”the Colonel General emphasized.

One of Alexander Sirsky's priorities is to establish the process of rotation of the personnel of combat brigades.

In particular, according to the head of the Ukrainian army, some military units are involved in the reflection of the armed aggression of the Russian Federation from the first days of a large -scale invasion. That is why it is important to pay more attention to the recreation of our soldiers, the completion of weapons and military equipment and the restoration of brigades in general.

“This process is already launched and in the future it will only increase. We intensify the defensive capabilities to continue to destroy the enemy and bring the victory closer, ”Alexander Sirsky summarized.
