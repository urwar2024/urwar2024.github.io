President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky held a meeting with Viceremier-Minister-Minister of Defense of the Kingdom Denmark by Troels Lund Polesen, who is in our country on a visit.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

First of all, the head of state thanked him and his team for the visit and continuous support of Ukraine by Denmark.

"We are sincerely grateful to the Danish people, who have been with us since the very beginning of the large-scale invasion. Thank you for all the defense aid packages from your government, they are very important for us," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

The President noted the recent visit of Prime Minister of Denmark Mette Frederiksen to Lviv, during which a bilateral agreement on security cooperation and long-term support was signed. The Head of State expressed confidence that the implementation of this Agreement would not only strengthen the Ukrainian army, but also help in the recovery of the country's economy.

The interlocutors condemned the latest massive missile firing of Russia of the territory of Ukraine and emphasized the urgent need to strengthen the Ukrainian air defense.

The preparation for the Danish-Ukrainian Conference of Defense Industries and the prospects for establishing direct interaction between companies of the MIC of the two countries and joint production of weapons was discussed.

Separately, the parties discussed cooperation within the artillery and aviation coalitions and training of Ukrainian pilots on F-16 aircraft, which Denmark and other partners are preparing to transfer to Ukraine.
