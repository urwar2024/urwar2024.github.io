Khmelnitsky became aware of two dead people and at least 8 wounded as a result of a Russian attack.

About thisreportedMinistry of Internal Affairs Igor Klimenko.

The minister also posted a video with a 21-year-old girl's rescue from a private home.

Also, according to the head of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, 6 injured in Zaporozhye, 3 persons are considered missing at the scenes. Information on the victims will be promptly specified.

“This morning, the enemy attacked infrastructure objects, there is a destruction of residential buildings and victims among civilians. Under the blows of the enemy were Zaporizhzhya, Khmelnytskyi, Odessa, Dnipropetrovsk, Poltava, Mykolaiv region, Vinnytsia, Lviv and Ivano-Frankivsk, ”the message reads.

All services are involved in eliminating the effects of the attack. Robotic technique is used in hazardous areas to minimize rescuers.

Every citizen can seek assistance to the SES and the National Police if necessary.
