In Ivano-Frankivsk oblast, rescuers extinguished a fire at a critical infrastructure facility caused by russian shelling.

About thisinformsSES of Ukraine.

The ignition report came to the rescue service at 03:40. The total area of the fire was about 900 square meters.

At 8:48 a.m., the fire was localized, and at 10:06 a.m., it was extinguished. According to preliminary information, 2 people were injured at the scene.

169 personnel and 56 pieces of equipment were involved in the elimination of the fire.

As reported by the Armyinform, in ZaporozhyeWork is ongoingfrom eliminating the consequences of Russian rocket strokes. 10 people were injured, 3 more are considered missing.
