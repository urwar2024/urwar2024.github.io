Soldiers of the 8th Regiment of the Special Operations Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed the enemy's mortar position and manpower with the help of FPV drones.

The video was published on the SCO YouTube channel.

The enemy's mortar position and personnel near it were discovered during reconnaissance operations in one of the operational areas.

Having established the coordinates of the targets, the operators of FPV drones inflicted damage, killing 5 Russian servicemen and wounding four more.

The position of 82 mm mortars was also destroyed and BMP was damaged.
