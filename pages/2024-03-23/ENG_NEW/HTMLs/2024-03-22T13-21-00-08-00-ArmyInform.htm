Today, Lithuania handed over anti-drone systems to the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

About thisinformsLithuanian Defense Ministry.

"Lithuania continues to provide uninterrupted military support to Ukraine - on March 22, the Lithuanian control system has reached the country with Lithuania," the statement said.

The Lithuanian Ministry of Defense added that the military support provided by the National Defense System to Ukraine includes a variety of weapons, ammunition, drones, antitrones, training of soldiers, repair of heavy equipment and all other support provided in accordance with the needs expressed by Ukraine.

"Lithuania has already developed a plan of long-term support for Ukraine, 200 million euros are planned for 2024-2026," the department said.

As reported by the Armyinform, LithuaniaannouncedOn the allocation of 35 million euros for the purchase of ammunition for Ukraine within the Czech initiative for ammunition.
