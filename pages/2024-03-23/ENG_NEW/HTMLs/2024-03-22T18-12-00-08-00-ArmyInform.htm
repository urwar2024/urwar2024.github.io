The US Forces Commander in Europe in 2014-17, Lieutenant General Ben Gogess stated that blows to Russian oil refineries have a significant impact on Russia's ability to wage war, so they should be continued.

He is about itstatedDuring the 16th Annual Kyiv Security Forum.

“Both Ukraine and the event have to win these competitions. It is about the production of ammunition, as well as the repair and maintenance of equipment so that Ukrainians can repair damaged weapons on the site, as well as strengthening of ammunition reserves, ”Ben Gogeges said.

“I would not expect a dramatic fall in Ukraine's ability to defend.
