Paris and Berlin have reached an agreement that allows the French-German defense company KNDS to establish a production unit in Ukraine.

About thisreportedThe head of the French Ministry of Defense Sebastian Lecorn on Twitter.

“Common production in Ukraine: CompanyKNDS will create a subsidiary as close as possible to the needs of the Ukrainian army, ”the post reads.

It is noted that the KNDS branch should produce ammunition as well as spare parts to serve German and French systems that are in Ukraine. Subsequently, it will be able to produce full systems.

As reported by the Armyinform, GermanyPrepareda new €500 million aid package for Ukraine.
