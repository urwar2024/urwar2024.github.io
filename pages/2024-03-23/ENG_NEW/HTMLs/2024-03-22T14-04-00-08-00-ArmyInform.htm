Due to the night attack of the Russian Federation, the power objects of Odesa region have undergone significant damage - without light 260,000 subscribers.

About thisreportedOleg Kiper, Head of the Odessa Military Administration in Telegram.

“Due to damage to energy objects, as a result of the night attack, about 300,000 subscribers in the Odessa region will fall under the schedules of shutdown. Experts carry out restorative work, ”he added.

Regarding critical infrastructure objects - boiler rooms, water pumping stations, hospitals and maternity hospitals.

Also, according to the head of the region, there are 399 points of indomitable in the region, another 413 are ready to deploy as needed.

As reported by the Armyinform, in Kharkiv because of enemy shelling without lightremained700 thousand consumers.
