Deputy Minister of Defense Kateryna Chernororenko expressed his gratitude to Lithuania for his assistance in closing the current needs of the Armed Forces, in particular for providing Ukraine with systems of combating drones.

About thisIt is saidOn the page of Ukraine's defense department in Telegram.

"The drone coalition is not only about drones for the Defense Forces, but also about countering enemy means. Lithuanian partners are always in touch and in the context of events. I am grateful to Lithuania for its help in meeting the urgent needs of our units," said Kateryna Chernohorenko.

She added that Lithuania has developed a plan for long-term support for Ukraine for 2024-2026 and planned 200 million euros for Ukraine's military support.
