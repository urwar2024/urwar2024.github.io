In Zaporozhye, police exposed a corruption scheme with the participation of officials who sold the MSEC certificate. The persons withdrawn cash with more than $ 1,200 and two kilograms of psychotrop.

About thisinformsNational Police of Ukraine.

“The official of the Center for Medical and Social Expertise, having influence on the heads of subordinate commissions, facilitated the military to obtain a fictitious disability group. He searched for clients and connected her relative, who was as a mediator. For example, the cost of referral to the Military Medical Commission (VLK) was $ 300, and obtaining the right conclusion-$ 3,500. During the investigation, the police established numerous facts of receiving the defendants of the case of undue benefit, ”the message reads.

The operatives and investigators conducted searches at the place of work and accommodation of participants of the corruption scheme. More than 1 million dollars, 103 thousand euros, 3.2 million hryvnias, 2 kg of psychotrop, records of rough accounting were seized.

“Based on the evidence obtained, one of the heads of the Center reported suspicion of abuse. The actions of the mediator are qualified under Part 2 of Art. 369-2 and Part 3 of Art. 307 (illegal production, production, purchase, storage, transportation, transfer or sale of narcotic drugs, psychotropic substances or their analogues), ”the message said.

It is noted that the issue of choosing a pre -trial detention is now being resolved.

"The collection of evidence to bring all persons involved in the crime continues. In addition, after examinations and a number of procedural measures, the actions of the defendants may be additionally qualified," the press service added.
