The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine initiated an independent assessment of the internal audit of the department.

About thisIt is saidOn the website of the department.

It is noted that the assessment is carried out by KPMG in Ukraine, which is a member of the International Network of Independent Firms KPMG. It is a part of the "large four" recognized at the international level companies providing audit, tax and consulting services.

International standards require at least five years a qualified independent assessor who is not an employee of the organization.

The Ministry of Defense was given this rating 7 years ago (in 2017). This process is an element of the reform of the financial control and management system of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine, an important component of which is the internal audit of the department.

“It is important to assess the current state of internal audit of the Ministry of Defense, the willingness to implement new approaches, methods and procedures during audit research, to improve the organizational and staff structure and professionalism of employees. And in this context, the involvement of leading experts in its evaluation is quite clear. A detailed report from KPMG will help the management of the Defense Ministry to better understand the current state of internal audit in the department, and in general more effectively control the progress of the reform, ”said Deputy Minister of Defense Yuri Dzhigir.

The department added that the process is carried out in accordance with the Program of the Special Advisor to the Minister of Defense of Ukraine on Defense against the United Kingdom with the financial support of the United Kingdom of the United Kingdom and Northern Ireland.
