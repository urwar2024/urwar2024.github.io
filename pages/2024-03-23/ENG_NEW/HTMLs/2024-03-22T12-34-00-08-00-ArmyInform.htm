The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine develops special anti -saucers, which will reduce the risk of injury to the legs during demining.

This is stated in a message onSiteDefense Office.

Currently, the Central Department of Development and Support Supports, together with the support command of support forces, is being tested.

“In fact, these are such multilayered berets with Kevlarov (ballistic) insole. Shoes will help you delay the wreckage in the sole and prevent serious injury to the sappers. The main task to perform such shoes is to keep the limb, ” - explained the Deputy Minister of Defense Vitaliy Polovenko.

According to preliminary requirements, the dinner berets must prevent the affected effect of certain types of anti -lifting explosive devices.

“Our central management specialists study different samples of such shoes. They work on improving tactical and technical characteristics. According to the results of positive trials and approved standard, such mine shoes will provide the Armed Forces, ”Vitaliy Polovenko added.
