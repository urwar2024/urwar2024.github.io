Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova stated that the department is currently looking for ways to increase the social protection of Ukrainian servicemen, including military doctors.

Natalia Kalmykova made a relevant statement live“Single news”March 19, 2024.

According to her, military doctors are now receiving surcharges for combat missions.

“Because they are really constantly on the first line, evacuating. Therefore, all possible surcharges for completing additional tasks are now settled, ”Natalia Kalmykova said.

“And we are looking for ways to increase our doctors' social protection. Like other servicemen, ”she added.

As reported by the Armyinform, on March 18, 2024 the Ministry of DefenseannouncedOn the introduction of a new form of study and procedure of obtaining officers for mobilized physicians.
