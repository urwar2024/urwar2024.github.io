Eight "tourists" detained police in fishing suits in the border village of Gradenitsa 500 meters from the border.

About thisShe reported March 19National Police of Ukraine.

According to the department, the organization of the processes was engaged in a resident of Odessa and previously convicted of theft and robbery a native of Donetsk region.

Men searched for "clients" who wanted to cross the state border of Ukraine illegally. Those were brought to groups and exported to one of the villages of the region, where they gave guidelines for further movement.

The cost of "Services" was $ 5300 per person.

Asreported the armyinform, two groups of mountain tourists were detained in Transcarpathia.
