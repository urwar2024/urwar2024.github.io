On July 18, the fourth summit of the European Political Community will be held in the UK at Blenheim Palace with the participation of about 50 European leaders.

About thisIt is saidon the site of the British government.

“I am glad that the United Kingdom will take the next meeting of the European political community at the Historical Blengem Palace. This is an important forum for cooperation of all of Europe on all of us, threaten our safety and prosperity, ”said Prime Minister Richa Sakak Prime Minister.

According to him, the meeting will bring together "European friends, partners and neighbors to solve common problems - from providing full support to Ukraine to stopping human smuggling and illegal migration."

The meeting will be held at the Blenheim Palace in Oxfordshire-the place of birth and family residence of the former Prime Minister Winton Churchill.

European political communityis a platform for political and strategic discussions on the future of Europe. It unites 27 members of the European Union and 20 of their partners, including Ukraine.
