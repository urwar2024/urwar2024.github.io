The Russian army attacked a civilian car from a drone in Kherson region, as a result of shelling, two civilians were injured.

The chief of Kherson Ova Alexander Proudin informs about it.

"At the entrance to the village of Osokorivka, the Novovorontsov community, Russian troops attacked a car with civilians," he said.

According to the head of the Regional Military Administration, the occupiers dropped an explosive object from a drone on the vehicle. As a result, the couple suffered.

“A 57-year-old woman and a 58-year-old man diagnosed with injury and acute response to stress. The victims were provided with the necessary medical care, ”Alexander Proudin said.
