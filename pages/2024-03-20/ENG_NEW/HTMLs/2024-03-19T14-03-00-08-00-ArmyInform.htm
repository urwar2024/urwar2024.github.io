Since the beginning of March 2024, Russian troops have applied 130 missiles of different types against Ukraine, more than 320 Shahaneda and almost 900 managed aircraft.

About thisreportedPresident of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky on Telegram.

"Every day and every night Russia, rockets and managed bombs, wages a terrorist war against our people, against the ordinary cities and villages of Ukraine," the Head of State added.

However, he noted that despite the fact that Ukraine can effectively knock down any missiles and drones of Russian terrorists and destroy Russian military aviation, we need more air defense systems.

“But we need more protection - a completely realistic number of air defense systems that have our partners. Patriot and other systems should do what they were created, namely to protect life, not to be dust -free. And I thank everyone in the world who understands it and helps us to protect life. Russian terror must lose, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy summarized.
