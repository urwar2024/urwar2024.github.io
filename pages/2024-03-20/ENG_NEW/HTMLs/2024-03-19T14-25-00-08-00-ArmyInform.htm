During the game on the Internet, a 24-year-old resident of Vinnytsia met with a man who promised to assist with the illegal crossing of the border to Moldova.

About thisShe reported March 19State Border Guard Service of Ukraine.

The evictor paid $ 3,000 for a journey to Moldova, arrived in one of the border villages and settled at the hotel.

With the onset of the night, the 22-year-old head of illegal tourism had to cross his "client" by a boat through the Kuchurgan estuary.

But even before the swim, both men were detained.

In relation to the "tour operator", a message to the National Police on the detection of signs of a crime under Art. 332 of the Criminal Code "Illegal Crossing of Persons across the State Border of Ukraine", and a protocol under Part 1 of Art. 204-1 of the Code of Administrative Offenses "Illegal Crossing or attempting to illegally cross the state border of Ukraine".

The case is sent to court.
