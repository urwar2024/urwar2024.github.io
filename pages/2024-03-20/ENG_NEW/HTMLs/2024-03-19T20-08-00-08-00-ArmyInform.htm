The Commander of the Air Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk showed combat work of air defense on the evening of March 19.

Mykola Oleshchuk about itreportedIn the telegram and posted the appropriate video.

"We continue to destroy the enemy UAVs in the south," he wrote, and thanks for the combat work of the Kherson anti -aircraft missile brigade.

As reportedArmyinform, today, March 19, two Russian reconnaissance drones were knocked down in Odesa region.
