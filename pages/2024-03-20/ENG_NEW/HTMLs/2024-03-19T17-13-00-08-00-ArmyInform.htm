In the state operator concluded by the state operator contracts for the supply of food for the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the price of eggs is set.

About thisinformsThe State Logistics Operator (DOT) is a state agency that meets the needs of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

"Step by step we move on so that the military is concentrated only on the main mission," the DOT emphasized.
