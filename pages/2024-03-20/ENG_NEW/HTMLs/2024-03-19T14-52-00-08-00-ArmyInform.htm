The Ukrainian army, Maria Sakharuk, won the License for Olympic Games in sports walking for 20 km. It happened on the results of the International Tournament in Slovakia.

About thisinformsThe Central Sports Club of the Armed Forces.

"At a distance of 20 km, our athlete showed the result 1: 29.14 hours, which allowed her to take the final second place, and at the same time fulfill the standard (1:29:20) for games 2024," - reported in the department.

Recall that Maria Sakharuk will be able to speak at the Olympics for the second time. At the 2020 games, it became the only one of the Ukrainian Skorokhorokhorokhorokhoroes to enter the first twenty: it took 19th place, overcoming 20 kilometers for 1:34:04 pm.

As reportedArmyinform, Nazar Chepurny Army Winner won the World Gymnastics Cup.
