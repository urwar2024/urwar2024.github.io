Canada will hand over $7.5 million worth of night vision devices to the Armed Forces of Ukraine, which will be purchased from the Canadian company Twenty20 Insight.

About thisReportsCanada's Ministry of Defense.

Defense Minister Bill Blair also confirmed that Canada has officially joined the Coalition of the Contact Group on Defense of Ukraine on the possibilities of drones, which is headed by Latvia and the United Kingdom.

“Canada's initial contribution includes over800 dronesfrom Teledyne, as recently announced byMinister of Defense. The first batch is expected to arrive in Ukraine spring," the message reads.
