Today, on March 19, the occupation troops of Russia were fired at Kherson region at lunch.

About thisinformsThe head of the Kherson Ova Alexander Proudin.

“Another blow to the central part of Kherson. And again under fire - residential buildings. After lunch, the Russian projectile "flew" on an apartment building, "he said.

According to the chairman of the Kherson Ova, in the apartment as a result of shelling, the windows were shot, the roof and walls were also trumpeted.

"Fortunately, none of the Kherson were injured as a result of these shellings," Alexander Proudin stressed.

As reported byArmyinform, near Kherson, three people came under Russian shelling.
