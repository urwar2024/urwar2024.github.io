President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky noted that the results of the next Ramstein will be new defensive packages, including scarce artillery.

The head of statewroteIn the telegram.

“Today is the next Ramstein, a meeting of our defense countries, our protection against Russian terror. This is a productive meeting, there are results. The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Umerov participated. I am waiting for his detailed report, ”he said.

Of course, the President emphasizes, remains unchanged in priority for air defense, edges and drones.

“I thank all the countries that really work for our priority to be filled with appropriate content. Ukrainian drones already produce strong results, and we need to significantly strengthen the international component of cooperation. The front should feel our strengthening of drones. I am grateful to all participants of Ramstein and the United States, the Austin Minister for leadership, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy summarized.

As reportedArmyinform, the main topics of the meeting of the 20th Meeting in the Ramstein format were: ammunition, air defense, F-16 and drones.
