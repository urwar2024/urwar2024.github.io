Poland and Germany will officially launch a coalition of armored opportunities for Ukraine on March 26.

About thisannouncedDefense ministers of the two countries Vladislav Kosiniak-Kkysh and Boris Pistorius at a briefing in Warsaw.

"This is one of the most important coalitions that has been formed. As two countries, we are the leaders of this project. Our other partners are also ready to join: Great Britain, Sweden, Italy declare their readiness to participate in this coalition," said the Polish Defense Minister.

He also thanked Germany for his contribution to the establishment of the Center for Training, Education and Training Ukraine - NATO in Bydgoszcz.

“This is the first tangible initiative of this kind. It is a structure that is being built and brings Ukraine closer to NATO. Germany will participate in this project, ”added Vladislav Kosiniak-Kkysh.

As reported by the Armyinform, in Germany todaywill take placeThe 20th meeting of the contact group on support of Ukraine in Ramstein format.
