More than 100 military doctors have already passed new courses that allow the officer's rank of Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Deputy Minister of Defense Natalia Kalmykov told this on the air of Televahorfon“Single news”.

"Not having a military department after mobilization, such doctors, unfortunately, do not get to the post of doctors in the Armed Forces ... Our task - to make all the doctors in their place," Natalia Kalmykova said.

According to her, the Ministry of Defense has reformatted the training program, and some courses can be held online.

“That is, you can go through training without a break from work. And then arrive at offline. This will allow you to give the officer rank faster to those doctors who are already serving in the Armed Forces, ”she explained.

Soldiers who want to join the program should write either a report to the commander or send the previous information to the e -mail addressdoz@mod.gov.ua.

After that, the command of the medical forces of the Armed Forces will process the application and launch the training process.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Ministry of Defense is introducinga new form of studyand the procedure for obtaining officers for mobilized doctors.

From now on, you can get the title of junior lieutenant and return to a more appropriate position in only 40 days, which include: online courses and 18 days of practical training at the walls of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.

The next course starts on April 8 at the Military Medical Academy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
