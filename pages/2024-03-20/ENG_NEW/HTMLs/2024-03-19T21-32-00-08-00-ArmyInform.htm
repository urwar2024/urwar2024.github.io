Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov reported on the results of the meeting of the Contact Group, noting that Ukraine is actively working with partners to ensure that F-16 aircraft are in our skies as soon as possible.

The head of the Defense Office of Ukraine was about thiswroteOn Facebook after meeting the contact group on defense of Ukraine.

"F-16s should help close our skies. We are actively working with all involved partners: the United States, Denmark, Belgium, the Netherlands, Romania and others to ensure that the planes are in Ukraine as soon as possible," he said.

Rustem Umerov noted that on the eve of Ramstin an important meeting took place in Visbaden with the military leadership of key partner countries.

“The Ukrainian delegation presented a plan for 2024, announced the critical needs of the Ukrainian defense forces, presented the latest achievements in the construction of fortifications. It is very important to maintain such a format of meetings on the eve of meetings of the contact group on defense of Ukraine, ” - said the head of the defense department.

On March 19, the Ukrainian team had a separate meeting with the US Secretary of Defense Lloyd Austin and the Chairman of the United States Chiefs of Staff General Charles Brown.

“They discussed the plan of Ukraine for 2024, construction of fortifications, strengthening of Ukrainian air defense, providing radio electronic struggle. A separate point was the long -range means of the defeat, ”Rustem Umerov summarized.

The Minister of Defense of Ukraine also reported that he had a number of bilateral meetings, in particular with the leaders of Germany, Sweden, Greece, France, Bulgaria, Slovakia, Portugal.

As reported byArmyinform, the main topics of the meeting of the 20th Meeting in the Ramstein format were: ammunition, air defense, F-16 and drones.
