On March 19, the Ministry of Finance of Ukraine on March 19 at an auction for the placement of internal state loan bonds (ATPP) attracted UAH 16.333 billion in the state budget.

About thisinformsMinistry of Finance on Facebook.

"Today, the Ministry of Finance has placed bonds worth more than UAH 16.3 billion in equivalent," the statement said.

It is noted that the Ministry of Finance has offered investors the hryvnia bonds:

  • UAH 3.018 billion at 16.46% with repayment in 1 year;
  • UAH 4.107 billion at 17.35% with repayment in 2 years,
  • UAH 3.370 billion at 18.3% with repayment in 3.3 years.

Also, the US government bonds were offered - 150 million at 4.66% with repayment in 1 year.

As reported by the Armyinform, Ukraine's monthly need for external financingreachesAbout $ 3 billion
