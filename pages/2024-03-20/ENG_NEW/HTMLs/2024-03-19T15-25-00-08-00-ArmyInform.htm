Ukrainian servicemen, under the guidance of Australian instructors of the Kudu operation, worked out the skills of storming the terrain with suburban buildings, in particular the assault and cleaning of buildings.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces.

Yes, the training of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces took place at one of the British landfills, where the urbanized area was realistically recreated: several -storey private homes, mini -hosts, gardens and gardens, outbuildings, fences and fences. The Ukrainian military learned to move forward, to choose the right positions for fire control, to use grenades and smokes, to save and evacuate the wounded, to act in groups.

“Particular attention was paid to analyzing the situation on the front. They determined the order of the reporters to always have up -to -date information and control the situation on the site, ”the Armed Forces said.

In addition to the military from the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, the Ukrainian Armies are prepared by the best instructors and instructors from Australia, Denmark, Canada, Lithuania, Estonia, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Romania, Finland and Sweden.

Operation InterflexIt was started in June 2022. This leading curriculum in the world, which is implemented throughout the United Kingdom, attracts recruiting recruits who joined the Armed Forces of Ukraine, having virtually no military experience, and teaches their skills necessary for survival and efficiency in the frontier.
