During the last day, there were 61 fighting. In total, the enemy caused 7 missile and 70 aviation strikes, and made 110 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

This was reported by the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its ownFacebook page.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy performed 3 unsuccessful storms of the positions of defense forces in the area of the settlement of Sinkivka of Kharkiv region.

In the estuary directionOur warriors repelled 4 attacks of the enemy in the area of the settlement of Terny of Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionOur defenders repelled 7 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Ivanivske, Khishchivka, Andriyivka of Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 9 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Berdychi, Orlovka, Tonenko, Nevelskoye Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the districts of Krasnogorivka, St. George, Novomikhailivka, Vodyane, a harvest of Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried 25 times to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy attacked the positions of our defenders 9 times in the areas of settlements of Staromayorsk Donetsk region and a robot of Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson directionThe enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. So, during the last day, 3 storms of the positions of our troops have been carried out.

In Volyn and Polissya directionsThe operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent the transfer of our troops to threatening directions, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border in Belgorod region.

At the same time, our warriors continue to be active in the occupation troops of losses in living strength and technology, depleting the enemy along the entire front line.
