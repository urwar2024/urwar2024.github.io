On the facade of the house in the Svyatoshinsky district of Kiev, where the employee of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Alexander Chub lived, a memorial plaque was installed.

About thisinformsDefence Intelligence of Ukraine.

“The warrior died on June 19, 2022 during the storm of fire positions of the invaders in Luhansk region. He was awarded the title of Hero of Ukraine and the Order of the Golden Star (posthumously), ”the department said.

Junior Lieutenant Alexander Chub has participated in the Russo-Ukrainian War since 2016. From the first days of a large -scale invasion, he was one of those who defended the Russian invaders and the region, performed special tasks in the southern direction of Ukraine, acquiring important intelligence, which helped to keep the enemy's armaments and military equipment of defense forces, to save the lives residents.

GUR also noted that the award from the President of Ukraine, which was awarded on the Day of Military Intelligence of Ukraine on September 7, 2022, received the scout's father.

“My son has gone a heavy, short but bright way. He was a prepared and motivated warrior. But, unfortunately, the number of enemies was too large, the fire was very dense ... But the love of Ukraine, which my son had in his heart, and will to win in the struggle for our land, for our will, for each of us - will live forever - forever live forever. In the hearts of everyone who knew him, who conveyed his experience and skills. I thank everyone who was around, who supported and knew my son. He will live until we remember him, honor his feat. Glory to Ukraine and its heroes, ”the father of the intelligence officer Sergey Chub said at the opening of the memorial plaque.
