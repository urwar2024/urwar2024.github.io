The attacker tried to identify the combat positions of the air defense complexes of the Armed Forces, for which he launched unmanned aerial vehicles, which were equipped with cameras.

About thisShe reported March 19SBU.

According to the department, the information obtained by the messenger transmitted the "connected" Russian special service.

He was a former employee of the disbanded special forces of the Golden Eagle, who is currently in the Crimea and cooperates with the occupiers.

The Rashists planned to use the information obtained to prepare sabotage and rocket strikes in the region, in particular for fire damage to Ukrainian air defense complexes.

The Security Service exposed the Russian informant at the beginning of his intelligence activity. This allowed the "step -by -step" to document the defendant and in advance to protect the location of the departments of the Armed Forces.

At the final stage of operational measures, the attacker was detained in his own home.

During the search, the reconnaissance drones, which he used to collect intelligence, were removed from his house. The detainee also found substances used for handicraft production of explosives.

According to the available data, a hostile associate could prepare a blast on the objects of defense forces that got to the cameras of his drones.

The investigation found that the former employee of the local defense plant was the attacker.

In the field of view of the invaders, he got through the publication of his own comments in Russian telegrams, where he supported the aggressive war against Ukraine.

Investigators of the Security Service have now reported the detainee of the suspicion under two articles of the Criminal Code of Ukraine.

The attacker is in custody. He faces up to 12 years in prison.

Asreported the armyinform, in Zaporozhye detained a Russian who was spying on the ships of the Armed Forces, pretending to be a trucker.
