Canadian Defense Minister Bill Blair announced at the 20th meeting of the Contact Group on Defense of Ukraine about the allocation of $ 40 million (almost $ 30 million) for the purchase of artillery shells for Ukraine.

About thisinformsDepartment of Defence Canada.

“This contribution will allow you to deliver several thousand ammunition to Ukraine. This obligation followed the signing last month of a Memorandum of Understanding on Defense Cooperation between Canada and the Czech Republic, which establishes a framework for defense cooperation between the two countries, including the support of Ukraine, ”the message reads.

He noted that during the discussion at the meeting, Ukrainian officials clearly made it clear that they needed more artillery ammunition.

“We strive to meet Ukraine's urgent needs as soon as possible. Supporting the Czech initiative to transfer to Ukraine a large number of artillery ammunition, Canada doubles its efforts to meet one of Ukraine's most remedial defense needs, ”Bill Blair added.

As reported by the Armyinform, FinlandprovidesEUR 30 million to Ukraine for the purchase of shells within the Czech initiative.

Currently, one and a half dozen countries, including Germany, France, the Netherlands, Canada and others, have already joined the Czech initiative.
