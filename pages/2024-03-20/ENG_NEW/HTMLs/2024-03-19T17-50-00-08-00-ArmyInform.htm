The Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine Ruslan Stefanchuk, at a meeting with the Head of the State Assembly of the Republic of Slovenia, Urshka, Klukochar-Zanchich discussed Ukraine's defense support.

About thisinformsVerkhovna Rada of Ukraine.

The parties also emphasized the importance of Slovenia's military assistance packages to Ukraine.

“At the same time, Ukraine's security and defense forces require additional units of military equipment. He outlined the urgent needs of the Armed Forces, ”said Ruslan Stefanchuk.

Another topic that was broken at the meeting was the sanctions against Russia. The parties stressed that it is important to continue the pressure on the Russian Federation to weaken its ability to finance the war.

“Finally, Ukraine was discussed. He thanked for already implemented projects in Kharkiv and Zhytomyr regions. I hope to further involve Slovenia in the restoration of Ukrainian cities, ”said Ruslan Stefanchuk, the Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine.
