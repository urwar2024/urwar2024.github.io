In Sumy region and Kharkiv region, the specialists of the radio broadcasting concern of radio and television have resumed access to the radio channels and Ukrainian television.

About thisinformsState Special Communications and Information Protection Service.

The department noted that engineers promptly ensured the restoration of power and repaired the equipment that was damaged. The signal was returned in Lozova, Kegychivka, Izium, Sumy, Trostyanka and Shostka.

In addition, restorative work is still ongoing on other damaged objects of Sumy region.

"Broadcasting is restored after a massive Russian attack by rockets and drones on infrastructure broadcasting objects of television and radio signal on the night of March 14," the department reminded.

He promises to report the complete restoration of the State Secretariat of Communications.
