The countries of the Contact Group on Defense of Ukraine have raised $ 88 billion in military support for two years.

About thisIt is saidOn the Pentagon site.

“For almost two years, more than $ 88 billion has been allocated to help Ukraine in the field of security. Support is not just helping Ukraine. This strengthens NATO and helps to strengthen the defense-industrial base in the United States, Europe and the world. This provides our own safety, ”said General Charles Brown, the chairman of the United States Chiefs of Staff.

US Secretary of Defense Lloyd Austin reminded that recently the United States has announced another $ 300 million security assistance to Ukraine.

"This money came from saving on the replacement of American opportunities that have gone to Ukraine since the beginning of Russia's war against Ukraine," he said.

At this meeting of the contact group, the first meeting of a group of coalition leaders of forces and means took place. The partners discussed progress and tried to coordinate the further path for cross -cutting issues. They lead in this group: Denmark, Estonia, France, Germany, Iceland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Norway, Poland and the United Kingdom.

“All of them have intensified to lead coalitions of forces and means and to allocate resources and personnel to accomplish this critically important task. And their leadership is a testament to the unity, determination and our insight today, ”said Lloyd Austin.

As reportedArmyinform, the main topics of the meeting of the 20th Meeting in the Ramstein format were: ammunition, air defense, F-16 and drones.
