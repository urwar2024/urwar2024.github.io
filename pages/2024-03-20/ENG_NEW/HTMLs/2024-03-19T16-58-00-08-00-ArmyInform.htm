Prime Minister Denis Shmigal states that Luxembourg has expressed interest in the reconstruction of the Kryvyi Rih district of Dnipropetrovsk region.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

“Luxembourg showed interest in the reconstruction of the Kryvyi Rih district of Dnipropetrovsk region - a powerful industrial center of our country. This is an extremely positive example, and we expect that the relevant projects will soon begin, ”the Prime Minister emphasized.

Denis Shmigal thanked for the help that Luxembourg provides a large -scale invasion from the first days. In particular, it is also to promote Ukraine Facility and the intention to join Ukraine's restoration.

As reportedArmyinform, Prime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal, at a meeting in Luxembourg with Prime Minister Luke Frieden, spoke about proposals on the mechanism of confiscation of frozen Russian assets.
