The court recognized the consolidated serviceman of the Rosgvardia Department in the Kemerovo region of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation is guilty of violating the laws and customs of war (Part 2 of Article 28, Part 1 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

ЯкShe reported March 19The Kyiv Regional Prosecutor's Office, he was sentenced to 11 years of imprisonment in absentia.

According to the investigation, in March 2022, during the occupation of Gostomel, convicted together with other unspecified servicemen of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation conducted illegal searches of buildings.

The occupier knocked the door, smashed windows and searched for military personnel and law enforcement officers.

In one of the houses, he, in conjunction with co -workers, threatened peaceful inhabitants of weapons and made them completely undress.

In the future, people without food and water were kept in the basement, beaten with examples on ribs and feet, and threatened with weapons.

Carrying out so-called filtration measures, he checked documents and took away mobile phones from civilians.

Якreported the armyinform, Prosecutor General of Ukraine and Poland appealed to the victims of the military crimes of the Russians.
