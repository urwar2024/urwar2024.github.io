President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky celebrated 186 defenders of Ukraine state awards, 100 of them posthumously.

The corresponding decree№160/2024Posted on the website of the Head of State.

The President decided to award defenders for personal courage, revealed in the protection of state sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine, and for selfless performance of military duty.

The Head of State awarded the Order of Bohdan Khmelnytsky II and III degrees, "for courage" I, II and III degrees, awarded medals "For Military Service of Ukraine".

As reported by the Armyinform, on the Day of Ukrainian Volunteer President presented awardsTo the heroes of Ukraine and the families of the dead.
