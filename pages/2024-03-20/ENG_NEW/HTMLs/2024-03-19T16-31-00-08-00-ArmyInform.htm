Latvia's Defense Minister Andris Prauds stated that his country had joined the Czech initiative to buy 800,000 artillery shells for Ukraine.

About thisIt is saidon his Twitter page.

“Today we have signed a memorandum on mutual understanding with the Czech Ministry of Defense for the purchase of artillery shells to support Ukraine. This initiative is crucial for ensuring the necessary combat capacity of our Ukrainian allies, ”the Latvian Defense Minister wrote.

As reported by the Armyinform, FinlandprovidesEUR 30 million to Ukraine for the purchase of shells within the framework of the Czech initiative.

Currently, one and a half dozen countries, including Germany, France, the Netherlands, Canada and others, have already joined the Czech initiative.
