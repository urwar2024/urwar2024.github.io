At once, two teams of conscripts who tried to illegally cross the border found border guards of the Mukachevo detachment in the mountains.

About thisShe reported March 19State Border Service of Ukraine.

Initially, four men found a border outfit yesterday afternoon, which hidden a mountainous area of the Ukrainian-Romanian border.

Three of the detainees came from Kharkiv, their companion - from Cherkasy. Men confessed that they aimed at the local resident illegally cross the border.

Each of the detainees paid $ 6 to $ 8,000 for the services of the cross -law. The detachment operatives have already installed and detained a man who provided the offenders with supporting them to the border.

Another group of five men of the border guards of the same unit was detained today at half in the morning.

During the realization of information provided by the staff of the operative-search department, the outfit stopped two residents of Kyiv region, a resident of Poltava, Kirovograd and Kherson region on the outskirts of Vyshkovo village.

Two of the detainees tried to cross the border outside the checkpoint. They made their first attempt at the border with Hungary.

It is now known that for their "journey" to the mountains, each of the five men paid $ 7 to $ 10,000.

In relation to the detainees, protocols on the administrative offense provided for in Art. 204-1 of the Code of Administrative Offenses "Illegal Crossing or attempting to illegally cross the state border of Ukraine".

The case is sent to court.

Asreported the armyinform, such schemes of evasion of mobilization, such as fictitious marriage, acting and counterfeit documents are opened.
