During the day, the army of the Russian Federation has attacked Nikopol region 10 times with heavy artillery and shock drones.

This was reported by the head of Dnipropetrovsk Ova Sergey Lisak.

“9 attacks by Kamikadze Droama and heavy artillery shelling. A aggressor weapons worked in Nikopol region during the day, ”he said.

According to the head of Ova, the enemy beat the district center, Marganets and Pokrovsk communities.

Sergey Lisak also stressed that a private home and excavator were damaged. The mutilated outbuilding, another - destroyed.

“It passed without the dead and the victims. In other areas of the region it was calm, ”he added.

As reported by the Armyinform, in Kherson region the occupiers from the dronedropped the explosive on the car: Two people were injured.
