In January-February, UAH 137.4 billion from UAH 428 billion of all expenditures of the General Fund of the State Budget of Ukraine was spent on the financial support of servicemen.

About thisHe reported March 19Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.

According to the government, the largest expenses in January-February were the following:

— UAH 179.9 billion — for salaries (including for the financial support of military personnel —
137.4 billion UAH);

- UAH 89.5 billion - for social security (payment of pensions, assistance,
scholarships);

- UAH 21.7 billion - to support the Armed Forces (purchase of military equipment, weapons, bulletproof vests, etc.);

- 22.3 billion UAH - for the implementation of the Program of State Guarantees of Medical
Population service.

Asreported the armyinform, The Cabinet of Ministers allocated UAH 5 billion for drones for the front.
