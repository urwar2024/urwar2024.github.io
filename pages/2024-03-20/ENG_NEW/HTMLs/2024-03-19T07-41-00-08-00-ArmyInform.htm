There are no ships in the Black and Azov Seas of the Russian Navy.

About thisreportedNavy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

There are three enemy ships in the Mediterranean, including two carbive carbon carriers, a total volley of up to 12 rockets.

In the interests of the Russian Federation, 19 ships passed to the Azov Sea in the interests of the Russian Federation, including 6 of them moving from the Bosphorus Strait.

To the Black Sea - 15 vessels, 4 of them continued the movement in the direction of the Bosphorus.

"The Russian Federation continues to violate the International Convention on the Protection of Human Life at the Sea of 1974 (Solas), turning off automatic identification systems (AIS)," the Navy emphasized.

Asreported the armyinform, the Navy of the Armed Forces named priority goals among the ships of the Black Sea Fleet.
