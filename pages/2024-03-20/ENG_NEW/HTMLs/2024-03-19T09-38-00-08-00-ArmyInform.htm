Prosecutors of the Luhansk Regional Prosecutor's Office sent to court an indictment against LNR Chairman Leonid Pasichnyk.

This is stated in a message onSiteProsecutor's Office.

He is charged with participation in the organization and conduct of illegal elections in the temporarily occupied territory, public calls for their holding and voluntary election to an illegal authority (Part 5 of Article 111-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

The investigation established that from September 1 to September 10, 2023, in accordance with the legislation of the russian federation and the "luhansk people's republic", illegal "elections of deputies of the people's council and deputies of the representative bodies of the municipalities of the luhansk people's republic" of the first convocation took place in the occupied territories of Luhansk region.

On September 23 last year, the person involved was re -elected to the post of "head of the LNR" on the submission of the President of Russia and swore allegiance to the Constitution of the Russian Federation and pseudo -republic. He joined the position of "leader".

The defendant continues to conduct active anti-Ukrainian activities on the territory of the pseudo-republic. He systematically participates in the Kremlin's propaganda media campaigns.

The prosecutor's office recalled that the accused, as the "head of the LNR", together with representatives of the Russian Federation, had previously created conditions for the annexation of the Luhansk region by holding a fictitious "referendum" and signing an agreement on the "accession" of the region to Russia.

For committing a number of crimes against the foundations of national security of Ukraine, the indictment against him was previously sent to court (Part 2 of Article 110, Part 2 of Article 28, Part 5 of Article 111-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

The special pre-trial investigation was carried out by investigators of the 3rd Directorate of the SBU Main Directorate in Donetsk and Luhansk regions.

As ArmyInform reported, based on the materials of the SBI, the courtappointed15 years in prison to three traitors who cooperated with the occupiers.
