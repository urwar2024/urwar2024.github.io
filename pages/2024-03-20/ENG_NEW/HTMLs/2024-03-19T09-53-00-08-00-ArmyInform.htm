In the Vinnytsia region, police and explosive equipment surveyed the places of falling hostile shock drones like ShahED.

About it reports the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.

One of the UAVs was found in the forest, he did not detonate. The fragments of others - found in the fields.

No injured people or damage to property were found. After documenting, some of the drones will be neutralized by specialists.

As reported by the Armyinform, Pyrotechnics of the State Emergency Service of UkraineDestroyedwarhead of the Kh-101 missile in the Vinnytsia region.

In addition, Vinnytsia region found fragments of a hostile drone with an unidentified combat unit. Explosives urge people not to approach such objects.
