In the Odessa region, the construction of fortifications for protection against aggressive actions of the Russian Federation continues.

About thisreportedThe head of the Odessa Regional State Administration Oleg Kiper.

"We are actively building fortifications, we establish barriers -" teeth of dragons ", anti -tank ditches and other non -clipping barriers," - the message reads.

Oleg Kiper noted that about 150 people and 80 pieces of equipment were involved in construction.

"Our willingness to counteract the enemy, as well as a systematic approach to the construction of fortifications - a powerful restraining factor for the aggressor," added the head of the Odessa Regional State Administration.
