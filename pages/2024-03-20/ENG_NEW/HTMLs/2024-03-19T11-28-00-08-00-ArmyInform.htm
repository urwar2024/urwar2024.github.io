In violation of international law, Russia does not allow representatives of the International Committee of the Red Cross to Ukrainian prisoners of war.

About thisreportedUkrainian Parliament Commissioner for Human Rights Dmytro Lubinets.

That is why, he said, the procedure for confirming their status does not occur in the extent that you would like.

At the same time, the Ombudsman emphasized that the status of prisoners of war and a person missing was not affected by repatriation. Almost 40% of those who had already returned home were considered missing.

“In order to speed up exchanges, Ukraine is trying to attract new intermediary countries. To date, the Ukrainian side has publicly joined the United Arab Emirates, ”Dmitry Lubinets said.

As reported by the Armyinform, to the International Coalition for the return of Ukrainian children, headed by Ukraine and Canada, alreadyIt joined33 countries.
