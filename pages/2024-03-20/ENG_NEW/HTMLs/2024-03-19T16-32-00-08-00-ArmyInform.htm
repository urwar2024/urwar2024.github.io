In Zaporozhye, the second recruitment center in Ukraine was launched in Ukraine.

About thisreportedDeputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova.

"The opening of another recruitment center of the Ukrainian army took place atCentral Boulevard, 27, and is the result of joint work of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine, the General Staff of the Armed Forces with the Regional Military Administration, ”she said.

The Center will work as an advisory and information office, which, if necessary, will support enrollment of those who want to join the defense forces. In particular, citizens can receive information about vacant positions in the Ukrainian army and consultations on all issues, calling +380 66 183 10 75 or +380 98 835 09 04.

“In November last year, when we just started developing this area, there was enough criticism and skepticism. But thanks to the coordinated work of the Ministry of Defense team, the General Staff of the Armed Forces, partners from private online sites, local self-government bodies and state authorities, the project has been “stamped”. Every week we have about ten thousand reviews from citizens for vacancies, ”Natalia Kalmykova said.

The plans, according to her, open recruiting centers in each area. They will be able to find out about existing vacancies in the Ukrainian army. Recruiters will also organize interviews with selected military units and accompany the process to appoint.

Importantly:Employees of recruitment centers of the Ukrainian army are civilians and do not issue stories, but only advise and accompany the recruiting process.

“I am convinced that recruiting will only gain momentum. We have a huge request from society. In total, 27 such recruitment centers in regional centers and large cities were planned in the first half of 2024, ”the Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine summarized.

As reported byArmyinform, Natalia Kalmykova checked the work of the first recruiting center in Lviv.
