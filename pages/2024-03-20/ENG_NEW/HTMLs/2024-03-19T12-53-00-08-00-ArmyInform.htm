President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky discussed further comprehensive support from the EU with the President of European Council Charles Michel.

The head of statereportedIn telegrams.

"First of all, they discussed further steps towards the actual beginning of Ukraine's accession to the EU, as well as further comprehensive support of Ukraine by the EU," - the message reads.

The President thanked Charl Michel for yesterday's approval by the EU Council Decision to establish a special fund of assistance to Ukraine within the European Peace Fund,a volume of 5 billion euros.

In addition, the interlocutors considered possible ways to increase the supply of artillery ammunition to Ukraine.

“They discussed the importance of continuing the autonomous trade preferences for Ukraine for another year. He stressed that the continuation of the regime of liberalization of trade with the EU is a critical factor in supporting the Ukrainian economy during the war, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy added.
