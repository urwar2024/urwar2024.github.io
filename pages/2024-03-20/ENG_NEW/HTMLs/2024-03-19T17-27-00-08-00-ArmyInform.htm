Negotiations with Estonia on the conclusion of a bilateral security agreement have been initiated to implement the G7 joint declaration.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

“It is important to enter into strong security agreements with all the Baltic countries, which give us a huge - in relation to their GDP - assistance. Since Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania are also bordered by the aggressor, our security obligations should definitely be mutual, ” - said Presidential Head Igor Zhovkva.

The Estonian delegation is headed by the Deputy Foreign Minister Kulik Sillaste-Ellling.

The parties began work on the project of the agreement and agreed the schedule of further negotiations.

As reportedArmyinform, Ukraine has started negotiations with Latvia on the conclusion of a bilateral security agreement.
