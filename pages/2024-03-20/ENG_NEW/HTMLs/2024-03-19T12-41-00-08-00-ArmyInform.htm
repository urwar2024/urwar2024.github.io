On the basis of the US Air Force Ramstein in the German city of Ramstein-Mizenbach, a 20th meeting of the Contact Group on Defense of Ukraine began.

About thisreported March 19Ministry of Defence Ukraine.

Priorities at this meeting for Ukraine are the provision of air defense systems and missiles to them, a missile of the far radius of action.

Artillery shells, especially 155 mm caliber, modern HR and Rer systems, armored vehicles, are a permanent discussion point.

Asreported the armyinform, Germany and Poland announced a coalition of armored vehicles for Ukraine.
