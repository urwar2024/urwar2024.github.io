Defense Minister Boris Pistorius announced a new package of military assistance to Ukraine worth 500 million euros.

He reported this at a press conference within the meeting of the contact group on support of Ukraine on a military base in Ramstay, reportsdaily News.

In addition, Germany will support Ukraine in the fight against Russian aggression in the short term further supplies of ammunition.

It is planned to transfer 10,000 artillery shells from the Bundesver warehouses.

The new aid package will also include 100 armored vehicles for infantry and 100 vehicles.

Boris Pistorius did not name the timing of the transfer of a new package of assistance. Usually the German government reports the transfer of equipment and ammunition already after providing Ukraine.
