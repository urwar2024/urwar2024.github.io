A spokesman for the Navy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the captain of the 3rd rank Dmitry Pletenchuk spoke about the plans of the Navy of the Armed Forces on joint exercises with NATO countries.

Dmitry Pletenchuk's commentsgaveDuring the live broadcast of the "One News" TV. March 19, 2024.

“With regard to NATO's exercises, of course, the North Atlantic Alliance conducts its training in the sea. We join them. In particular, abroad. The northern part is also meant. Our ships are involved in them, ”the Navy spokesman said.

According to him, in 2024 Ukraine will join NATO military exercises.

“During these exercises, we undergo compatibility assessment with partner countries. For example, our Sandan -level ships have already received the first level. Now this year we hope to get the second. This is an opportunity to work as a composition of a ship group, ”Dmitry Pletenchuk said.

As reportedArmyinform, March 18, 2024 in the Navy of the Armed Forces named the priority goals among the ships of the Black Sea Fleet.
