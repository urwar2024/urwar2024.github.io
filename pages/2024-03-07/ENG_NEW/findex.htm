For the main sergeants of higher military educational institutions and military units, the course of training of the academic level instructor is provided.

Why is it carried out in the Armed Forces of Ukraine? Which country helped us to implement this course? But what is a "result system"? The answers were answered by the Army Correspondent.

“We teach people how to make a program”

- The course lasts 10 days: 8 days of educational and 2 - administrative, - says the head of the course of preparation of the instructor of academic level Dmitry.

According to him, the course is based on the Systems Approach Training system:

- This is a system, let's just say, achievement. It is based on writing programs, drawing up standards in the Army of Canada, the United States. Specifically, the US has the Corps of Marines.

Dmitry emphasizes that the mentioned system involves avoiding excessive learning. It involves learning because it is really necessary, provides maximum efficiency at a minimum cost.

- That is, in this course we teach people how to make a program. To study the people who need it now and have studied - in the minimum time, - adds the course manager.
