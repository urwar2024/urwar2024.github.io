Today, on March 6, the Ukrainian Veteran Fund launched applications for a veteran business financing program "It is worth starting your own business".

About thisinformsMinistry of Veterans of Ukraine.

Yes, to start or develop their own business veterans and their families can getfrom 500 thousand to 1.5 million hryvnias.

Applications will last from 6 to March 19, 2024.

More about the conditions and step -by -step instructions - onSiteUkrainian Veterans Fund of Ministry of Ministers.

The department noted that the question or clarification on the submitted application package should be sent to the mail uvfprojects@veteranfund.mva.gov.ua with the mandatory indication of the topic of the letter "Competition of Projects" should start your own business "and the application number.
