Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova held a meeting with a special representative of the NATO Secretary General for Women, Peace and Security Irene Felin and NATO Chairman in Ukraine Karen Maktir.

About it Natalia Kalmykovareportedon Facebook.

"They discussed providing the principles of gender equality in the Armed Forces of Ukraine and the challenges that are now facing the army, as well as providing women with military form and individual means of protection," she said.

Separately, the participants of the meeting stopped at work to overcome gender stereotypes and realize the potential of servicemen in positions that provide for operational planning and command of units.

"The interaction and assistance of NATO structures is essential for us in the practical implementation of the gender equality policy in the Armed Forces of Ukraine," said the Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine, and thanked NATO representatives for the opportunities that the Alliance provides Ukraine on the way to this purpose.

Natalia Kalmykova assured that Ukraine will use them as effectively as possible for interaction and development, including both in training, intellectual assistance, and assistance in the implementation of projects related to the financial support of military personnel.

"They agreed on the exchange of experience, consultations and mutual assistance on the implementation of the principles of gender equality in the security and defense forces of Ukraine," the Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine summarized.

As reportedArmyinform, The Ministry of Defense will cooperate with Pulse in preparation with such.
