The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine continues to hold a number of meetings at which the military tells the developers about their needs for technologies and give feedback on improving software decisions.

This is stated in a message onSiteDefense Office.

This time, the guest was the commander of the battalion of percussion unmanned complexes "Achilles" Yuriy Fedorenko and his deputies.

“I am glad that such an opportunity has come to bring your needs to those who can solve them immediately. We are currently engaged in both intelligence and direct goals, so we need solutions that will allow us to fly into the rear of the enemy. I hope we can find approaches together for this, ”the Achilles battalion commander addressed the participants.

The participants of the meeting were addressed by the Deputy Minister of Defense for Digital Development, Digital Transformations and Digitalization Kateryna Chernororenko.

According to her, the interaction of the ministry and developers is an important component for the development of military technologies, and the involvement of profile military is indispensable feedback.

“Time is the most important resource, and we strive to use it for benefit. We have created a community of innovatives to combine developers, engineers, communications and military, and to provide effective communication between them, ”Kateryna Chernororenko said.

The community of innovators has collected more than 400 domestic developers working on the creation of drones, sustainable communication and remote verification tools. In order to join, you need to registerin shape.

Within the community, participants will receive:

  • Mentor's support from leading industry experts
  • Feedback from the military as end users of your decisions
  • cooperation and exchange of knowledge with like -minded people.
