These funds are included in the regional program "The First Zaporizhzhya Billion for the Army", which provides for the purchase of equipment for the military in the Zaporozhye direction.

About thisreported March 6Head of Zaporizhzhya Regional Military Administration Ivan Fedorov.

According to him, the first tenders will be announced this week.

“They have collected applications from all military units to FPVs, coptors, rebes, charging stations, repeaters, TVs, laptops and other equipment. We invite all suppliers to participate in tenders! ” - Ivan Fedorov wrote.

The First Zaporizhzhya Billion for the Army unites the efforts of regional and city budgets, community budgets, business and state companies.
