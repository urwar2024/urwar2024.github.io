As a result of the next shelling of Rosarmia Nikopol in Dnipropetrovsk, a residential building was lit: a roof and an overlap, a woman was injured.

About thisinformsSES of Ukraine.

“Today, Russian occupation troops attacked Nikopol again. The invaders were killed in the civilian population, ”the SES said.

Rescuers managed to eliminate the fire of an apartment building that arose as a result of a hostile blow. The building was engaged in the roof and overlap.

“As a result of the shelling, a woman was injured, born in 1948. She refused hospitalization, ”the department said.

As reportedArmyinform, Russian troops fired at the village in Donetsk region, a woman was killed.
