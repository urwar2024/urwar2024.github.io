Three trailers of howitzers "MSTA-B", as well as three MTLBs and one truck "Urals" were eliminated by the fire of our artillery.

Appropriate videoHe outlined March 6Commander of the National Guard Alexander Pivnenko.

According to him, the enemy prepared his technique for the storm, but the artillerymen from the brigade of the National Guard "Rubiz" disrupted these plans.

The Msta-B Howitzes can fire at a distance of more than 28 kilometers and are the most widespread systems of long-range artillery in the army of the Russian Federation.
