The Chairman of the European Council, Charles Michel, responded to Russia's missile stroke at Odessa at a time when President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Prime Minister of Greece Kiriakos Khatyakis were visiting.

He is about itwroteon your Twitter page.

According to Charles Michel, the Russian attack on Odessa during the stay of Ukrainian and Greek leaders there "is another sign of Russia's cowardly tactics in its aggressive war against Ukraine."

“It is a decent condemnation and even more than the Kremlin. The full support of Ukraine and its brave people on the part of the EU will not weaken, ”the chairman of the Euro Council emphasized.
