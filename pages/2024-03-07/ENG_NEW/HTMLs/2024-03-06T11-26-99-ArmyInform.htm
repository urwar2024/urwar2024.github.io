As a result of the Russian attack on the city of Pokrovsk on the night of March 6, all four missiles of the C-300 complex were hit by the city center.

About thisreportedPokrovsk City Military Administration.

Shellinghappenedabout the first o'clock in the night. In total, 12 high -rise buildings, seven private homes and two administrative buildings were damaged.

“This is another terrorist act on the part of the Russian Federation. All victims are assisted in the form of building materials. As always we "close" windows, we help people, " - said the head of Pokrovskaya MVA Sergey Dobryak.

Due to the enemy shelling, some buildings Pokrovskaya were temporarily left without power supply.

Work is underway to eliminate the effects of shelling.
