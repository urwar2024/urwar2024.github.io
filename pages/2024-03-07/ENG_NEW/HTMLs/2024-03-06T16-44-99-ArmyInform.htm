Spain will continue programs for the treatment and rehabilitation of Ukrainian servicemen and veterans affected by the war.

About thisreportedDeputy Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada of UkraineElena Kondratyuk duringMeetings with the Ambassador of Spain to Ukraine Ricardo Lopez Aranda Hagu.

“We are grateful to Spain for comprehensive assistance and support of Ukraine. Spain among the arms supply leaders for the Armed Forces and made one of the largest contributions to the European Peace Fund for 400 million euros. We calculate the help in engineering equipment. This is one of the urgent needs of our defense forces, ”saidElena Kondratyuk.

The parties also discussed the continuation of the Program of treatment and rehabilitation in Spain of wounded Ukrainian military and veterans, the organization of recreation of Ukrainian children affected by the war.

Ricardo Lopez Aranda Haga stressed that Spain will continue to actively support Ukraine. In particular, in matters of safety, humanitarian aid and gender equality. Spain will take an active part in the initiative to return the illegally deported Russian children.

The Deputy Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada emphasized that Ukraine values law and proactive parliamentary diplomacy, which is carried out by Spain's Parliament to support Ukraine.

“I am grateful to the Head of the Congress of Spain Francini Armenngol personally for joining the joint letters of the heads of parliaments of G7, the EU and the partner states to the speaker of the US House of Representatives M. Johnson. The purpose of the appeal is to unlock the consideration of the bill HR 815 with a package of military assistance to Ukraine for $ 60 billion. US, ”she stressed.
