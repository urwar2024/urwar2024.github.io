Warriors of a separate Regiment of the SCSU of the Armed Forces found and destroyed the radar complex of the occupiers "Zoo-1".

About thisinformsCommand of the SCA of the Armed Forces.

«During the reconnaissance in the estuary direction, the operators of the intelligence BPAC of the tactical group" Medoid "of the 3rd separate regiment of the SCO revealed the radar 1L219" Zoo-1 ".

The SCO operators accompanied the target for some time and provided information to the missile and artillery unit of defense forces for further damage, ”the message reads.

It is noted that as a result of fire damage, the enemy radar station was destroyed together with the crew.

The SCA of the Armed Forces added that this seven radar of this type was destroyed by "mediaders".
