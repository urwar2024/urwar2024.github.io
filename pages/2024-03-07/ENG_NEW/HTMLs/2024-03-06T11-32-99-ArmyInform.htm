The court found a resident of Nikolaev guilty of unauthorized dissemination of information on the location of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, committed in the conditions of martial law.

About thisreported March 6Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, convicted through groups on social networks and messengers, administered by the Russian special services, passed the enemy accurate coordinates of the positions of Ukrainian military, the location of military equipment and the routes of their movement in the region.

Prior to the entry into force of the sentence, it will be in custody.
