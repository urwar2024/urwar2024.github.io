Within the Multinational Interflex Operation Operation, instructors from the United Kingdom teach Ukrainian soldiers to storm the area with suburban buildings, including storming and cleaning buildings.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces.

“Under the leadership of the British, the military of the Armed Forces of Ukraine learned to move forward, the house behind the house, to choose the right positions for fire control, to use grenades and smokes, to save and evacuate the wounded, to act coherently in groups.
Particular attention was paid to the analysis of the situation on the front. They determined the order of the reporters to always have up -to -date information and control the situation on the site, ”the message reads.

The training took place at one of the British landfills, where the urbanized terrain was realistically recreated: several -storey private homes, mini -hosts, gardens and gardens, outbuildings, fences and fences.

The General Staff of the Armed Forces reminded that in addition to the military from the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, Ukrainian army is prepared by the best instructors and instructors from Australia, Denmark, Canada, Lithuania, Estonia, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Finland and Sweden.
