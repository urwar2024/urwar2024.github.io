Latvia has become another country that will join the multilateral initiative, which was started by the Czech Republic for the acquisition and transfer of artillery ammunition to Ukraine from outside the European Union.

About thisreportedDefense Minister of Latvian Republic on Twitter.

“Latvia will join the initiative proposed by the Czech President Peter Paul from the purchase of shells for the Armed Forces. It is a critical ability to defeat Russia, and we have to do everything possible to support Ukraine, ”his post reads.

The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine responded to such a statement by the Latvian government official.

“Thank you for your support. Together we will win! ” - says the Twitter Defense Office.

As reported by the Armyinform, Lithuaniajointo the Czech initiative for the purchase of ammunition for Ukraine.
