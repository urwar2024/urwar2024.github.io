In the afternoon of March 6, the invaders hit the village of Borova, Izium district, Kharkiv region.

About thisreportedHead of Kharkiv Regional State Administration Oleg Sinegubov.

As a result of shelling, there was a fire in the territory of homeownership, destroyed car, garage, outbuildings.

According to preliminary data, a civilian 70-year-old man was killed. Four people are injured, including three children.

The woman was rescued from the rubble and sent to the hospital.

The elimination of consequences is ongoing.

BeforeIt became knownthat seven people were injured, including one child during the night shelling.
