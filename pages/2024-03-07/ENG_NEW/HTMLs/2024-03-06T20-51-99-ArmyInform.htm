As a result of a rocket strike on the port infrastructure of Odessa, five people were killed, several more people were injured.

About thisreportedThe spokesman of the Navy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine is the captain of the 3rd rank Dmitry Pletenchuk on the air of the Teleterna "Unified News".

“We can state the fact of the Russian aggressor of another blow on the port infrastructure of the city of Odesa. Unfortunately, we have five dead and several wounded, ”he said.

Currently, according to Dmitry Pletenchuk, investigative actions are ongoing, and all the circumstances of this regular war crime by the Russian Federation are being clarified.

As reportedArmyinformIn Odessa, the President heard a briefing on a military command.
