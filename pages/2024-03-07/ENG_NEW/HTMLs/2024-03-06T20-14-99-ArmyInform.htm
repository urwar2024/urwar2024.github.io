The situation on the front remains difficult but controlled. The Armed Forces of Ukraine plan to carry out counter -struggles this year.

About thisstatedCommander of the Land Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk on the air of the Telernorphone "Unified News".

At present, the enemy is trying to seize the Ukrainian territories as much as possible before the election of the President of Russia, so "everything is possible and impossible."

“Everything is done to take the initiative. Our task is to stabilize the front line, to maximize the enemy that we do, and to make the maximum regrouping to remove parts that require long -term recovery, to landfills to create a shock grouping and this year to have contradictory actions. We succeed. The situation is stabilized, ”Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk emphasized.

According to him, there are several "hot" directions where the enemy concentrates a lot of strength, it is:

  • Avdiivka;
  • the direction of the time ravine;
  • The direction of the thorny towards the estuary.

The commander of the Land Forces noted that there are heavy battles daily in these areas, but Ukrainian soldiers keep and cause maximum losses to the enemy, it is visible from the daily rankings of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.

"I think we will stabilize the situation in the near future and do everything to prepare the troops to move to more active actions and intercept the initiative,"-assured Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk.

As reportedArmyinform, at the front per day - 76 combatants: the forces of defense repelled hostile attacks in four directions.
