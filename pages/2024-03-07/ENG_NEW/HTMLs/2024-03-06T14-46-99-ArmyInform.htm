In the south of Ukraine, the forces of defense eliminated 67 Russian invaders and enemy equipment, among which, in particular, the MSTA-B gun and the station of the IP "Field-21".

About thisreportDefense forces of South of Ukraine.

During the last day, thanks to the Armed Forces, the enemy on the left bank has become less than 13 guns, another station of radio electronic wrestling station, tank, mortar, 2 boats, 18 units of auto and armored vehicles.

The defense forces also destroyed the enemy volume, night vision camera and generator.
