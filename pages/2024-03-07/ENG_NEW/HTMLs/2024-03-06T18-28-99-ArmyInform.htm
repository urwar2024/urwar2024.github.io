President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky, together with Prime Minister of Greece, Kiriakos Kharotakis in Odessa, visited the Transfiguration Cathedral.

The head of statereportedIn his Telegram.

He noted that the temple had suffered significant destruction due to a Russian rocket strike.

“The vast majority of Russian targets are ordinary civilian objects. We have to restore everyone. I thank Greece for their willingness to participate in the reconstruction of our cultural heritage, in particular in Odessa, ”the President wrote.

As reported by the Armyinform, Ukraine and GreecePreparation beganConcerning a security agreement.
