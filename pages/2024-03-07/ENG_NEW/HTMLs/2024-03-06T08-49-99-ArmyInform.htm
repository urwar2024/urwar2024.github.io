The first meeting of the project for the Jatec (Nato-Ukraine Joint Analysis, Training and Education Center) was held in Bidgosch, the first joint institution of the Alliance and Ukraine.

About thisreported March 6Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.

"We are considering the Center, along with the mechanisms of the Council of Ukraine-NATO as an additional structural element for our integration into the Alliance," said Olga Stephanyshyn Viceremier-Minister for European and Euro-Atlantic Integration of Ukraine.

She called on a probe team to focus on topical issues that will help to find effective security and defense decisions, in particular in the context of Russian aggression.

"Ukraine will be useful for any practical knowledge and experience that can help us protect people and ensure our own safety, even on earth, in the sky, at sea or in cyberspace," Olga Stephanyshyn said.

The decision to launch Jatec was made on the basis of the meeting of the Council - NATO at the level of defense ministers on February 15, 2024.

The Center's tasks will be:

  • joint analysis and generalization of defense and safety experience;
  • improvement of the training and military education system;
  • promotion of interoperability;
  • Development of the ability of Ukraine's defense forces.

The work of the Center is aimed at achieving the full mutual mutual mutual mutual NATO forces.
