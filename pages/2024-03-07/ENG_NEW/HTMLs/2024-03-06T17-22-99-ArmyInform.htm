The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine welcomes the European Defense and Industrial Strategy (EDIS).

About thisinformsDefense Office of Ukraine.

“This is a significant step towards victory of the democratic world over Russia. We are ready to cooperate with European defense institutions, support defense projects between Ukraine and the EU and further integration into the global defense space, ”the message reads.

The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine thanked the European partners and colleagues from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine for cooperation with partners in facilitating the development of the first such EU document.

“We congratulate the fact that the future security system of the United Europe is being built now, taking into account the future process of Ukraine's accession to the EU. The unique combat experience gained by Ukraine in confrontation with the Russian aggressor will continue to be the key to European security, ”the defense department of Ukraine summarized.
