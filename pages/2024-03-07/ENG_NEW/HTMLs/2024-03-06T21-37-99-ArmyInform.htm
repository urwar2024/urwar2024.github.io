The duration of the fuel in the reactors of the Zaporozhye NPP floats, but Russians cannot restart it.

About thisstatedNNEGC Petro Kotin.

He noted that the occupation administration is not capable of overloading fuel. Its six -year stay in the reactors ends.

“To do this, you need to disassemble the reactor, access it, and then have a very complex technological operation to overload. It requires licensed staff who are not present now. It also requires complex management and technical solutions - what to do with the fuel that will be unloaded. There are people who have never done it, ”Petro Kotin explained.

At the same time, the head of Energoatom reminded that the places of Ukrainian specialists at the ZPP were taken by inexperienced Russians and people from cities that near Energodar, who have no idea about the operation of nuclear objects.

As reported by the Armyinform, during the occupation of the Rus more than 150 timesThey were brokenNuclear safety conditions.
