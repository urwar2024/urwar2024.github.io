The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov called the IT coalition an important long-term initiative and thanked the partners for their support.

About thisreported March 6Ministry of Defence Ukraine.

"Estonia and Luxembourg demonstrate true leadership and partnership as countries that are presiding in a coalition and help us to attach new participants," Rustem Umerov said.


According to him, the initiative has already united 11 states and accumulated enough contributions for the first procurement on priority requests of Ukraine.

"We invite all our partners to join the IT coalition to be part of a technological breakthrough in this struggle," Rustem Umerov said.

Estonia's Defense Minister Hanno, Makur, expressed his hope that the Purchase of IT Coalition would help the Armed Forces to win as soon as possible.
