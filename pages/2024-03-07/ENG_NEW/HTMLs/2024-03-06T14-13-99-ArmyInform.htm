In the post of Inspector of the Citizenship Department, the collaborator issued occupation documentation on the forced deportation of Ukrainians.

About exposing a collaboratorShe reported March 6SBU.

According to the department, after the capture of the city, the woman supported the invaders, and in June 2022 entered the occupation "Main Directorate of the Ministry of Internal Affairs in the Kherson region". Her responsibilities included the illegal certification of Kherson residents.

After Kherson's release, the collaborator remained in the city, but changed their place of residence and moved to the apartment of acquaintances. In this way, she hoped to "lie down" and avoid justice.

However, she failed. Now the person involved is in custody without the right to make bail.

She was informed of suspicion under Part 7 of Art. 111-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (collaborative activity).

The attackers are threatened with up to 15 years in prison with confiscation of property.
