Aero-intelligence officers operating near the Ukrainian-Russian border have discovered an autonomous mobile complex of video thermal surveillance "Moore-P" and destroyed it by the FPV-arable.

About thisreported March 6Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

According to intelligence, during the reconnaissance in the specified square, the GUR operators of the MoD of Ukraine also tracked the car that moved Russian invaders. They burned it.

The cost of such a complex is about $ 50 thousand. Murom optics are able to detect a person up to 10 km in a camera and up to 4 km of thermal imaging.

BeforeIt became knownthat the rare reconnaissance complex and a brigade command point were destroyed in the Tavriya direction.
