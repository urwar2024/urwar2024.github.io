The Security Service of Ukraine demonstrated the first Sea Baby sea drone, which was made due to a large -scale volunteer fee.

This is stated inmessageSBU.

The first sample in this party, which the SBU showed during the tests, was called "Avdiivka".

It differs from the previous generation, in particular, in appearance. The Sea Baby 2024 is a new generation drone that is improved and even more deadly to the enemy.

Like every model of marine drones, Avdiivka will pass a full cycle of maritime tests.

In the process, the SBU developers will adapt the latest onboard weapons, specially designed and manufactured for use on multi -purpose platforms, which are SEA Baby.

“The new Seababy 2024 has advanced technical characteristics, greater combat and maneuverane capacity. It is able to deliver almost a ton of explosives for more than a thousand kilometers. The SBU was conceived by this drone as a universal platform, which will be placed different weapons, " - said Hunter, Brigade General of the SBU.

The collection was organized by the Fandering Platform United24 together with Monobank and Oleg Gorokhovsky, volunteers Sergiy Sternenko and Igor Lachenko.

In record terms, thanks to Ukrainian business, international donors and Ukrainians have raised nearly 300 million hryvnias for 35 Sea Baby Sea Drones.

As reported by the Armyinform, with the help of the Sea drones of the SBU undermined the Crimean bridge andImpressedIn total, 11 Russian warships.
