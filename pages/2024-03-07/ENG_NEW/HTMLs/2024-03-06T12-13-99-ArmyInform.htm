Documented blasting activity of the Metropolitan Archpriest of the Cherkasy Diocese of the UOC (MP), which justified the armed aggression of the Russian Federation.

About thisShe reported March 6SBU.

According to the service, on his own page on Facebook, Klik heroned Russian invaders and denied their war crimes against Ukraine.

He also spread the pro -Kremlin narratives on the social network, which he tried to discredit Ukrainian defenders.

In particular, he accused Ukrainian defenders of preparation for military provocations in Odessa and called the soldiers of the special unit "Azov" - "Nazis".

The defendant supplemented his posts with hostile slogans, photos and videos from pro-Kremliv Internet resources.

During the search at the place of residence, the cleric revealed forbidden symbols and the mobile phone from which it distributed anti -Ukrainian agitation.

The SBU initiated by the SBU confirmed the facts of the criminal act of the person.

On the basis of the evidence collected by the Metropolitan Archpriest, it was reported suspicion under Part 2 and Part 3 of Art. 436-2 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (justification of armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine, glorification of its participants).

The issue of choosing a preventive measure is currently being resolved.

The attacker faces up to 8 years in prison with confiscation of property.
