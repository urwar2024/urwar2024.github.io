The Military Medical Commissions (VLK) in Kiev delayed the terms of the commission, worked part-time, and there were no electronic queues.

This was stated by the Minister of Health Viktor Lyashko, reportsInterfax-Ukraine.

According to the minister, this was, in particular, the reasons for conducting inspections of the work of the VLK in the capital.

“In Kiev, doctors were not taken out of the shopping center, there was no electronic queue. In addition, we see that the VLK worked until 14:00 and then closed for some reason. This is a violation of the standards, ”he said.

Viktor Lyashko noted that the working group of the Ministry of Health, which will carry out inspections of the VLK, in the first stage "will focus on resolving organizational issues that can be solved quickly, and then we will understand each complaint."

The minister said that, according to the norms, during the HCP for each patient in the e -health system, they open an electronic medical record that should be closed within four days.

At the same time, in some clinics, regulated for HCPs, in particular in Kiev, such records remained open for 10 days or more.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Ministry of HealthStarts checkingAll military medical commissions in Kiev.
