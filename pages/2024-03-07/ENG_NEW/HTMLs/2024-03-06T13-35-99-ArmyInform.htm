The projectile famine, replenishment of reserves and Russian rocket terror are the challenges that arise now, but Ukraine will find answers to them.

About thisstatedNSDC Secretary Alexei Danilov on Facebook, commenting on the need to find compromises from the Russian Federation.

"By prejudice with all kinds of" insights ", expert forecasts, comments of" anonymous sources "that were activated on the next wave of conversations about" the need to achieve consensus "," ceasefire "," Searching peaceful ways "," start consultations and negotiations ", would like make several conceptual remarks. This is necessary in order to sober up those who have believed too and in advance in the "weakness of the West", "Decline of America", "Russian power", "Ukrainian fatigue" and other landmarks of the type "Rashi Tudei", - the message reads.

Alexei Danilov stressed that the Ukrainian peace formula is a document not of the general declarations, but of practical implementation.

“If someone still does not understand this, it is worth getting rid of the illusions of Ukrainian compliance in this matter and understanding the level of our firmness. No one will ever apologize to the death of innocent Ukrainian children. Until there is a fair trial and punishment, there can be no contact with the current Kremlin gum, ”the NSDC secretary said.

He also emphasized that the restoration of Ukraine's territorial integrity is the unconditional and alternative absolute of the Ukrainian position to start any proposals for the settlement of war.

"In Ukraine, the land is not divided, we will bury it in it those who dared to check it," Alexei Danilov emphasized.

He acknowledged that the situation on the front is difficult, and Ukrainians were tired of war, but at the same time stressed that Ukraine would find answers to the challenges that arise, and Ukrainians, choosing between fatigue or slavery, would always choose freedom and will fight as much as it will be necessary . Alexei Danilov believes that Russia will not be able to demoralize and split Ukraine from the inside.

“The efforts of international intermediaries representing powerful and powerful countries should be directed not to Ukraine's conviction, but to the return of Russia to actions within the framework of international law, and in addition, more importantly, to eliminate sources of supply of military goods to the territory temporarily existing Russian Federation, ”the NSDC secretary summed up.
