During the last day, 86 combat clashes took place on the front. In total, the enemy caused 4 missile and 106 aviation strikes, carried out 154 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its Facebook page.

In the area of responsibility, the "KhortytsiaIn Kupyansk directionThe enemy of the offensive (assault) did not take action.

In the estuary directionOur warriors repelled 6 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Verkhnyamyansk and Torni of Donetsk region.

In the Bakhmut direction, 2 attacks of the enemy in the areas of settlements Ivanivske and the White Mountain of Donetsk region were reflected.

In the area of responsibility, "Tavriyain the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders reflected 23 enemy attacks in the areas of Berdichi settlements, Toneyke, Pervomaisk, Vodyane, Nevelskoye, Semenivka and Orlovka of Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the districts of Georgiyivka, Novomikhailivka, Prychistivka and Krasnogorivka of Donetsk region, where the enemy, 39 times tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy made 5 attacks in the areas of settlements of Gulyaypole, Malynivka and the robot of the Zaporozhye region.

In the area of ​​responsibility, "Odesa"in the Kherson directionDefense forces continue to keep positions. Despite significant losses, the enemy does not leave the attempt to knock out our units from the positions. So, during the last day, the enemy performed 3 unsuccessful assault actions.

In the area of responsibility, the "north"in Volyn and Polissya directionsThe operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent the transfer of our troops to threatening directions, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border in Belgorod region.
