In Transcarpathia, there was a whole scheme to transport the military to Hungary.

About thisShe reported March 6Special Prosecutor's Office of the Western Region.

According to the investigation, the Transcarpathian was provided with housing and subsequently sent to a settlement in the Berehiv district.

The organizer of the scheme followed the rules of conspiracy: he paved the route through the control posts of the border service, used several different cars, and was convinced in the absence of observation.

Near the border, the crossbiver ordered men to run towards Hungary. Such services cost $ 6500 per person.

The suspect has now been a precautionary measure in the form of a 24 -hour house arrest with an electronic control. In addition, involvement in the crime of others is checked.
