On Wednesday night, March 6, the Russians struck a rocket strike around the city of Pokrovsk in Donetsk region.

About thisreportedHead of Donetsk Ova Vadim Filashkin.

About the first o'clock in the morning, the enemy released four C-300 missiles around the city, hit a residential building.

As a result, the civilian was injured. 11 high -rise buildings, 3 private homes, educational institution, bank and enterprise are damaged.

“This blow is another reminder that there are no absolutely safe places in Donetsk region. Keep yourselves! Evacuate, ”Vadim Filashkin called.
