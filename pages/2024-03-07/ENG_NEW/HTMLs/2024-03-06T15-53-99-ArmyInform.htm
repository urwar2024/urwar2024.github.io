In Transcarpathia, border guards detained 11 conscripts who wanted to cross the border with Romania illegally.

About thisinformsState Border Service.

At first, border guards near Tisza found a group of seven men. At the request to stop, the violators rushed to the side. Five of them jumped into the water. Realizing how deep and rapid the river, three returned and tried to hide in the shrubs. The other two had to save the border guards.

“Two more were found with a drone. Residents of Ternopil, Sumy, Kharkiv, Rivne and Kyiv region were detained. Men confessed that they used the services of the cross -riders, which each of them had to pay from $ 4 to $ 8.5 thousand, ”the military said.

However, in addition to this group, the border guards of the Tyachiv department near Tisza detained three more men from Kirovograd, Kyiv and Kharkiv region.

On all detained violators, the border guards drawn up protocols on administrative offenses: "illegal crossing or attempting to illegally cross the state border of Ukraine."
