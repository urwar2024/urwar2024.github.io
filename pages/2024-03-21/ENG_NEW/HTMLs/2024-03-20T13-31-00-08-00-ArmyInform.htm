Employees of the Chop detachment operative-search unit eliminated a stable channel of illegal crossing of conscripts across the Ukrainian-Slovak border.

About thisinformsState Border Guard Service of Ukraine.

As noted in the department, the local taxi driver sought immigrants from the eastern and southern regions of Ukraine and for the monetary remuneration organized their illegal crossing of the State Bank within the Uzhgorod district.

In particular, at the beginning of March this year, the smuggler contacted a resident of the Odesa region in the city of Uzhhorod and offered to take him to the border for 2500 US dollars and indicate the further route. He took an advance payment of $1,500 from the "client" for the transaction and agreed with him on the date and time of the meeting.

“On a certain day, men came together on a taxi to a place where Odessa had to move on on foot. The cross -drill and his "client" was stopped 800 meters from the border in the area of the settlement of Palas Komarivtsi during the transfer of the second part of the funds in the amount of 38600 hryvnias, " - the message reads.

The State Border Service said that the organizer of the illegal scheme was detained in accordance with Article 208 of the CPC of Ukraine. He was suspected of committing a criminal offense under Part 3 of Article 332 of the Criminal Code "Illegal Transportation of Persons across the State Border of Ukraine" and chosen a preventive measure in the form of detention for a period of 60 days with the right to make a bail in the amount of UAH 151,400.

Measures to document and liquidate the channel were carried out by the staff of the National Police and the SBU, under the procedural guidance of the Transcarpathian Specialized Prosecutor's Office in the field of defense of the Western region.

Investigative actions are currently underway.
