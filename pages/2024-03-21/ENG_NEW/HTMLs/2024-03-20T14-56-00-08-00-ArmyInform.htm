During the combat day, the Russians launched an airstrike in the Antonivka area of the Kherson region and launched guided aerial bombs in the area of Temyrivka, Rabotyne and Mala Tokmachka in the Zaporizhzhia region.

About thisreported on March 20Defense forces of South of Ukraine.

The enemy continues the terror of the civilian population of the frontal territories of Dnipropetrovsk, Zaporizhzhya, Mykolaiv and Kherson regions.

During the day, the invaders used 169 FPV-oules, 13 Lacet UAVs and dropped 150 fragmental ammunition.

As a result of the blows of enemy UAVs in the Kherson region, 2 civilians were injured, a preschool, a dwelling house and a civilian car were damaged.

The hostile artillery fire was the water area of Ochakivska and the territory of the Kutsurub communities in Mykolaiv region. It was without the victims.

Asreported the armyinform, as a result of the air strike in Sumy region, a person was killed.
