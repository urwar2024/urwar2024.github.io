The occupying forces attacked cars with civilians in the Kherson community.

About thisreportedKherson Ova Chairman Alexander Proudin.

According to the information, it happened on the road between the settlements of Sadove and Antonivka.

According to preliminary information, two men were killed as a result of shelling. Apparently, they were 40 years old.

As reported by the Armyinform, as a result of the night attack of Russian troops in the Sinelnikov district of Dnipropetrovsk regionwoundedSix people, five of them are in hospital.
