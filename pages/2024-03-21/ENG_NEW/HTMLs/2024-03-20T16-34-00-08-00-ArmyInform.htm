The procurement agency of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine "State Telle Operator" (DOT) has concluded contracts for food supply for the Armed Forces for the second quarter.

About this DOTreported March 20.

According to the agency, saving on food sets is UAH 2 billion. It is expected that quarterly savings on products will be more than UAH 500 million.

This indicator is achieved by saving on ten most used goods, how to compare with prices in previous contracts:

  1. Pork, bone without bone - 17% savings
  2. Pork, thigh frozen - 31% of savings
  3. Chicken fillet frozen - 27% savings
  4. Buckwheat Buckwheat - 51% of savings
  5. Pork, bone without bone - 25% savings
  6. Sunflower Oil refined - 31% of savings
  7. Sugar - 27% of savings
  8. Onion fresh - 36% of savings
  9. Pork, ticks chilled - 17% of savings
  10. Ham, brisket (smoked) - 14% savings

DOT explained that the cost of products is based on the consumer price index and is revised on a monthly basis, reflecting the dynamics of the market. Flexible approach to pricing allows you to buy quality goods at their market value.

Asreported the armyinform, DOT changed the requirements for three categories of products to provide the Armed Forces.
