President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky has a telephone conversation with India Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

The head of state thanked the interlocutor for the support of Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for humanitarian aid that India provided to our country.

The President noted a constant dialogue between the two countries at different levels, including supporting the Ukrainian peace formula.

Volodymyr Zelensky thanked for the active participation of India's representatives at meetings at the level of national security and foreign policy advisers and emphasized the importance of India's participation in the Indian Peace Summit, the preparation of which continues in Switzerland.

The President of Ukraine and the Prime Minister of India have discussed the development of bilateral relations, one of the elements of which should be a meeting of teams and meetings of the Intergovernmental Ukrainian-Indian Commission for Cooperation in Delhi in the near future.

As reported by the Armyinform, President of Ukraine Volodymyr ZelenskyyHe notedthat the results of the next "Ramstin" will be new defensive packages, including scarce artillery.
