The recruiting center for the Armed Forces of Ukraine opened in the Dnieper. This is the first of the eight centers in the Dnipropetrovsk region.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

"The joint project of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine, the General Staff of the Armed Forces, the City Council and the Dnipropetrovsk Ova opened its doors and is ready to receive citizens at the address: Slobozhansky Avenue 8, Left Bank CNAP," the statement reads.

It is noted that at the Center, anyone will be able to get information about the possibility of using their professional skills in the country's defense forces. The staff of the recruiting center will be civilian workers, and its main purpose is to enable people to find a place in the army.

“The General Staff, together with the Ministry of Defense, has developed rules that will ensure the candidate's part of his chosen part to the respective position under the contract. This algorithm has been brought to each military unit, training center and shopping center and JV, ”said Colonel Alexei Panov.

Hundreds of vacancies of military units of OK East, the National Guard of Ukraine and Territorial Defense are already available at the center. The positions cover combat and supportive directions, including chefs, drivers, repairmen, UAV operators, accountants and many others.

During April this year, recruiting centers are planned in six other cities of Dnipropetrovsk region, including Kryvyi Rih, Kamianske, Pokrovsk, Pavlograd, Sinelnikovo and Novomoskovsk.

You can get information about possible employment in the Armed Forces and work by profession by visiting the recruiting center in person or by phone number+380 66 073 34 78.

The Defense Ministry added that in total, 27 centers of recruitment of the Armed Forces were scheduled to be opened by the country by the end of the first half of 2024.
