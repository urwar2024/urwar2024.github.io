During the last day, 80 combat clashes took place on the front. In total, the enemy caused 4 missile and 86 aircraft strikes, and made 114 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its Facebook page.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy made 10 unsuccessful storms of the positions of defense forces in the area of the settlement of Sinkivka of Kharkiv region.

In the estuary directionOur warriors repelled 14 enemy attacks in the areas of the settlements of Bigorivka, Luhansk region, thorns and controversial Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionOur defenders repelled 7 enemy attacks in areas of settlements Predictine, Tickyevka, White Mountain of Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 13 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Berdychi, Orlovka, Tonenko, Pervomaisk, Nevelskoye Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of Krasnogorivka, St. George, Novomikhailivka, Vodyane, Harvest, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops 29 times.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy attacked the positions of our defenders 6 times in the areas of settlements of Staromayorsk Donetsk region and a robot of Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson directionThe enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. So, during the last day, 1 assault on the positions of our troops.

In Volyn and Polissya directionsThe operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent the transfer of our troops to threatening directions, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border in Belgorod region.
