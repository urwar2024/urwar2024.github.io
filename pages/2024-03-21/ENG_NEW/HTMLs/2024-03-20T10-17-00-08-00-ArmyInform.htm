The Ministry of Veterans Affairs announced the launch of a single veterans line in cooperation with the Ukrainian Red Cross.

This is stated in a message onSiteOffice.

"The introduction of a telephone" single veterans line "is an important step and a component of a new veteran policy in support of Ukrainian defenders and defenders and their relatives," the message reads.

By phone 0 800 505 217 Qualified specialists will give advice on urgent issues to veterans and veterans, their families, as well as families of fallen soldiers.

There is a single veteran line daily from 08:00 to 20:00 in test mode.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Administrative Services Center (CNAP) will become“Single window”Obtaining services for veterans and their families.
