In the early days of a large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation, the attacker explored the places of basement and movement of Ukrainian troops that fought for the regional center.

About thisShe reported March 20SBU.

He was most interested in the locations of fire positions of air defense complexes of the Armed Forces.

Such information was especially valuable to the occupiers. This allowed Russian bombers to strike the city bypassing air defense.

On the eve of the liberation of the region, the suspect 'lay low' — he suspended contacts with russian intelligence and almost never left his own house on the outskirts of the regional center.

However, the SBU officers systematically followed the "trail" of the offender and detained him in the apartment where he was listed.

According to the investigation, the hostile agent was a local resident who was recruited by Russian military intelligence before the start of large -scale aggression against Ukraine.

His close relative is one of the commanders of the Russian sabotage and reconnaissance detachment "Irbis", which uses the call sign "Bagira".

It was she who was a "connected" person.

The information received from her relative was transmitted by a personnel employee of Russian intelligence to plan combat operations on the Eastern Front.

Currently, investigators of the Security Service have informed the enemy agent from Kharkiv about suspicion under Part 1 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (State betrayal).

The attacker is in custody. He is threatened with 15 years in prison with confiscation of property.
