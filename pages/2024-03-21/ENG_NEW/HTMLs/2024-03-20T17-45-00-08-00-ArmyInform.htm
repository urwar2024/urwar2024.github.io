Military doctors conducted a successful operation and seized a ball of 7.62 mm caliber from the leg of the Ukrainian defender.

“We thank the medical professionals of all medical evacuation units for tireless service. Despite all the difficulties associated with active fighting on the front, you are always on the guard of life! ” - added to the command.
