As a result of a Russian blow to Kharkiv, a multi -storey building and a production shop were damaged on March 20. Three people were killed, five more were injured.

About thisinformsHead of Investigative Directorate of the SUNP in Kharkiv Oblast Sergey Bolvinov on Facebook.

"At about one o'clock in the afternoon, the enemy shelled the city, damaging an eight-story building and a production workshop in the Kholodnohirsk district. The fire covered more than 1000 square meters. An ordinary printing house, a workshop for the production of furniture and paints and varnishes," the message says.

Among the wounded employees of the enterprise, two of them are in serious condition. Rescue operations and fire extinguishing are ongoing.
He added that the number of casualties and victims could be greater.
