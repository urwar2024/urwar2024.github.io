They are found guilty of the State Executive, collaboration, participation in the terrorist organization and activities not provided for by law.

They are also assigned to confiscation of property.

About thisreported March 20Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, the resident of Donetsk region in May 2023 voluntarily moved to the side of the enemy. He joined the illegal armed formation - the so -called "People's Police of the DNR".

The man was enrolled in the Rifle Battalion as a machine gunner. The convicted person fought in settlements of Donetsk region.

Another convicted - a resident of Donetsk mobilized to the ranks of illegal armed forces "DNR". As an arrow of the platoon, it was involved in the fighting against the Armed Forces. During the month of "services" the Donetsk resident received 76 thousand rubles.

The convict carried out the criminal orders of his commanders in various areas of the front near the village of Vesele, Kharkiv region, as well as in the Donetsk region - near the city of Mayorsk and the village of Novobakhmutivka, Pokrovsk district.

Both men were detained by the Armed Forces. They were waiting for the courts of the courts.

ForArmyinform data, 30 collaborators from Zaporizhzhya region, who stole and tortured people, were reported.
