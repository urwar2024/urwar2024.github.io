One of the topics of Denis Shmigal and Charles Michel's conversation has become the importance of legal and practical decisions on the use of frozen Russian assets for Ukraine's needs.

The Head of the Ukrainian Government thanked for the financial assistance and the decision to open negotiations on Ukraine's accession to the EU, which was adopted in December last year.

The parties discussed the economic and social support of our country, as well as steps towards European integration. The Prime Minister of Ukraine emphasized the importance of accepting the negotiation framework as soon as possible.
