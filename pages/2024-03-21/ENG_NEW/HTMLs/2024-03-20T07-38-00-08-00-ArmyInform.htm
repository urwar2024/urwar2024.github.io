The Russian troops on Wednesday were fired at the Sinelnikov district of Dnipropetrovsk region. Previously, 5 people were injured.

About thisreportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Ova Sergey Lisak.

Among the victims, a whole family whose house was trusted by the invaders.

Brother and sister-12 and 19 years old, 57-year-old father-in a state of moderate. The 47-year-old mother was seriously injured and in serious condition. All four - in the hospital.

The territory affected by the enemy damaged the dwellings. The information about the destruction is still clarified.
