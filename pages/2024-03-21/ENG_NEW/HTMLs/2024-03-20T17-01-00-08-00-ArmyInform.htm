During the Large War, Ukraine received nearly 98 billion euros, including € 33.1 billion in military assistance from the EU and Member States.

About thisreportedPrime Minister Denis Schmigal during the Prescribe following the 9th meeting of the Council of the Association between Ukraine and the EU.

“The Association Council has congratulated the general support provided by the EU and its Member States in the amount of nearly 98 billion euros since the beginning of Russia's aggressive war against Ukraine, including € 33.1 billion in military assistance. She also congratulated the preparation of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, which is conducted by the EU military assistance mission, ”the message reads.

The EU has also confirmed its determination to meet the urgent military and defense needs of Ukraine (including the supply of ammunition and missiles, in particular, given the obligation to give Ukraine one million artillery ammunition).

"Ukraine has congratulated the successful implementation of the EU military assistance mission to support Ukraine (EUMAM), including a new purpose to prepare another 20,000 Ukrainian soldiers," Denis Shmigal added.
