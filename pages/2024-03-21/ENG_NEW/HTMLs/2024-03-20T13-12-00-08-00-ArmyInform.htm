The Prime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal with a high representative of the European Union Josep Borrel took place in Brussels. On the eve of the meeting of the Council of the Association, Ukraine -EU parties discussed the key priorities of Ukraine.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

"In particular, issues such as the use of frozen Russian assets for the needs of Ukraine, support of the formula of peace and preparation for the Global Peace Summit, approval of the negotiation framework and the beginning of negotiations on Ukraine's accession to the EU were raised," the statement reads.

The meeting also discussed further military assistance, in particular within the framework of the European Peace Facility (EPF) and the EU's European Defence Industrial Strategy.

Denis Shmigal thanked the EU for an additional 5 billion euros for Ukraine's military support. At the end, the Prime Minister expressed his hope for fruitful meetings of the Association Council.

As reported by the Armyinform, March 21-22 in Brusselswill take placeEU summit. The leaders of the countries will discuss "the further support of Ukraine and its people in response to Russia's military aggression."
