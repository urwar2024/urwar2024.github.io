According to the results of the fourth in 2024, the wave of applications for veterans grants identified 39 winners. They will receive from the state 19 million UAH for start or development of their business.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

“39 veterans and others of their spouses were recognized as the winners of the fourth this year's wave of veterans component of the Program“ own business ”. The state invests in starting or scaling their business through grants 19 million UAH. In total, 407 veterans and members of their families have received a positive decision to grant a total amount of UAH 178 million, ”said Deputy Minister of Economy of Ukraine Tatiana Berezhna.

Among the 39 winners of the fourth this year, a wave of grant applications for grants 16 participants of hostilities, 23 members of veterans families. 1 winner received a grant up to 1 million UAH, 33 - up to 500 thousand UAH, 5 - up to 250 thousand UAH.

The most popular areas in which they plan to develop their business granting are wholesale and retail, temporary placement and food organization, processing industry.

According to the conditions of the program, participants of hostilities or persons with disabilities as a result of war, as well as the second of their spouses can receive the following grants:

  • up to UAH 250 thousand, up to UAH 500 thousand and up to UAH 1 million for a veteran, subject to the creation of one, two and four jobs respectively;
  • up to UAH 250 thousand and up to UAH 500 thousand - for the second of the veteran spouse, subject to the creation of one and two jobs, respectively.

Upon receipt of a grant from UAH 500 thousand to UAH 1 million, the veteran must be registered as a FOP for at least 3 years. In addition, such a grant is provided with co -financing with the recipient in the ratio of 70/30, that is, the grant recipient should invest 30% of their own funds, 70% of the funds will be provided by the state in the form of a grant.

Grant applications are submitted through the portal with the business plan.
