By the decision of the Economic Court of Kyiv, the claim of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine for the recovery of UAH 96.3 million was satisfied.

About thisShe reported March 20Specialized Prosecutor's Office of the Central Region.

According to the department, at the end of December 2020, a contract was concluded between the Ministry of Defense and one of the state -owned enterprises for the purchase of special purpose repair services.

However, contrary to the terms of the contract, the state -owned enterprise failed to fulfill its obligations for timely repair of special purpose equipment.

As a result, the defense agency is deprived of the possibility of operation of this equipment in the conditions of active phase of hostilities.

The Special Prosecutor's Office of the Central Region has filed a lawsuit with the requirement to recover from the enterprise subscription under the state contract and penalties for its improper execution.

According to the court decision, the debtor is obliged to pay in favor of the defense department funds for a total amount of UAH 96.3 million.

Asreported the armyinform, the Defense Ministry told how to solve problems about defense purchases.
