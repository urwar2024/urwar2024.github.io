EU High Representative for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy Josep Borrell has suggested that EU member states use 90% of the profits from frozen Russian assets for military aid to Ukraine.

He is about itwroteby Twitter.

“Today I presented the suggestion of the Supreme Representative to use unforeseen income from Russian frozen assets to support Ukraine in its struggle for victory. 90% will be allocated through the European Peace Fund and 10% through the EU budget. I count on the approval of the Council as soon as possible. This will demonstrate the unity of the EU and devotion to the support of Ukraine and its people, ”the post reads.

Josep Borrel added that the rapid adoption of this decision by the EU Council will demonstrate the unity of the EU and the dedication of Ukraine's support.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the EUThey called itA condition in which the assets of the Russian Federation will be used to purchase weapons for Ukraine.
