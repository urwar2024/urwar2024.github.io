Counterintelligence of the Security Service has broken an attempt to obtain the coordinates of defense forces in the border areas of Chernihiv region.

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

It is noted that as a result of the special operation, an FSB agent was detained, who tracked the location of fortifications and strongholds that ensure the defense of the northern borders of Ukraine.

He also tried to identify the orientation locations of the anti-aircraft missile complexes of the Ukrainian air defense.

“To collect the intelligence, the persons traveled to the bicycle and hidden photography of the location of the defense forces. He had to pass the information obtained through the messenger in the form of media files with a binding to the terrain, ”the department said.

The SSU was proactive, detaining the russian agent in advance and thus preventing the transfer of intelligence to the aggressor.

According to the investigation, the local communal, who was remotely recruited by the FSB Border Guard Officer in the Bryansk region, was remotely recruited. His identity has already been installed by the Security Service.

“In the field of view of the invaders, the traitor got back in late 2023 because of his anti-Ukrainian posts in pro-Kremlin telegrams. Subsequently, it was involved in intelligence and subversive activities in favor of the FSB, ”the message reads.

During the search, mobile phones, computer equipment and other material evidence of criminal activity were removed in the apartment.

Investigators of the Security Service informed him about suspicion under Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (State betrayal committed in martial law).

The attacker is currently in custody. He is threatened with life imprisonment.

Counterintelligence measures were carried out by SBU staff in the Chernihiv region under the procedural guidance of the regional prosecutor's office.
