The Russian serviceman is suspected of violating the laws and customs of war combined with a premeditated murder committed by a group of persons.

About thisreported March 20Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, at the end of February 2022 in the village of Nova Basan, Nizhyn district, a Russian serviceman attacked a resident of a neighboring settlement, who stopped near a column of Russian equipment and left his own car.

At the same time, the civilian man did not pose any threat to the troops of the aggressor country, he was unarmed.

The suspect shot him from the machine. He died on the spot from injuries. At the same time, another military of the Russian Federation, which was in the APC, fired from the machine gun from a man and his car.

Currently, measures are being taken to announce the suspect in the wanted, as well as the identification of another involved in the crime of the military.

Asreported the armyinform, reported suspicion of a Russian officer who shot civil cars in Gostomel.
