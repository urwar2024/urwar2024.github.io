Thanks to the resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, the issues of support and support of persons in Russian captivity, they will undergo a complete cycle of recovery and rehabilitation, developed according to Western methods together with foreign specialists.

About thisShe toldDeputy Minister of Defense Natalia Kalmykova.

In particular, servicemen released from captivity will be provided with recovery courses in rehabilitation centers, where they will receive medical and psychological assistance. They will also undergo a program of reintegration measures for recovery.

“We start working together on the creation of reintegration centers, taking into account both domestic and foreign experience of partners in rehabilitation and psychological assistance. Ukrainian experts have been trained with the assistance of the Alliance's partners. Therefore, our task is to create the most comfortable conditions for the liberated defenders of Ukraine and provide them with professional assistance, ”the Deputy Minister stressed.

After sending the rehabilitated centers to the rehabilitation centers, they will be provided with complete post -insulation and care of various specialists. Rehabilitation assistance will be done with the complete confidentiality of all information of the person and his data.

"Such support will also be provided to members of the families of the former prisoners to overcome difficult life circumstances and to minimize negative consequences," Natalia Kalmykova said.

The Deputy Minister also added that the Defense Ministry on the basis of NATO Alliance Standards begins the development and implementation of mechanisms of reintegration measures for all defense forces of Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Ministry of DefensepreparesMechanisms for counteracting space intelligence in the war against Ukraine.
