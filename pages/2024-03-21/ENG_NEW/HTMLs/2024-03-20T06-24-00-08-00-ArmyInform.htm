He starred in the Ukrainian tapes "Another Doctor", "Real mysticism", "Women's Doctor", participated in international projects, and for almost ten years managed the children's theater, which has repeatedly become a laureate of international competitions and festivals.

Now Vladimir responds faster to his call sign "Space" than by name, because now a 51-year-old man carries a Ukrainian unicorn-despite age, despite the lack of combat experience ...

While his unit is on restoration, the Army Correspondent spoke with Ukrainian artist Volodymyr Shamshurin.

- When the question of the choice of pseudo, the unit already had both Maestro and Artist. But I stopped at Space - it is as a mention of the Space neighborhood in Zaporozhye, where I spent my childhood, - says the actor in the first minutes of dating.

But she adds that people are a little strange for others, allegedly arrived in this world "from space".

The great invasion of the Muscovites began for Vladimir with a call from a friend - fired Saltivka (Kharkiv neighborhood. - Ed.), there is horror in the streets. The man remembers - he did not delay, on February 25, 2022 he went to the nearest shopping center and JV.

- when he made the documents, he did not even look there, being sure that he got into the TRO, because he did not have combat experience. I thought that I would dig trenches, bring the shells.

He now learned that he was now a serviceman of the National Guard, on the fifth day of service, being under a hostile shelling, when his unit reflected the first attack of the invaders near Kharkiv - Vladimir tells about this episode with a sincere smile.

The warrior remembers his emotions during the first fights. He manages to convey them as artists can only:

- On the one hand, it is an animal fear, a sense of self -preservation with your eyes involuntarily looking for a place where to hide, where to go. But at the same time-the willpower, our Ukrainian stubbornness, is pursued: you eventually decide to fight the last ...

The man tells that at times it seemed that the Ukrainian land became a continuous hell - this is the end, from this forest you would not come out, it is the last day of your life:

- But you come back. Someone dies nearby, and you come back. And you constantly consider - how does it work? And you can't find answers ...

For 11 months, the Space Unit defended the front area under Kreminna. They were the first to go to the Serebryansky Forest. During the next three months, they moved to the strategic-important highway in Luhansk region, selecting each meter with blood and sweat.

The fighting in that place is compares with the tug of drag: today - the initiative on the side of the units of the Defense Forces, tomorrow - the Russians will hit with new force and will be forced to move away from the occupied position. The next day the situation changes again ...

The next rotation of "Cosmos" passed under Bakhmut. His unit fought for a mite. Angnded by previous failures, the enemy did not allow you to approach positions, did not allow to equip trenches - "crown" from everything that was in service. So they had to hide in the "burrows", which were dug by the Russians.

The Army says that the enemy FPV-drones, which were in the sky of Donetsk region, were especially presented, and mortars who worked for a continuous one:

- The orcs put their pieces five in one place - until one is charging, the other shoots. And it lasted for hours. And they did not even try to storm - they just used the tactics of "burned ground".

But despite the prevailing forces of the enemy, the Ukrainian units were constantly trying to knock out the occupiers from the village. Several times the "Space" mouth was stormed - the boys returned to the wounded, many fell on that piece of Ukrainian land, but they completed the combat mission - Tickyivka is still under the control of Ukrainian forces.

Even in the most difficult battles, Vladimir does not forget about art - for combat outputs, the man takes the GPRO camera and films everything that happens around - both for history and for the Hague court, where they will surely judge the Putin regime.

In collaboration with informal cinema association Babylon`13 was createdThe War-Nanager Project. He screens short literary works by Ukrainian authors and authors that defend Ukraine's independence with weapons in their hands.

In particular, "Space" became one of the heroes of the project of Portrait of now Ukrainian director Yuri Rechinsky. He filmed one day from the life of the military of the 5th Slobozhansky NSU Brigade. The project is currently under production.

And after the victory, the man dreams of returning to the acting. And also become a director of your own ribbon, where the war will be shown as it is - without lies and jewelry, and the characters will be his brothers.
