The deputy commander of the platoon of the special forces of the Russian troops of the Russian Federation on the Krasnoyarsk region was informed of suspicion of violating the laws and customs of war.

About thisreported March 20Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, the suspect and his subordinates received an order from his commander to kill the civilian population in the occupied Gostomel.

On February 25, 2022, while in combat positions in the area of Shevchenko and St. Pokrovskaya streets, he opened fire from small arms on a civilian car moving on the street. Shevchenko in the direction of Bucha.

The driver of the vehicle suffered numerous gunshot wounds. Subsequently, the victim was able to escape.

In total, the detachment servicemen are involved in the execution of 12 vehicles in Gostomel, resulting in killed 11 civilians and 15 were injured.

ЯкThe ArmyInform reported earlier, two Russian generals who bombed Borodyanka were notified of suspicion.
