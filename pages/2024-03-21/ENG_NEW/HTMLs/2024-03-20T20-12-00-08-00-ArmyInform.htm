In Ukraine, almost 30 thousand people are considered missing in special circumstances and illegally deprived of personal freedom through armed aggression of the Russian Federation.

About thisreportedIn the office of the Commissioner of the Verkhovna Rada for Human Rights Dmitry Lubin.

According to him, on Wednesday, March 20, a meeting of the Commissioner for Human Rights in the system of bodies of security and defense sector Alexander Kononenko with representatives of the Moscow mechanism of the OSCE was held.

The representative of the commissioner noted that almost 30 thousand people are considered missing in special circumstances and illegally deprived of personal freedom as a result of the armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine in accordance with the Unified Register of Persons missing in special circumstances. There are three categories: children, civilians and prisoners of war.

It is noted that this registry is currently in a state of filling, so the relevant data can be much larger.

Oleksandr Kononenko noted that about 1,600 people were unlawfully detained and condemned by the Russian Federation.

According to him, among the illegally detained Russian persons are sick and vulnerable groups, as well as three OSCE employees.

“The Commissioner Secretariat works to protect the right of civilians who illegally detained and condemned the Russian Federation. It is extremely necessary for this flashy problem to sound internationally, ”the Ombudsman's office emphasized.
