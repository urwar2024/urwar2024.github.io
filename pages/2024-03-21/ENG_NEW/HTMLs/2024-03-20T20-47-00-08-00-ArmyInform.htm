In Kharkiv, the number of wounded due to the Russian attack in the afternoon on March 20 has increased.

About thisreportedHead of the Regional Military Administration Oleg Synigubov.

"As of now, the number of victims has increased to 8. Seven of them were hospitalized, one person has been assisted on the spot," the statement said.

Five dead are also known. The information is updated.

As you know, due to a missile strike onKharkovThere was a large -scale fire with increased complexity. The correspondent of the Armyinform reported that the blow came across the building where a number of enterprises were located.
