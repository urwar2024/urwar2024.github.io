As a result of the night attack of Russian troops in the Sinelnikov district of Dnipropetrovsk region, six people were injured, five of them are in hospital.

About thisReportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Regional State Administration Sergey Lisak.

Among the victims, a 13-year-old girl and a 15-year-old boy.

5 private houses were destroyed. There are also damaged houses. Power lines and a gas pipeline were affected.

In addition, in the morning the enemy fired at Nikopol artillery. A 27-year-old man was injured.

The city has a store and a utility company, housing, outbuildings and cars.
