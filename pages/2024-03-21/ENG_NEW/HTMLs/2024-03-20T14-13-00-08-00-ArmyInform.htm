The State Budget of Ukraine received the first tranche in the framework of transitional financing of € 4.5 billion in accordance with the financial instrument of the European Union Ukraine Facility.

This is stated inmessageon the government portal.

Within the framework of transitional financing before Ukraine, there are 5 reforms on the agenda in the following areas: public finance management, fight against corruption, development of business environment, etc.

Ukraine may receive the next tranche of EUR 1.5 billion within the framework of transitional financing as early as April based on the results of satisfactory implementation of such reforms and a positive assessment by the European Commission.

Today, the financial support of international partners plays an extremely important role both in ensuring macroeconomic stability and as a foundation for further economic growth, in particular through the implementation of structural reforms within the framework of such development programs.

The raised funds will help to finance the priority social and humanitarian expenditures of the state budget in a timely and fully finance.

Ukraine facility is a tool that provides Ukraine with Ukraine in 2024-2027 EU financial and technical assistance totaling EUR 50 billion.

Of these, 38.27 billion euros will be directed to budget support, the investment fund - 6.97 billion euros, and technical and administrative support - € 4.76 billion.

As reported by the Armyinform, the EU Supreme Representative and Security Policy of Josep BorrelsuggestedEU Member States use 90% of the profit from frozen Russian assets for military assistance to Ukraine.
