During the two -day summit, European Union leaders will discuss further support of Ukraine against the background of Russia's aggressive war.

This is stated in the message of the European Council.

The EU Summit will take place in Brussels on March 21-22. The leaders of the countries will discuss "the further support of Ukraine and its people in response to Russia's military aggression."

It is emphasized that the EU will continue to provide political, financial, economic, humanitarian, military and diplomatic support for as long as necessary.

"Today, the EU and its Member States have allocated more than 138 billion euros to support Ukraine," Brussels said.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the EU finallyguessedallocation of EUR 5 billion for military aid to Ukraine.

Also in the EUtoldDetails of creating a special fund for Ukraine for 5 billion euros.
