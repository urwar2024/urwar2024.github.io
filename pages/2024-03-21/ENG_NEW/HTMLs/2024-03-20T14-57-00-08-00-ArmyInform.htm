Three violators and a conductor were detained in Bukovina, trying to transport them to a neighboring country outside the checkpoint.

About thisinformsState Border Guard Service of Ukraine.

According to information, operative staff of the Chernivtsi Border Detachment received information on preparation for illegal crossing of the state border of Ukraine with Romania on the section of the department "Krasnolsk".

“A resident of Chernivtsi and two residents of Ivano-Frankivsk regions have agreed with a local resident that he would secretly brought them to a neighboring country. Everyone paid 5500 euros for this, ”the department said.

However, the campaign of the "tourist" group was stopped by border guards near the state border line. The detainees drawn up the administrative offense protocols.

With regard to the organizer of illegal transition across the border to law enforcement agencies, a notification of detection of signs of a criminal offense provided for in Article 332 (332 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine. The information was entered in the ERDR.
