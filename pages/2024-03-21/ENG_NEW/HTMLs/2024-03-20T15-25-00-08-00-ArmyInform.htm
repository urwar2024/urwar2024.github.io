Lithuania announced the allocation of 35 million euros for the purchase of ammunition for Ukraine within the Czech Initiative for ammunition.

About thisreportedLithuanian Prime Minister Ingrid Shimonite on Twitter.

“The Lithuanian government has allocated 35 million euros for ammunition for Ukraine as part of the Czech ammunition initiative. Together, whatever you need to win! ” - She says.

The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov expressed gratitude to the Lithuanian government and the people for another important package of military assistance for Ukraine.

"The power of unity brings the victory," he wrote.

As reported by the Armyinform, one and a half dozen countries, including Germany, France, the Netherlands, Canada and others, have already joined the Czech initiative.

Yesterday became known that FinlandprovidesEUR 30 million to Ukraine for the purchase of shells within the framework of the Czech initiative.
