The US Ministry of Defense spokesman, Major-General Patrick Ryder, answered questions about possible reservations by Western partners for Ukraine on the use of F-16 aircraft, which the Armed Forces of Ukraine should soon receive.

Patrick Ryder provided his comments ininterviewjournalist of the Ukrainian Radio Liberty, which was shown in the telethon "United News" on the morning of March 20, 2024.

Thus, answering the question of what reservations there are for Ukraine regarding the F-16 and whether Russia's border will become a "red line" in this case, the Pentagon spokesman said that the West sees Ukraine using these aircraft only within its sovereign territory.

According to Patrick Ryder, having received the F-16, Ukraine will have a "unique air potential".

"Which aims to focus on Ukraine's sovereign territory and within Ukraine's sovereign borders," he emphasized.

The Pentagon spokesman added that the Air Coalition was currently created to support Ukraine by its Western partners to teach Ukrainian pilots and techniques of control and support of these aircraft.

The air coalition also takes care of the delivery of the F-16 to Ukraine.

As ArmyInform reported, on March 19, 2024, Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem UmerovReportedThe results of the contact group meeting (Ramstein), noting that Ukraine is actively working with partners to make the F-16 aircraft in our sky as soon as possible.
