Today, at about 1:00 pm, a terrorist country has struck Kharkiv. The Russian army kissed a civilian object located in the city industry.

According to the correspondent of the Armyinform, the blow came across the building where a number of enterprises were located. There was a large -scale fire at the site of arrival.

According to a spokesman of the SES of Kharkiv garrison Yevgeny Vasylenko, the area of ignition is more than 2 thousand square meters, with increased level of complexity. 35 fire payments, more than 180 rescuers were involved in eliminating the fire.

According to Volodymyr Timoshko, Kharkiv Oblast Chief of Police, seven victims are now known. They were all sent to medical facilities. The condition of one of them is evaluated by doctors as severe. It also became known about the five dead who were victims of the Russian terrorist attack. There is information that up to 10 people can be in the rubble.

According to the head of the prosecutor's office of Kharkiv region Alexander Filchakov, it was previously established that Russian terrorists hit the enterprise with a missile X-59. On this fact, criminal proceedings were opened under Article 438 of Clause 2: "Violation of the laws and customs of war, combined with premeditated murder."
