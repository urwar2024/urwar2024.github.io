He starred in the Ukrainian tapes "Another Doctor", "Real mysticism", "Women's Doctor", participated in international projects, and for almost ten years managed the children's theater, which has repeatedly become a laureate of international competitions and festivals.

Now Vladimir responds faster to his call sign "Space" than by name, because now a 51-year-old man carries a Ukrainian unicorn-despite age, despite the lack of combat experience ...

While his unit is on restoration, the Army Correspondent spoke with Ukrainian artist Volodymyr Shamshurin.

- When the question of the choice of pseudo, the unit already had both Maestro and Artist. But I stopped at Space - it is as a mention of the Space neighborhood in Zaporozhye, where I spent my childhood, - says the actor in the first minutes of dating.

But she adds that people are a little strange for others, allegedly arrived in this world "from space".

The great invasion of the Muscovites began for Vladimir with a call from a friend - fired Saltivka (Kharkiv neighborhood. - Ed.), there is horror in the streets. The man remembers - he did not delay, on February 25, 2022 he went to the nearest shopping center and JV.

- when he made the documents, he did not even look there, being sure that he got into the TRO, because he did not have combat experience. I thought that I would dig trenches, bring the shells.

He now learned that he was now a serviceman of the National Guard, on the fifth day of service, being under a hostile shelling, when his unit reflected the first attack of the invaders near Kharkiv - Vladimir tells about this episode with a sincere smile.

The warrior remembers his emotions during the first fights. He manages to convey them as artists can only:

- On the one hand, it is an animal fear, a sense of self -preservation with your eyes involuntarily looking for a place where to hide, where to go. But at the same time-the willpower, our Ukrainian stubbornness, is pursued: you eventually decide to fight the last ...

The man tells that at times it seemed that the Ukrainian land became a continuous hell - this is the end, from this forest you would not come out, it is the last day of your life:

- But you come back. Someone dies nearby, and you come back. And you constantly consider - how does it work? And you can't find answers ...

For 11 months, the Space Unit defended the front area under Kreminna. They were the first to go to the Serebryansky Forest. During the next three months, they moved to the strategic-important highway in Luhansk region, selecting each meter with blood and sweat.

The fighting in that place is compares with the tug of drag: today - the initiative on the side of the units of the Defense Forces, tomorrow - the Russians will hit with new force and will be forced to move away from the occupied position. The next day the situation changes again ...

The next rotation of "Cosmos" passed under Bakhmut. His unit fought for a mite. Angnded by previous failures, the enemy did not allow you to approach positions, did not allow to equip trenches - "crown" from everything that was in service. So they had to hide in the "burrows", which were dug by the Russians.

The Army says that the enemy FPV-drones, which were in the sky of Donetsk region, were especially presented, and mortars who worked for a continuous one:

- The orcs put their pieces five in one place - until one is charging, the other shoots. And it lasted for hours. And they did not even try to storm - they just used the tactics of "burned ground".

But despite the prevailing forces of the enemy, the Ukrainian units were constantly trying to knock out the occupiers from the village. Several times the "Space" mouth was stormed - the boys returned to the wounded, many fell on that piece of Ukrainian land, but they completed the combat mission - Tickyivka is still under the control of Ukrainian forces.

Even in the most difficult battles, Vladimir does not forget about art - for combat outputs, the man takes the GPRO camera and films everything that happens around - both for history and for the Hague court, where they will surely judge the Putin regime.

In collaboration with informal cinema association Babylon`13 was createdThe War-Nanager Project. He screens short literary works by Ukrainian authors and authors that defend Ukraine's independence with weapons in their hands.

In particular, "Space" became one of the heroes of the project of Portrait of now Ukrainian director Yuri Rechinsky. He filmed one day from the life of the military of the 5th Slobozhansky NSU Brigade. The project is currently under production.

And after the victory, the man dreams of returning to the acting. And also become a director of your own ribbon, where the war will be shown as it is - without lies and jewelry, and the characters will be his brothers.

How is a mobilized to get a military rank ahead of schedule

Author: Army Inform
Categories: Новини, Соціальний захист
Description: The Armyinform tells when and what titles are granted, why they may not be assigned ...
Published Time: 2024-03-20T06:56:00+08:00

There are primary and regular titles in the Armed Forces of Ukraine. They are assigned under certain conditions. At the same time, during the martial law, quite significant changes occurred in the order of assignment.

ArmyinformHe tells when and what titles are granted, why they may not be assigned, and under what conditions the titles may be deprived.

Primary military ranks

Recruit.A serviceman who entered the service of his oath. As soon as he takes the oath, he receives the title of a soldier.

Junior Sergeant.This primary rank under normal conditions is received by the military servicemen who have been trained and are appointed to the positions of the Sergeant and the officers. But during the action of martial lawallowedTo assign the title of junior sergeant Private, who did not take place, but have combat experience.

Junior Lieutenant.Due to the large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation in Ukraine, there was a significant need to complete the units of primary officers. Therefore, there were perhaps the most changes. In particular, younger lieutenants can be sergeants and non -higher education sergeants but have experience. So, to whom the military rank is given:

  • servicemen who graduated in wartime higher military educational institutions, a military educational unit of higher education institution under a reduced training program;
  • The persons of the sergeant and sergeant composition who are contracting or military service under the conscription during the mobilization, for a special period, have higher education, have passed (if necessary) a course of military training in a direction corresponding to the profile of official activity, in particular who serve in the military reserve, while admission to military service under the contract of officers and appointment to positions, taking into account the need for officers of such a specialty;
  • Sergeant and sergeant servicemen who are assigned to the positions of officers in wartime and successfully perform their duties;
  • Supporters of the rankings, the sergeant and sergeant staff, who are conscript during the mobilization, for a special period, have a higher education degree not lower than the bachelor's degree, have undergone a military training course under the officers training and have taken place;
  • The persons of the sergeant and sergeant staff who were appointed to the positions of officers and received higher education.

The Ministry of Defense is also introducing a new procedure for mobilized doctors to obtain officers.

Regular military ranks

Obtaining regular military ranks depends on two factors - the time of service in the previous rank and position. At the same time, during the martial law, the time of service in the previous rank is much smaller than it would be in peacetime. In particular, the following terms of service in the military rank have now been set:

  • 1 year-for the junior and senior sergeant and sergeant (junior sergeant, sergeant, senior sergeant, chief sergeant, headquarters), junior officers (junior lieutenant, lieutenant, senior lieutenant, captain);
  • 2 years-for the highest sergeant and sergeant (master-stateant, senior master-assistant, chief masterpad), senior officers (Major, Lieutenant Colonel, Colonel).

But even if you have served in your rank set deadlines, the rank also requires a position that provides a military rank higher than what is present at this time. That is, if you are a younger lieutenant, and you are in a position on which the older lieutenant should be on the staff list, then you can get the next title. If you are already a senior lieutenant, then there will be no increase in the rank in this position.

The regular military ranks can be obtained ahead of schedule. In particular, in order to encourage command. This impliesLaw of Ukraine “On the Disciplinary Statute of the Armed Forces of Ukraine”. At the same time, if earlier early assignment was possible only once in the entire period of service, then in the title of major this can be done twice.

“During the period of martial law or repel of armed aggression against Ukraine, promotion in the form of early assignment of the next military rank can be applied to the servicemen .

Cases in which military rank is not assigned

In addition to the fact that the regular title may not be given in the case of official restrictions, there are also other factors.

In particular, if a serviceman is used disciplinary punishment - a warning of incomplete service. Either a court sentence is imposed not related to deprivation or restriction of liberty, arrest, detention in a disciplinary battalion, or exemption from serving a sentence with a test.

Degradation in rank or at all deprivation

The lowering in the military rank can also be as a disciplinary penalty. In this case, the decrease goes one degree for Sergeant and Chiefs of Sergeant, as well as officers. Exception for junior sergeants and junior lieutenants. This penalty is 1 year. But after the expiration of his validity or early removal in the previous military rank, the serviceman is not renewed.

They may deprive the military on a sentence of court in connection with conviction for committing a serious or particularly serious crime. Such servicemen are excluded from the lists of personnel of the military unit. And the territorial centers of staffing, which take such servicemen on registration, assign them to the military rank of a soldier of the reserve.
