Units of the Defense Forces stopped two attempts to infiltrate enemy sabotage and reconnaissance groups in the Sumy region.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its Facebook page.

The sabotage groups were neutralized in the areas of old Guta and bars.

It is also reported that the enemy has struck aviation in areas of settlements Velika Pisarivka, Old Huta, Ryzhivka, Popivka, Yamne, Oleksandrivka, Lugovka of Sumy region.
