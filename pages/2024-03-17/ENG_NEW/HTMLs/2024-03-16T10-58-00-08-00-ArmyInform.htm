Vladislav Olegovich Lonsky-commander of the 2nd Airborne Assault Company of the 3rd Battalion Tactical Group of the 80th Separate Airborne Assault Brigade-February 24, 2024 was awarded the highest state award-the title of Hero of Ukraine (posthumously).

About thisToldIn the assault troops of the Armed Forces, reports the Armyinform.

The commander of the Airborne Troops of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, who at that time commanded the 80th brigade Brigade General Igor Skibiuk characterizes the paratrooper as follows: “He was a reliable reserve of the brigade, stretched complex operations on himself, was always focused on successful combat. This is a good commander who really deserved the highest distinction.

On the first day of the large-scale war, Strik performed combat missions in the area of the Antonovsky Bridge of the Kherson region as a deputy commander of a landing assault company. On the same day, his commander was seriously injured, and he accepted his mouth command.

“By order, we received and transferred two seriously wounded officers to the medical center after leaving the environment. He gave the command to evacuate all the wounded, and he remained fighting and covering our Evacogroups. He left no one in trouble, ”the paratrooper brother tells.

The mouth under his command successfully fought defensive fights, assault actions and effectively destroyed the enemy. "Fighting for Lisichansk, Serebryanka, the Virgin, a counter-offensive in Kharkiv region ... He participated in the shooting battles side by side with soldiers," the fighting brother recalls.

Near Kryvyi Rih, Lonsky has competently managed the storm actions and returned two settlements under the Ukrainian flag. Colonel Pavel Razlach, who at that time was the commander of the 3rd Battalion Tactical Group of the 80th Brigade of the DShV, recall those days that Lonsky's mouth also displaced the enemy who seized the bridgeheads on the bank of the Siversky Donets River and participated in the destruction of the hostile crossing.

On May 22, the paratroopers destroyed the BMD-1 and living force in the area of the Bakhmut district in the Donetsk region during the maintenance of the defense border as a result of the shooting fight.

On June 13, in the area of the Virgin Kramatorsk district in Donetsk region, under the command of a bold captain, she destroyed 2 Russian tanks and infantry fighting.

On July 2, Vladislav Lonsky received a heavy injury in the Upper Kamyanka area during a tank shelling, but returned to the unit a week. During the raid actions in Kharkiv region, in the area of the settlement of Babenkovo district, the unit of Captain "Strike" took captivity of Russian officers, soldiers, captured the enemy equipment: T-72B3 tank, self-propelled artillery installation 2C3 "AKACA-2", MT-LB. Two cars KAMAZ and 2 BMP-1.

Already on September 11, during offensive and search-and-strike operations, the 2nd company tactical group under the command of Captain Lonsky crossed the Oskil River and gained a foothold on the left bank.

“When Oskil was cleaned, he reported to me that the bridge was not broken across the river. I gave the command to cross and provide a bridgehead on the left bank. The enemy could not knock them out of them, so he destroyed the dam. The bridge washed, the mouth remained cut off. We made them crossings on boats, pulled ropes, boats took away the wounded ... ”,-said his former commander of the 3rd BTGR.

Then there was a sharp rise in water by 4 meters. It was not possible to return the equipment. The commander of Lonsky was set to stay with part of the personnel on the left bank to keep the bridgeheads. The stability in the implementation of this solution in the future allowed to safely install the pontoon bridge to move our main forces across the Oskil River. For more than a week, the lard officer held defense with his paratroopers and other representatives of the Defense Forces of Ukraine. On September 20, as a result of the KSP missile firing, Captain Vladislav Lonsky was killed.

The paratrooper Vladislav Lonsky on the pseudo "Strik" to the last brave and effectively defended Ukraine, showed courage and professionalism in the command of the unit, died by the hero ...
