Deputy Defense Minister Vitaliy Polovenko and Dmitry Klimenkov, as well as the General Director of the State Tyle Tale Arsen Zhumadilov, held a working meeting with companies that will supply food for the Armed Forces of Ukraine within two weeks.

Dmitry Klimenkov about itreportedOn your Facebook page.

The meeting discussed the organization of the supply and quality control process.

“These are real Ukrainian products. They have production facilities, warehouses, transportation for delivery, ”Dmitry Klimenkov said.

He stressed that the purpose of the Ministry of Defense so that the Ukrainian military has a continuous and best nutrition.

"We strive for modern standards of supply chain management and the introduction of new technologies to provide our military with products. We must create a reliable system that can be relied on at any time and in any conditions," the Deputy Minister of Defense added.

As reported by ArmyInform, the State Logistics Operatorregulated suppliersfood for the Armed Forces of Ukraine in 13 sectors covering the entire Ukraine. The average price for a set is UAH 111.49.

Based on the results of two rounds of collecting commercial proposals, 5 suppliers were selected, whose offer was the most cost-effective.
