The new recruitment algorithm to the ranks of the Armed Forces of Ukraine has been brought to all military units, to all CCCs and JV and all training centers.

About it on airTelelorphoneThe only news was told by the Commissioner of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine for recruitment Alexei Bezhevets.

"Accordingly, the entire military vertical knows how it works. If there is a misunderstanding, a person has the opportunity to demand compliance with clear rules that have been proven," said Oleksii Bezhevets.

According to him, the algorithm was created for people who want to find themselves in the Armed Forces.

"A person can come, ask all the questions, find out the vacancies, what are the units, what are the specific requirements, communicate with the authorized officer of the unit," explained Oleksii Bezhevets.

He emphasized that the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine is currently providing an algorithm for volunteers.

“We are talking about people who voluntarily came, asked, chose. There is a clear algorithm on how to realize yourself and get into a specific military unit, ”added the Commissioner of the Ministry of Defense.

Alexei Bezhevets noted that regardless of the chosen specialty, every person undergoes a basic general military training for a minimum of one month.

“Further, if a person comes to a position with a certain specialization, then professional training takes place. A person is taught to the position she enters the military unit. The previous experience and profession are also taken into account, ”he added.

As reported by the Armyinform, the first in UkraineCenter for RecruitingThe Ukrainian army opened in Lviv. The second center next weekOpensin Zaporozhye.

The purpose of the project is to inform the population about how to join the ranks of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
