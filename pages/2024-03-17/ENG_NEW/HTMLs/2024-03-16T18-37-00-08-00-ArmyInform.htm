In Odessa, work on the site of yesterday's rocket attack of the Russian Federation continues, 64 houses were damaged as a result of shelling.

About thisreportedMayor of Odessa Gennady Trukhanov in a telegram channel.

“It is known about the damage to 64 homes of Odessa, another 4 private homes were completely destroyed. The examinations continue. The operational headquarters works on the spot, ”the message reads.

The mayor of Odessa added that utilities eliminate the consequences and, together with charitable organizations and funds, help the windows closing with film and slabs.

On Saturday, the number of rocket stroke of Russia in Odessaincreased to 21. Another SES employee was killed in the hospital from a serious injury.

More than 70 more were injured in varying degrees, there are "heavy".
