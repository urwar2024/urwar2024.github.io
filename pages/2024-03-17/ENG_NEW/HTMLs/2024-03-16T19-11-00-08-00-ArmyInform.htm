The russian military shelled the Nikopol district of the Dnipropetrovsk region eight times during the day, hitting with kamikaze drones and artillery.

About thisreportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Ova Sergey Lisak.

Nikopol and the Chervono -Grigoriv community were suffering from Russian terror. A man of 75 years and a 77-year-old woman were injured.

More than three dozen private homes are damaged. One of them was engaged - the fire rescuers tamed.

Two outbuildings are destroyed, 6 more - mutilated. Drilled garages and cars, loader, greenhouse.

There is a destruction in the infrastructure object. The transmission lines and gas pipelines were affected.

The consequences of the shelling, which occurred in the evening, are still being clarified.

As the army reported, work on the site of yesterday's rocket attack of the Russian Federation continues in Odessa, 64 houses were damaged as a result of shelling.
