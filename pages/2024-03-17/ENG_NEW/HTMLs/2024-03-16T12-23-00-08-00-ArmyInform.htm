In the temporarily occupied Mariupol, pseudo-elections of the President of the Russian Federation are underway.

About thisReportsMariupol City Council, ArmyInform reports.

The invaders set themselves the goal of showing a high turnout in order to legitimize their power. However, Mariupol residents ignore the election farce, so visiting Russian builders are driven there.

To inflate the turnout, the occupiers introduced an innovation — now you can vote not at the place of registration. Therefore, the pseudo-government set a plan for construction companies to send Russians to the presidency. They come in groups to the polling station for propaganda videos.
