Colonel Yuri Ignat reserves the post of the Public Relations Service for the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

About thisreportedLieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk, Commander of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.

“Today I thank Colonel Yuri Ignatus Uglyat. I thank for the productive work as the Chief of the Public Relations Service for the Air Force Command. It will remain in the Air Force and will work in another direction no less successful. I have no doubt, ”the message reads.

The commander noted that Yuriy Ihnat performed a huge amount of work.

“And most importantly - the result of fighting the enemy on the information front! Both in Ukraine and abroad ... for which he received a well -deserved state award from the President of Ukraine, ”Mykola Oleshchuk added.
