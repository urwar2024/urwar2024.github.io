About 20 settlements of Kharkiv region have suffered artillery and mortar shelling: Ambarne, Stroiivka, Sagittarius, two -year, Brest.

About thisinformsThe head of Kharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov in his telegram-channel, reports the Armyinform.

In the village of Okhrimivka, Chuguev district, as a result of an attack of hostile UAV, two men 48 and 35 were wounded.

“Today, at 02:25, in the village. Kozacha Lopan of the Kharkiv District of the Shakh MAKCLIA in the village. The critical infrastructure object is damaged. There are no victims, ”the message reads.

As a result of the shelling of the village of Bugayivka of the Chuguev district, the outbuilding burned. Without the victims.

Also in the village of Zolochiv, Bohodukhiv district, the Russians struck the Iskander-M ballistic missile. 16 private homes, hospital premises were damaged. The hostel building, four vehicles of ambulance, four cars were destroyed. Without the victims.
