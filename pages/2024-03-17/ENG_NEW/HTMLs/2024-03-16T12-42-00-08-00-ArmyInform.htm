Over the past day, the enemy made 3 attempts of storms on the Left Bank of the Dnieper and 7 - in the area of the robot in Zaporizhzhya region.

About thisreportedDefense Forces of the South of Ukraine.

“In the operating area of the South Defense Forces, the daily counter -battering struggle continues. The enemy does not leave the intentions to knock out our units from the occupied positions, ”the message reads.

Having suffered significant irreversible and sanitary losses, he went to starting positions. Our fighters take comprehensive measures to keep and strengthen positions.

It is also reported that the invaders do not stop air intelligence even under difficult weather conditions, press artillery shelling, use a large number of shock drones of different types.

In addition, the enemy continues to use tactical aviation in Robotyn and launched two guided aerial bombs.

“There are no ships of hostile non -fleet in the Black and Azov Seas. Rocket launchers for more than three weeks do not leave the base points, ” - said the defense forces of southern Ukraine
