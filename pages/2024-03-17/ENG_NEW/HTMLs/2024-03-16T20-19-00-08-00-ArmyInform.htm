Russian civilians are discussing evacuation in the border areas of the Belgorod region of the Russian Federation and the continuation of mobilization measures.

The audio recording of the talk was published by the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

“We will watch, I don't know. If it is already impossible at all, we will be in Belgorod. They say they leave massively, everyone who can. Well, who is richer, who has where to go, ” - say the Meshkanka Bєlgorodsko.

She also tells that the offensive of Russian volunteers has been ongoing, who intended to liberate Russia from the Putin regime.

"So they are everywhere, there is fear of what is happening in our country. Grayvoron evacuates in Rakitna. So they give and give, Sasha. They give and give, they don't stop. Idea? They are going to give them Lithuanians as well," she said.

She also says that the Russian military is charging equipment and says that there will be additional mobilization among the population.

"They caught up with a lot of our equipment. There will be 100% mobilization. People are talking from the military registration and enlistment office. Maybe they'll recruit a little people to help you finish Sasha. And what to do? Glnya, then the 200s, then the 300s. And who should fight? Maybe they will take someone who was in Chechnya, someone else," she said.
