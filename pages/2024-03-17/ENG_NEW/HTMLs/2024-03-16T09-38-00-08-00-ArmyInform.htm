As of 9am, 40 people affected by yesterday's rocket attack of Russia in the city of Odesa continue to be treated in the medical institutions.

About thisreportedThe head of the Odessa Ova Oleg Kiper in his telegram-channel, reports the Armyinform.

In addition, 9 injured in serious condition.

“Today, 20 dead were announced in the city and region, as a result of the most victims of the Russian terrorist attack in Odessa. Once again I express my sincere condolences to the families of the dead, ”the message reads.

Earlier the Armyinformreportedthat 20 people have already died in Odessa.
