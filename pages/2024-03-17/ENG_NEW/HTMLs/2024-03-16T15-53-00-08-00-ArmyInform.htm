The fighters of the Burevi National Guard team repelled the enemy's sudden assault.

Despite the surprise, the National Guard destroyed a Russian tank and mowed down the infantrymen who had already entered our positions.

Lviv paratroopers from 80 brigades stopped the large -scale offensive of the Russians in the Bakhmut direction.

The enemy, in several columns of more than a dozen pieces of equipment, tried to storm the positions of our fighters from different directions. As a result, some of the occupiers' equipment was destroyed, several Russian vehicles got stuck in the field, and others fled the battlefield.

SBU drones again attacked oil refineries in the Samara region. According to Army TV sources, three refineries were hit that night. In the city of Syzran, a fire broke out on an area of 500 square meters.

Two "swimmers" planned to illegally enter Hungary across the river with children's life jackets, and another swam to Romania — all three were detained by our border guards.

These and other topics are discussed by our presenters - Senior Lieutenant Artem Golub and Lieutenant Colonel Sergei Misyura.
