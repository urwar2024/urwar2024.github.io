In the Nikolaev area, the DOK-Ing MV-10 demining system has already been operated by the European Union.

About thisreportedState Service of Special Transport of Ukraine.

Currently, the mine for the mine already operates remotely and disposes anti -personnel and anti -tank mines, as well as other ammunition in Mykolaiv region.

It is the most powerful system in the world in its category. Designed for work in large areas. Due to remote management, it is especially safe in operation.

The military of the State Special Transport Service called her "Hope".

The Head of the Administration of the State Special Transport Service, Major General Bohdan Bondar, was convinced of the effectiveness of the work of "Nadiya", which will improve the capabilities of Ukrainian troops and allow sappers to demine the de-occupied territories faster and safer.

As reported by the Armyinform, in early February European UnionHe transferred to UkraineModern DOK-Ing-10 demining system. It was received by the State Service of Special Transport of Ukraine.

It is a supply within the European Union support package for humanitarian demining in Ukraine in the amount of 25 million euros announced at the EU-EU summit in February 2023.
