In the city of Selidovo, Donetsk region, the Russian Fab-1500 was seized and destroyed by the Russian FAB-1500 with the UPMC control module.

About thisreportedSES of Ukraine.

The bomb fell in the open area in the private housing sector and did not detonate it.

It should be noted that in the explosion of this type of bombs, fragments can affect up to 500 m.

In order to protect the local population during the work, employees of the State Emergency Service, together with employees of the National Police, notified people.

The dangerous munition was seized by pyrotechnics of the State Emergency Service and destroyed in a special place.
