President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky thanked the forces of defense for the strikes about industrial sites in Russia.

He is about itstatedIn the evening address.

The President said that these weeks, many have already seen that the Russian system of war has vulnerability and that we can achieve these vulnerabilities with its weapons.

"And I thank the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the Security Service of Ukraine and the General Directorate of Intelligence for a new Ukrainian range," the Head of State said.

He also thanked the defense-industrial complex-everyone who works for the sake of Ukrainian force.

“Because it is really Ukrainian farming - what our own drones can. Ukraine will always have its own shock force in the sky now, ”the President emphasized.

It should be noted that on the night of March 16 at three oil refineries in the Samara region of the Russian Federation there were explosions after hitting drones.

Attacks on Russian refineries in two days touched more than 10% of the production capacity of the aggressor country.
