President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said that Ukraine will ensure that the Russians are held accountable for war crimes, including the bombing of Mariupol.

This is stated inmessageHeads of State in Telegrams.

“Mariupol. Drama Theater. The inscription "Children". Two years have passed ... The world must remember everything that Russia has done against Ukraine and Ukrainians, and every Russian killer must be responsible for the committed. We will provide such responsibility, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

"The bright memory of all whose lives took away Russian terror," the President added.

On March 16, 2022, at about 10 a.m., Russian aircraftstruck a stroke on a drama theaterin the center of Mariupol. More than a thousand civilians were hiding here. Many with children.

A few days before the Russian crime, Mariupolka wrote in capital letters in front of the theater - "Dots". They hoped that it would stop the invaders.

But the Russians did not stop it. They dropped two airbags to the theater. The death toll can reach 600 people. Many of them are women and children.

On this day, the occupying troops performed 22 aircraft on Mariupol, dropping about 100 bombs to the city. One of them hit the building of the Neptune basin, where residents of Kalmius district gathered.
