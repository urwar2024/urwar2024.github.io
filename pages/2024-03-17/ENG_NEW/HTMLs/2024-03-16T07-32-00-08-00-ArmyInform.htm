In Ukraine, the number of people entitled to pay for housing and communal services is increasing.

Who is eligible and can those who live in the rented room can be received? Where and what documents to file and how is the accrual?Armyinformanswers these and other questions.

According to the Law of Ukraine“On the status of war veterans, guarantees of their social status”Participants of hostilities, persons with disabilities as a result of war, members of families of dead defenders and defenders are entitled to benefits for housing and communal services:

  • 75% of rent and utilities for UBD;
  • 100% discount on rent and utilities for persons with disabilities as a result of war;
  • 50% of the rent and utilities for families of fallen defenders and defenders.

However, it should be understood that these benefits are not automatically provided with the status. They need to be decorated. Currently, the registration is through the Pension Fund of Ukraine.

Let's start with what documents you need to prepare - make scancopy:

  • passport;
  • documents confirming the right to the privilege (eg, UBD certificate);
  • The taxpayer's registration card number.

When you prepared the necessary documents, then you need to submit two applications to the Pension Fund:

1. On entering information in the Unified State Automated Registerthere). Without this accrual of privileges are impossible;

2. On providing privileges for payment of housing and communal services (purchase of solid fuel, liquefied gas). (A statement form can be foundthere).

You can apply remotely onWeb portalselectronic services of the Pension Fund of Ukraine. It's not difficult. Need access to the Internet and a little free time. The portal must be authorized by electronic signature, in the section "All services" select the option "Application for a housing subsidy or privilege". And then fill in the proposed forms.

You can also submit documents personally in the PFU or send them by mail. Family members can also privilege. In particular, husband or wife. The power of attorney does not have to be notarized. The commander of the military unit can certify it.

Is it possible to apply if you rent a home? - So

But renting an apartment or house should be official, that is, a housing lease agreement is required. When submitting applications, you must specify the place of residence and add the lease scancopy.

How is the benefit provided?

Currently, the benefits for housing and communal services are provided in cash. By default, the Pension Fund can open an account with Savings Bank and notify the beneficiary. And the privilege must sign a bank account agreement. If you want money to be transferred to another bank, then you should apply to the territorial body of the Pension Fund. For registration you need to provide your account where the money will be charged. Usually, it takes some time to design and accrual. But then the money comes.

In general, the privilege provides for such a process-you pay full amounts for housing and communal services, which are indicated in payments. But the card is received in the form of compensation. It should be remembered that if you do not pay for the utility, then the benefits can be stopped.
