Pyrotechnics of the State Emergency Service of Ukraine destroyed the combat part of the X-101 missile in Vinnytsia region.

About thisReportedMinistry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.

The explosive object was found the day before in the forest area of Zhmeryn district.

In addition, Vinnytsia found a fragments of a hostile drone with an unidentified combat unit. Explosives urge people not to approach such objects.

The weight of the unidentified combat part is about 50 kg. The wreckage of drone flew within a radius of 200 m.

Even though the drone was shot down, the danger of it is no less. If you find a potentially dangerous item, you must call 101 and 102 immediately.

After documenting the event, some drone will be removed and destroyed.
