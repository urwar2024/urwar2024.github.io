In the capital there was a seventh artistic action of art therapy for the military within the only workshop in Ukraine "Draw with a warrior".

The purpose of the action is the psychological rehabilitation of our soldiers by means of fine arts. It is with the help of drawings that defenders can heal the souls crippled and find dreamy peace.

First of all, it gives the soldiers understanding their own versatility and allows you to discover new talents about which they have not even guessed.

It should be noted that the event took place in the exhibition hall, where the walls were presented with the works of talented defenders, which trace the change of the topics and colors used to create paintings. In the works of their predecessors involved in the master class, the military was able to note how the black and red horrors of the war turned into Ukrainian landscapes and romantic landscapes.

“The cultural heritage reflects the spirituality and wealth of the nation. Art during the war is a special kind of art. Not in vain, the enemy destroys our culture, destroys historical monuments, tries to assign and rewrite our rich history, export our children. For this is the only way to kill the soul of the nation, to erase its identity and culture, so by combining the efforts of artists and warriors, together we fight with the enemy on the cultural front, healing our own souls in a master class, ”-emphasized the action curator military psychologist Inna Chernenko.

As in the previous times, teachers and students of the Department of Graphics of the Publishing and Printing Institute of NTUU KPI them joined the action. Igor Sikorsky, who perform art accompaniment, communicating and drawing with warriors.

Also, a talented sculptor, author of the legendary trident on the shield of the Motherland Motherland, Alexei Parchgenschyn, joined the action.

In addition, the People's Orchestra "Svyatogray" teachers of the Children's School of Art. G. Zhukovsky inspired his speech by warriors and artists to create wonderful works.

The event is organized by the Institute of Contemporary Art of the National Academy of Arts of Ukraine with the participation of the National Union of Artists of Ukraine, KPI them. Igor Sikorsky and the National Military Medical Clinical Center "Chief Military Clinical Hospital".
