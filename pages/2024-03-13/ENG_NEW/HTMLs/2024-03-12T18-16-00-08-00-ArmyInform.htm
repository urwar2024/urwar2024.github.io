President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky celebrated 425 defenders of Ukraine state awards, 236 of them - posthumously.

Appropriate decrees№151/2024and№152/2024Posted on the website of the Head of State.

The President decided to award defenders for personal courage, revealed in the protection of state sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine, and for selfless performance of military duty.

The head of state awarded the Order of Bohdan Khmelnytsky II and III degrees, Danylo Halytsky, "For Courage" of II and III degrees, awarded medals "for military service of Ukraine", "Defender of the Father ”.

As reported by the Armyinform, March 8 President of Ukraine Volodymyr ZelenskyyHe notedState awards 256 of the defenders of Ukraine, 186 of them - posthumously.
