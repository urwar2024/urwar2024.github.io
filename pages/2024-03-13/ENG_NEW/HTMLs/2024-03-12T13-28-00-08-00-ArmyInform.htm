Procedural actions were carried out within the framework of criminal proceedings on the facts of obtaining undue benefit, obstruction of the legitimate activity of the Armed Forces of Ukraine and other military formations, as well as the illegal transfer of persons across the state border of Ukraine (Part 5 of Article 27 of Part 3 of Article 368, Part 1 114-1, Part 3 of Article 332 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).
