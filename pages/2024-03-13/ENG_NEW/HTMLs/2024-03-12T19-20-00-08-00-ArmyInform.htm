Polygon tests have already passed more than 50 robotic ground platforms.

About thisreported March 12Viceremier-Minister for Innovation, Development of Education, Science and Technology Mikhail Fedorov.

According to him, the tests passed the platforms-Kamikadze, Tureli, platforms, which destroy the positions and equipment of the Russians, change, exchange, evacuate the wounded and bring ammunition to positions.

The works have been successfully pronounced at the landfill, in a few months they will be on the battlefield - hundreds of different platforms will be purchased through United24.

The main purpose of land robots is to minimize human participation in the battlefield. This will help to maintain the lives and health of Ukrainian soldiers.

“Ground robotic complexes will become the next gamechair (those who are changing the rules of the game, the ed.) Of this war, which already has drones. This is an asymmetrical answer to the quantitative advantage of the enemy, ”Fedorov wrote.

According to him, the cluster Brave1 is doing everything to scale the production of robots, and the state begins to buy them massively. The Brave1 platform registered more than 140 robotic systems, 96 of them have undergone a defense examination, 14 developments were codified according to NATO standards. Some of these robots are already working on the front, such as Turel Saber.

BeforeThe Armyinform reportedthat in the seven regions of Ukraine will teach drones operators.
