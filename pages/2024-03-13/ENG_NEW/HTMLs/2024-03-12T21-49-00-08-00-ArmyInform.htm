Ukraine and the European Union have today signed a credit agreement and a memorandum on transitional financing for Ukraine under the Ukraine Facility.

About thisreportedMinistry of Finance of Ukraine.

The total amount of preferential funds provided - 6 billion euros.

It is expected that the first payment of € 4.5 billion will go to the State Budget of Ukraine in March.

The rest of this amount - EUR 1.5 billion - Ukraine will be able to receive in April 2024 after the European Commission evaluation of the executed indicators.

Within the framework of the transitional financing mechanism, Ukraine sells 5 indicators in the fields of public finance management, corruption, business environment development, etc.

Following the approval of Ukraine's plan for Ukraine Facility, 1.9 billion euros of unconditional financing will be directed to the state budget. Ukraine will receive the rest of the budget support on a quarterly basis after the performance of the indicators provided in the plan of Ukraine.

Recall that 50 billion euros within Ukraine Facility will be focused on budget support (EUR 38.27 billion), investment fund (6.97 billion euros), technical and administrative support (4.76 billion euros).
