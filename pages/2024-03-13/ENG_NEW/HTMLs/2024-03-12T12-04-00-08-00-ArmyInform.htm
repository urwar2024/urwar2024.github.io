The court satisfied the statement of claim of the Kharkiv Regional Prosecutor's Office in the interests of the state to the agricultural firm on elimination of obstacles to the state in the use and disposal of defense lands.

About thisinformsOffice of the Prosecutor General.

“This land plot in the Bohodukhiv district in Kharkiv region illegally dismissed from state property to communal and was leased to agricultural resources for agricultural purposes. Its area is 24.3 hectares, the cost is over UAH 150 million, ”the message reads.

In this case, the land plot is in fact the land of the border strip and should be used solely for the erection and operation of engineering protective structures for protection against aggression of the Russian Federation.

“Such lands can be state -owned and should be used to defense the country and ensure the activities of border authorities.

The use of these lands for agricultural needs, departure from state ownership and renting a private entity makes it impossible to use it to protect our country, ”the department said.

It is noted that the work in this direction continues in close cooperation with the 4th border detachment of the State Border Service of Ukraine.
