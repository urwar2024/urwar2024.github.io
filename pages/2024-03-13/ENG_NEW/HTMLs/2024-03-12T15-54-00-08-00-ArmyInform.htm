The company hopes that the center will become one of the ways of strengthening the defense-industrial complex and will help to solve the issue relevant to the veteran community.

About thisreported March 12JSC "Ukroboronprom".

The task of the center is not only the employment of veterans and veterans and involvement in military enterprises with combat experience, but a systematic work aimed at comprehensive support and adaptation of such people to civilian life.

“Veterans and veterans will be useful during the development, production or repair of products. I am sure that people who have passed the war will strengthen the Ukrainian defense team, ”said Herman Smetanin, CEO of Ukroboronprom JSC.

It is also about the preparation of enterprises for return to work veterans, the organization of training for managers and workers, retraining and advanced training for veterans (if necessary), involvement in research and testing of products, taking into account combat experience, etc.

Psychological and physical recovery programs and patronage programs are provided.

It will interact with mobilized workers and their families, provide them with information and consulting, legal, psychological and other support through their own resources and partnership with state institutions and public organizations.

Particular attention will be paid to the inclusivity of jobs, improving mutual understanding and creating a proper corporate culture. To do this, the center will work with the teams where the former military arrives.

Today, more than 500 veterans of the Russian-Ukrainian war are working in the campaign, and more than 1600 weapons protect Ukraine as a part of the defense forces.

As beforereported the armyinform, Ukroboronprom plans to produce small arms according to NATO standards.
