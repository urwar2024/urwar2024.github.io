Last night, March 11, the aggressor fired at Nikopol region three times. From heavy artillery he aimed at the district center and the Chervonohrigor community.

About thisreportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Ova Sergey Lisak.

Due to enemy shelling, 49-year-old men and women 42 and 48 were injured. Outpatient will be treated.

“The hostile shells destroyed the private house, 17 more - they were mutilated. 11 outbuildings, 5 cars, shop. And also - gas pipelines and transmission lines. More than 1700 families remained without light, ”the Ova chairman said.

According to Sergiy Lisak, the explosions subsided at night.

"Everywhere in the region - without attacks," he added.
