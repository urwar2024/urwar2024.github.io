The Security Service has blocked new schemes for evasion and illegal departure abroad of conscript men. In different regions of Ukraine, the organizers of the equipment were detained, which sold the conscript for counterfeit documents for escape to the European Union.

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

It is noted that the cost of forgery was up to $ 16,000.

Thus, in the Nikolaev area a criminal group was neutralized, which traded fictitious certificates of unsuitability for health service.

“Among the persons involved is the head of the local charitable foundation who searched for the evasants, and her accomplice - the hospital official. False fake was transferred to the deputy chief physician of the regional center of medical and social examination, who "confirmed the diagnosis" of fugitives and appointed them a group of disability, ”the message reads.

Therefore, more than 15 evil people planned to use it to leave for the EU, but the security staff prevented escape.

In the Poltava region, the SBU employees exposed a local lawyer, who for money offered two options for "resolving the issue" for money. She gave them fabricated conclusions of the VLK, or brought to the Way information system to travel abroad as drivers of international flights.

In Khmelnytskyi, another scheme of illegal migration abroad has been eliminated. The military hospital and a representative of the Military Medical Commission were involved in its organization, who sold fictitious information on "poor health".

In the Carpathian region, a diller was detained, which "bogged" the evasants from mobilization. To do this, he sold them fake conclusions about disability, which he received through his own links at one of the Kiev medical and social examination centers.

Another escape channel abroad was eliminated in Lviv, which operated under the guise of freight. During the complex measures, a diller was detained, which issued its customers with the drivers of the charity fund.

As reported by the Armyinform, in UkraineEliminatedAnother 5 "evaders" schemes, one of them provided for the sale of medical records about "bad" health.
