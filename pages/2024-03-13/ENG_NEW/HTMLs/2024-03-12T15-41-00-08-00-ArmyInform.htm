The Commander of the Air Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk showed combat work of air defense at night on March 12.

Mykola Oleshchuk about itreportedIn the telegram, publishing the appropriate video.

«Fighting work on enemy UAVs in the south on the night of March 12, 2024! I thank the soldiers of the South Air Command for the result, ”he wrote.

As reportedArmyinform, in the south of air defense at night, she destroyed the enemy rocket X-59 and 8 Shahas.
