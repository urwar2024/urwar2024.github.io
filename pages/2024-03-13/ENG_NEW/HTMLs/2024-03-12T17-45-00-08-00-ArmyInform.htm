Denmark has announced a new large package of military assistance that will provide Ukraine with artillery self -propelled installations, mortars and ammunition.

This is stated in a message onSiteDenmark's Ministry of Defense.

The Defense Office on Thursday informed about a new package of assistance committee in the Denmark Parliament.

This package of assistance is 16th in account and is about 2.3 billion Danish crowns ($ 337 million).

The help package provides artillery self-propelled Caesar installations, 120mm mortars and appropriate ammunition.

“Artillery and mortar systems are very needed by Ukraine. With this package, we make a significant contribution to the Ukrainian struggle for freedom in the form of additional Caesar artillery systems and related ammunition. This assistance is provided in cooperation with our allies and is an important signal that we support Ukraine, ”said Defense Minister Troels Lund Polesen.

It is noted that the allocated money will co -finance for the purchase of Caesar self -proceeds in cooperation with France and 155 mm artillery shells in cooperation with Estonia and the Czech Republic.

“The great political and economic will is needed to fulfill its ambitions to support Ukraine. This is especially true of artillery ammunition. We show this package of assistance to Ukraine and our European allies that Denmark has the will and the ability to continue to support Ukraine as much as it will be needed, ”said Denmark Minister Lars Lokke Rasmussen.
