The Security Service of Ukraine prevented Russian diversion against the command of defense forces on the Southern Front and detained a traitor who tried to poison the Armed Forces command.

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

"As a result of the special operation, the FSB agent was detained, who tried to deadly poison the commanders of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces in the Zaporozhye region," - the message reads.

The attacker was an active serviceman of the Ukrainian army. To commit the crime, he planned to add a poisonous substance to the water of the bath-pre-complex, which was used by the Armed Forces command warehouse.

The SBU staff exposed the traitor to the Russian special service in advance and documented his subversive activity against Ukraine.

This made it possible to carry out a set of measures to protect Ukrainian defenders, as well as to detain the FSB agent at the stage of its preparation for sabotage.

In addition, during the investigation, the Security Service found that the attacker had performed another task - he explored for the FSB the location of the dislocation and the movement of units of the Armed Forces on the forefront.

The person involved was recruited through his parents living in the temporarily occupied part of the Luhansk region.

As the investigation established, the invaders abducted the husband's relatives directly from their apartment and then imprisoned to a local Russian torture. There, the victims were subjected to cruel torture, demanding that they persuade their son to work in favor of the aggressor country.

Further communication between the traitor and his Russian curator took place through an anonymous chat in the messenger.

On the basis of the evidence collected, investigators of the Security Service informed him of the suspicion of treason committed in martial law. The attacker is currently in custody. He is threatened with life imprisonment.
