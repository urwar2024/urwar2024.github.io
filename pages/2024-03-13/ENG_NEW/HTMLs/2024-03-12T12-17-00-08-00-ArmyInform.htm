Today, on March 12, at 2:00 pm, an online meeting with the Deputy Director of the Department of Social Protection-Head of the Department of Ministry of Veterans Ruslan Kuchuk will be held.

About thisinformsMinistry of Veterans.

The topic of the meeting- providing the statuses stipulated by the Law of Ukraine "On the status of war veterans, guarantees of their social protection".

"You can ask your expert questions in the comments on the Ministry of Veterans and during the broadcast," the department said.

Direct inclusion will be held on the official Ministry of Veterans on Facebook and YouTube channel.
