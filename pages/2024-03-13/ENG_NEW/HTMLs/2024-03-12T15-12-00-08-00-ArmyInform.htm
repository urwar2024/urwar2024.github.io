The forces of Ukraine stopped the offensive of the Russian troops in the eastern direction of the front.

About thisstatedPresident of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky in an interview with French media.

“The situation on the front is much better than the last three months. We had some difficulties because of the lack of shells and density of drones of the Russian Federation. We worked well on Russian aviation, leveled the situation. The promotion in the east of the Russian troops is now stopped, ”said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

The President stressed that the occupation troops destroyed Avdiivka, but lost many personnel, equipment, and aviation.

“Russia is stopped now. To date, the command and our military promotion has been stopped in the East, ”said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

At the same time, he stressed that a month later the situation may change if Russian troops receive assistance in shells and Ukraine will not receive the same support from Western partners.

"In the Black Sea, we continue to act powerfully, these actions continue," the head of state added, noting that he will not disclose the details.

As reported by the Armyinform, during the last dayhappened72 combat clashes. Most of all, the enemy attacked in Novopavlov and Avdiivskyi.
