As a result of the Russian attack in Kryvyi Rih, two people were killed on Tuesday night.

About thisreportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Regional State Administration Sergey Lisak.

“Another tragic evening for the area. There are two dead in Kryvyi Rih, ”he said.

Sergei Lisak also added that there were victims, which doctors provide the necessary medical care.

“More than 30 victims have already accepted Kryvyi Rih hospitals. Five in the operating room. "Heavy." Among them is a child, ”he wrote.

Earlier it was reported that as a result of an enemy attack in Kryvyi RihThere was a fireIn a multi -storey building.

The air forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine were warned about the launch of the Russians of the managed aviation missile, probably X-59, in the direction of Kryvyi Rih.
