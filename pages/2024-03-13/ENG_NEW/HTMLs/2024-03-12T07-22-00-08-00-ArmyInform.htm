The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov and the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, Colonel-General Alexander Sirsky conducted a conversation with the head of the Pentagon Lloyd Austin. The parties discussed the preparation for the next meeting of the Contact Group on Defense of Ukraine (Ramstein).

About thisinformsHead of the Defense Office of Ukraine.

“Together with the Armed Forces commander -in -chief, they held a telephone conversation with Pentagon Lloyd Austin, during which they discussed the preparation for the next meeting of the Contact Group on Defense of Ukraine. He told up -to -date information about the operational situation on the battlefield, ”the head of MOU wrote.

The parties also discussed the medium -term and short -term needs and specific requests of Ukraine. Among the key priorities are ammunition and air defense.

“We look forward to more detailed interaction with each of the coalitions of opportunities during our meeting in Ramstein format. I am grateful for the clear and consistent support of Ukraine, ”the Minister of Defense emphasized.

Rustem Umerov also expressed gratitude to the United States for clear and consistent support of Ukraine.

As reportedArmyinform, The Ministers of Defense of Ukraine and Greece discussed the need for the Armed Forces for artillery and ammunition.
