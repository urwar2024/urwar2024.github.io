On the night of March 12, Russian occupation troops dumped a managed airbot into the residential area of Kupyansk Kharkiv region.

About thisinformsMinistry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.

“The impact was destroyed in a 5-storey apartment building. There were several ignition cells, ”the message reads.

According to the head of the region, three apartments were burning from the first to the third floor in one entrance and another apartment in the other entrance of the building. The fire was extinguished.

Rescuers surveyed the damaged building, no casualties and victims.
