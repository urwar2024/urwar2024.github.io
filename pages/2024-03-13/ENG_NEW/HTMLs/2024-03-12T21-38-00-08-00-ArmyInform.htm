President Volodymyr Zelenskyy heard the report of the commander-in-chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, Colonel-General Alexander Sirsky.

About it the head of the statedeclared on the evening of March 12.

“The head Syrsky reported on our actions on the battlefield, the actions of our scouts and the achievements of Ukrainian drones. The head is now on the front. And the dynamics of action is needed for Ukraine, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

The President also received preliminary results on the use of our drones from the Armed Forces, GUR and SBU.

“I think everyone see that our drones are working. And work long -range. Our range is a real approximation of security for all, ”said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

He also commented on the rocket strike of the Russians on Kryvyi Rih.

“We will hurt the Russian state in response - quite fair. They should learn to the Kremlin to the fact that terror is not unpunished for them. These patients are no longer cured of evil, but they will feel the losses. The Russian state will lose, and only it can make it safe for neighbors, ”the President said.

Asreported the armyinform, the death toll in Kryvyi Rih increased to three.
