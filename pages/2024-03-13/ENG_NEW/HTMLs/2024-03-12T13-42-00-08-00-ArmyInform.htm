The Defense Agency of the Ministry of Defense has announced the selection of suppliers for the purchase of 154 Pick-UP cars in the Prozorro system.

This is stated in a message onSiteDefense Office.

The total expected cost is UAH 206.5 million (VAT). Supply: up to 90 calendar days from the date of signing the additional budgetary agreement.

“This is only the beginning of large -scale work on the supply of Ukraine's armed forces. The purchase of these cars is important for meeting the needs of the military in modern equipment and their combat readiness, ” - said Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Dmitry Klimenkov.

The purchase will be made by applying a framework agreement. This will allow you to buy equipment in a competitive way without opening sensitive information for general access.

“Now the Defense Agency is working on the preparation of direct transactions with pickup producers. At the same time, we work with the Ministry of Defense and MPs on changes in legislation that will allow us to conclude such contracts, " - said Maryna Bezrukov, director of the Defense Procurement Agency.

Pick-up vehicles must have a 4x4 wheel drive and a load capacity of at least 0.8 tons (with the possibility of towing the trailer with a full weight of at least 0.85 tons). Diesel engine with a specific capacity of at least 136 horsepower and compliance with ecological standards not lower than the level of "Euro-2".

The body (cargo platform) of the pickup should be metal with the ability to accommodate the personnel in the amount of at least four people, as well as allow the installation of additional equipment for weapons.
