Russian troops at night and in the morning fired at Sumy region border, 96 explosions were recorded.

About thisinformsPress service.

“At night and in the morning, the Russians carried out 9 shelling of border areas and settlements of Sumy region. 96 explosions have been recorded, ”the message reads.

The shelling was suffered by Khotyn, Belopilsk, Krasnopil, Velikopysarivska, Znob-Novgorod, Seredi-Buda communities. From the artillery, the enemy fired at Velikopisarevsk (19 explosions), Krasnopil (3 explosions) and Belopolsk (3 explosions) of the community.

The enemy dropped 2 mines into the territory of the Znob-Novgorod community. At the same time, the Khotyn community fired at mortars (10 explosions).

Also, from the reactive systems of the volley fire, the occupiers fired at the Word-Budsk and Velikopisariv communities (4 explosions).

As reportedArmyinform, Russian troops attacked the Dnipropetrovsk region, were affected by civilians and there are large -scale destruction.
