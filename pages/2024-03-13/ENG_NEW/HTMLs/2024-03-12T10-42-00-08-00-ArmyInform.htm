Information about the possible offensive of the Russian army in Sumy region, which is spreading social networks, is not true. It is a fake aimed at destabilizing and increasing panic among the residents of the region.

About thisThey saidin the Sumy Regional Military Administration.

Volodymyr Artyukh, the head of the Sumy Regional Military Administration, noted that as of today, the enemy groups near the borders of Sumy region have not been recorded.

“Sumy Regional Military Administration and District Military Administrations, Community and Police Management is on the ground. The Armed Forces of Ukraine are ready for any challenges and can give a decent repel of the enemy if necessary, ”the message reads.

The Ova added that the area contains danger from the air and shelling of border communities.

As reported by the Armyinform, Russian military per night and morningThey were struckAlmost a hundred blows in Sumy region.
