The White House has published a US budget proposal for the 2025 Financial Year, which has $ 1.5 billion to counter Russian aggression. Of these funds, $ 482 million was determined for direct assistance to Ukraine.

About thisIt is saidOn the official website of the US State Department.

Funds for Ukraine, together with an additional request for national security, will help restore the economy of our country, strengthen Ukraine's stability and its ability to defend themselves against a large -scale invasion of Russia and will ensure progress in carrying out reforms necessary for Ukraine's accession to the EU.

Today, more than 50 countries have responded to the call of America to allocate funds to support Ukraine.

“United constant support will provide Ukraine with what it needs to win. The request provides for this powerful support, ”the US State Department noted.

A total of $ 482 million will be divided into:

  • $ 250.0 million will go to help in the economy and support the public,
  • $ 95 million is intended to assist the security sector,
  • $ 71 million is defined for health care programs,
  • $ 66 million - to help in the field of safety and demining.

“With the support of its partners, Ukraine can protect its sovereignty and democracy. We know the history that dictators will not stop, they continue to operate and the price for America will increase, ”the message reads.
