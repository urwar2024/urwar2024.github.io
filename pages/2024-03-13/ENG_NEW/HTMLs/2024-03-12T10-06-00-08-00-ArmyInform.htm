Last week, 173 loans for a total amount of UAH 282 million were issued for the purchase of housing under the Esosel program, 90 of them and security officers.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

From the beginning of the program, loans received 7946 families worth over UAH 12.1 billion.

Yes, Ukrainians received: last week under the Esosel program:

  • 90 servicemen and security forces;
  • 9 doctors;
  • 24 teachers;
  • 4 scientists;
  • 39 families without their own home;
  • 2 VPO;
  • 5 veterans.

As reported by the Armyinform, GovernmentMade changesto the Eoseli -Power Mortgage Program. One of the changes applies to veterans, people who have disabilities as a result of war, their families, and families of the dead.
