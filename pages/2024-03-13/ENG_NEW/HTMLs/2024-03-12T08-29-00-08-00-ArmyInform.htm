Last day, on March 11, the enemy fired at Kherson region again, as a result of the attack, one person was killed, and 3 more was injured.

About thisinformsHead of Kherson Ova Alexander Proudin.

“Last day, the enemy was fired by Antonovka, Republican, Kachkara, Giant, Red Lighthouse, Stanislav, Groupytsya, Obradokamyanka, Zmiivka, Dniprovske, Lviv, Ivanivka, Poniatovka, Berislav, Tyaginka, Zimivnik and Kerson.

According to the head of the Ova, the Russian military kissed in the residential quarters of settlements of the region, in particular 13 private homes were damaged.

In addition, a private home and a car were damaged in Kherson.

"As a result of hostile attacks, hits and outbuildings were recorded in the settlements of the region," Alexander Proudin added.

We will remind, the army of the Russian Federation fired at14 settlements of Zaporizhzhya region: A man was killed.
