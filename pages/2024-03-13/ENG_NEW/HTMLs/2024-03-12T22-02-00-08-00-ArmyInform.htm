In the Kharkiv region, the construction of fortifications continues, hundreds of people are involved in the works.

About thisreportedThe head of Kharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov.

On the territory of Kharkiv region there are "teeth of the dragon", dig anti -tank ditches, build strongholds and dugouts.

To date, almost hundreds of objects have been started, and the territories have been pre -agreed.

“More than 500 employees and more than 200 pieces of equipment - we make every effort to execute President Vladimir Zelensky's assignment according to the deadlines. All schedules are agreed, the supply of reinforced concrete structures is free of charge, ”Oleg Synigubov said.

He added that builders work 24/7 in extremely difficult conditions under hostile blows.

“It is, without exaggeration," special operation "under constant shelling of the occupier. We continue to strengthen the country's defense capability daily, ”the head of the Kharkiv Ova emphasized.

As reported by the ArmyInform, in the Kherson region there is an arrangementthe second line of defense, including anti -tank ditches, anti -tank barriers and strongholds.
