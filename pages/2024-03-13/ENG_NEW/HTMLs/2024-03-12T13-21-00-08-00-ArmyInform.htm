Russian troops on Tuesday morning were struck by the Air Force on the Greater Posisariv community of Sumy region.

About thisreportedSumy Ova.

On March 12, at 9:20, the hostile aircraft was dropped by managed aviation bombs to the residential sector of the settlement. Two explosions were recorded.

Previously, a large family was injured as a result of the shelling: two children aged 6 and 10 were injured. One adult was also injured.

Five private residential buildings, garage were damaged.

All the necessary services work on the spot, assistance to the victims.

As reported by the Armyinform, Russian invaderscontinue to fireBorder territories of Chernihiv and Sumy regions. In total, 75 shelling (490 explosions) from different types of weapons were charged during the day.
