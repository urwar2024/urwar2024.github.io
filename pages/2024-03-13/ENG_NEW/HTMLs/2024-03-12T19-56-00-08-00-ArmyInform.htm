The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov thanked Denmark for a new package of military assistance.

This is stated in a message onSiteDefense Office.

"I am grateful to Denmark to the Denmark of Trolsa Lund Poolsen and the Denmark people for a significant package of military assistance and strengthening Ukraine's defensive capabilities," Rustem Umerov said.

DenmarkannouncedAbout the 16th military aid package of Ukraine totaling 2.3 billion Danish crowns ($ 337 million).

The new package provides for the provision of CAESAR artillery systems, self-propelled 120mm mortars and 155mm ammunition.

Denmark is funded with France by the transfer of Caesar installations and together with Estonia and the Czech Republic-155 mm artillery shells.
