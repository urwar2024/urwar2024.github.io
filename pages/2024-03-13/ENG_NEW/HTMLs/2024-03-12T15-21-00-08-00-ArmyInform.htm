More than 15 group participants have been exposed, four key defendants have already been detained.

About thisShe reported March 12SBU.

According to the department, the organization acted "under the cover" of the UOC (MP) and tried to destabilize the situation in Ukraine.

The attackers massively produced and "accelerated" the pro-Kremliv narratives and provocative messages that were to destabilize the socio-political situation and ignite religious hatred in Ukraine.

Among them is one of the heads of the UOC's capital (MP), who was part of the agent apparatus of the Russian special service.

According to the SBU, it is one of the largest FSB networks that has operated in Ukraine since the beginning of a large -scale invasion. It had a clear hierarchy and the distribution of functions, as well as the constant connection with the largest media of the Russian Federation, which immediately picked up the "necessary" content.

According to the investigation, the SBU Cleric was detained in Ukraine in Ukraine. He was responsible for the spread of materials that were incited to inter -denominational enmity and justified the aggressive war against Ukraine.

To do this, the person personally instructed the "performers" - other participants in the criminal organization, who acted under the guise of "polytexes" and prepared provocative posts and video.

The prepared content was distributed through dozens of control channels on Telegram and YouTube, as well as on Facebook pages and official sites of different dioceses of the UOC (MP).

Immediately after the publication of "custom" materials, they were immediately "picked up" by the central TV channels and the Internet edition of the Russian Federation to distribute fakes about Ukraine.

In carrying out information, the enemy hoped not only to loosen the socio-political situation in Ukraine, but also to discredit our country in the international arena, especially in front of Western partners.

During the searches at the places of stay of the persons involved in Kiev, mobile phones and computer equipment were used in criminal acts in favor of the aggressor country.

On the basis of the evidence collected by four detainees and ten more participants of the criminal organization, it was reported suspicion of the following articles of the Criminal Code of Ukraine:

  • Part 2 of Art. 111 (treason committed in martial law);
  • Part 6 of Art. 111-1 (collaborative activity);
  • Part 4 of Art. 28, Part 1, Part 2 of Art. 255 (creation and participation in a criminal organization);
  • Part 3 of Art. 161 (ignition of religious enmity and hatred committed by an organized group);
  • h. 1, 2 436-2 (justification, recognition legitimate, denial of armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine).

The issue of choosing a preventive measure is currently being resolved. Investigation is ongoing to establish all the circumstances of the crime and prosecute the perpetrators.

The attackers are threatened with life imprisonment.
