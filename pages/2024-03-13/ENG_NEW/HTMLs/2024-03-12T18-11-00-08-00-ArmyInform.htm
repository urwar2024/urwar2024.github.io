The government has increased the amount of demining services from UAH 2 to UAH 3 billion.

About thisreported March 12Prime Minister Denis Shmigal.

According to him, 80% of the cost of mine work will be compensated. The purchase of services will be due to Prozorro, which minimizes corruption risks and will create competition in the humanitarian demining market.

"We expect that the first such purchases will take place in May-June this year," Denis Shmigal said.
