Ternopil region joined the project "Adminservice" Veteran ", which is implemented with the assistance of the Ministry of Veterans. From now on, at the Center for Administrative Services, war veterans, their families and families of fallen soldiers will be able to quickly and qualitatively receive administrative services in a single window format.

About thisinformsMinistry of Veterans of Ukraine.

According to Ruslan Prikhodko, the director of the Social Corps of the Ministry of Veterans of Ukraine, today provides 19 veterans in the center.

"Specially prepared CNAP administrators will help veterans of war and their relatives to register marriage, issue documents at birth, resolve land issues, register their place of residence, etc.", - the message reads.

The CNAP is equipped with the requirements of barrier. There is a terminal for fast payment, Wi-Fi, electronic queue and play space for children.
