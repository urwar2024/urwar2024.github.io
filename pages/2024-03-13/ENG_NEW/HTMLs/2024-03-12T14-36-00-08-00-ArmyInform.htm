Planned military exercises began today, March 12, in Kharkiv.

About it on the air of a national telephonereportedMayor Igor Terekhov.

“Today, planned military training has begun. In particular, this applies to checkpoints, ”the head of Kharkiv emphasized.

He urged local residents to keep calm.

“I ask the Kharkiv resident to be calm, nothing wrong has happened. We conduct such exercises at certain intervals. Everything is planned, ”Igor Terekhov summarized.

As reportedArmyinformIn Kupyansk, Kharkiv region, the invaders dropped the airbomble on a high -rise building.
