In the operating zone of the Tavriya operational and strategic group, the enemy struck 23 aircraft, 3 missile strikes during the last day, made 44 combat clashes, made 1094 shelling and 75 kamkadze shot.

About thisreportedCommander of the operational-strategic group of Tavriya troops Brigadny General Alexander Tarnavsky.

According to him, the situation remains difficult, but controlled.

As it is noted, the total losses of the enemy in the power were 420, in service and military equipment - 38 units, not including UAV.

“In particular, these are 6 combat armored vehicles, 6 artillery systems, 1 air defense agency (Buk-M2) and 24 cars. 328 UAVs of different types have been neutralized or destroyed, ”said Alexander Tarnavsky.

In addition, the commander noted that the forces of Ukraine's defense also destroyed 5 ammunition warehouses, 1 PMM and 2 hostile armor.

We will remind, on the left bank of the Dnieper of the Armed Forcesdestroyed the Russian Taran Radio Intelligence Complexand drones "Lancet".
