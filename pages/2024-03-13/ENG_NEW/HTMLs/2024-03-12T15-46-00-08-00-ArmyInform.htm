The legislation of Ukraine on mobilization does not meet the task of wartime challenges, in particular in the issue of demobilization of servicemen.

About thisstatedPresident of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky in an interview with French media.

The head of state explained that the legislation was developed in peacetime and now needs changes.

“There is a debate from parliamentarians, they argue when it is possible to release guys when you can't, which one can be done and so on. There is no demobilization during the war in ordinary legislation. No one was counting on it. Therefore, there are different questions: about rotation, vacations, ”said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

Asked if Ukraine plans to carry out large -scale mobilization, Volodymyr Zelenskyy said: "Mobilization in Ukraine has been from the very beginning of the war and it continues every few months."

“The mobilization has been from the beginning of the war and continues every few months. It is a prolongation of the appropriate decision in parliament. Is it perfect? Of course not. When you are in a state of war, you come to more practical details. And these changes are being considered in parliament, ”the President added.

He also stressed that there is a lot of misinformation around mobilization issues.
