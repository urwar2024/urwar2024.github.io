The United States Presidential Administration will provide Ukraine with a new $ 300 million military assistance package.

About thisannouncedUS Presidential Advisor Jake Sullivan at a White House briefing.

"On behalf of Baiden President, I announce an emergency security package with weapons and equipment worth $ 300 million to meet the most critical needs of Ukraine," said Jake Sullivan.

According to him, the funds for security assistance were found due to savings in the contracts already concluded by the Pentagon for the replacement of weapons transferred to Ukraine.

Jake Sullivan said that the help package provides artillery shells and ammunition for HIMARS systems.

This is the first US assistance package in 2024. It was announced against the backdrop of blocking additional funding in Congress.
