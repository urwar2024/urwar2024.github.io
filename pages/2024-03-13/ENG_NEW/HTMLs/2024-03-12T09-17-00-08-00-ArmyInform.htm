On February 12, Russian occupation troops released six C-300 missiles in Selidovo in Donetsk region. The attack was injured by two people.

“On March 12, 2024, at about 01:30, the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation hit 6 s-300 missiles in Selidovo. As a result of the hit of one of them, two sisters were injured in the territory of the residential quarter, ”the message reads.

It is noted that both women were taken to a hospital with mine-explosive injuries, gardens and slaughter.

At least 18 private and 14 multi -storey buildings, administrative buildings, 2 garages, 5 cars and power lines were damaged due to hostile shelling.
