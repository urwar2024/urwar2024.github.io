As before, the leaders in the number of hostile attacks are the Avdiivsky and Novopavlovsky (former Marinsky) directions.

About thisreported on the evening of March 12The General Staff of the Armed Forces.

On Volyn and Polissyadirections operational situation without significant changes. No signs of formation of offensive groups were detected.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in the border areas, conducts sabotage and intelligence activities, carries out shelling of settlements from the territory of the Russian Federation, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border of Ukraine.

In Kupyansk directionOur defenders repelled 4 enemy attacks in the areas of Sinkivka and Tabaivka of Kharkiv region, where the enemy tried in vain to displace the units from the positions.

In the estuary directionOur warriors repelled 6 enemy attacks in the areas of the settlements of Bigorivka, Luhansk region and Terny of Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionThe forces of defense repelled 6 enemy attacks in the areas of the Eastern ravine, Ivanivsky and Klischivka of Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to improve his tactical position.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 22 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Berdychi, Orlovka, Tonenko, Pervomaisk Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the districts of Krasnogorivka, St. George, Novomikhailivka, Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops 19 times.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy, with the support of aviation, was in vain in vain attacked the positions of our defenders in the areas of Staromayorsky Donetsk region, western Verbov and robot Zaporizhzhya region.

In the area of ​​responsibility of the operational-strategic group of the troops "Odesa",in the Kherson direction, the enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. Thus, during the day, the enemy performed 3 in vain assault actions on the positions of our defenders.

During the day, the Aviation of Defense Forces struck 9 areas of concentration of personnel, weapons and enemy military equipment.

The missile troops have led to the area of concentration of personnel, artillery and 2 enemy aircraft.

BeforeThe Armyinform reportedthat the front, fortifications and production of shells were discussed at the meeting of the Supreme Commander -in -Chief.
