The King of the Netherlands Willem-Alexander visited a landfill in the United Kingdom and talked with the Ukrainian military who were trained there in the framework of the Interflex Operation.

About it reports the Royal House of the Netherlands on Twitter.

Operation Interflex leads Ukrainian recruits into a military. The Netherlands makes a contribution with, among other things, shooting skills, medical care and war law. The king visits the operation in England and speaks instructors and Ukrainian soldiers.https://t.co/cAWDdkxqAKpic.twitter.com/E3nZOJihYj

- Royal House (@koninklijkhuis)March 20, 2024

"The Netherlands is contributing, for example, to training shooting, medical care and military law," the statement reads.

Last week, a new group of Ukrainians from two hundred military began there. Eight more of such groups will be trained this year.

The training ground is located in the north of England. The Netherlands has been involved in this preparation program since September 2022.
