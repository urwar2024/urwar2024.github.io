Russian volunteer formations are not intended to be limited to hostilities only in the territory of Belgorod and Kursk regions.

According to the Armyinform, this is stated in a joint statement of Russian volunteer formations, which was voiced at the first press conference dedicated to limited military operations in the territory of Belgorod and Kursk regions.

“Soon we will go to other cities. We urge Russians from all countries, people of any nationalities to join us and fight against Putin's dictatorship together. Only with weapons in our hands, being together, will we defeat and create a new Russian Federation - a strong, peaceful country that is respected, to be a citizen of which is not ashamed, ”he said.

According to the Siberian Battalion Fighter with the call sign "Cold", the Russian liberation movement once again showed the weakness of Putin's regime.

“Together with our associates, we have proven to the world that Putin is losing control of the situation more and more. The Russians also see it, and more and more compatriots enter our ranks, "Kholod said.

The Siberian Battalion fighter noted that the Kremlin failed to hold elections in the areas where real fights were being.

“In the Presidential Election Days, the Putin Army could not resist us. Citizens of Belgorod and Kursk regions did not go to polling stations under the bombs of Putin aviation and artillery fire. No one in common sense will be able to claim that Putin was chosen legally, ”he said.
