The head of the NATO Military Committee Admiral Rob Bauer noted the successes of Ukraine's defense forces in the war against the Russian aggressor.

According to the correspondent of the Armyinform, he stated this during a speech at the Kiev Security Forum.

“You fight for the civilization itself. The Ukrainian people and Ukrainian armed forces do it as we have never seen before. You have radically changed many aspects of modern war, ”said Rob Bauer.

Admiral noted that having faced with a combination of trenches and permanent artillery shelling of the First World War with drone and artificial intelligence of the 21st century, the Ukrainian defense forces quickly adapted and repelled.

"You are the pioneers in innovations, using a combination of Soviet equipment with modern Western weapons," he said.

Rob Bauer stressed that despite all the losses, Ukraine has achieved considerable success in the battlefield.

“Ukraine has suffered incredible suffering. She experienced unbearable. Lost people and things that can never be replaced. And yet ... to call you the victim - it will not be fair for your immense power. This would not be fair about the success you have achieved on the battlefield, ”the NATO Military Committee Head emphasized.
