Ukraine has come close to full membership in the North Atlantic Alliance as never before.

According to the Armyinform, NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg said in a video message during the annual Kiev Security Forum.

According to Jens Stoltenberg, Putin wanted to ban Ukraine to choose his own way by removing from the Euro -Atlantic family.

“However, it has achieved the opposite results. Today, Ukraine is a candidate for EU membership. And you were approaching NATO as never before, ”he said.

Secretary -General also stressed that Putin underestimated the unity of NATO and the dedication of the Alliance to support Ukraine.

“NATO countries have provided 99% of all military assistance to Ukraine for tens of billion dollars. And it will be even more. We help make your armed forces and equipment more compatible with NATO allies, preparing for Ukraine's accession to the Alliance. This is not the question "if", this is the question "when", - said Jens Stoltenberg.

He added that Ukraine has already caused heavy losses to the Russian forces, rejecting the Russian Black Sea Fleet and opening a corridor for export of grain and other goods.
