The prison term was received by a teacher of a local higher vocational school, who was spying for the FSB from his own cottage.

About thisShe reported March 21Security Service of Ukraine.

According to the service, for the collection of intelligence information, a man arranged a "observation post" on his own cottage, which is located in a garden society near the Southern Front.

From there, the Russian informant tried to identify the locations of the Armed Forces units using binoculars and other optics.

He also traveled the surrounding area on a bicycle, where he recorded the movement of Ukrainian military equipment.

The SBU staff detained the attacker in July 2023 during his intelligence.

Considering the cooperation with the investigation, the court appointed the FSB Information 9 years in prison.

According to the investigation, the hostile association supported communication with the invaders through the "liaison".

He was the blogger of the pro-Kremlivsky telegram-channel, who was in the temporarily occupied part of the region and worked at the FSB.

It was he who remotely involved the teacher of Zaporizhzhya university in cooperation after he saw his anti -Ukrainian comments in the popular messenger.

On the basis of materials of the SBU, the court found the attacker guilty of unauthorized dissemination of information about movement, movement of the Armed Forces, committed in martial law.

Asreported the armyinform, in Zhytomyr region, the traitor increased 2.5 times.
