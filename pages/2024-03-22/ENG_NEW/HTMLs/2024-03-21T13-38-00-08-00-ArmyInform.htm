The Kyiv City Prosecutor's Office sent an indictment to the court regarding the former Prime Minister of Ukraine and his assistant in the Russian Federation.

About thisinformsOffice of the Prosecutor General.

“Both public calls for violent change and overthrow of the constitutional order or the seizure of state power, encroachment on the territorial integrity and inviolability of Ukraine, justification and recognition of legitimate, denial Part 2 of Article 110, Part 2 of Article 28 Part 2, 3, Article 436-2 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine). The expression minister is also charged with state treason (Part 2 of Article 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine), ”the message reads.

The investigation found that the former Prime Minister of Ukraine, while in the territory of the aggressor state, created a telegram channel. There he placed publications of a propaganda nature with the narratives of the Kremlin, who form anti -Ukrainian sentiment in society. The materials are aimed at the detriment of sovereignty, territorial integrity and integrity, defense, state, economic or information security of Ukraine.

“Two assistants of the expolvets were engaged in administration of telegrams. One of them held the position of head of the organizational department in the capital district state administration. Her case is already under consideration of the court, ”the department said.

Another assistant is a former employee of the same RDA, who has now left the territory of the Russian Federation.

It is noted that the accomplices together coordinated the information propaganda activity of the expression minister in the telegram channel, as well as in other social networks and helped organize and spread anti-Ukrainian interviews and comments by the Russian media.

By maintaining active relations with the expression minister and his entourage, women contributed to the conduct of information and subversive activities, which is coordinated by representatives of Russian special services and aimed at discrediting the current government in the international arena.

They did not stop helping the aggressor state even after a large-scale invasion.

The conducted forensic psychological and linguistic examinations of the published materials fully confirm the facts of the illegal activity of the persons involved.

The pre -trial investigation was carried out by the investigative department of the Security Service of Ukraine in Kyiv and Kyiv region, operational support - the Office of Counterintelligence of the SBU in Kyiv and Kyiv region.

As reported by the Armyinform, detainedtwo collaborators from Kherson region, which were pretended to be displaced.
