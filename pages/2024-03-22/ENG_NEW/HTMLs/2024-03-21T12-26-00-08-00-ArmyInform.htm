During a business trip to Visbaden (Germany), Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk, commander of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces, met with the commander of the US Land Forces in Europe and Africa General Dariel Williams.

About thisreported March 21Alexander Pavlyuk.

“Mr. General introduced the situation on the front and critical needs of the Armed Forces. In focus - air defense, ammunition, remote miners and more. Continuation of the support of the defense forces will save life and free Ukraine from the invaders, ”Alexander Pavlyuk wrote.

According to him, the improvement of the preparation of Ukrainian units, with the maximum consideration of the realities on the battlefield, and the prospects for the development of land troops, was also discussed.

Asreported the armyinform, US Presidential Advisor Jake Sullivan said Washington believes in the victory of Ukraine in the war with Russia.
