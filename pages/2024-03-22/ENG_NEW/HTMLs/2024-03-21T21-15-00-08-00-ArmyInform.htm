The procurement agency of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine "State Tale Operator" (DOT) during the event "Victory Strategy: System Changes in Security Sector in 2024" presented the results of the team work for the first 100 days of the agency.

About this DOTreportedon Facebook.

Thus, the general director of the state operator of the rear Arsen Zhumadilov during the presentation stated that in 100 days it was possible:

  • 5 billion hryvnias save the state budget due to the expansion of competition,
  • Benchmark among customers in the field of defense for the purchase of fuel and lubricants,
  • Use of non-price criteria to ensure the best balance “price-quality” in material security,
  • ensuring the continuity of food supply for 2 quarters,
  • Development and implementation of an IT system to collect food applications from military units to increase the transparency and accountability of the system.

"This IT system is one of the main tools for implementing the reform and will be developed with the support of the Special Defense Advisor Program on the UK Embassy in Kiev," Arsen Zhumadilov said.

It should be reminded that since the launch and formation of the state operator of the rear, the Support Program for Defense of the Embassy of the United Kingdom in Kiev was one of the first to support the procurement reform and continues today.

"The DOT team appreciates the support of international partners and continues to reform defense purchases," said Tale Director General Arsen Zhumadilov.

As reportedArmyinform, The state of the rear operator has concluded food contracts: savings on kits is UAH 2 billion.
