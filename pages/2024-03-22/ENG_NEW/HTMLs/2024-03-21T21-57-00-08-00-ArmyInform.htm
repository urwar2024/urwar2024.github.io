Servicemen who have been recognized as limited to the service will have to undergo a re -examination. Today, the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine has abolished the concept of "limited" for service in the Armed Forces.

About thisreportedDeputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova on the air of the Teleterna "Unified News".

“Those people who have received the conclusion of the VLK as“ limited suitable ”will be inspected and their suitability will be determined. In the future, the Military Medical Commission will not issue such a conclusion, ”she said.

Natalia Kalmykova added that such changes will facilitate the lives of tens of thousands of people.

“Both the relatives and the military themselves appealed to the Ministry of Defense and other authorities to resolve this issue. And we are very pleased that the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine supported these changes, ” - said the Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova.

As reportedArmyinform, The Council adopted a law on social protection of servicemen.
