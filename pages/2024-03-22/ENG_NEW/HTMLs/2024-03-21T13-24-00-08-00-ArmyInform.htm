Foreign Minister of Ukraine Dmytro Kuleba met with NATO Military Committee Chairman Admiral Robom Bauer, who is visiting Ukraine.

About thisinformsMinistry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine.

The Foreign Minister called for the increase of Ukraine's support from NATO, especially with flying weapons, return to the practice of training in Ukraine, deployment of repair bases in Ukraine.

“Dmytro Kuleba noted the important role of NATO member states in providing Ukraine with comprehensive assistance to reflect the armed aggression of the Russian Federation. He emphasized the importance of continued support for Ukraine, in particular, providing our country with critical weapons and military equipment, ”the message reads.

The interlocutors discussed the prospects for ensuring combat training of Ukrainian servicemen and close cooperation of the defense industries of Ukraine and allies.

The interlocutors also discussed the expectations from the Washington NATO summit.

As reported by the Armyinform, the highest NATO military leader - the chairman of the Alliance Bauer Military Committee, came to Ukraine for the first time since the beginning of the invasion. He spoke at the Kiev Security Forum andstatedthat Ukraine's defense forces have radically changed many aspects of modern war.
