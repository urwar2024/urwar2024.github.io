Estonia's Defense Minister Hanno Makur today in Kiev at a meeting with a Ukrainian colleague Rustem Umerov reported that Estonia will send to Ukraine another package of military assistance worth 20 million euros.

About thisinformsEstonia's defense agency.

It is noted that the new package includes artillery ammunition that are critically necessary to counteract Russian aggression.

"The package includes anti -tank guns, explosives, various types of artillery ammunition, gas masks, sniper equipment, smaller caliber ammunition, and more," said Defense Minister Hanno Mikur.

The last package of assistance includes 155 mm caliber shells, which is an additional contribution of Estonia to the European Union initiative to send a million projectiles to Ukraine. The replenishment of Estonia's contribution is partially funded by Denmark. The exact number of ammunition is not disclosed for safety reasons.

As reported by the Armyinform, UkraineShe started negotiationswith Estonia on the conclusion of a security agreement.
