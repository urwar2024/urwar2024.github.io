In the Kiev region, as a result of the Russian rocket attack, six apartment, 73 private homes and two educational institutions were damaged.

Such clarified informationprovidedin the police of the Kiev region.

“Damage has been recorded - six apartment, 73 private homes, two premises of educational institutions and 14 vehicles. Unfortunately, four people were injured, ”the message reads.

Police, explosives and SES workers worked on the sites of military crimes.

As reportedArmyinform, in Kiev, the combat part of the X-101 missile was removed and destroyed.
