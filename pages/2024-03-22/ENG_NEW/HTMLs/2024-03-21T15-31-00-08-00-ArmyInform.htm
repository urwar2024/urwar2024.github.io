The order of deprivation of the title of "Honorary Citizen of Kherson" Vyacheslav Gromikhin, which was awarded in August 2012, was signed.

About thisreported March 21Head of Kherson Military Administration Roman Mrochko.

According to him, Vyacheslav Gromikhin has been the chief architect of Kherson for over 20 years. During the occupation, the Russian troops remained in the city and evacuated to the left bank shortly before de -occupation. The hostile telegrams of the channels have recently spread the video, where the former chief architect of Kherson eagerly communicated with the Russian propaganda near one of the polling stations in the temporarily occupied territory during the illegal elections of the President of the Russian Federation.

“Gromikhin made his choice, so no longer an honorary citizen of Ukrainian Kherson! People who have disgraced themselves to cooperate with the Russian occupation authorities have no right to remain honorary citizens of Kherson! ” - Roman Mrochko wrote.
