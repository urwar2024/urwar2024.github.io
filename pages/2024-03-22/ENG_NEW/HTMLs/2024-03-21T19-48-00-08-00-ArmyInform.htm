With the support of public prosecution by the prosecutors of the Odessa Regional Prosecutor's Office, the court found a resident of the Black Sea guilty in the unauthorized dissemination of information on the location of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

About thisinformsOffice of the Prosecutor General.

She was sentenced to 8 years in prison.

"From July to August 2023, the convicted person transferred information on the dislocation of forces and means of the Armed Forces in the territory of Chernomorsk, Odessa region, as well as the existence of servicemen in separate administrative buildings in the region," the statement reads.

She also monitored the movement in the airspace of the military aircraft of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

The enemy's hostage was collected through Telegram, along with the Maps screenshots. The convicted person handed over at least 9 military objects to the occupiers.

The woman was detained in August 2023 at the stage of preparation for the next transfer of the enemy collected intelligence. At a court hearing, she pleaded guilty.
