Today, March 21, NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg, at a meeting with the Prime Minister of Slovenia Robert Golob, stressed that Ukraine needs more support, and it needs it now.

About thisinformsNATO website.

“Today, at our meeting, we touched Russia's war against Ukraine. Despite the heavy losses, President Putin did not give up his military goals, ”NATO Secretary General.

He stressed that the situation on the battlefield in Ukraine remains very difficult. Ammunition ends in the defense forces.

“Therefore, Ukraine needs even more support. And they need it now, ”Jens Stoltenberg said.

NATO Secretary General also greeted the contributions that Slovenia had already provided to Ukraine, in particular humanitarian assistance and security assistance.

As reportedArmyinform, Presidents of Ukraine and Slovenia discussed defense needs and a bilateral security agreement.
