As a result of a special liberation operation in the territory of the Russian Federation conducted by Russian volunteers who are fighting on the side of Ukraine, 651 military Russian Federation was liquidated.

This was stated during the press conference by UAV operator, the volunteer of the Legion "Freedom of Russia" Alexei Baranovsky, the call sign "Lutik".

“We have caused significant harm to the military of the Russian Federation. According to the information we have, the irreversible losses of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation make up - 651 military, sanitary losses - 908, another 37 were captured. Also, the Russian liberation forces destroyed 121 units of enemy equipment, and another 50 were disrupted, ”said Alexei Baranovsky.

According to him, thanks to this limited military operation of the Armed Forces, they were able to stabilize the front and cover the areas where the massive attack of Putin troops was expected.

According to a representative of the Legion "Freedom of Russia", the Russian army planned to carry out "significant" attacking actions, confusing the offensive to the "elections", or more precisely - to the future inauguration of Vladimir Putin.

“Our operation in the military sense disrupted these offensive plans for the Russian army. They (Russian servicemen.-Ed.) Were forced, first, to transfer considerable forces, in particular from Kharkiv and Kupyansk directions, in order to simply keep their Belgorod and Kursk regions, ”said Alexei Baranovsky.

As it was reported, on March 12, the Legion "Freedom of Russia", RDC and "Siberian Battalion" - Volunteer Russian formations, which are fighting on the side of Ukraine - in the morning entered the Kursk and Belgorod regions of the Russian Federation within a joint operation.
