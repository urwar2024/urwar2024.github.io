The commander and founder of the Russian Volunteer Corps Denis (White Rex) stated that the former fighters of the Wagner PEC in the ranks of RDCs participated in hostilities, in particular in the so -called liberation operation, in the territory of the Russian Federation and proved well.

He said this at a press conference, answering the question of the correspondent Armyinform on recruiting to the RDC of prisoners of war. In particular, the former fighters of the Wagner and Storm Z PVC.

"Former Wagner PEC fighters have already participated in the RDC (in hostilities. ”, - said the RDC commander.

He noted that these fighters voluntarily stood in the RDC's ranks because they considered it correct.

"The decision to speak on the side of the RDC is made by these people quite quickly, and so far we nor they have regretted the decision," said Denis White Rex.

In his opinion, this experiment can be considered absolutely successful.

“We do not just give these people the opportunity to fight in our benches, to change Russian pixel to RDC camouflage - we give these people the opportunity to start life from the beginning. Of course, we will continue to involve in this struggle, in our ranks of other fighters of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation, ”added White Rex.

As it was reported earlier, last year the fighters of the Russian Volunteer Corps and the Legion of Freedom of Russia, who are fighting on the side of Ukraine, declared "the beginning of the liberation of Russia" from the Putin regime. In particular, they conducted operations on the border of Russia, temporarily capturing the border points.

The representative of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense Andriy Yusov said that these citizens of Russia in the territory of Ukraine act as a part of the defense forces, and "at their home they have the right to do everything they see fit." He also stated that the Russians use trophy weapons.
