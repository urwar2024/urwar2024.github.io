After a pause in 44 days, the enemy struck another rocket strike in Kiev. The enemy used the winged missiles X-101/X-555/X-55 from the sides of strategic bombers of Tu-95MC and ballistic missiles from the territory of the Russian Federation.

About thisreportedKyiv City Military Administration.

Air alarm in the capital lasted almost 3 hours. After complex maneuvers in adjacent areas, rockets went to the capital at the same time from different directions.

About three dozen hostile missiles, including ballistic, were affected by the forces and means of air defense over Kiev and in the capital.

According to preliminary data, as a result of rocket stroke:

  • In the Shevchenkivskyi district, a multi -storey residential building with the fire of the apartment was damaged, the evacuation of people is carried out; At the other address, cars caught fire; In some homes, explosive waves are damaged by windows.
  • In Svyatoshinsky district, the wreckage damaged the building of the preschool educational institution, also recorded damage to a multi -storey apartment building, and the car was burning in the area;
  • In the Podilskyi district, the wreckage fell into the territory of non -residential buildings and on the roof of an apartment building with subsequent fire; Also recorded on the roof of a non -residential two -storey building.

Kyiv Mayor Vitaliy Klchokreported10 people were injured in Kiev as a result of a rocket strike in Kiev.

Two of them were hospitalized, other doctors were assisted on the spot. In particular, six people were injured in the Shevchenkivskyi district of the capital. Four - in Svyatoshinsky.
