On the second anniversary of the liberation of the Kiev region from Russian invaders, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky honored the memory of defenders who died during the defense and liberation of the village of Moshchun, and presented state awards.

About thisreported March 21President's office.

The warriors of the Honorary Guard installed a basket of flowers from the head of state to the commemorative sign "Angels of Victory".

“Today we remember the battle that happened two years ago, but which we can never forget. At that time, he largely determined the fate of the Ukrainian state. It was here, in the battles for Moshchun, and not far, in the battles for Gostomel, for Irpin, for other cities and villages of Kyiv region, the fate of Ukraine and the Ukrainian capital was determined. Then it was not just that not to let the Russian army into Kiev. At that time, it became obvious to all in the world what the Ukrainians are and what Putin Russia is, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy emphasized, addressing the audience.

The head of state noted that two years ago the world did not believe in Ukraine and did not understand the ominous essence of Russia, but saw the power of unity of our people, the courage and efficiency of Ukrainian soldiers.

“Everything changed then-in February-March 22, it is in such battles, in such manifestations of the heroism of our people and the vicinity of our enemy. At that time, we did not yet have such a strong weapon that is now, but we had the main thing then - it was the courage of people and the ability of Ukrainians to make the right choice. Choice for the sake of Ukraine, for the sake of humanity, for the sake of victory in the fight against Russian evil. I thank everyone who defended Kiev then. I thank everyone who fights for our country, our independence, our people, ”said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

The attendees paid a moment of silence to the memory of warriors who died for the freedom of Ukraine.

During the moment of silence, the names of 118 defenders who died in the battles for the village of Moshchun were performed, reflecting the invasion of Russian invaders.

Volodymyr Zelensky also presented state awards to servicemen of the Armed Forces, the State Border Service, the National Guard, the Security Service of Ukraine.

In particular, the honors of the President "Cross of Combat Merit" was received by the Defense Participant of Kyiv, Lieutenant Colonel of the State Tax Service of Ukraine Mykola Demianyuk, who organized the defense in the most dangerous and difficult direction of promotion of the enemy in the Moschun. He also distinguished himself during the battles in Kharkiv region.

In addition, the Head of State presented the orders of Bohdan Khmelnytsky III degree, Order "For Courage" of the II and III degrees, the medal "for military service of Ukraine".

In the events on the territory of the future Memorial "Angels of Victory" were also present relatives, close and brothers of the fallen defenders of Ukraine.

The Victory Angels Memorial is created on the territory of the forest near Moschun, where one of the most fierce battles of Kiev's defense from Russian invaders took place in March 2022.

Asreported the armyinform, The President commented on today's blow of Russia in Kiev.
