On the night of March 21, the enemy attacked the territory of Ukraine with ballistic and winged missiles, and more than 30 units were shot down.

About thisreportedCommander of the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk.

The enemy launched two ballistic/aerobalist missiles "Iskander-M" (KN-23)/X-47M2 "Dagan", as well as 29 winged missiles X-101/X-555 with 11 strategic bombers TU-95MS (PUSKO-Volgodondi. , Engels - Russian).

The rockets came from the north, the main direction of the impact is Kiev. As a result of successful combat work, all the hostile missiles were shot down in the Kyiv region.

Air Force Air Force and Mobile Fire Defense Defense Forces of Ukraine are involved in the reflection of the air attack.

As reported by the Armyinform, in Kiev as a result of rocket attack of the Russian Federationaffected10 people.
