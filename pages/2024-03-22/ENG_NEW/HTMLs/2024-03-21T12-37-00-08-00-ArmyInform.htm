Russia's war against Ukraine has never been associated with a real threat to its security by Ukraine or the North Atlantic Alliance.

According to the correspondent of the Armyinform, the NATO Military Committee Chairman Admiral Rob Bauer stated this during a speech at the Kiev Security Forum.

“And no propaganda will be able to hide the truth that President Putin has not achieved any of his strategic goals. And how can he? Russian troops have no idea what they are fighting for ... You have. You are fighting for civilization itself, ”Rob Bauer said.

He stressed that Russia is desperately trying to portray the image of force, but they only demonstrate weakness.

“No propaganda will be able to hide the coffins that fly back to the Russian Federation. No propaganda will be able to answer the questions of the families of dead soldiers. This war is the fear of President Putin to something much more powerful than any physical weapon on earth: democracy, ”said Admiral Rob Bauer.
