President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky has a telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Greece Kiriakos Kharatotakis.

He is about itwroteIn his telegram channel.

According to Volodymyr Zelenskyy, they spoke about the implementation of the agreements reached during his recent visit to Ukraine, to continue defense cooperation, in particular the strengthening of Ukrainian Air Defense.

“The need for reliable protection of the sky, Mr. Prime Minister was convinced in Odessa on March 6. I also told him about the morning Russian blow to Kiev, ”the President of Ukraine said.

In addition, they agreed to accelerate the work on a bilateral security agreement within the G7 Vilnius Declaration.

As reported by the Armyinform, Volodymyr Zelenskyy honored the memory of warriors,Fun in the Battle of the Bulch.
