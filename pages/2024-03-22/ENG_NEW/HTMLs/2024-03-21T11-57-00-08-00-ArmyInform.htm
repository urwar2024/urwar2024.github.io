Together with the Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Yuri Dzhigirm, the Minister of Defense of the Netherlands Kaisa Ollogren visited the positions of the operational-strategic group of troops (Osv) "Khortytsia" and combat units of the defense forces in the eastern direction.

About thisreported March 21Ministry of Defence Ukraine.

The delegation heard the commanders of the current situation on the line of fighting, got acquainted with the urgent needs of the military at the front and priorities in the supply of weapons and military equipment.

Kaisa Ollogren spoke with the defenders and east defenders. Together with her colleagues, she visited the seats of Russian rockets and drones in the Dnieper and laid flowers on the heroes.

“It is significant and valuable that Kaisa Ollogren took time and visited our units in the Khortytsa Osv before official measures and meetings of delegations in Kiev. I thank Ms. Kaisi and the Government of the Kingdom of the Netherlands for the lead in support and extreme attention to Ukraine, ”said Deputy Minister of Defense Yuri Dzhigir.

The Armyinform reportedthat the Netherlands will allocate more than € 150 million for the purchase of ammunition for Ukrainian F-16.
