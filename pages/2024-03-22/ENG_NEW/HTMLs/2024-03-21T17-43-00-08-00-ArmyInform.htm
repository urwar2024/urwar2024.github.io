The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine welcomes the publication of the report of the UN High Human Rights Commissioner on the human rights situation in the territories of Ukraine, temporarily occupied by the Russian Federation as a result of a large -scale military invasion, and in the territories of our country, which subsequently restored control of the Government of Ukraine.

About thisIt is saidon the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine.

"We urge the international community to double the efforts in the fight against the impunity of Russia, to hold the aggressor state accountable for all crimes committed, to establish justice for all its victims," the department said.

It is noted that the document indicates gross violations by the state-aggressor of human rights to TT.

In particular, from the first days of occupation, the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation resorted to the practice of mass arbitrary detention of civilians, which was often accompanied by torture and ill -treatment.

The Office of the High Commissioner for Human Rights has identified numerous crimes of Russian invaders, including the suppression of peaceful protests, arbitrary executions and murder, restriction of freedom of speech and movement of residents, regular searches that were often accompanied by physical violence, including sexual and theft of property.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs also appealed to the UN Member States calling for international efforts to implement the principles of the Ukrainian peace and creation of the world, in which the use of power will be unthinkable, and human rights and human dignity will be of paramount importance.
