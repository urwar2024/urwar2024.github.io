President Volodymyr Zelenskyy commented on another large -scale missile attack by the Russians.

He is about itwrote on the morning of March 21.

“The new Russian rocket attack against Kiev. More than 30 rockets have been shot, including aerobalistic ones. People were injured. Everyone is provided with the necessary help, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

In his view, world unity is able to stop this air terror if it assists Ukraine with air defense systems.

"There are no rockets for Russian terrorists that could bypass the protection of Petriots and other leading world systems," the President said.

According to him, now this protection is needed here in Ukraine. From Kiev to Kharkiv, from Sumy to Kherson, from Odessa to Donetsk region. This is quite possible if partners have enough political will.

“We need to show that the terror is always in losing. We must prove to Russia that it will be forced to put up with a normal free life in Ukraine. We need partners support. And I am grateful to everyone in the world who really helps, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.
