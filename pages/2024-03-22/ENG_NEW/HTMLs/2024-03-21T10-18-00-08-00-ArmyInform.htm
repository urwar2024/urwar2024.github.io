The FSB agent was detained, who tried to spy on the SBU staff and adjust the missile strikes at the enterprises of Zaporizhzhya region involved in the defense-industrial complex.

About thisShe reported March 21Security Service of Ukraine.

It is established that at the request of the FSB of the Russian Federation the defendant spied on the local SBU staff to install spare points of the dislocation of the service.

According to the available information, the enemy planned to use this information to prepare rocket strikes.

In addition, the malefactor tried to establish the exact coordinates of the enterprises of the defense-industrial complex of Ukraine.

To do this, she received from the occupiers the indicative geolocation of defense plants, and then near them conducted an intelligence on the ground.

When tracking, the person recorded the maximum information about the objects: from the mode of their work to the intensity of movement of cargo equipment in the open territory of the shops.

She also used her friends and classmates, in whom she questioned the information she needed during his friendly conversations.

According to her guidance, the invaders hoped to strike sight rocket-arable blows to production enterprises.

However, the Security Service exposed the criminal intentions of the aggressor in advance and detained the Russian agent at the stage of collecting intelligence information.

According to the investigation, a 19-year-old student of a local university, who was in view of the FSB in the fall of 2023, was an enemy hostile.

Subsequently, the girl was remotely recruited because of her baptized - a collaborator, which is part of the leadership of the occupation administration of the Russian Federation in one of the temporarily captured cities in Zaporozhye.

It was through her that the Russian intelligence service gave instructions to a published agent in the regional center and accepted "reports" about the tasks completed.

Currently, the detainee has been reported suspicion under Part 2 of Art. 28, Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine.

She is in custody. She is threatened with life imprisonment.

Asreported the armyinform, in the north of Ukraine, a cyclist was detained, who collected data on the Armed Forces for the Russians.
