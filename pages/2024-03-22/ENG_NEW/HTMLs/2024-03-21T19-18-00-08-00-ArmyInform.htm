67 combat clashes were recorded during the day. In total, the enemy caused 36 missile and 50 aviation strikes, and made 96 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy, with the support of aviation, carried out 1 unsuccessful assault of the positions of defense forces in the area of the settlement of Sinkivka of Kharkiv region.

In the estuary directionOur warriors reflect 10 enemy attacks in the areas of the settlements of Bigorivka of Luhansk region and thorny, controversial, Vesele, Rozdolivka of Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionOur warriors reflects 14 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Bogdanivka, Ivanivske, Khishchivka, Andreevka, Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders reflect 4 attacks of the enemy in the areas of settlements Berdichi, Semenivka, Tonenke, Pervomaisk Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of settlements of Krasnogorivka, Novomikhailivka, Vodyane, Urozhizhne, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried 24 times to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy 6 times, with the support of aviation, attacked the positions of our defenders in the areas of settlements of Staromayorsk Donetsk region and a robot of Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson directionDuring the day, the enemy of the offensive (assault) did not take action.

We will remind, in the south the soldiers of the Armed Forces“Landed”Russian drones SuperCam and Lancet.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Armed Forces in the SouthDestroyedFlame grenade launcher and the ENB station of the occupiers.
