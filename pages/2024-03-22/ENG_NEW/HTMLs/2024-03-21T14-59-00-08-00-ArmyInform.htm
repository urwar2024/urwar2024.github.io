At a meeting on March 21, the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine approved in the second reading and as a whole the law on the Law on Amendments to Certain Laws of Ukraine on Ensuring the Rights of Servicemen and Police for Social Protection (Reg. No. 10313).

About thisinformsThe press service of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine.

This law ensures the rights of servicemen to information, medical support, leave, police rights to one -time financial assistance in the event of death, determination of disability.

“The purpose of the law is to improve the mechanisms of securing some rights of servicemen, in particular, information, medical support, leave, determination of disability, legal regulation of other issues related to the exercise of the rights of military personnel and military officials during military service. In addition, it is envisaged to ensure the rights of police for one -time financial assistance in the event of death (death) and determination of disability, ”the message reads.

AsreportedMember of the Verkhovna Rada Committee on National Security, Defense and Intelligence Irina Fries, the law will be given the opportunity to stay servicemenOn treatment up to 12 months.

"In particular, abroad, without the need for 4 months, to pass the VLK personally," she emphasized.

At the law level, clear payments are established in case ofthe death of a serviceman in the amount of UAH 15 million.

The provisions for additional excessive regulation of humanitarian aid.

“It should be implemented by the relevant legislation, and extend the restrictions under the beautiful sign of the Social Security of the military is false and unacceptable. The committee rejected all the rules concerning this issue, ”the People's Deputy wrote.
