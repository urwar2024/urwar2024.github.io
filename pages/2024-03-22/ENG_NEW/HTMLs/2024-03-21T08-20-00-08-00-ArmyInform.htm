According to preliminary information, 10 people were injured as a result of hostile shelling, one of them.

In total, 79 people were evacuated. The information on the victims is clarified.

About thisreported on the morning of March 21State Emergency Service.

On the morning of March 21, the enemy carried out another missile attack on the capital. The explosions were heard in Podilskyi, Shevchenkivsky and Svyatoshinsky districts.

In the Podilskyi district, at one address, the fire occurred in a two -storey building on an area of 30 square meters. m. The first floor and partially the second were destroyed.

At the other address, there was a transformer substation ignition, and at another address of the fire occurred in a warehouse on an area of 150 square meters. m.

Shevchenkivskyi district: a fire in the apartment on the 5th floor of a 6-storey apartment building occurred at one address.

Also in the area there was a fire in 3 cars and damaged windows of an apartment building.

Svyatoshinsky district: windows were damaged in an apartment building.

There were 177 rescuers and 38 units of fire and rescue equipment on the ground.

Asreported the armyinform, all the missiles that the Russians attacked Kiev on the morning of March 21 were destroyed.
