Today, March 21, after a monthly pause, Russia brought submarines with winged missiles into the Black Sea.

About thisreportDefense forces of South of Ukraine.

"After a pause a month, the enemy brought to the Black Sea 2 underwater rocket launchers," the message reads.

It is noted that the total volley of caliber is up to 8 rockets.

The defense forces of the south of Ukraine specified that the rest of the enemy ships are at the points of base.

"The level of rocket danger is very high," the military said.
