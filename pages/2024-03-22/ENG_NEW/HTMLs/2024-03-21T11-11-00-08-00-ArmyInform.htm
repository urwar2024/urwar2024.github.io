The former chief of the Central Food Supporting Department of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine was informed of suspicion of illegal enrichment.

About thisreported March 21Office of the Prosecutor General.

The exponent is currently detained.

According to the investigation, the suspect was already released from office, being an authorized person to perform the functions of the state, acquired assets by almost UAH 58 million.

It is a car toyota, 2022, an apartment in Kyiv, a regional base of special medical purpose in Khmelnytskyi with a total area of 2022 square meters. m, 55 plots in different regions, as well as the only property complex of Khmelnitsky State Experimental Prosthetic Orthopedic Enterprise.

The amount of illegally acquired funds by more than six thousand five hundred non -taxable minimum incomes of citizens exceeds its legal income.

Currently, a search is underway at the place of residence of the exponent. The issue of election of a suspect in the form of detention is also resolved.

The court made an arrest on illegally acquired assets.

Asreported the armyinform, military counterintelligences of the Security Service of Ukraine exposed to corruption the head of the Main Directorate of Capital Investments of the Ministry of Defense.
