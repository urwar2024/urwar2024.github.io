Since the beginning of a large-scale invasion of the Russian Federation, the EU and its Member States have provided military assistance to Ukraine for € 31 billion.

About thisstatedThe high representative of the EU on Foreign Affairs and Security Policy Josep Borrel.

“My latest report on Ukraine's military support, I spoke about 28 billion euros. Now this figure is 31 billion euros. So, it changes daily. Yes, since the beginning of the war we have provided € 31 billion military aid, and this year we have planned € 20 billion. So we continue to support Ukraine, ”the message reads.

According to Josep Borrel, the EU has transferred nearly 500,000 shells to the supplies of ammunition.

“I assume that the leaders will support my proposal with the European Commission on the confiscation of income from frozen assets of Russia. I have sent this proposal to the Council, it should make a decision, and I expect that the leaders will tell their ministers today to quickly approve this proposal that will provide [approximately] EUR 3 billion a year for Ukrainian defense, ”he added .
