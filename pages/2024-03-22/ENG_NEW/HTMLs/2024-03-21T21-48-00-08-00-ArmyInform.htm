This is how the servicemen of the Armed Forces of Canada escorted our soldiers to Ukraine after completing their studies abroad.

PhotoExposedOn the Unifier Canadian Training Mission.

"We, together with our partners from the UK Armed Forces and Lithuanian Armed Forces, support Ukraine and give honor to their courage, while they return to their country to protect their land and people from the uncontrollable Russian aggression," - said in posts.

Recall that after the beginning of a large -scale Russian invasion, Canadian instructors left the territory of Ukraine, but within a few months of study were restored in the United Kingdom, Poland and Latvia.
