At the meeting of representatives of the Ministry of Strategic Industry of Ukraine and Entrepreneurs with a delegation from the Kingdom of the Netherlands, 5 documents on cooperation in the field of defense industry were signed.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

“This first trade delegation is a specific implementation of the security agreement that the Netherlands and Ukraine signed 3 weeks ago. It assumes that both countries are obliged to cooperate in defense industries. Both countries will benefit from this cooperation, ”said the Minister of Defense of the Netherlands Kaisa Ollogren.

Signed documents concern cooperation between Ukrainian and Dutch companies in the field of defense sector innovation.

“During today's meeting, we discussed the priority areas of cooperation between defense companies of Ukraine and the Kingdom of the Netherlands. These areas include joint development of autonomous systems, solutions based on CI and other technological products. We plan to work out a number of common projects in the near future, ”said Deputy Minister for Strategic Industry Strategic Industry.

It should be noted that the Ukrainian side was attended by Deputy Minister for Strategic Industry Sergey Boyev and Anna Gvozdyar, CEO Andriy Rez and co -founder of the Association of Ukrainian Defense Companies Denis Svyatokum.

From the Netherlands, the meeting was attended by Defense Minister Kaisa Ollogren, CEO of Policy MON KUN Davidse, Head of the Coordinating Group on Military Support of Ukraine at MO Simon Rinke Vauda, Commissioner for Military Production of the Ministry of Economy Jan Christiaan. Hoppenreis, as well as the Netherlands Ambassador to Ukraine Yennes de Mol.

As reportedArmyinform, the King of the Netherlands visited Ukrainian military training in Britain.
