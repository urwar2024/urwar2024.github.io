Australian Defense Minister Richard Marls said Australia would join the drone coalition.

He is about itreportedOn joint with the State Secretary of Defense of the United Kingdom, a chaps of the Press Conference in Canberra.

“We announce that Australia will participate in a drone coalition headed by the United Kingdom and Latvia. This is an important opportunity for us to continue to make a contribution to keep Ukraine's chosen course and be able to resolve this conflict on our own conditions, ”said Australian Defense Minister.

Deputy Minister of Defense Kateryna Chernororenko thanked Australia and Richard Marlz for such a decision.

"Expanding the Ukrainian initiative and joining partners from all over the world is a symbol of Ukraine's support for the world and real steps to strengthen our technological army," she said.

Reference: The drone coalition officially started as part of the contact group on Defense of Ukraine. The coalition is the United Kingdom and Latvia. The coalition also joined: Denmark, Estonia, Lithuania, Canada, the Netherlands, Germany and Sweden.
