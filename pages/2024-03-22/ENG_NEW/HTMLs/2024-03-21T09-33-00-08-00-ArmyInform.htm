Prosecutors defended in the Supreme Court a sentence of a native of Kharkiv region for state treason (Part 2 of Article 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

About thisreportedOffice of the Prosecutor General.

The convicted person was sentenced to 15 years in prison with confiscation of property.

According to the department, the man in early March 2022 was in favor of the enemy in order to harm Ukraine's defense capability and state security.

He transferred representatives of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation on the location of personnel and military equipment and the Armed Forces of Ukraine in the territory of Kharkiv region. The information was forwarded through the Telegram messenger.

The Criminal Court of the Supreme Court agreed with the prosecutor's arguments that the qualification is correct, and the punishment imposed by a fair measure of coercion for the traitor.

The convicted person did not recognize his guilt in the incriminated crime during the pre -trial investigation and trial proceedings in all instances.

Currently, a man is serving a court sentence.

AsThe ArmyInform reported earlier, almost 128,000 military crimes of Russia are documented in Ukraine.
