In the morning of March 21, Poland raised fighter jets due to the fact that Russian troops fired Ukraine from strategic bombers.

About thisreportedin the press service of the Polish Army operational command.

The report said Poland applied all the necessary procedures to ensure the safety of its airspace.

"The Armed Forces Operational Command is watching the active activity of long -term aviation of the Russian Federation, related to the task of air and missile strikes in the objects located in the territory of Ukraine," the command said.

The population was warned that the fighters of Poland and allies have been raised into the air, which is why people can hear increased noise, especially in the southeastern part of the country.

As reported by the Armyinform, on the night of March 21st opponentattackedMore than 30 units have been shot down by ballistic and winged missiles.
