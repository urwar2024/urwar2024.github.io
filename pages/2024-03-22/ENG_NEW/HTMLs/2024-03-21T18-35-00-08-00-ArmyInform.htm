Since February 2022, Norway provided more than $ 500 million financial assistance to Ukraine.

About thisreportedMinister of Finance of Ukraine Sergey Marchenko.

"In particular, a grant of about $ 485 million was directed within the framework of the World Bank project" Supporting Public Expenditures to ensure sustainable public administration in Ukraine ". The funding involved was aimed at compensating for the state budget expenditures for priority social and humanitarian needs. These are pensions, remuneration of employees of education and health care, social protection of the population, humanitarian support, ”the message reads.

He added that recently Norway joined the multilateral donor coordination platform during the eighth meeting of the Leadership Committee.

Norway has also become one of the four new platform member states that have committed themselves to allocate more than $ 5.5 billion in total for the restoration and development of Ukraine's economy.

The Minister of Finance said that this year Ukraine needs about $ 38 billion. US external funding from partners.

As reported by the Armyinform, Norwayprovides140 million euros to the Czech initiative to purchase ammunition for Ukraine.
