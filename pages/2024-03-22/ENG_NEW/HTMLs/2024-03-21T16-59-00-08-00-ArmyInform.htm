Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Ivan Havrylyuk thanked Estonia's Defense Minister Hanno Petus for providing urgently necessary military assistance.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

He also expressed gratitude to Estonia for joining the mission of training in Ukrainian military Interflex.

"Quality training of the military is a key component of further success in the battlefield," said Ivan Havrylyuk.

The Deputy Minister also emphasized the importance of technological innovations for the advantage of the enemy that Ukraine introduces with the help of Estonian partners.

As you know, a new military assistance packagewill includeArtillery ammunition, anti -tank weapons, explosives, sniper equipment and other weapons. Estonia also transmits 155 mm caliber shells, which is an additional contribution to its country to the European Union initiative.
