During the day, the army of the Russian Federation attacked Nikopol three times with shock drones.

About thisreportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Ova Sergey Lisak.

“Three attacks on Nikopol per day. Dron Kamikadze kissed the city in the morning. And twice the Russian army sent drones in the evening, ”he said.

The head of Ova also noted that no one was injured today from hostile terror.

"It was calm in the rest of the territory of Dnipropetrovsk," said Sergei Lisak.

As reported by the Armyinform, in Kherson region through Russian attacksTwo people were killed.
