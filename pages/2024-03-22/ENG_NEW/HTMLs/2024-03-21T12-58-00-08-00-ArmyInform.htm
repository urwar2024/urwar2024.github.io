On the territory of one of the forestry of the Holy Mountain National Nature Park, after partial mine demining of the territories, about 10,000 pine seedlings were planted.

About thisreported March 21Head of Donetsk Military Administration Vadim Filashkin.

"All seedlings are grown in the Lviv Forest Breeding Center from the seeds collected in the forests of the Lyman region, and were transferred to us for free," Vadim Filashkin said.

According to him, more than 10,000 hectares of forest were destroyed in the territory of the Liman Community alone.

In 2024 it is planned to restore about 100 hectares of forest in the National Nature Park "Holy Mountains".

The Armyinform reportedthat 20% of the Nature Reserve Fund of Ukraine suffered from war.
