Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova at the annual congress of Society for Brain Mapping & amp; Therapeutics, which took place in the United States, spoke online about Ukrainian experience in the treatment and rehabilitation of the military who received traumatic brain and spinal injuries and injuries.

She is about itreportedon Facebook.

Natalia Kalmykova noted that Ukrainian doctors and rehabilitation professionals have ten years of experience with wounded servicemen.

“Unfortunately, since 2022, their number has increased significantly. Today, among all combat injuries of the head, neck, spine, 1/3 of all cases, ”the Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine said.

According to Natalia Kalmykova, Ukrainian physicians have worked out a clear algorithm for evacuation, assistance and rehabilitation, which is based on extensive domestic experience and best practices of Western partners.

“At the same time, we have many challenges. This is especially true of loading on the rehabilitation system from patients with severe traumatic brain and spinal trauma, as well as their further socialization and creating conditions for their return to civilian life. Our team is working on solving these issues today, ”Natalia Kalmykova said, and thanked the delegates of the Society for Brain Mapping & Amp; Therapeutics for constructive discussion.

As reportedArmyinform, the Ministry of Defense showed how medical cars were equipped to evacuate the wounded.
