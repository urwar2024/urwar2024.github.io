According to Presidential Decree Volodymyr Zelensky No. 143/2022, a minute of silence is spent daily at 9:00, it is announced in all media.

"In order to honor the bright memory, civic courage and dedication, fortitude, resilience and heroic feat of soldiers who died while performing combat missions to protect the state sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine, civilians who died as a result of the armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine, I decide: to launch a nationwide minute of silence daily at 9:00 a.m. for compatriots who died as a result of armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine, which should be announced in all media, regardless of ownership," the document says.
