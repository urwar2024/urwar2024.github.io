Canada's Armed Forces are teaching the soldiers of the Armed Forces of Ukraine how to identify, avoid and dispose of explosive objects.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Canadian military stationed in the United Kingdom as part of the Multinational Interflex Educational Operation and that is where they conduct training for our soldiers.

Mines and other explosive objects pose a constant danger on the modern battlefield.

"The Armed Forces are grateful for the support and assistance of Canada and the United Kingdom," the General Staff said.

Earlier, the Armed Forces General Staff showed how Ukrainian servicemen under the guidance of Australian instructors of the Kudu operation operationworked outSkills of the area with suburban construction, including storming and cleaning of buildings.
