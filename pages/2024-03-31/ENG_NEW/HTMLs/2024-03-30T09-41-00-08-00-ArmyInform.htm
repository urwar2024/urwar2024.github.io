The russian military in the Horlivka direction withdrew its artillery to the maximum distance so that its defenders could protect it from Ukrainian drones.

About it on the airArmy TVThe commander of the company of shock unmanned aviation complexes "Rarog" 24 separate mechanized brigade named after King Danylo with the call sign "Hassan" told.

“In the Gorlovsky direction, the enemy has mainly defensive actions. As of now, the enemy applies mainly artillery, which is located at a fairly long distance, which complicates us flights to them, ”Hassan said.

He noted that it was before, when enemy artillery remedies stood at a distance of 10-12 kilometers, now is not observed.

The commander of the RUBAK also said that the enemy is creating fictitious positions, trying to deceive Ukrainian UAV pilots.

“We even noticed that burned equipment from the right flank was dragged to the left, masking at night, digging capons, and opening the barrels, as if it were good weapons. This is a pretty cunning maneuver, but we know it and FPV-therots do not spend on such fictitious positions, ”he said.

Hassan also told how the invaders prepare traps for such drones,як «Баба Яга»(This is what Russians call a large impact hexacopter Vampire).

"They disguise a piece of metal about two meters by two, as if it were a technique, and it "glows" into a thermal imager. A MON-50 mine is installed on top. The idea is that when a drone descends to drop ammunition, a magnetic field is triggered, the mine explodes and damages the drone with debris," he explained.
