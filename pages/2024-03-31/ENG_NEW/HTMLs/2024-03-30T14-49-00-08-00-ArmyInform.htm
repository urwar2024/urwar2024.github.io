As a result of the Russian firing of Krasnogorivka in the Donetsk region, two people were killed and one was injured.

About thisreportedHead of Donetsk Ova Vadim Filashkin.

The city was under enemy fire during the night and in the morning-a 70-year-old woman and a 73-year-old man were killed. Another person was injured and was taken to the hospital.

"Krasnogorivka has been on the front line for 10 years and remains one of the most dangerous places in the country," Vadim Filashkin said.

As reported by the Armyinform, during the day Russian troops442 struck9 settlements of Zaporizhzhya region.
