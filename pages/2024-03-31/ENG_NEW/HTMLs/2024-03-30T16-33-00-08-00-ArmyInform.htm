In the first three months of 2024, the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine admitted more than 80 samples of weapons and military equipment of domestic production in the army.

This is stated inmessageThe Ministry of Defense of Ukraine with reference to the statement of the Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Lieutenant General Ivan Havrylyuk.

The largest group is ammunition. Different types and calibers.

The second group by number is UAV. In this line, in particular, there are completely new drones of different types: both FPV-Kamikadze and repeated UAVs, designed for different distances.

The third group - ground robotic complexes and remedies.

As reported by the Armyinform, Ivan Havrylyuktoldthat in Ukraine the production of ammunition has increased at times, how to compare with the beginning of 2022.
