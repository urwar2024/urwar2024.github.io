The Belgium Government has approved a new package of 100 million euros to Ukraine, which will support the F-16 coalition.

This was reported by the Minister of Defense People Dedonnder.

"The Council of Ministers has approved the 25th Package of Assistance to Ukraine: 100 million euros will be allocated for service and support for the F-16 within the international coalition," the statement reads.

Belgium is one of the thirteen countries that are involved in the F-16 fighter fighters.
