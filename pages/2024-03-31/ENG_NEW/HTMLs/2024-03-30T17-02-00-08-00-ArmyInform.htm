In the Dnipropetrovsk region, the construction of fortifications continues, and the work is carried out simultaneously in several directions.

About thisreportedHead of Dnipropetrovsk Ova Sergey Lisak.

At the sites of future fire points and armor, they create rubble bases. Reinforced concrete structures are widely used. They are now waterproofing to protect from the negative impact of rain and wastewater, etc.

“Such structures should serve skillfully, keep defense firmly and reliably, providing protection of the military. Everything, as provided by the Ministry of Defense's projects, ”added Sergei Lisak.

In the process of construction of fortifications, more than 42 km of anti -tank ditches have already been dug.

The number of tetrahedras - special concrete pyramids, which has already been installed over 11 thousand, is also increasing.

As reported by the Armyinform, fortifications are being built in other regions. In the Kyiv region alreadyestablishedNearly 10,000 concrete pyramids have been equipped with an anti -tank ditch.
