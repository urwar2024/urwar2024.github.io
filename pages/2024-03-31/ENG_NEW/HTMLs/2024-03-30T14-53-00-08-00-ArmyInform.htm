Mayor of Belaya Tserkov, Kyiv region Gennady Wild to join the ranks of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

He stated this inVideoMarch 30, 2024.

"I have the military rank of officer, and I also have a military specialty. I am an air defense officer. I decided to join the Armed Forces of Ukraine. With hundreds of thousands, millions of our fellow citizens to stand up for the state," he said.

As reported by the Armyinform, since February 2024 in Ukraine the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine began to open recruiting centers to mobilize people in the defense forces of Ukraine. As of the end of March 2024, there were already five such centers. Currently to these centersMore than 450 people applied.
