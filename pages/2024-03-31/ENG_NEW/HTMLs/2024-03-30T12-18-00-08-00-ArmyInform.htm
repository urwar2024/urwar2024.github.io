In the Kiev region, work on the arrangement of fortifications continues to increase.

About thisreportedThe head of the Kiev Regional State Administration Ruslan Kravchenko.

The fortifications under construction provide a branched system of trenches, armor and other types of shelters.

In addition, barriers - "teeth of the dragon", anti -tank ditches and other non -clipping barriers - are installed. Almost 10,000 concrete pyramids have already been installed, an anti -tank ditch has been equipped.

“We are constantly communicating with the military to build the most effective structures with the most reliable protection. We mobilize all the strengths and resources to accomplish the tasks, ”Ruslan Kravchenko said.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Zaporozhye direction of fortificationbuildin three shifts. In particular, the state allocated UAH 1.3 billion from the budget for the construction of fortifications.

Generally all over the front lineDefense boundaries are being built, install special antitrone equipment.
