The spring wave of forced mobilization in the so-called "LNR" began with the conscription of the male gender of 1994-2006.

About thisreportedChief of Lugansk Ova Artem Lisogor.

“Those who have been 18 years old will be found quickly, because the personal data of young occupants have been collected quickly for these two years, in particular, through educational institutions. Their location is known, ”Artem Lisogor explained.

According to him, the so -called Ministry of Internal Affairs "LNR" was ordered to take all necessary measures to establish the actual location of citizens who do not appear to the military enlistment offices and their delivery of force.

The forced campaign will last during April-July. The relevant decree was signed by the occupying power of the region.

The Kremlin has once again emphasized the full certification of the population in the occupied territories. According to the invaders, it has already been "completed" at least twice.

“But when it took an increased turnover of the President of the Russian Federation, the problem was manifested again. Therefore, they allowed to vote without documents of the Russian model, and sometimes - and in general without documents. The next deadline for full forced certification is 2026, ”said the head of the Lugansk Ova.
