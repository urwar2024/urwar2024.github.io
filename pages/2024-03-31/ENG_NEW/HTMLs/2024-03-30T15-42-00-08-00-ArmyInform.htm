The border guards of the Guard Brigade of the offensive of the “steel border” raised over the settlements of Shabelne, Sand and Degtyarne, which are located near the state border from the Russian Federation in the so -called “gray zone” in Kharkiv region, flags of Ukraine.

"However, the border guards of the Guard Brigade" Steel Border "control the terrain and remind the enemy that it is our land and it is not a place here!" - The message says.
