79 Separate Airborne Tavriya Brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine adopted the football players of Yevgeny Seleznev and Artem Milevsky.

This is stated inmessagebrigades.

"The legendary players of the national football team Yevgeny Seleznev and Artem Milevsky came to the Donbas to support Tavriya paratroopers," the unit said.

Former Dynamo and Shakhtar forwards were brought to the drone brigade.

The instructors of the brigade, who exactly conducted the training, shared with the players the skills of apt shooting.

“And the ex-infantries suggested that the soldiers play football. And we love football! As a result - a fighting draw, ”the brigade reported.

As reported by the Armyinform, in November 2023 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyyassigned79th separate landing brigade of the landing troops of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Honorary name "Tavriya".
