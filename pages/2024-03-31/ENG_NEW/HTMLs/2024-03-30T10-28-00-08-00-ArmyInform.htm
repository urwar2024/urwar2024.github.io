The Verkhovna Rada Committee on National Security, Defense and Intelligence has completed the consideration of all 4,269 amendments to the mobilization bill.

About thisreportedThe Chairman of the Committee People's Deputy Alexander Zaitnevich.

Representatives of profile ministries and deputies, as well as deputies-authors of the relevant amendments, participated in the meetings of the committee.

The procedure will now additionally develop committee amendments and the final comparative table will be formed.

The table will then be transferred to the Main Legal Department of the Verkhovna Rada, after receiving the conclusion, the bill will be considered in the parliament hall.

“In general, I am grateful to all participants of the meetings. Yes, the degree of discussion was sometimes maximum, so, sometimes the political component of belonging to one or another political force was signaled, but all participants of meetings are aware of the tension and complexity of the situation that our country is experiencing, the importance of compromises for the sake of consolidation of society, for the sake of victory. I hope that the same atmosphere will be in the session hall and the deputies will support the bill, ”Alexander Zaitnevich wrote.
