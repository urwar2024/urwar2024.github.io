The Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine spoke about Russia's intensification of measures to strengthen sections of the Russian border in the Belgorod, Kursk, and Bryansk regions after the raids of the "Siberian Battalion", the "Russian Volunteer Corps" and the "Freedom of Russia" legion.

This is stated inmessageGur.

According to Ukrainian intelligence, the personnel of the troops of the Russian Federation "Kursk", "Bryansk" and "Belgorod" train to identify and fight with sabotage groups, to conduct assault actions in the conditions of urban development, protect and defend objects of critical infrastructure.

"The occupants are conducted, in particular, in the occupied Mariupol and other occupied cities of Ukraine to strengthen the border Russian regions," GUR reports.

The enemy also strengthens, enhances and masks fortifications in Kurshchyna, Belgorod and Bryansk region, tries to increase the number of means of combating drones, HR and air defense there.

In particular, the Kursk region plans to move units of 5 anti-aircraft missile brigade of the enemy, which is now in the occupied territory of Lugansk region.

The engineering units of the Russians increase the geography of mine-explosive barriers in the border areas.

"Among the local population of these areas is increasing the level of dissatisfaction with the actions of municipal officials, power in the Kremlin and Russian propagandists, who recommend the residents of the border to" tolerate "and to immerse in sorrow for killed during the terror act in the Moscow terrorism," Gur reports.
