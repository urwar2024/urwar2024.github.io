On the night of March 30, 2024, the enemy hit the Donetsk region with four anti-aircraft guided missiles C-300/C-400, as well as drones from Cape Chaud (Crimea).

About thisreportedAir Force Commander Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk.

In total, 12 shock UAV-136/131 shock UAVs were launched, nine of them were destroyed by units of mobile fire groups of Defense Forces of Ukraine.

The percussion drones were destroyed. On the limits of Kherson, Odessa, Dnipropetrovsk and Poltava regions.
