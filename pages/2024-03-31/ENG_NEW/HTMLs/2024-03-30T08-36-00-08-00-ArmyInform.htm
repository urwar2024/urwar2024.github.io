Payments to servicemen in 2024 account for 70% of the budget of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

Yuriy Dzhigir, Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine, told about it in exclusiveInterview with Armyinform.

Yuri Dzhigir explained that the budget of the Defense Ministry in 2024 can be divided into three large packages. NATO states are guided by this approach, they categorize their defensive expenditures.

“So the first and largest are cash and combat payments. In 2024(in accordance with the Law of Ukraine "On the State Budget of Ukraine for 2024".—Order.)This allocations in the amount of UAH 862.6 billion has been allocated, which is more than 70% of the IUU budget, ”Yuri Dzigir said.

According to him, this block includes the financial support of the military, payments to the dead, payments for injuries, for staying at the first and second line of combat collision, nutrition of personnel, material support, remuneration for destroyed equipment.

At the same time, Yuri Jigir noted that the dynamic of the situation at the front and the growth of the needs of the Armed Forces of Ukraine should be taken into account.

"In the event of an increase in the intensity of hostilities, an increase in the number of troops, there is a need to attract additional financing, which, accordingly, entails changes in the budget and proper government decisions," the Deputy Minister of Defense said.
