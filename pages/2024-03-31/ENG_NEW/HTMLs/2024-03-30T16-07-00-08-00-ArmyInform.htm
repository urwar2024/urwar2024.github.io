On March 30, 2024, the head of the National Assembly of the French Republic of the French Republic visited the seaport in Odessa, which was repeatedly attacked by Russian missiles and drones.

About thisreportedChairman of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine Ruslan Stefanchuk.

According to him, in the port he and Yael Bron-Powder got acquainted with the functioning of the "grain corridor".

“The routes of civilian vessels to and from the Black Sea ports were discussed in detail, as well as the challenges and danger they have to meet. They discussed the importance of restoration of port infrastructure and its protection against systemic attacks by Russia, ”Ruslan Stefanchuk notes.

It is important, as the speaker emphasized, that Western partners, including France, understand the importance of the functioning of "grain corridors" to ensure food security.

As reported by the Armyinform, in March 2024 Ambassador of the United StatesShe saidMore than a thousand ships have used the Ukrainian corridor in the Black Sea.
