The forces of the South of Ukraine further inflict fire damage to the locations of the enemy, fire positions and rear.

About the loss of the enemy over the past day in this direction is reported inTelegramsThe forces of the South Defense.

Defenders two anti-aircraft missile complexes "Arrow-10", four artillery systems and two mortars.

In total, 137 invaders and 33 units of weapons and military equipment were eliminated, including 12 units of auto-bang, reconnaissance UAVs, 9 aerial photo fixation stations and 3 boats.

The hostile fuel supply and ammunition composition were also destroyed.
