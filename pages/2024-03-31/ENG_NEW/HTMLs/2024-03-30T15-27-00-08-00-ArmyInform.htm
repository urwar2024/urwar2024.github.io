In the Dniprovsky district of Dnipropetrovsk region there was a air defense, successfully shot down the hostile rocket.

About thisreportedEast Air Command.

"At about 2:50 p.m., in the Dnieper district of the Dnipropetrovsk region, a unit of the East Air Command destroyed a Kh-59 guided air missile," the statement said.

The air forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine reported the activity of hostile tactical aviation in the northeast direction.

Due to the threat of the use of aircraft defeat in several areas, they declared air alarm.
