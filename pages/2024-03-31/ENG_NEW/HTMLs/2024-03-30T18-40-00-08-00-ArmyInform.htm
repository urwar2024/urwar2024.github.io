In the Nikopol region of Dnipropetrovsk region, several infrastructure objects, administrative buildings, entertainment center, farm, non -residential two -storey, beaten fountain and a monument have been damaged due to enemy blows.

This is stated inmessageHead of Dnipropetrovsk Regional State Administration (OVA) Sergey Lisak.

“The enemy is in the Nikopol region all day. From the morning - almost two dozen attacks. Terrorizing the area, the invaders used artillery and drones, ”he said.

According to him, Nikopol, Manganese, Pokrovsk and Mirovsk communities were suffering.

“It has gone without the victims. But there are many destruction, ”the head of the Ova said.

In addition to the above destruction, three apartment and 10 private homes, 2 outbuildings, cars were also discharged. The power lines were also affected.

As reported by the Armyinform, in Dnipropetrovsk regionAlready dug42 km of anti -tank ditches and installed 11 thousand tetrahedra.
