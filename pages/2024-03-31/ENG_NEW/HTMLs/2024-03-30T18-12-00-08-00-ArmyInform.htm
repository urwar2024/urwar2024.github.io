Colonel Bogdan Senik, Head of the Public Relations of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, explained the important role of the press and military administration of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.

This is stated inmessageThe General Staff of the Armed Forces.

March 30, 2024 at the Military Institute of Kyiv National University. Taras Shevchenko hosted students of advanced training courses on public information and media cooperation.

During his speech, Bogdan Senik thanked the management and pedagogical staff of the military university for organizing and conducting such events.

He stressed that truthful, objective and operational communication with society is the basis and the core on which the counteraction of destructive information influence of the enemy is based.

“Presofiter is first and foremost a advisor for his external communication commander. He should be the main expert on these issues in his military unit. And for this purpose it is necessary to understand the processes that occur in the information space, to find an understanding with media representatives, to know the regulatory framework, to be able to analyze events and to provide well -being proposals for command, ” - said the Head of Public Relations of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, since February 2024 in Ukraine the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine began to open recruiting centers to mobilize people in the defense forces of Ukraine. As of the end of March 2024, there were already five such centers. Currently to these centersMore than 450 people applied.
