The Cabinet of Ministers amended the order of formation of housing funds intended for temporary residence, accounting and provision of such housing for temporary residence of internally displaced persons.

About thisinformsMinistry of Reintegration.

This resolution aims to provide some IDP categories with additional guarantees to prevent their forced eviction from housing for temporary residence during the period of martial law and within 6 months after its termination or cancellation.

Thus, the internal displaced persons belonging to the vulnerable categories of citizens will not extend the restriction on the term of residence, which is provided for in paragraph eight of Article 9 of the Law of Ukraine "On Ensuring the Rights and Freedoms of Internally Displaced Persons".

Such changes are aimed at strengthening the guarantees of legal and social protection of internally displaced persons.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Government of March 22acceptedThe resolution developed by the Ministry of Social Policy and specified the list of IDPs that will be eligible for payments for another 6 months.
