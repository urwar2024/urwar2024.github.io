At night and morning, the Russians carried out 8 shelling of border areas and settlements of Sumy region. 32 explosions were recorded.

About thisReportedSumy Regional Military Administration.

Myropillia, Krasnopillia, Velyka Pysarivka, Svesa, and Znob-Novhorodske communities were shelled.

The enemy carried out artillery shelling, shelling with the use of UAVs, and also struck with MLRS.

In addition, mortar shelling was recorded in one of the communities.

As reported by the Armyinform, during the day Russian troops442 struck9 settlements of Zaporizhzhya region.
