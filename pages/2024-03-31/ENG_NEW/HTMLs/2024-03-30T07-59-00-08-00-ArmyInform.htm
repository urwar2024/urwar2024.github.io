There are no enemy ships in the Black and Azov Seas in the morning.

This was reported by the Navy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In the Mediterranean there is one enemy ship - the carpet carrier "Caliber", a total volley of up to 8 rockets.

In the interests of the Russian Federation, 20 vessels passed the Kerch Strait during the day, 6 of them moved from the Bosphorus Strait.

17 vessels were passed to the Black Sea, 7 of them continued the movement in the direction of the Bosphorus.

"The Russian Federation continues to violate the International Convention on the Protection of Human Life at the Sea of 1974 (Solas), turning off automatic identification systems (AIS)," the Navy emphasized.
