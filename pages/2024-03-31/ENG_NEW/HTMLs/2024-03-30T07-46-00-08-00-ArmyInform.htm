The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov had a telephone conversation with American colleague Lloyd Austin.

He is about itwroteIn the social network H.

"He provided updated information about recent Russian missile attacks on Ukrainian cities," the minister said.

Rustem Umerov emphasized the importance of providing more air defense systems and missiles for them.

"I am grateful to the Austin Minister for his constant support," he added.

TheSiteAccording to the results of the conversation, the Pentagon published a statement, which is stated, Lloyd Austin discussed with Rustem Umerov the latest events on the battlefield, as well as the devastating influence of ruthless missile attacks by Russia on Ukrainian cities and critical infrastructure.

The ministers have discussed Ukraine's needs for additional artillery, air and other means, as well as the urgent need to adoption the US Congress, which will allow Ukraine to continue the fight against Russian aggression.
