The military of the Russian Federation began to use upgraded fugas aviation bombs for strikes in the frontal regions of Ukraine, including Kharkiv.

About it on airTelelorphoneThe spokesman of the Air Force of Ukraine Major Elijah Yevlash told.

According to him, the Russians are dumped from the FAB-250 UMPB D-30-a modernized fugas bomb, which has a fairly powerful destructive force.

"A special device with a micromotor is attached to it, and all this becomes similar to a rocket to a certain extent. It is launched from the Su-34 or Su-35 and then controlled and can go to a certain target," Ilya Yevlash said.

The spokesman added that the approximate range of such a bomb can reach 70-80 km, but it depends on how close the pilot dares to fly to the line of contact.

Elijah Yevlash stressed that there are more Western Air Defense systems in the regions to effectively confront such threats.
