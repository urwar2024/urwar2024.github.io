Residents of Volyn and Poltava region spent 8 thousand euros, but could not illegally cross the state border.

About thisShe reported March 23State Border Service of Ukraine.

According to the department, the border outfit during the patrolling on the section of the department found a movement towards the neighboring Romania of three people.

The border guards caught up with the violators and detained them 50 meters from the government.

During further measures, the operative staff of the Chernivtsi Border Detachment found that residents of Rivne and Poltava regions paid 8 thousand euros to the locals for developing and indicating the route of the influx to Romania bypassing the checkpoints.

The detainees of administrative offenses have been drawn up on detainees.

The search for the organizers of the illegal scheme is ongoing.

Asreported the armyinform, in Zaporozhye, MSEC officials earned $ 1.2 million on help for evidence.
