Due to the security situation in three settlements of Sumy region, a decision was made to evacuate children in a compulsory way.

About thisreportedIn the Ministry of Reintegration.

The corresponding decision was agreed by members of the coordination headquarters on compulsory evacuation of the population in the conditions of martial law.

Children, along with their parents or other legal representatives, will be evacuated to safe placement.

In total, it is planned to evacuate 54 children from three settlements of Shostka district to different regions, who expressed their readiness to accept them.

Evacuation is free of charge. For more information, you can contact the Sumy Regional Contact Center hotline:

  • (0542) 66-33-77 (free for residents of Sumy and Sumy district),
  • 0-800-301-501 (free for residents of Sumy region).

Currently, the hotline runs daily from 8:30 to 5:30 pm.

You can also report to the desire to be evacuated on the hotline of the Ministry of Reintegration 15-48 or write in the messengers of WhatsApp/Telegram/Viber to the number: (096) 078-84-33.

As reported by the Armyinform, during the last dayrecorded257 hostile blows in the territory of Sumy region.
