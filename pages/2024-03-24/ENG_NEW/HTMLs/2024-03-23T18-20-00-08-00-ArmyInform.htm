He tried to go abroad by marrying a woman with a disability.

About thisShe reported March 23State Border Service of Ukraine.

According to the department, the border guards of the Belgorod-Dnestrovsky detachment at the checkpoint "Starokozacha" were discovered abroad.

A 52-year-old man and a 47-year-old woman who has a disability was married on Wednesday, March 20, in Odessa.

In such circumstances, the man can cross the border, so the newlyweds decided not to postpone their wedding journey and went to the border with Moldova a day after the marriage.

Already at the checkpoint, the couple told the border guards that they have known for a long time and their love from their childhood.

However, the stories of wife and husband were very different. Even the place of cohabitation did not coincide.

It turned out that they knew each other badly, and they got acquainted, rather shortly before their wedding, if not in Rats.

In addition, the border guards found that last year the Odessa was already married and left her ex-lover abroad in Moldova, but he never returned to Ukraine.

As a result, the husband was refused missing across the border. His bride also decided to go back.

On the fact of detection of signs of a crime under Art. 332 of the Criminal Code "Illegal Crossing of Persons across the State Border of Ukraine", border guards sent a message to the National Police.

Asreported the armyinform, in Transcarpathia, the carrier of the evaders was assigned a pledge in a quarter of a million.
