At night and morning, the Russians carried out 5 shelling of border areas and settlements of Sumy region.

About thisreportedSumy Ova.

The shelling was suffered by Velikopysariv, Vorozhbian and Khotinsk communities. 35 explosions were recorded.

In the Velikopysarevsky community, the enemy hit the RSSV (7 explosions) and artillery (19 explosions).

The Vorozhbian community suffered from the Kabami Air Force (3 explosions). In the Hotin community, Artobstil (6 explosions) were recorded.

As reported by the Armyinform, overnight enemyattackedNikopol district of Dnipropetrovsk region, 3 UAVs and 2 shelling were recorded.
