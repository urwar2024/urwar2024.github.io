The UK Ministry of Defense has announced a new military assistance package to Ukraine, which includes air defense systems.

This is said inmessageOffice in the social network H.

"A new $ 60 million military support package from the International Fund for Ukraine, administered in the United Kingdom, will provide new modern drones to support the Armed Forces of Ukraine," the department said.

It will also include £ 20 million in air defense systems that will help protect the sky of Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy stated that Russian terror is now possible only because Ukraine does not have enough modern air defense systems, that is, political will to provide them.

According to him, all partners know what it is necessary and who can make truly rescue decisions.
