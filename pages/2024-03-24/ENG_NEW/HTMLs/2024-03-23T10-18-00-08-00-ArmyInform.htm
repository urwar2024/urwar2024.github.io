In the Zaporizhzhya direction, the Russians are actively involved in the night FPV-pions, dumping from drones poisonous substances, and also try to find weaknesses in the defense of Ukrainian positions.

About it on airArmy TVThe officer of the press service of the 128th Separate Mountain Assault Transcarpathian Brigade Major Yaroslav Galas told.

According to the officer, active assaults using a large number of armored vehicles for the last four days.

“A few weeks ago, up to 10 units or more were used by Russians, they tried to break through our defense. This is not. Artillery shelling is currently underway, managed aviation bombs are being used, and the FPV-a-kirds is undergoing considerable use of nighttime, ”he said.

Yaroslav Galas said that in recent days there were the efforts of the Russians in small storm groups to soak weaknesses in defense in those areas that are considered relatively calm.

"Before dawn, three or four stormtroopers tried to approach our positions as much as possible, but were either rejected by our fire or destroyed," the military said.

Also, according to him, the Russians began to use poisonous substances - gases of tearful, dropping them from drones. Such substances cause respiratory edema, complicated breathing.

“I spoke with our combat doctors - such cases have been recorded repeatedly. Fortunately, there were no critical consequences, but for a while they managed to disable mortar calculation or infantry group, ”Yaroslav Galas explained.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Orikhiv direction the occupiers 10 times, with the support of aviation,attackedThe positions of our defenders in the districts of Settlements of Staromayorsk, south of Novodarivka of Donetsk region and near the Robozhny in Zaporozhye.
