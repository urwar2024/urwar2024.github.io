On the night of March 23, 2024, the enemy attacked Ukraine with C-300 rockets and percussion drones.

About thisreportedCommander of the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk.

The enemy used four anti-aircraft managed missiles C-300 (in Donetsk region) and 34 shock UAVs of the Shahed UAV (Kursk Region-Russian Region, Cape Chauda-Crimea).

As a result of the airfare combat, mobile fire groups of defense, anti -aircraft missile units and air forces were destroyed by 31 Shahaned.

The air purposes were destroyed within the framework of Poltava, Mykolaiv, Dnipropetrovsk, Zaporizhzhya and Kherson regions.

As reported by the Armyinform, during the last day69 combat clashes occurred. In total, the enemy inflicted 88 rocket launchers and 143 aircraft strikes, and made 92 shelling from rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.
