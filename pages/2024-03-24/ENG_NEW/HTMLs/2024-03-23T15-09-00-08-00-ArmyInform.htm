In total, 297 mine demining groups continue to clean up the liberated regions of Ukraine from explosive residues of war.

About thisreported March 23Ministry of Defence Ukraine.

Over the last week (from 15 to March 22, 2024) was examined and cleared:

- 4 766,52 ha of terrain;
- 4 700.3 hectares of agricultural land;
- 11.93 km of highways;
- 0.78 km of pipelines.

The works are carried out with the involvement of special equipment for mechanized demining.

In total, 320,700 explosive residues of war were discovered and destroyed by the sappers of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.
