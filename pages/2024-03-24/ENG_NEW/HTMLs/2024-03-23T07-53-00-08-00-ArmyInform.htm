There were 69 combat clashes during the last day. In total, the enemy inflicted 88 rocket launchers and 143 aircraft strikes, and made 92 shelling from rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its Facebook page.

In Volyn and Polissya directionsThe operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy maintains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent our troops to be transferred to threatening directions.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy, with the support of aviation, carried out 3 unsuccessful assaults of the positions of defense forces in the areas of settlements Sinkivka, Tabaivka and Berestovo of Kharkiv region.

In the estuary directionOur soldiers repelled 7 enemy attacks in the areas of Bigorivka settlements of Luhansk region and controversial in Donetsk, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionOur warriors reflected 5 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Ivanivske, Andreevka and Khishchiyivka of Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 10 enemy attacks in the areas of Berdychi, Toney, Pervomaisk and Nevelskoye.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of settlements Krasnogorivka, Georgiyivka, Novomikhailivka, Vodyane and Urozhizhne, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops 28 times.

In the Orikhiv directionThe invaders 10 times, with the support of aviation, attacked the positions of our defenders in the areas of Settlements of Staromayorsk, south of Novodarivka of Donetsk region and near the Robovy in Zaporozhye.

In the Kherson directionThe enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. Thus, during the last day, 2 storms of positions of Ukrainian troops have been carried out.

At the same time, our warriors continue to be active in the occupation troops of losses in living strength and technology, depleting the enemy along the entire front line.
