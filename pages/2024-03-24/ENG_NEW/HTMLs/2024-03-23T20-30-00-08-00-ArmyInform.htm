Military psychologists, financiers, translators, international relations experts, as well as military journalists completed their studies, despite the fact that in February 2022 were forced to interrupt the educational process and join Ukraine's protection.

About thisreported March 23General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

The masters were released at the Military Institute of Kyiv National University. Taras Shevchenko.

Some of the cadets gave their lives in battles against the invaders, most of them returned to training as soon as the opportunity appeared.

AsThe ArmyInform reported earlier, at the National Academy of Land Forces named after Hetman Peter Sahaidachny, they will be inspected after a number of complaints.
