The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov thanked Denmark for the announced 16th large package of military assistance worth $ 330 million and for the preparation of Ukrainian pilots on F-16 fighters.

According to the correspondent of the Armyinform, the Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov emphasized this during the Danish-Ukrainian Conference of Defense Industry in Kiev.

“The last Denmark military aid package of $ 330 million is necessary for our ability to continue to reflect Russian aggression. Thank you very much for this support, ”Rustem Umerov said.

He stressed that Denmark helps Ukraine in many areas, provided valuable artillery and ammunition.

“Your country helps train Ukrainian pilots on the F-16. As it was said earlier: "The Ukrainian sky should belong to the Ukrainian people," and your country helps us in this, "the Ministry of Defense head said.

Rustem Umerov recalled that at the beginning of the war Denmark gave Ukraine anti -ship missiles.

"This decision has strengthened our ability to limit the Russian Navy, and gave us impetus to develop our own systems, such as sea drones," he added.

The head of the Ukrainian defense department also emphasized on a bilateral security agreement, which, in his opinion, was a clear strategic choice of Denmark in support of Ukraine.

"We are grateful to you for this, because Russian aggression against Ukraine is an existential threat not only for Ukraine but also a threat to all democratic states," the Minister of Defense of Ukraine said.
