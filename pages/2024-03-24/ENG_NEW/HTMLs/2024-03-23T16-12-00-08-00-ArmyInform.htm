On March 22, 2024, two explosions took place in the territory of the temporarily occupied Melitopol.

About thisreported March 23Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

As the message said: "As a result of the special action of the GUR of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine and the local movement of the resistance of successful fire damage, the group of Russian invaders and their military equipment was caused."

According to preliminary data, there were about 20 Russian invaders at the epicenter of the explosions, two tetled KAMAZ trucks and Patrio UAZ.

The exact losses of the enemy are specified.

Asreported the armyinform, GUR declared Moscow's attempt to discredit Ukraine in the Middle East.
