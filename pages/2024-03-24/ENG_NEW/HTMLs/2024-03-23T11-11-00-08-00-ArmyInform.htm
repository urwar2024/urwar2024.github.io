President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said that on Saturday he received a report of a military command on the situation at the front.

This is said inmessageIn the channel telegram.

“On the front is stable. I am grateful to all soldiers and commanders for preserving our positions and the necessary actions, ”the President said.

The Head of State noted that he received reports on restoration work in our areas after the Russian strike on Friday: reports from the Ministry of Internal Affairs, Energy, and Regions.

“Work continuously everywhere. I thank every repair team, all employees of energy companies, our rescuers from the SES of Ukraine, all of the municipal services involved, ”he said.

The president added that the most difficult situation in Kharkiv. Connection to household consumers continues. The pace is maximum of the possible now.

In Dnipropetrovsk, the whole area is connected, except for part of consumers in Nikopol.

«Sumy region, Poltava region, Odessa, Vinnytsia, Frankivshchyna, Khmelnytskyi, Mykolaiv and Zaporizhzhia - in fact, the technical possibility of supplying electricity has been restored everywhere. There are emergency schedules in some communities, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.
