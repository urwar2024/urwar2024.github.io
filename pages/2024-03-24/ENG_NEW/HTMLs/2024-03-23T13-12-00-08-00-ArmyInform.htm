Already, a fourth hundred military doctors have gone for psychological rehabilitation in Sweden as part of the Repower Charitable Foundation.

About thisreportedin the command of the medical forces of Ukraine.

The RePower Charitable Foundation is a charity fund whose activities are focused on working with military physicians who serve in the war zone.

“You have done a lot and deserve to switch the focus a little, spend time with benefit and restart. We have a lot of work ahead! Victory - we will only be able to reach us and we will only be able to unite around common goals, ”says Colonel of Medical Service Yuriy Podolian, Deputy Commander of the Armed Forces of Ukraine of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

RePower organizes initial measures aimed at physical and psychological recovery and prevention of emotional burnout and exhaustion.

For two weeks, participants will hold:

  • group and individual psychological sessions
  • Body-oriented practices
  • cultural and educational activities,

In addition, there will be an exchange of experience with representatives of the Swedish Armed Forces.

In the command of the medical forces of the Armed Forces, they also thanked the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine for trust and cooperation.

As the Armyinform Wrote,persons who were in Russian captivity,will pass the full cycle of recovery and rehabilitation,It is developed according to Western methods in conjunction with foreign specialists.
