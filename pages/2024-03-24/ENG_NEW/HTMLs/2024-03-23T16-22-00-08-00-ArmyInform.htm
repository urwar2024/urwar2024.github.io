The forces of the South of Ukraine continue to inflict fire damage to the enemy, fire positions and rear.

Statistics of hostile losses over the past day are given inTelegrams.

During the day, the Defense force was confirmed that in the enemy in the Orikhiv and Kherson directions, less than 152 invaders and 73 units of weapons and military equipment.

In particular, 5 tanks, 13 guns, 8 mortars and 37 units of auto-brontechnics were destroyed.

In addition, 2 radar stations, 2 MUR-M video surveillance complexes, 2 video surveillance cameras, a set of antenna UAV control systems, aerial photograph and boat were affected.

Three more hostile felds of ammunition were destroyed.
