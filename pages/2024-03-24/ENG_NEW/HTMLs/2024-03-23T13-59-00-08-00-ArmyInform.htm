In Kiev, a memorial of the memory "Eternal Glory to the dead heroes" was opened. He was dedicated to the fallen servicemen of the 25th Brigade of Public Order named after Prince Askold of the Northern Association of the National Guard of Ukraine.

About thisinformsNational Guard of Ukraine.

“The Command of the National Guard of Ukraine remembers anyone who has given their lives for Ukraine and bows their heads to the families of the dead. This is a common loss for us. We will always support the families of the victims because we are one common family, ”said Colonel Valentin Ostryna, a temporarily acting deputy commander to work with personnel.

The attendees honored the memory of the dead moment of silence and laid flowers at the memorial.

After the grand opening, Colonel Valentin Ostizh presented his close state and departmental awards.

The solemn events were attended by the National Guard Command, representatives of the Kyiv City State Administration, Solomenskaya Regional State Administration, relatives, friends and brothers of fallen servicemen, veterans.
