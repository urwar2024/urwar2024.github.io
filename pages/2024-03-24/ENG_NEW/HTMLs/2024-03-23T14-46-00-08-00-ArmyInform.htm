On Saturday in the afternoon, the Russians launched a ballistic missile in the Odessa region, probably Iskander-M.

Information on destruction and victims did not receive.

It is also reported that the enemy continues active unmanned reconnaissance from the sea. The defense of the South of Ukraine over the Black Sea destroyed two Zala reconnaissance drones.
