Medical units of the Armed Forces of Ukraine were entitled to use narcotic painkillers.

About thisreportedCommand of the medical forces of the Armed Forces.

It is noted that the procedure for licensing medical units to work with controlled substances is simplified.

The medical unit can now obtain a license within a few working days.

“Medical units have also been granted the right to store controlled substances in civilian hospitals closest to the place of their dislocation. Provided that those medical institutions meet the requirements of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine and the Ministry of Health of Ukraine for the storage of controlled substances, ”the medical forces report.

The command emphasized that "our defenders will now receive anesthesia on time and freely."

It is noted that the changes were made possible by the work of the medical forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine together with the State Service of Ukraine for Drugs and Drug Control, the Ministry of Health of Ukraine and the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine in February announced that he would cooperate with the State Service of Ukraine for Medicinal Products and Drug Control.
