Retraining courses are available to the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine and Sergeant, who have higher medical or pharmaceutical education and certificate of specialist doctor up to 55 years.

About thisreported March 23Ministry of Defence Ukraine.

The entry procedure provides a report to the commander of the military unit, as well as sending a copy of the report to doz@mod.gov.ua.

The courses will be held at the Ukrainian Military Medical Academy, where servicemen will be left on a call in case of enrollment.

The possibility of contracting is not obligatory, but is considered at the request of each serviceman.

After successful completion of the courses and the availability of vacant officers of the medical service, servicemen have the opportunity to get attitude from the commander of the military unit and return to their part.

Asreported the armyinform, The Ministry of Defense implements a new system of training for mobilized doctors, which allows to obtain an officer rank in 40 days.
