In the morning of March 23, Russian servicemen fired at the village. Vovchansky farms. A 72-year-old man was killed, and dwellings were damaged.

About thisreportedOffice of the Prosecutor General.

Previously, the invaders fired from the artillery of the village. Glushkivka of Kupyansky district. Private households were damaged.

In the evening, Kupjiansk was under the blow. Previously, there was an explosion due to the discharges of ammunition from the FPV-aroma. Private apartment building was damaged.

Pre -trial investigations on the facts of violation of the laws and customs of war (Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine) have been initiated.
