According to the results of repatriation measures, 121 of the fallen defender was returned to Ukraine.

This was announced by the coordination headquarters on the management of prisoners of war.

In particular, 12 bodies of defenders who fought in the Luhansk direction, 107 fallen soldiers from Donetsk and two defenders from the Zaporozhye direction were repatched.

The Armed Forces of Ukraine will transport repatriated bodies and remains to certain government agencies. Law enforcement officials and forensic experts will identify the dead.

After the identification of the bodies of our dead defenders, they will be transferred to families for a decent burial.

The fallen was returned due to the interaction of the coordination staff on the management of prisoners of war with the United SBU Center, the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine, the Secretariat of the Commissioner for Persons of missing persons in special circumstances, the Armed Forces of Ukraine (CVS CVS), the State Emergency Service of Ukraine, other representatives Defense of Ukraine.

The International Committee of the Red Cross also contributed to its return.
