Five Russian Air - Earth Aviation missiles, which did not work during the launch, were identified and destroyed by employees of the Sumy Region Police Explosive Service.

About thisShe reported March 29National Police of Ukraine.

According to the Head of the Department of Explosive Service of the National Police in Sumy Oblast Igor Chernyak, these are unmanaged C-8 aviation missiles, which were developed in 1955 and in several modifications are in service with Russia to this day.

“They are armed planes and shock helicopters. During one of the hostile aviation strikes in our territory, some of them did not work and stuck in the soil, ”said Igor Chernyak.

Police are reminiscent of citizens: in the case of detection of non -broken artillery shells, mines, grenades, etc., in any case, do not touch them, try to move from the place or disposal independently.

Mark the location of the find, take a safe distance, more than 100 meters, and call the police by phone 102 or SES at 101.

Asreported the armyinform, in a day the enemy fired at Sumy 28 times.
