At about 5:30 am, on March 29, the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation attacked the objects of critical infrastructure in Chernivtsi region.

About thisreportedOffice of the Prosecutor General.

The attack was damaged by non -residential premises and structures that were at the epicenter of the fall of debris.

Emergency services and law enforcement officers work at the scene.

The pre -trial investigation into the violation of the laws and customs of war (Part 1 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine) was started.

Asreported the armyinform, in the Donetsk region rescuers got out of the rubble the body of the dead as a result of a Russian blow.
