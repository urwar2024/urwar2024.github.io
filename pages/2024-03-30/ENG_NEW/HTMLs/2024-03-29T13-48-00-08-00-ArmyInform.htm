Flags of those brigades, battalions and units of defense forces that defend the Donetsk region from the Russian occupier were raised at the entrance to the entrance to the Donetsk region.

About thisMarch 29 reportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces.

Stella at the entrance to the Donetsk region has a Ukrainian national element - a mosaic embroidery and is inherent in this region only.

For example, coat of arms: in a gold field on a black curved blue palm tree. The shield is crowned with a golden stylized crown of chestnut leaves, and frames a wreath of green oak branches, cut with a purple ribbon with the motto: "The opportunity is proven in practice."

Once in 1896, Koval Alexei Mertsalov made a palm tree from a rail. Palma Mertsalova points to the economic potential of the region, which is associated with coal, etc. And yellow embodies the hot steppe climate.

On the letters on the ceiling, you can see many inscriptions and blue-yellow ribbons. These are personal autographs of fighters and even a message - an aggressor that went to the direction where the Russian ship was sent ...

Most military, volunteers, doctors, chaplains who go to the war area or leave there after completing the tasks, are readily photographed here.

The fighters of one of the units restored the foundation near the ceiling, cleared the place of garbage, welded and painted metal poles for flags.
