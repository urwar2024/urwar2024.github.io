Volodymyr Zelenskyy warned that Russian blows to Ukrainian energy can lead to environmental disasters.

The President stated this on March 29 in his address.

“He spent the bet today - in many respects the consequences of Russian blows last night and these weeks. The main target for the enemy now in rocket terror is energy, different parts of our power system. Thought in Russian blows, including hydropower, ”said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

According to him, all aspects of protecting objects, corresponding in Ukraine and with partners, discussed on the raft. Because it is important not only for Ukraine, but also for neighbors, and the continent in general.

“There were blows at the Dniester Station, at the Kaniv Station, and this apparently shows that Russia wants to repeat such a catastrophe, which they arranged at the Kakhovskaya Station. It should be a common task - not only Ukrainian - so that such environmental disasters are no longer in our Europe, ”the President said.

According to him, on the diplomatic front, Ukraine spends a lot of effort not only to counteract Russian influences, but also to persuade partners to increase the supply of air defense systems and missiles.

“The second question on the rate is the supply of weapons and shells for the front, for our actions now and in the coming months. We clearly understand what Russia is preparing. They are sick with war - they will not stop themselves. And we do everything to be able to counter enough, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.
