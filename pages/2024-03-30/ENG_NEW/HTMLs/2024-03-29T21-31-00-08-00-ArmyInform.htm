Of these, 101 are about violence against men.

Such figuresWith reference to the Attorney General's Office, were published in the documentary-investigation "Investigation.Info", which tells about Russian torture in Kherson.

Executive director Anna Babinets confessed at the presentation that the film became the worst of everything she had previously worked as a co -author.

«The theme of sexual violence is tabooed by society, so I am sincerely grateful to men who told this story. Not only in Ukraine but also in the world must be known about the crimes committed»,- said Anna Babinets.

One of the heroes of the film, the Orthodox priest Sergey Chudinovich explained that he sought to encourage others to speak about the violence experienced.

“I saw a video of people who were captured by the Russians and then carefully bypassed the topic. This creates the wrong information background: well, I was captured, well, they talked ... All this is a deception. And against deception, one must stand and tell the truth at the level you see and understand it, ”said Sergey Chudinovich.

According to the Deputy Head of the Department of International Legal Cooperation of the Prosecutor General Irina Didenko, the whole array of the assembled evidence proves that violence against civilians is systemic.

“We managed to determine who organized the work of the torture in Kherson. This is the whole organization, system, not emotions. When we started exploring the torturers in the occupied territories and in Russia - it turned out that there were special rooms for torture, torture. Therefore, the office of the Prosecutor General continues to prove the involvement of the entire vertical of the Russian authorities. And bringing to justice through international instances of the Supreme Management of the Russian Federation, ”Irina Didenko said.
