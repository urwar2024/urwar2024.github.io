Counterintelligence of the Security Service prevented the new attempts by the aggressor to cause fire damage to the places of defense forces in Sumy region.

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

"As a result of complex measures, the FSB agent was detained, which prepared a series of air strikes in the region, including with the use of managed airbags," the statement said.

As it is noted, the main goals of the enemy were the defensive positions of the Armed Forces in the 5-kilometer zone near the state border of Ukraine. In order to complete the task, the person involved in the locations of the Ukrainian troops, mark their coordinates on the map and send the appropriate "report" to the occupiers.

“First of all, she tried to identify the supporting fortifications and establish the location of the defense units. To do this, the Russian agent under the guise of an average citizen bypassed the border area and hiddenly recorded the "necessary" objects on the camera of their own phone, "the SBU said.

The department noted that the SBU staff documented the criminal acts of the person and detained it on hot when she tried to make a new intelligence.

At the same time, complex measures were carried out to protect personnel, weapons and ammunition of Ukrainian troops in the region.

According to the investigation, a local resident, an ideological supporter of Rashism, was an enemy hostile. After the beginning of a large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation, she expressed anti -Ukrainian narratives in Russian social networks.

So she got into the field of view of the FSB and was subsequently published by the hostile special service.

The investigation found that at first the traitor was spying for the invaders in the border area where she lived. But after his shelling, the woman, along with her fellow villagers, was evacuated to another settlement of Sumy region.

There she took shape as a migrant and planned to collect intelligence on the FSB forces of defense on the task of the FSB.

During the search, the mobile phones she used to communicate with the Russian curator were removed.

“Currently, investigators of the Security Service have informed her suspicion under Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (State betrayal committed in the conditions of martial law). The malefactor is in custody. She is threatened with life imprisonment, ”the message reads.

The operational and investigative activities were carried out by SBU staff in Sumy region under the procedural guidance of the regional prosecutor's office.

As reported by the Armyinform, to the court365 persons have been transferredwho are suspected of committing war crimes.
