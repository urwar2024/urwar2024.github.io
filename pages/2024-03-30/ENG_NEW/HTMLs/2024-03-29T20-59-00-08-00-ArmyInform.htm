A criminal group of three locals caught panels from the territory of power plants located near the front line.

About thisreported March 29Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, one of the suspects developed a scheme of "fast" enrichment of property of one of the energy generating enterprises, which was left by the owners through a full -scale invasion of the Russian Federation and placement near the Defense Line of Zaporizhzhya region.

Realizing that due to active fighting in the Vasyliv district, solar panels, which are located in the territory of two power plants near Stepnogorsk, were left without protection and proper supervision, decided to steal them.

He attracted two acquaintances to the implementation of the criminal plan, who proposed the direct dismantling of energy equipment. He also put an observer function on the surrounding area in order to prevent exposure.

In September-November 2023, the members of the group managed to dismantle and steal more than 350 solar panels worth more than UAH 1.2 million.

Now all three have been declared suspicion.

Asreported the armyinform, in Britain there was a theft of property for the Ukrainian military.
