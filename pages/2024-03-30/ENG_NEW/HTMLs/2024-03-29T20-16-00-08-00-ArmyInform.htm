The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov in Kyiv met with the head of the French National Assembly of Yael Bron-Pow.

About thisreported March 29Ministry of Defence Ukraine.

The minister thanked the comprehensive support that France provides to Ukraine, in particular for signing a bilateral security agreement.

"I support views on the ways of repeling Russian aggression against Ukraine, which was recently expressed by French President Emmanuel Macron," Rustem Umerov said.

He noted that he appreciates fruitful cooperation with his French colleague Sebastian Lecorton, who is in close contact.

The Ukrainian delegation introduced French partners to the current situation at the front, the urgent needs of the Ukrainian military, including air defense and artillery ammunition.

Rustem Umerov noted that Ukraine is ready to co -invest in French defense technologies and to buy French products directly.

"I am convinced that the capacity of the Ukrainian defense industry should be included in the European Military Production Plan," he said.

As reported by the Armyinform, nearly 10,000 Ukrainian soldiers have been trained in France.
