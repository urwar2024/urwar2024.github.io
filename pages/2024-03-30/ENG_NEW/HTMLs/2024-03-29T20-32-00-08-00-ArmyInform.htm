The hostile rocket was discovered in the forest near one of the villages of Sumy region. It did not explode during the fall, but received damage to the body and lost the engine.

About thisreported March 29National Police Department in Sumy Oblast.

The rocket was found by a community police officer Ivan Kovalenko, who patrolled the community territory and noticed an oblong object of gray at the edge of the forest. Once the officer came closer, the officer realized that he found a Russian rocket. He photographed the find and reported it to line 102.

Therefore, the employees of the explosive service department, having gone on departure, already understood that this time they would deal with an aviation missile X-59 "Ovod". This rocket of the Air - Earth class, about seven meters long, weighs 930 kilograms and has a powerful combat part weighing more than 280 kilograms. When hitting the target - causes serious destruction and death of people. Such weapons, in case of detection, are destroyed by air defense systems in flight, but if it has not exploded during a fall, it is neutralized by sappers at the site of detection.

The mobile group of police sappers of the SUNP Explosive Service in Sumy Oblast, inspecting the rocket and its parts, remotely separated the combat part from the body, and then made it destroying it by means of an overhead charge of the explosion near the location.

Dry grass on the forest near the field was taken from the explosion, so the police also had to extinguish the fire so that the flames did not spill into the forest.

The police remind that in the event of any ammunition, suspicious objects similar to ammunition or their remains, especially bombs, unmanned aerial vehicles of different types, missiles or their remains, in any case do not approach them, do not touch them, do not touch them They do not conduct any manipulations with them.

There is evidence that the enemy equips some of his weapons, which he uses against Ukrainians with special devices of delayed action, which can work even after a few hours or even after falling.

The first thing to do in the case of finding any explosive object is to move away from the find at least one hundred meters and to inform the authorities of the authorities, the military or the police, calling on line 102. Police sappers will arrive at the place and neutralize the found explosive object .
