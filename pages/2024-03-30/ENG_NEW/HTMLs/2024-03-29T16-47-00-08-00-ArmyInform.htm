Lithuanian President Gitanas Nauseda has called for the goal of helping Ukraine to win the war with the Russian Federation, celebrating the 20th anniversary of the country's accession to NATO.

He is about itwroteIn the social network H.

First of all, Gitanas Nauseda believes that the decision to join NATO has become the best in the recent history of Lithuania.

“Which Lithuanian solution was the best in recent history? It was a decision to join NATO. 20 years ago, Lithuania decided to become part of the Western Collective Safety System. Today we are single and stronger than ever. Today, our goal should be to help Ukraine win, ”the president of Lithuania wrote.

As reported by the Armyinform, the United States Ambassador Bridget Brinka againcalledTo strengthen assistance to Ukraine against the background of the next rocket attack of the Russian Federation.
