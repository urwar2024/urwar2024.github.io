On the night of March 29, the enemy struck a powerful rocket and audience in the objects of the fuel and energy sector of Ukraine, applying different types of missiles and strikes. In total, the enemy involved 99 air assaults, of which 84 were destroyed by Ukrainian defenders of the sky.

About thisreportedAir Force Commander Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk.

According to available information, the enemy applied:

  • 60 shock UAVs type ShahED-136/131 (the area of the Primorsko-Akhtarsk, Kursk region -RF);
  • 3 aerobalist missiles X-47M2 "Dagger" (from three MiG-31K fighters-Ryazan region -rf);
  • 2 Iskander-M ballistic missiles (Crimea);
  • 9 controlled Aviation missiles X-59 (from nine Su-34 aircraft-Belgorod region);
  • 4 Winged Iskander -K missiles (Kursk region -rf);
  • 21 Winged Rocket X-101/X-555 (from eleven strategic TU-95MC bombers-Engels -rf)

"Air Force and Land Forces, mobile fire groups, means of radio electronic struggle of the Defense Forces of Ukraine were involved in the reflection of rocket and aviation strike," Mykola Oleshchuk said.

According to the commander, as a result of the air combat, 84 air purposes were destroyed:

  • 58 SHAHED-136/131 shock UAVs;
  • 17 winged missiles X-101/X-555;
  • 5 managed aviation missiles X-59;
  • 4 Winged Iskander-K missiles

“Honor and praise to all who protect Ukraine from Russian terror. Thank you for your combat work, thank you for the result! ” -said Lieutenant General.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Dnipropetrovsk region the power of air defenseDestroyed24 Air Objectives.
