The Ministry of Veterans work on the creation of an all -Ukrainian electronic map of burial places of fallen defenders and defenders.

About thisreported March 29 CabinetWith reference to the acting Minister of Veterans of Ukraine Oleksandr Pazhun.

“Currently, the National Military Memorial Cemetery is developing a very powerful site where the burial map will be made directly at the memorial cemetery. In addition, we have ambitious plans to make such a map of burials throughout our country. However, it is this project that will be open to the public after our victory, ”said Alexander Pryhun.

The map developed by the state institution will place a brief biography about every defender and defender who gave their lives for Ukraine and are buried at the NVMC.

Asreported the armyinform, the first burials at the National Military Memorial Cemetery can take place by the fall.
