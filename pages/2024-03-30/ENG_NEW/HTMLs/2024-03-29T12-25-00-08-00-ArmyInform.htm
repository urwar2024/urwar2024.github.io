Romania found fragments similar to the elements of the drone, near the Danube River and the border with Ukraine.

This is stated in the statement of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

"The Ministry of Defense, together with specialized structures on Friday, March 29, 2024, conducts special operational procedures for investigating the event that happened," the statement said.

The wreckage was found on agricultural lands on the island of Great Breil.

According to Romanian media, the wreckage was found 12 km from Breil. The fall of the drone shortly after midnight, was accompanied by a loud blow.

Previously, at least five incidents were recorded with the fall of drones in the Romanian territory. Drones attacked Ukrainian ports on the Danube.
