To collect information, analyze it and propose decisions will be the SBU, the State Secretariat, Ministry of Defense and Secretary of the National Security and Defense Council.

About the online casino Volodymyr Zelenskyyspoke in his evening address March 29.

“Today, very quickly gathered the necessary votes of the petition on the subject of casino-online platforms of gambling. About how it is widespread and how it affects part of society, ”the President said.

He noted that next week he was waiting for the proposal to resolve this issue.

The petition on the President's website on the restriction of online casino in the south gained the required number of signatures.

Asreported the armyinform, Kharkiv, who collected "donate" for the military of the Armed Forces and then spent them on online casinos, will be tried for fraud.
