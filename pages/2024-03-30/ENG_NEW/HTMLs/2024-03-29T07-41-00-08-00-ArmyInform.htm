Over the past day, the occupiers have made 386 beats on 9 settlements of Zaporizhzhya region.

About thisreportedHead of Zaporizhzhya Ova Ivan Fedorov.

Eight aviation strikes were carried out by Komyshivas, Malynivtsi, Small Tokmachka and Robot.

27 UAV attacked Guliaypole, Levadne, robot, small Tokmachka, Malinovka and railway.

5 RSZV-laps came across a small tokmachka, robot and Novoandriivka.

Also, 346 artudars were inflicted on the territory of Guliaypol, small tookmachka, robot, Novoandriivka, Malynivka, Levadny and Chervonodniprovka.

The 67-year-old man was injured as a result of the fall of UAV in the Pologovsky district.

There were 56 reports of destruction of residential buildings and infrastructure.
