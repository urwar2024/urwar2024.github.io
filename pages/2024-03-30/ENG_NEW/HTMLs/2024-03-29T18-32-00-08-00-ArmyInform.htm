Another release on the training of military chaplains took place. The solemn event was held at the Sofia Kyivska National Reserve.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

As it is noted, within eight weeks 29 military chaplains of the Armed Forces of Ukraine were held all over the city and professional training.

“This is the fifth issue of a set of advanced training courses on the military chaplaincy of the Taras Shevchenko Military Chaplains of the Military Chaplains of the Taras Shevchenko KNU. 147 servicemen of the Armed Forces of Ukraine have already been trained under this program. In total, 259 military chaplains were assigned to the Armed Forces, ”the department said.

The Ministry of Defense added that the next set of military chaplains will also be held from the personnel of the GUR MO and the State Special Transport Service.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Chaplaincy Service of the Land ForcespresentedA new manager.
