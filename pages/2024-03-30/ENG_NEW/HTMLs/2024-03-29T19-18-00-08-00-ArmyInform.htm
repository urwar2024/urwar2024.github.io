The court of the case of 15 defectors is expected, 5 more traitors have been informed of suspicions.

About thisreported March 29State Bureau of Investigation.

According to the materials of the DBR, the head of the police of the temporarily occupied bare pier was convicted in Kherson region under the article collaboration (Part 7 of Article 111-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine). He was imposed in the form of imprisonment for a term of 15 years with confiscation of property.

Five former rescuers from the Luhansk region, four employees of a detention center in Donetsk, a former law enforcement officer from Mikhailivka, Zaporizhzhya region, expert enforcement officer from Vovchansk in Kharkiv, two former law enforcement officers from the occupied Starobyl region, have been sent to the court.

In collaborative activity (Part 5, Part 7 of Article 111-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine), an ex-law enforcement officer from Kherson and the former forester of SE "Kupyansky Forestry" are accused. The indictments are sent to court.

Suspicion of the State Trease (Part 2 of Article 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine) was informed by the former Ukrainian military, two expreces to the employees of SE "Melitopol Forestry" and two former employees of Volnovakha's tax.

The DBR reminds that the sanctions of articles of state treason and collaboration are imprisonment for a lifetime with confiscation of property.

Asreported the armyinform, two citizens of Ukraine were informed about suspicion of assistance in deportation to Russia 15 Ukrainian children.
