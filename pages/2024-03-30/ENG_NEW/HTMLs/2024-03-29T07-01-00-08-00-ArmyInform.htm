The United States Ambassador Bridget Brynk has again urged to strengthen assistance to Ukraine against the backdrop of the next missile attack of the Russian Federation.

She is about itwroteIn the social network H.

The Ambassador noted that during the night, Russia had ruthlessly launched rockets and drones against Ukraine's energy infrastructure and all Ukraine was in a state of air alarm.

“Russia's barbaric efforts aimed at switching off electricity for millions of men, women and children continue uncontrollably. Our help is needed now, ”Bridget wrote.

On Friday night, the Russian military attacked Ukraine with winged rockets from Tu-95MC aircraft, as well as aerobalist missiles "Dying".

During the rocket attack, explosions in the Dnieper, Khmelnytskyi and Ivano-Frankivsk were erupted.
