During the last day, on March 28, the enemy fired at Kherson region again, as a result of attack 1 person was killed, and another 8 were injured.

About thisinformsHead of Kherson Ova Alexander Proudin.

According to him, last day the enemy fired Kizomis, Berislav, Golden Beam, Dudchan, Mykilsk, Tyaginka, Novovorontsovka, Antonovka, Prydniprovsk, Stanislav, Mikhailivka, Lviv and the city of Kherson.

In addition, Oleksandr Proudin noted that the Russian military kissed the residential quarters of the settlements of the region, resulting in damaged 21 private house.

"The administrative building, the higher education institution, the object of critical infrastructure, the cellular tower, the agricultural enterprise, the farm, the shop, garage and warehouses and cars were also recorded," the head of the Ova added.
