In the Zaporozhye direction of fortification is built in three shifts. In particular, the state allocated 1.3 billion from the budget for the construction of fortifications.

About thisreportedandPublicatedThe corresponding video is the head of Zaporizhzhya Ova Ivan Fedorov.

"Military and civilian engineers have united to make a large -scale modern and reliable project of fortifications," he said.

According to the head of the OVA, the contractors perform work on the schedule approved by the President of Ukraine and under the close control of the Ministry of Defense.

“The pace of construction does not like the enemy. Therefore, builders have to take into account the security situation. But the Zaporizhzhya fortress will be erected, because it is protection not only for the frontier regional center, but also of the inhabitants of all Ukraine, ”Ivan Fedorov emphasized.

As reported by the Armyinform, all over the front lineDefense boundaries are being built, install special antitrone equipment.
