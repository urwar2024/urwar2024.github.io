Three more children from Kherson region managed to return to Ukraine controlled territory. These are boys aged 5, 11 and 14 years.

About thisreportedHead of Kherson Ova Alexander Proudin.

According to him, the parents of the children were contacted on their own with the representatives of the Save Ukraine Charitable organization and asked to help escape from the hell of the occupation. They had already lived in such conditions.

“Nowadays, long -term rehabilitation and work with psychologists are waiting for families. They are in a safe place where they receive the necessary help, ”the head of the Ova said.

Alexander Proudin also added that from the beginning of 2024 47 children from the Kherson region have been returned to Ukraine -controlled territory.

"I am grateful to all who unite their efforts to return to the homeland of all the little citizens of Ukraine," he stressed.

As reported by the Armyinform, over 10,000 peaceful Ukrainianshold Russians in occupied territoriesand in the territory of the Russian Federation.
