Former Secretary of the National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine Alexei Danilov will continue his work in the diplomatic direction.

About thisdeclared in the address March 29President.

“I agreed to his candidacy as a new ambassador of our country to the Republic of Moldova. He told me about such a vision of his further work on Ukraine. For us, Moldova is an extremely significant state - both from the point of view of security challenges in the region and from the point of view of our bilateral interaction, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

Asreported the armyinform, the President identified the main tasks for the updated National Security and Defense Council.
