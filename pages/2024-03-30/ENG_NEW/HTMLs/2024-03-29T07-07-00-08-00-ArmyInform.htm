During the last day, there were 59 combat clashes. In total, the enemy caused 16 rocket launchers and 131 aircraft strike, making 128 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its Facebook page.

In Volyn and Polissya directionsThe operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent the transfer of our troops to threatening directions, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy of the offensive (assault) did not take action.

In the estuary directionOur warriors reflected 6 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements of Bigorivka of Luhansk region and Terny of Donetsk region.

In the Bakhmut directionOur warriors reflected 13 attacks in the areas of settlements of the time of Yar, Novy, Klyshchiyivka, Andreevka of Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 3 attacks in the area of the settlement of Semenivka, Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to knock out our units from the occupied borders.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of settlements Novomykhailivka, St. George, where the enemy with the support of aviation 22 times tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy attacked the positions of our defenders 8 times in the areas of the robot, northwestern willow and north of the Greater Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson directionThe enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. During the last day, 2 attacks have been carried out on the positions of our troops.

At the same time, our warriors continue to be active in the occupation troops of losses in living strength and equipment, depleting the enemy along the entire line of combat collision.
