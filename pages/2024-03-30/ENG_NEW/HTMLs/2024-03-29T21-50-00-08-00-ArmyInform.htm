In the evening of March 29, the shutdowns took place in Kharkiv, Dnipropetrovsk, Donetsk, Kirovograd, Poltava, Sumy and Zaporizhzhya regions.

About thisreportedMinistry of Energy.

The reasons for shutdowns are as follows:

  • network restrictions on the main lines of the transmission system operator (the inability to transmit all the required volume of electricity to consumers of energy deficiency regions from "surplus" regions);
  • lack of local generation through Russian shelling;
  • Increase in consumption in the evening peak hours.

We remind that planned shutdowns are still in place in Kharkiv and Dnipropetrovsk region.

The Ministry of Energy asked the Ukrainians to understand temporary difficulties.

Asreported the armyinformLast night the enemy's goals became Kaniv and Dniester hydroelectric power station.
