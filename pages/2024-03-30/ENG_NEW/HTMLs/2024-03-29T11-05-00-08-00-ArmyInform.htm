President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky spoke about the need to strengthen Ukraine's air defense, Russia's counter -offensive actions, and why the support of the United States is important to us.

He is about ittoldIn his telegram channel.

“We are grateful for all the United States, for great help for us. But one very serious moment - the Patriot systems located in us, did not come from the United States. Funds-$ 1.5-2 billion-stood out for these systems and shells to them, but the systems themselves arrived from different countries. The money was never come to us, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

Volodymyr Zelensky also stressed that Putin is not tuned for negotiations and ending the war, so Ukraine should become strong in the battlefield. This requires 2 things - long -range missiles and more Patriot systems.

“Ukraine is not as strong as it can be. We need to allow us to act on the battlefield. Two things are needed for this: Patriot systems that can pass the United States and parity in artillery. Patriot systems we unlock the sky, and our boys will go forward, ”the head of state summarized.
