President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky declared updating the work of the National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine and identified the main tasks.

This is stated inmessagein the television channel of the Head of State.

The first task, he said, is to strengthen Ukraine's ability to predict and influence the processes on which the national security of our country depends.

The second is the continuation of all current tasks of the NSDC system, especially regarding the sanction policy of our country.

The third task is doctrinal work, that is, the clarity of national landmarks and systematicity in their implementation, in particular after the war.

Fourth - cybersecurity and information. The NSDC has the potential to do more to protect against hostile destabilization operations and to coordinate all state institutions in this area.

“And the fifth - the work of the bet, the decisions of decisions, the control of the implementation of decisions - all this, of course, should be continued. The obvious priority is the defense of our country and the protection of our society, ”the President summarized.
