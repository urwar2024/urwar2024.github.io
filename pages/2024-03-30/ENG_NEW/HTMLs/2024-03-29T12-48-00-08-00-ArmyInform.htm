Employees of the State Bureau of Investigation reported suspicion of officials of one of the military units of the Nikolaev area, which allowed the unjustified transfer of the monthly salary of fighters to the account of a civilian.

This is stated inmessageDBR.

Due to the negligence of the head of the financial and economic service of the military unit, more than 800 thousand hryvnias, which were intended for payment of servicemen, were mistakenly transferred to the account of a civilian.

It is noted that the official did not check the accounting and financial documentation, which made such a mistake.

She was informed of suspicion of negligent attitude of the military official to the service, if it caused grave consequences (Part 4 of Article 425 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine). The sanction of the article provides for imprisonment for up to 8 years.

The damage caused to the state is currently compensated by the military and the military received payments.
