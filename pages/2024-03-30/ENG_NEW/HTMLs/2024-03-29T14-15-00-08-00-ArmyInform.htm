The imprisonment was given to militants who tried to "break" the defense of the Armed Forces under the robot.

About thisShe reported March 29Security Service of Ukraine.

According to the department, the attackers were part of the occupation groups of the Southern Military District of the Armed Forces of Russia.

In the ranks of the invaders, they were attracted to the "meat storms" of the leading positions of the Armed Forces on the Eastern and South Fronts.

First of all, we are talking about repeated attempts by the occupiers to "break" the defense of Ukrainian defenders in the area of the Robovy in Zaporozhye.

All four traitors were captured during fierce fighting.

According to the SBU materials, the court appointed them 15 years in prison.

According to the investigation, two convicted are residents of temporarily occupied units of Donetsk and Luhansk regions.

Both voluntarily "mobilized" to the Russian troops at the beginning of a large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation.

There they were given automatic weapons with ammunition and appointed shooters.

After short classes at the landfill, one of the fighters was sent to the Kharkiv direction, the other - under Mariupol.

Two more convicts are residents of the temporarily occupied Crimea. They "enrolled" in the fall of 2022.

They were first transferred to Berdyansk for the arrangement of Russian fortifications and checkpoints.

Subsequently, both traitors were sent to the area of the Armed Forces defense positions for storming.

On the basis of the evidence collected, the court found two fighters guilty under three articles of the Criminal Code of Ukraine:

  • Part 2 of Art. 111 (treason committed in martial law);
  • Part 1 of Art. 258-3 (participation in a terrorist group or terrorist organization);
  • Part 2 of Art. 260 (participation in the unforeseen law of militarized or armed formations).

Criminal acts of two more intruders are qualified under two articles of the Criminal Code of Ukraine:

  • Part 2 of Art. 111 (treason);
  • Part 7 of Art. 111-1 (collaborative activity).

Asreported the Armyinform,Two traitors were detained, who guarded the Russian torture during the occupation of Kherson region.
