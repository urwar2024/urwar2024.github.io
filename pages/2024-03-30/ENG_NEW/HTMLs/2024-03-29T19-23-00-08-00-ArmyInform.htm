At the seven hundred sixty -fifths of large -scale armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine there were 61 fighting.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces, reports the Armyinform.

In Volyn and Polissya directionsOperational situation without significant changes. No signs of formation of offensive groups of the enemy were detected.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in the border areas, conducts sabotage and intelligence activities, carries out shelling of settlements from the territory of the Russian Federation, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border of Ukraine. The enemy struck in the areas of settlements Bochkovo, Uspenivka, Kharpuns of Kharkiv region. About 10 settlements, including Zarichia, Leonivka, Chernihiv region; Oleksiyivka, Miropillya of Sumy region; Veterinary, Vovchansk, Stichonov Kharkiv region.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy of the offensive (assault) did not take action. Under the artillery and mortar fire were about 15 settlements, including Sinkivka, Stepova Novoselivka, Kotlyarivka of Kharkiv region.

In the estuary directionOur warriors reflect 10 enemy attacks in the areas of Ternya, Donetsk region and Bigorivka, Luhansk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops. Also, the enemy struck in the areas of settlements of Izium Kharkiv region, Novoyegorovka of Lugansk region and Yampolivka of Donetsk region. More than 10 settlements, including Bigorivka, Nevsky Luhansk region and Serebryansk forestry, were affected by artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Bakhmut directionOur warriors reflects 2 attacks in the areas of settlements of Kingyshiyivka, Andriyivka, Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to improve the tactical position. Also, the enemy struck in the areas of the settlements of the Yar and Ivanovo Donetsk region. There were more than 10 settlements under artillery and mortar fire, including Bogdanivka, Kalynivka, Stupochka of Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders reflect 16 attacks in the areas of settlements of Berdychi, Uman, Tonny, Pervomaisk, Nevelskoye Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to knock out our units from the occupied borders. Also, the enemy struck in the areas of the settlements of Novobakhmutivka, Mezhnovo, and Yusnobrodivka of Donetsk region. About 15 settlements, including Novokalinove, Novobakhmutivka, Semenivka, Donetsk region, have experienced artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Novopavlovsky directionDefense forces continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of settlements of Krasnogorivka, St. George, Novomikhailivka, Vodyane Donetsk region, where the enemy has tried more than 20 times to break through the defense of our troops. The enemy also struck in the areas of settlements Krasnogorivka, Konstantinovka of Donetsk region. Under the artillery and mortar fire of the invaders were more than 10 settlements, among them Paraskovka, Konstantinovka, coal of Donetsk region.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy, with the support of aviation, made 2 unsuccessful attacks on the positions of our defenders in the areas of Staromayorsky Donetsk region and northwestern Verbov Zaporizhzhya region. The enemy struck aviation in the areas of Orikhiv settlements, Novodanilovka and the robot of Zaporizhzhya region. About 10 settlements, including Malynivka, Red, Belogorye and Shcherbaki, Zaporizhzhya region, have experienced artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Kherson directionDuring the day, 2 attacks of our troops on the left bank of the Dnieper were carried out by the enemy. More than 10 settlements, Tyaginka, Ivanivka, Mykilsk, Kherson region and Kherson city, have experienced artillery and mortar shelling.
