49 combat clashes were recorded per day. In total, the enemy caused 23 rocket launchers and 61 aircraft strike, and fired 41 firing of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisInformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In the estuary directionOur warriors repelled 3 enemy attacks in the area of the settlement of Terny of Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops. Also, the enemy struck in the areas of settlements May Day of Kharkiv region and Yampolivka of Donetsk region. More than 10 settlements were affected by artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Bakhmut direction7 attacks in areas of settlements of Bigorivka of Luhansk region are reflected; Ivanovskoye, Khishchivka, Andreevka, Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to improve the tactical position. The enemy struck aviation in areas of settlements of the Yar Time, Razdolivka of Donetsk region. There were more than 10 settlements under artillery and mortar fire.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 9 attacks in the areas of settlements of Semenivka, Uman, Pervomaisk Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to knock out our units from the occupied borders. Also, the enemy struck in the areas of settlements Novobakhmutivka, Berdichi, Orlovka of Donetsk region. About 20 settlements have undergone artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Novopavlovsky directionDefense forces continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of settlements of St. George, Novomikhailivka and Easier of the Vodyany Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried 16 times to break through the defense of our troops. Also, the enemy struck in the areas of Poland and Urozhyene of Donetsk region. Over 10 settlements were found under artillery and mortar fire.

In Orikhiv direction the enemyWith the support of aviation, he made 2 attacks on the positions of our defenders in the districts of Staromayorsky Donetsk region and a robot Zaporizhzhya region. About 20 settlements have undergone artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Kherson directionDuring the day, the enemy made 2 attacks of our troops on the left bank of the Dnieper. More than 10 settlements have undergone artillery and mortar shelling.

As reported byArmyinform, 85 combat clashes took place during the last day.
