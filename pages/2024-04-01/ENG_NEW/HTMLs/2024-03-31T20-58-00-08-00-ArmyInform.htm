The Security Service of Ukraine commented on the statement of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation, in which, in particular, Russia demands from the authorities of Ukraine to arrest and issue the Russian Federation of the SBU head Lieutenant-General Vasyl Malyuk.

The SBU's comment was provided by the Army of the Office.

The special service recalled that Putin is expected in The Hague.

"Trying to appeal to the norms of international law, namely to the convention on combating terrorism, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation forgets that it is Putin who is officially put on the international wanted list — in particular, he is expected at the Hague Tribunal for the abduction of Ukrainian children," the statement said.

Especially cynical, according to the SBU, the statements about terrorism sound from the terrorist country itself against the background of the anniversary of the liberation of Bucha and the atrocities that the Russians have done there.

"We remember every dead Ukrainian and do everything to get the enemy a fair retribution," the SBU emphasized.

Therefore, according to the statement of the Ukrainian special service, any words of the Russian Foreign Ministry are worthless.

"In fact, the Russian Federation can make only one statement that is worthy of attention — to announce its defeat in the war and the withdrawal of occupation troops from Ukrainian land to the whole world," the SBU noted.

The special service, together with the brothers from the forces of defense and security, according to the statement, is doing everything possible to speed up this time.
