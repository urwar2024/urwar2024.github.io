Lithuanian Armed Forces (LKM) completed international instructor courses where military personnel from Ukraine improved their knowledge and skills. The course was conducted by the NATO School Instructors, who gained experience and knowledge in different NATO countries.

This is stated inmessageof the Armed Forces of Lithuania.

During the five -week course, Ukrainian soldiers mastered the basics of military training, shooting and weapons, skills necessary for self -planning and training.The training was held at the LKM and Brigade General Kazios Veverskis, where the knowledge gained during the field exercises was assigned to practice - during preparatory combat exercises.

The Ukrainian military, who were in Lithuania, visited large cities, museums, met the country and its history. Also, servicemen met with the II LKM sergeants of the Sergeant Career and shared their thoughts on the importance of the professionalism, leadership and motivation of the sergeant in the course of tasks.

"Servicemen who have passed international instructor courses will be able to effectively teach their brothers and use the experience and knowledge gained in the training centers of their country after returning to Ukraine," the message reads.

As reportedАрміяInform, the Interflex commander spoke about the training of the Armed Forces.
