Today, March 31, the Russian army struck a rocket strike in the village in the Zmiiv district of Kharkiv region.

About thisreportedHead of Kharkiv garrison Sergey Melnyk.

“The enemy struck a rocket strike in the village in the Zmiiv district of our region. It is known about five victims, ”the message reads.

Sergey Melnyk also stressed that the threat of repeated blows remains for the city and the region.

As reportedArmyinform, in Kharkiv region, on March 30, 2024, a person was killed in Russian shelling.
