On the second anniversary of the liberation of Bucha from the Russian invaders, Moldovan President Maia Sandu called on democratic countries to continue supporting Ukraine.

The leader of Moldova said thiswroteIn Twitter.

"Today marks two years since the liberation of Bucha, which revealed unspeakable atrocities against the civilian population," she stressed.

Maya Sandu said she honored the victim's memory with Ukraine and called on the democratic countries of the world to join efforts to "achieve justice and continue Ukraine's support."

It should be noted that in June 2022 President of MoldovaVisitedBorodyanka, Bucha and Irpin to see with their own eyes the consequences of the Russian occupation of Ukrainian territories.

As reported byАрміяInform, on the second anniversary of the liberation of the Kiev region from Russian invaders, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy honored the memory of Ukrainians killed by invaders during the occupation of the Buchan community.
