Under the rubble of the administrative building of the object of critical infrastructure in Lviv region found the body of another dead man.

Rescuers continue searching.

As reportedArmyinform, On March 31, 2024, the enemy attacked the winged rockets of Lviv region. As a result of the attack, a man was killed.
