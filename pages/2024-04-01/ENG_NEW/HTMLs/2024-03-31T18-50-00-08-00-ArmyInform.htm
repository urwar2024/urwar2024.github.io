After a large -scale Russian invasion of Kiev, hundreds of kilometers of fortifications were built and several rings of defense were created. Today, this protection continues to intensify.

This was stated by the commander of the Lieutenant General Oleksandr Pavlyuk on the air of the Telernorphone "One News" on March 31, 2024.

It should be noted that at the beginning of a large -scale invasion of Russia in Ukraine, Oleksandr Pavlyuk headed the Kyiv Regional Military Administration for several months, and also commanded the Kiev forces of Kiev.

“For the year in this position we did a huge amount of work. It is about a thousand kilometers of fortifications, three rings of defense of Kiev. The Chernobyl direction was greatly intensified. Until now, it has not been taken into account as a defensive border. We did everything to prevent the options for the sudden event of the enemy, ”the Land Forces Commander emphasized.

According to him, now there is an extension in all directions, the defense of the capital of Ukraine continues to intensify.

“Long -term concrete fortifications are already being built. Because to have such a neighbor - you need to be constantly prepared for war. And Kiev is the heart of Ukraine, and on how it functions, it depends whether Ukraine will live or will not, ”said Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk.

As reportedArmyinform, Commander of the Land Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Oleksandr Pavlyuk noted that in the week from March 24 to March 31, 2024, 5420 personnel of the enemy were eliminated by the Defense of Ukraine and 1088 units of his weapons and military equipment were destroyed.
