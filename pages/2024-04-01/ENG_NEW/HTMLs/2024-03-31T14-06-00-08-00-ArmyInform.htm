Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov congratulated all Christians and servicemen who celebrate Easter on March 31, 2024.

The head of the Defense Office of Ukraine was about thiswroteon Facebook.

“I congratulate all Christians who celebrate Easter today. I congratulate all the servicemen who celebrate Easter in the service, ”he said.

Rustem Umerov emphasized that thanks to the bravery and dedication of servicemen, thousands of Ukrainian families today have the opportunity to celebrate one of the most important Christian holidays at home, in comfort.

“Thank you, warriors. Good, unity and peace in your homes. Let all your prayers be heard, ”the Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov emphasized.

As reportedArmyinform, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy also congratulated Ukrainians on Easter, calling Easter a holiday that reminds of fortitude.
