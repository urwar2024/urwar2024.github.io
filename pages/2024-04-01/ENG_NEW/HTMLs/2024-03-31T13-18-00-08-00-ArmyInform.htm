On Sunday, March 31, Ukraine celebrates the second anniversary of the liberation of Bucha of the Kiev region from Russian invaders. A day that has forever entered the history of Ukraine.

About thiswrotePresident of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky and showed a photo of what a city had after the de -occupation.

"When our warriors expelled Russia from Bucha, from Irpen, from Borodyanka, from all over Kyiv region, and then from Sumy region, from Chernihiv - everyone was able to see that it was not only Ukrainian courage and weapon, but also human morality," he said .

The head of state urged the world not to forget how Ukrainians stopped Russian evil and then expel the invaders from their land.

"Let the whole world never forget what price this battle is given and what evil we stop here on our earth, when Ukrainians stop and expel Russian invaders," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

As reportedArmyinform, The President of Ukraine spoke about the war with the dominance of "Velikoy Russian culture".
