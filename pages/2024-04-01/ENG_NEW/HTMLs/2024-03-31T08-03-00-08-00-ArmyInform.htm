The General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine provided operational information as of 6:00 on March 31, 2024 on the Russian invasion: seven hundred sixty -seventh days of large -scale armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine began.

During the past day, 85 combat clashes took place, according tomessageHSF.

In total, the enemy caused 17 rocket launchers and 83 aviation strikes, making 87 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

At the end of the last day, Russian invaders attacked Ukraine again, applying 11 shahamed UAVs. Forces and means of air defense of Ukraine 9 UAVs were destroyed, and already in the morning of the current day, the enemy involved the winged rockets of different types to strike. The last attack data is specified.

The settlements of Neskachny Sumy region have suffered from the last day of aviation strikes; Grigorivka, Sinkivka, Petropavlivka, Kupyansk-Nusal Kharkiv region; Novoyubivka of Lugansk region; Torni, ponds, Yampolivka, Rozdolivka, Yar, Ivanivske, Friendship, Berdichi, Semenivka, Vodyane, Alexandropil, Staromayorsk Donetsk region; Malynivka of Zaporizhzhya region.

Under the artillery fire were more than 100 settlements of Chernihiv, Sumy, Kharkiv, Lugansk, Donetsk, Zaporizhia, Dnipropetrovsk, Kherson and Mykolaiv regions.

In Volyn and Polissya directionsThe operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent the transfer of our troops to threatening directions, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy of the offensive (assault) did not take action.

In the estuary directionUkrainian soldiers repelled 6 attacks in the areas of the settlements of Terny of Donetsk region and Bigorivka in Luhansk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionUkrainian warriors reflected 7 attacks in the areas of settlements of the Yar, Ivanivske, Klischivka of Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionUkrainian defenders reflected 24 attacks in the areas of settlements Berdychi, Semenivka, Uman, Pervomaisk, Nevelskoye, where the enemy tried to knock out our units from the occupied borders.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of settlements of Krasnogorivka, Vage, Novomikhailivka and Easier of the Vodyany Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to break through the defense of our troops 36 times.

In the Orikhiv directionThe invaders made 5 attacks on the positions of Ukrainian defenders in the districts of Staromayorsky Donetsk region and a robot Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson directionThe enemy attacked the positions of Ukrainian troops 2 times on the Dnieper Left Bank.

During the last day, the Aviation of Defense forces has struck 4 anti-aircraft missile complexes and 11 areas of personnel concentration, weapons and enemy military equipment.

The missile troops were defeated by 4 radar stations, 1 management point, 1 area of personnel concentration, 4 air defense and 1 enemy artillery.

As reported by the Armyinform, 79 combat clashes with the invaders were recorded in the past 24 hours.
