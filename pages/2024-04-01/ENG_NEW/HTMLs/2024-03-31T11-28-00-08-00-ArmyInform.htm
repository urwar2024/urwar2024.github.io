Ukrenergo National Energy Company spoke about the problems that the Ukrainian energy system faced as a result of another rocket attack by Russian invaders.

This is stated inmessageUkrenergo.

“At night, the Russians again struck energy. The purpose was Ukrenergo's high -voltage substations in the Southern region. Due to damage to energy equipment, they were forced to use emergency shutdowns in Odessa and surrounding areas, ”the message said.

It is emphasized that when consumption increases, the amount of emergency shutdowns in Odessa may increase.

“Consumption restrictions in Kharkiv and Kryvyi Rih continue. At night, the schedules were not used. But because of the growth of consumption, and accordingly the load on the power system, in the afternoon, and especially in the hours of the evening maximum, it is possible to shut off. Please consume electricity, ”the company said.

As reported by the Armyinform, fordataThe Air Force of the Armed Forces, on the night of March 31, 2024, the Russian invaders attacked 14 winged missiles X-101/X-555, 11 shaped UAVs, Iskander-M ballistic missile and a controlled X-59 aviation missile.

At the same time, Ukraine's air defense has shot down 9 winged missiles X-101/X-555 and 9 shock UAVs Shahd-136/131 Russian invaders.
