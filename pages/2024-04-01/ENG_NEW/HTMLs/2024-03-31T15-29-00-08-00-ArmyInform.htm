On the second anniversary of the liberation of the Kiev region from Russian invaders, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky honored the memory of Ukrainians killed by invaders during the occupation of the Buchan community.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

Volodymyr Zelensky installed a lamp to the wall of memory near the Church of the Apostle Andrew the First Called and All Saints in Bucha.

“The wall of memory consists of several ceilings, each of which - plates with the carved names of the dead. The memorial is currently containing509 Civilian nameswhich have been identified. Some plates are still nameless, ”the message reads.

The President and the other attendees honored the memory of the dead moment of silence.

It is noted that the temple of all the saints of the Ukrainian, whose parish belongs to the Diocese of the Orthodox Church of Ukraine, during the Russian occupation in March 2022 became a symbol of the stability and indomitability of Bucha residents. On its territory there is a fraternal grave, from which after the release of the Kyiv region exhumed116 bodies of city residents. Subsequently, they were transferred to the cemetery.

The Russian army entered Bucha on February 27, 2022. In 33 days of occupation, the Russian Federation committed more than 9,000 war crimes in the Buchansky district and killed more than 1,400 civilian inhabitants, including 37 children.

The Armed Forces of Ukraine liberated the city on March 31. It was after the liberation of the Kyiv region that the first statements of the world leaders about the genocide nature of the Russian army in Ukraine were made.

As reported by the Armyinform, Head of StatecalledThe world does not forget how Ukrainians stopped Russian evil and then expel invaders from their land.
