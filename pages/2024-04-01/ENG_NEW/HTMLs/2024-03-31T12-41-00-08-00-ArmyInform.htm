Head of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Lieutenant General Kirill Budanov told what is the situation in the village of Totkino in the Kursk region of the Russian Federation, which was previously reported by Russian volunteers.

Cyril Budanov provided his comments duringlive etherOn March 31, 2024.

In particular, when asked by the presenter, who is now controlled by Totkin, the head of GUR noted that it is now a gray zone.

“I think so that no one (controls, ed.). This is such a gray zone. There is not a lot left there, ”Kirill Budanov said.

When asked whether Russian volunteers now remain in the Bryansk, Kursk, Belgorod regions of the Russian Federation, the head of GUR reported that mainly in the Belgorod region.

“For the most part, it is the Belgorod region. And you see may have heard from telegrams, Russian, first of all, that the Grayvorone district-it has already been depopulated, because there are constant fighting. Something happens in the city of Belgorod. The situation is difficult there. The Russians began to feel like this - to live in the war, ”Kirill Budanov said.

As reported by the Armyinform, the fighters of the Legion of Russia, the Russian Volunteer Corps and the Siberian Battalion, who were fighting on the side of the forces of Ukraine against the Russian troops, raids on the territory of the Russian Federation were arranged in March 2024. Cyril Budanovpredictsthat they will still move deep into Russia.
