Lieutenant General Kyrylo Budanov, Chief of the Main Intelligence Directorate of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine, denied claims that the Russians allegedly used the ODAB-1500 (thermobaric volumetric detonating aerial bomb weighing 1500 kg) for the first time in the Sumy region.

Cyril Budanov provided his comments duringlive broadcastOn March 31, 2024.

"I can directly refute this. Because the ODAB-1500 has been used more than once," said the head of the Main Directorate of Intelligence.

According to him, the enemy has already used the ODAB-1500 both on the front line and when the operation of the "Russian Volunteer Corps" and the "Freedom of Russia" legion was active on the territory of the Russian Federation.

“There was also Odab. That is, there is nothing new in this, ”Kirill Budanov emphasized.
