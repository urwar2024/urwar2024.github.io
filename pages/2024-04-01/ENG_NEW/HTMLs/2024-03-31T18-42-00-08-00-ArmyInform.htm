During the anxiety, which was announced in the Rivne region on March 31, air defense knocked down two rockets: X-55 and Iskander.

About thisreportin the police of Rivne region.

Explosive equipment, investigators and criminalists worked on the sites of the fragments of rockets.

“During the next attacks, the Anto-Defense Country also worked in two districts of Rivne region: the X-55 and Iskander missiles were knocked down. Fortunately, no one was injured, ”the message said.

Law enforcement officers documented the events. Some of the wreckage was removed and will be sent for examination.
