During the week from March 24 to March 31, 2024, 5420 personnel of the enemy were eliminated by the Defense forces of Ukraine and 1088 units of his weapons and military equipment were destroyed.

About thisReportedCommander of the Land Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Alexander Pavlyuk.

Significant losses, according to him, suffered weapons and military equipment of Russian troops, namely:

  • 90 tanks,
  • 146 combat armored vehicles,
  • 195 artillery systems,
  • 5 Rsw,
  • 0 air defense settings,
  • 310 units of auto -engineering,
  • 45 units of special equipment.

"In addition, our defenders destroyed 56 enemy missiles and 221 UAVs," said Lieutenant General Oleksandr Pavlyuk, commander of the Land Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
