French Armed Forces Minister Sebastian Lecorton said France would put hundreds of armored vehicles, Aster missiles and remote control within a new military assistance package to Ukraine.

He reported this in an interview with La Tribune,passesUkrinform.

"To keep such a large front line, the Ukrainian army needs our armored personnel carriers: they are absolutely necessary for the mobility of the troops ... We can talk about hundreds of units in 2024 and early 2025," the minister said.

French armored personnel carriers, as noted, for more than 40 years, but they are suitable for operation. In the French Army, VAB is now replaced by a new generation of Griffon.

Sebastian Lecorton said that the help package would include Aster 30 missiles for SAMP/T Mamba air defense complexes.

"We are also developing remote controls in a very short time that will be delivered to Ukrainians this summer," he added.
