On March 31, on the second anniversary of the liberation of Kyiv region from Russian invaders, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited Bucha, where he honored the memory of the Ukrainians killed by Russians killed by Russians

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

“In this war, it is global. It is a war for the right to exist for our country and at the same time for the right to live for everyone. For dignity for our people and for every nation who seeks their fate. It is a war for no one to dare to become a beast against others, ”the Head of State said.

Volodymyr Zelenskyy stressed that our people killed by the occupiers, whose bodies were lying on the streets of Bucha, were evidence that "no one in the world should stay away from this battle."

"It is here that in Ukraine, in defending our people, our lives, our country, humanity wins," he summarized.

According to the President, in order to start such aggression against others, Russia initially destroyed morality and made violence and hatred with its ideology.

“Such systems do not stop just like that. The power stops them. The unity stops them. Determination and understanding of what they want to destroy. I thank everyone in the world who really helps us. Glory to our strong people, our soldiers! Glory to all who protect humanity and life! Glory to Ukraine!" - said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

As reported byАрміяInform, in Bucha, after the de-occupation, 422 bodies of civilians killed by the Russians were found.
