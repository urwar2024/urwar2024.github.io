Poland has stopped operating its aviation in Poland in connection with the decrease in the threat of Russian aviation missile strokes in Ukraine.

This is stated inmessageOperational Command of the Armed Forces of Poland.

It is noted that the deployed forces and means have returned to the regular regime.

"Despite the festive period, it was an next night of increased readiness of the Polish systems and intensive service of soldiers of the Polish army, which are constantly monitoring the situation in the territory of Ukraine and remain in constant readiness to ensure the safety of Poland's airspace," the message said.

Poland announced the rise of its aviation at 6:00 on March 31, 2024.

As reported by the Armyinform, fordataThe Air Force of the Armed Forces, on the night of March 31, 2024, the Russian invaders attacked 14 winged missiles X-101/X-555, 11 shaped UAVs, Iskander-M ballistic missile and a controlled X-59 aviation missile.

At the same time, Ukraine's air defense has shot down 9 winged missiles X-101/X-555 and 9 shock UAVs Shahd-136/131 Russian invaders.
