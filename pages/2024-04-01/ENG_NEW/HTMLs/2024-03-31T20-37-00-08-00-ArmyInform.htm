President Volodymyr Zelenskyy thanked the servicemen of Ukraine, who in the last week of March showed effective actions at the front.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

“Thank you to our soldiers, and this week is especially worth noting the 95th Brigade, Seventystyevyatka DShv, 44th and 55th separate artillery… Thank you, soldiers! I am proud of our people! ” - the head of state emphasized.

Volodymyr Zelensky assured that Ukraine would necessarily win.

“The main thing is not to lose faith in ourselves, to our people and not to lose a single day when you can add results for Ukraine. Therefore, Ukraine has risen and dispels the darkness of captivity, ”he summarized.

The President also thanked everyone who helps and those who are with Ukraine and Ukraine.
