The Bilhorod-Dnistrovskyi detachment informed what is needed to obtain permission to enter, stay, and live in the border zone.

About it in TelegramreportDefense forces of South of Ukraine.

In order to obtain a permit for entry, stay, accommodation, conduct of work and pass to the border strip in the area of responsibility of the Belgorod-Dnestrovsky detachment (Odessa, Belgorod-Dnestrovsky, Bolgrad districts), the person must formally send a message to the address:

  • For submission of appeals in writing (personally or by mail): 67707, Belgorod-Dnestrovsky, vul. Military Glory, 14.
  • To appeal in electronic form:

e-mail: bilgorod_dnistrovskiy_zagin@dpsu.gov.ua

  • For more information, please contact: (04849) -6-01-98.
