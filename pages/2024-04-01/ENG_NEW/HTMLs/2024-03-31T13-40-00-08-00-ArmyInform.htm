In the south of Ukraine, the invaders intensified air intelligence, pressed artillery shelling, continue to use shock drones

About the situation on the battlefieldtoldin the forces of the South Defense.

"The enemy does not abandon its intentions to dislodge our units from their positions. They carried out 5 assaults in the Orikhiv direction, of which 3 in the area of Robotyne, and 2 in the area of Staromayorske. On the left bank of the Dnieper, the enemy conducted 2 assaults," the message reads.

According to the military, traditionally the enemy does not attract armored vehicles. With significant losses, the invaders went to their starting positions. At the same time, our fighters continue to take comprehensive measures to keep and strengthen positions.

"With the change of weather, the invaders intensified air intelligence - more than 200 reconnaissance per day was recorded in the operating area," - said the defense of the South.

Artillery shellings continue on the battlefield, and the enemy uses percussion drones of various modifications, including with the discharge of fragmental ammunition.

"The enemy applies aviation in the Orikhiv direction," the military added.

As reported byArmyinform, over the past day, the enemy attacked 128 settlements of Ukraine.
