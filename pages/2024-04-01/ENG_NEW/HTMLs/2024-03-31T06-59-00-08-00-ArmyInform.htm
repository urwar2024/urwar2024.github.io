To declare a volunteer on a contract or to express a desire to formally mobilize before the end of the martial law to the ranks of the Armed Forces of Ukraine is not enough to get into the service.

The health should allow you to effectively and without harming you yourself and the dangers of others to perform the task that you will occupy in the Armed Forces.

Suitability for the service is established by the VLK-the Military Medical Commission. Therefore, when going to a contract or mobilizing, you should be prepared for a thorough professional medical examination.

Will have to spend from 2 to 4 days

From the obvious disadvantages - it will not be possible to pass the VLK lightning, it will take you several - at least two to four - days of time. This is inevitable because the results of the tests do not occur instantly - in many cases they are only provided the next day.

In addition, some additional documents will be required to pass the VLK. If you prepare them in advance, it will accelerate the process. In the absence, you will have to spend time on their receipt. Accordingly, it will slow down your movement - and instead of two days on the passage of VLK will have to spend already 3–4.

Contractors and mobilized - green street

A positive factor that will accelerate the passage of HCP to contractors and mobilized is the priority opportunity to get to the commission doctors. Green Street is valid from the moment of registration and allows you to undergo an inspection of all VLK specialists on the first day for several hours.

This will allow the same day (or the next day in the morning, if required by the medical procedure) to pass the necessary tests and give the time to receive additional necessary documents if you have not prepared them in advance. In this case, the employees of the VLK will provide clarification on any issue that will be unclear to you.

Starting document

At the beginning of the VLK, you will be issued a document called "Survey and Medical Examination Card" (Appendix 13to the order of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine№ 402 dated 08.08.2008with changes made in accordance with the Ministry of Defense Orders№ 524 dated 12.08.2014,№ 490 dated 18.08.2023).

Note that it will require a 3x4 cm photo card. The examination and medical examination card will be entered during the examinations and as a result of analyzes and research.

What documents can be prepared in advance

In order to speed up the passage of VLK, it is necessary to prepare three documents in advance, the presence of which on the first day of passing the VLK will significantly accelerate the process. How to get them can be explained by the family doctor with whom you have a declaration.

  • Certificate of passing previous, periodic and extraordinary psychiatric examinations, in particular for the use of psychoactive substances (form 100-2/o)
  • Help from skin and venereological dispensary (together with a copy of the micropraitation reaction with cardiolipin antigen (RW))
  • Help from the TB dispensary (together with a copy of the radiological (fluorographic) study of chest organs)

The absence of prepared certificates will not be an irresistible obstacle, but the process of passing the VLK will slow down somewhat - in two days it is definitely not time.

How and where these documents can be obtained, they will be told in VLK. This will have to spend extra time and, if it is a quick receipt of a psychiatric examination certificate, there are also some funds, as this certificate is provided, including by paying medical institutions.

Importantly: Help should be "fresh" issued within a week or two before the VLK.

Analyzes and research

All or the vast majority of analyzes and research can be done in the hospital on which the VLK works. Here is their list:

  • General blood test
  • Biochemical blood test (bilirubin, ALT, glucose)
  • General urine analysis
  • Serological blood test for antibodies to human immunodeficiency virus (HIV)
  • Serological blood test for antigen to the hepatitis B and C Hepatitis virus
  • Microprecipitation Reaction with cardiolipin antigen (RW)
  • Analysis to define blood group and rhesus.
  • X -ray (fluorographic) examination of chest organs
  • Electrocardiogram

If you take care of the above certificates in advance, you will already have any information about the part of the above analyzes. It is a copy of the microprecipitation response and a serological blood test for antibodies to HIV, which are in combination with a help with the skin and venereological dispensary.

Also, if you have a certificate from the TB dispensary, you will already have a copy of the radiological (fluorographic) study of the chest.

If any of these analyzes cannot be done in the health care facility on the basis of which the VLK works, you will be provided with information about the establishments where you can do it.

Final document

After all the necessary information has been collected and recorded in the "examination card and medical examination", it is based on the "Certificate of the Military Medical Commission" (Appendix 4to the order of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine№ 402 dated 08.08.2008with changes made in accordance with the Ministry of Defense Order№ 466 dated 01.08.2011).

This document draws the final conclusion on the fitness of health before the contract.

Don't hope for condescension

If you go to a contract or voluntarily mobilize, you hope that when you undergo VLK, your health will be examined less carefully given the voluntarily discovered desire to serve in the Armed Forces, you are deeply mistaken.

Experts from the medical commission will come to your health state equally meticulously and meticulously. With the slightest doubt about suitability, you are sent for additional examinations, analyzes and research.

In this case there is a risk not to invest in the work allocated to work 3–4 days, however, it can be certain that the suitability of health care before the contract will be determined unmistakably.

Remember: the state of your health in the Armed Forces is not only your personal business, it depends on the life and health of the brothers and the ability to perform the task. It is much better to identify and fix all the problems (if any) when passing the VLK and even refusing to contract the contract than to harm yourself, brothers or disrupt the performance of an important task.
