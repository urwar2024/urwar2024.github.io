To the night of March 31, 2024, the energy infrastructure of the Odessa region was again under the sight of the Russian invaders.

This is stated inmessageOleg Kiper, head of the Odessa Regional Military Administration.

8 enemy widowers managed to destroy the forces of Ukraine's aircraft.

However, the fall of the whipped drone caused a fire on one of the energy objects of the region, which resulted in the disconnection of several settlements.

The fire was promptly eliminated.

"Fortunately, people were not injured," Oleg Kiper notes.

As of 10:00 without electricity supply, there are 10 thousand subscribers of Odessa and Odessa district.

Recovery work is ongoing.

As reported by the Armyinform, fordataThe Air Force of the Armed Forces, on the night of March 31, 2024, the Russian invaders attacked 14 winged missiles X-101/X-555, 11 shaped UAVs, Iskander-M ballistic missile and a controlled X-59 aviation missile.

At the same time, Ukraine's air defense has shot down 9 winged missiles X-101/X-555 and 9 shock UAVs Shahd-136/131 Russian invaders.
