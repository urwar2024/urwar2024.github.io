The head of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Lieutenant General Kirill Budanov predicts that in the near future Russian invaders, after a long pause, will again strike in Ukraine with winged rockets "caliber".

Cyril Budanov made his statement duringlive etherOn March 31, 2024.

"Calibers" have accumulated all this period. They are absolutely ready for use ... In the near future, I think, we will see "calibers" again, because the number of X-101 has decreased significantly, "-said the head of GUR.

According to him, the Russians do not use "calibers" now because they have accumulated.

“Don't be used. The decision was made due to the fact that the "caliber" is a lot effective than the X-101 rocket, as long as the X-101 ... is now technically ready for them ("caliber"-ed.) Application. While the fleet was accumulating rockets, the air space forces of the Russian Federation used the X-101 missiles, ”Kirill Budanov said.

As reported by the Armyinform, earlier the spokeswoman for the Defense Forces of South of Ukraine Natalia Humeniukexplained the absence"Calibers" in hostile blows.
