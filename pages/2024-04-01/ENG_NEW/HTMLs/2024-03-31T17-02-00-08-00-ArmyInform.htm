On the second anniversary of the liberation of the Kiev region from the Russian invaders, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy took part in the raising of the State Flag of Ukraine on the flagship of the memorial of the memory of the defenders of the Buchan community, and also presented state awards to military personnel.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

Volodymyr Zelensky presented the orders of Bohdan Khmelnytsky II and III degrees, "for courage" of II and III degrees, medals "for military service of Ukraine", "For perfect service" of the third degree and "For saved life".

Yes, awards from the hands of the head of state receivedColonel of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Oleksandr VoloshinandCaptain Alexei Fandetskythat provided the evacuation and support of cars with civilians from Bucha to Bigorodka. At that time, more than 300 cars with citizens were evacuated during March 9-10, 2022.

Award-winning alsoLieutenant Colonel Andriy Kolodyazhny, Sergeant Alexander Fesik, Sergeant Myron Khimitsa, Senior Sergeant Alexander Martyniuk, who in March 2022 took part in the destruction of enemy convoys in the settlements of the Bucha district of the Kyiv region.

The President awarded andJunior Sergeant Sergei Doroshenko and Captain Ruslan Mamchik. They installed mine and engineering barriers, which successfully reflected enemy assaults.

The awards were also awarded to the soldiers of the National Guard of Ukraine - Sergeant Maxim Bovchalyuk and Sergeant Sergey Kvitko, who participated in fierce battles against Russian invaders in the Kyiv region, patrol police Oleg Shvets, who participated in stabilization activities in the cities Ivan Dulkai, who, since the beginning of a large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation, has documented and investigated the bold military crimes of the Russian army.

As reportedArmyinform,On March 31, on the second anniversary of the liberation of the Kyiv region from Russian invaders, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited Bucha, where he honored the memory of the Ukrainians killed by the Russians.
