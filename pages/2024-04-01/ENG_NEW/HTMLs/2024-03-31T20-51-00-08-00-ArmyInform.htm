Another mobile medical brigade of the Ukrainian Red Cross was launched in Sumy region. It will provide primary care in remote settlements of the Krasnopil community for free.

About thisinformsMinistry of Reintegration.

The Mobile Medical Brigade of the Ukrainian Red Cross consists of a family doctor, nurses and a driver who travel to where people have restricted access to medicine.

During their departure, specialists of the mobile medical crew advise and examine the health of people, measure blood pressure, sugar and blood oxygenation, prescribe treatment regimen and give out -of -the -counter drugs to facilitate a person.

Recall that today there are six such mobile crews in Sumy region. They assist in Krolevets, Trostyanets, Glukhiv, Nedryhaylovsk, Putivl and Krasnopil communities.

For more than a year and a half of their work in the Sumy region, such teams have provided more than 23 thousand consultations.

As reportedArmyinform, during a large -scale war, Russia damaged or destroyed in Ukraine 1736 medical facilities.
