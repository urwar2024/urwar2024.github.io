In the Khmelnytsky region, a chat bot "Veteran of Khmelnytsky region" was launched. It was created that veterans and their relatives could receive the necessary information in a convenient way.

About thisreportedIn the Ministry of Reintegration.

The chatbot contains the main and additional menu with sections in which users will be able to receive detailed information on legal and psychological assistance, benefits, payments and subsidies, employment, treatment, prosthetics, and volunteer assistance.

There are five sections on the Chat Botte menu:

  • "Servicemen with wounds".
  • "Veterans."
  • "Families of military and veterans".
  • "Psychological support".
  • Legal Assistance.
