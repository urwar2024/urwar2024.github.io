The management of the city of Bucha handed the defenders of Ukraine cars, special equipment, drones and charging stations for combat missions. This will help Ukrainian soldiers successfully perform their work to protect their homeland.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces.

Yes, a separate team of territorial defense of the Armed Forces, which participated in the reflection of the offensive of the enemy and the liberation of the city of Bucha, received assistance. The brigade received a passenger bus for transportation of personnel. This will improve logistics.

“We are grateful to the residents of Bucha and everyone who helps the Ukrainian army! We believe in our victory! ” - noted in the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Armed Forces.

We will remind, on Sunday, March 31, in Ukraine honor the second anniversary of the liberation of Bucha of the Kiev region from Russian invaders. PresidentcalledThe world does not forget how Ukrainians stopped Russian evil and then expel invaders from their land.
