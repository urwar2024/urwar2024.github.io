Head of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Lieutenant General Kirill Budanov commented on statements that Russian occupation troops could try to move back to Kiev again.

Kyrylo Budanov provided his comments during thelive etherOn March 31, 2024.

In particular, the question of the president on which the French President Emmanuel Macron relied on when he said that in the event of a breakthrough of Russians to Kiev or Odessa, French troops may be in Ukraine.

“You said it was his assumption. There can be a lot of assumptions. You know, in the assumptions we can also go to Moscow now. But there is no that in fact, ”the head of GUR replied.

He added that as of today, there are no threats on the territory of Belarus.

“There are no threats to Belarus in Belarus. And we do not see the tendency to make them appear in the near future, ”Kirill Budanov said.

As reported by ArmyInform, in March 2024, information about the alleged preparation of the Russians for an attack on Sumy region also spread through the media.
