In 2024, almost UAH 5.75 billion was laid in the State Budget for providing veterans and family members of fallen defenders and defenders. During the year, the government expects to pay compensation under three programs of the Ministry of Veterans and cover more than 2000 people.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

Today, the right to receive monetary compensation for the purchase of housing has:

  • persons with disabilities as a result of groups of group I and II (ATO/OOS/defense measures, participation in hostilities in the territory of other states, participation in the Revolution of Dignity);
  • IDPs who are participants in hostilities or persons with disabilities as a result of a group III (ATO/or OOS by June 1, 2018);
  • Members of the family of the person who died (disappeared) died as a result of the ATO/OOS/Defense Measures, participation in hostilities in the territory of other states.
