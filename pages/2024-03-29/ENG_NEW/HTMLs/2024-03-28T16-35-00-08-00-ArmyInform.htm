As of the beginning of March 2024, nearly 10,000 military Ukraine have been trained in France since the beginning of the conflict.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

As it is noted, in addition to supplying weapons and military equipment, France supports Kiev through training of the Ukrainian military to increase their skills according to their needs so that they can continue to defend their territory.

The department also noted that training is one of the aspects of French support of Ukraine together with the transfer of equipment and financial support.

Training is carried out in the territory of France and Poland, mainly within the framework of the European Union's military assistance mission to support Ukraine (EUMAM). In total, since the beginning of the conflict, France has prepared nearly 10,000 Ukrainian soldiers, including 8.8 thousand in 2023.

"We have opened doors to train pilots of Ukrainian fighters with several other European countries," said the President of the French Republic Emmanuel Macron.

Learning, first of all, occurs in three directions:

  • Training related to the tactics, implementation and maintenance of numerous equipment. Among this equipment, CAESAR guns, Crotale NG missiles, AMX 10-RC combat tanks, launching installations of jet fire and even medium-range air (Earth) system (SAMPT).
  • Specialized exercises aimed at critical know-how or know-how with high value added and cover different areas, such as health, logistics, material and intelligence.
  • Formation of tactical parts. Focused on fighting in cities and trenches, their goal is to strengthen the skills of Ukrainian officers to command a mouth or battalion.

"In 2024, the goal is to spread this training to the air and naval component of the forces of Ukraine," the General Staff emphasized.

As reported by the Armyinform, all over the front lineDefense boundaries are being built, install special antitrone equipment.
