Germany handed over a new military assistance package to Ukraine, which included artillery shells, tanks and mine equipment.

The relevant information is posted onSiteGerman government.

Ukraine received 18,000 155 mm ammunition, ammunition for Leopard 2A6 tanks, as well as 14 VECTOR reconnaissance UAVs and 30 RQG-35 Heidrun UAV reconnaissance UAV.

Germany also handed over a number of tracked and car equipment:

  • 5 Crowning and Evacuation Machines Warhog,
  • 9 Crowning Command Machines Warhog,
  • 6 Machines of Visent 1,
  • 2 bridgers of Beaver,
  • 1 Engineering Brem Bergepanzer 2,
  • 1 bromm 2a1 dachs,
  • 9 mine traces,
  • 3 cars to protect the border,
  • 6 Mercedes-Benz Zetros fuel tanks.

In addition, the help package included Satcom System, 330 infrared cameras, 2056 RGW90 Matador grenade launchers and 70 automatic GMG machine grenade launchers, 3,000 camouflage nets, 2 thousand ponchos and 2 emergency electric generators.

The German government also updated the list of assistance that Ukraine plans to provide. It, among other things, lists the missiles to Patriot, 20 MARDER combat armored vehicles and more.
