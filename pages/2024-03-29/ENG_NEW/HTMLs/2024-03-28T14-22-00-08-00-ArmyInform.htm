The Defense Procurement Agency has signed a contract with the Ukrainian enterprise for the supply of military equipment, and the state operator of the rear for the purchase of real property.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

“We are working to provide the Armed Forces of Ukraine with products of Ukrainian production. This applies not only to weapons, but also to various military equipment and rear property, which help the military to perform tasks more efficiently and to establish life, ”said Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Dmitry Klimenkov.

The director of the Defense Procurement Agency Marina Bezrukova noted that the development of domestic production is an important focus in the work of the agency.

"Contracts with Ukrainian manufacturers make it possible to receive the best price, the fastest supply and the opportunity to take into account the wishes from our military, while making a contribution to the development of the Ukrainian economy during a full -scale war," she said.

At the same time, the General Director of the Tyle Tyle State Operator Arsen Zhumadilov emphasized that the share of Ukrainian producers who supply rear goods for the Armed Forces of Ukraine is growing.

“Increasingly, medium and small manufacturers are the winners of the tenders, especially the purchase of real property. We will continue work aimed at increasing the share of the Ukrainian manufacturer in our purchases through better planning and concluding long -term contracts. We are already working on this with the Ministry of Defense, ”said Arsen Zhumadilov.

As reportedArmyinform, before supplying the Armed Forces of Ukraine, clothing and ammunition are tested for quality in three stages.
