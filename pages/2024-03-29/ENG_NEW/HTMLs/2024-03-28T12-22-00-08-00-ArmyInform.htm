Military counterintelligence of the Security Service disrupted attempts to "get" its agent to the headquarters of the Armed Forces Air Force Street.

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

It is noted that as a result of a special operation in Kyiv, an accomplice of the Russian special service was detained, who, under the guise of a chaplain, tried to join the Ukrainian paratroopers.

"The agent wanted to" enroll "in one of the combat crews of the DShV to spy in places of its base and move in the direction of the front line. Subsequently, the person had to establish a trusting relationship with the command of the units of the Air Force troops of the Armed Forces, ”the message reads.

The investigation found that in this way he hoped to expand his "capabilities" for gathering data on weapons, work plans and combat operations of Ukrainian defenders.

However, the SBU staff exposed the "mole" in a timely manner, installed his Russian curators and documented their intelligence activity.

"The next stage was the detention of the defendant, when he was already being interviewed at the headquarters of the Command of Ukrainian paratroopers for 'enlistment' in the ranks of the Armed Forces of Ukraine," the department said.

According to the investigation, the detainee is a chief specialist of one of the departments of the Kyiv Regional Military Administration.

"The official was remotely recruited by a staff member of the 317th Intelligence Center of the Main Directorate of the General Staff of the Russian Armed Forces (known as the GRU). In order to pretend to be a chaplain, the Kyiv resident made himself a fake "certificate" about allegedly belonging to the parishioners of one of the denominations," the SBU noted.

Law enforcers established that before that, the Russian special service gave him "test" tasks while he was working in the military administration.

“He supported communication with the aggressor through an anonymous chat in the messenger. During the detention, a mobile phone was removed from the traitor with evidence of his correspondence from the Russian GRU, ”he added in the department.

Currently, the person has reported suspicion under Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (treason, committed in martial law) and a preventive measure was selected - detention.

The investigation is ongoing. The attacker is threatened with life imprisonment.

The special operation was carried out with the assistance of the Kyiv Regional Military Administration and with the procedural guidance of the Prosecutor General's office.
