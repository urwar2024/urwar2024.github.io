The Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, Colonel-General Oleksandr Sirsky, had a telephone conversation with the chairman of the United States Committee on US Headquarters General Charles Brown.

About thisreportedGeneral Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

During the conversation, the needs of the Armed Forces of Ukraine in the coming months, the issue of necessary assistance for the United States for Ukraine, in particular regarding the strengthening of protection against Russian missile and bomb blows, the topic of fortifications, and some strategic issues for 2024 were analyzed.

General Charles Brown closely monitors the situation on the battlefield in Ukraine from the very beginning of the Russian invasion.

"Strengthening the Ukrainian army is a long -term strategic interest not only of the United States but also by NATO partners and allies," he said.

As reported by the Armyinform, Minister of Defense Rustem UmeroovHe spokeAt an extraordinary meeting of the Council of Ukraine - NATO, which took place at the request of our country and provided a detailed overview of the urgent needs of Ukraine's air defense.
