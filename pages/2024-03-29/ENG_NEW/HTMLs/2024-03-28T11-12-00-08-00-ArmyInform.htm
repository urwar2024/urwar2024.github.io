The Ministry of Reintegration has approved changes to the list of territories in which) hostilities or temporarily occupied by the Russian Federation.

About thisinformsMinistry of Reintegration of Temporarily Occupied Territories of Ukraine.

Yes, the Office with its orders of March 19, 2024 No.77and from March 22, 2024 No.79amended the list of territories on which (conducted) fighting or temporarily occupied by the Russian Federation.

“The specified list was approved in December 2022 and is updated periodically. The amendments to it are made in agreement with the Ministry of Defense on the basis of proposals of the respective regional, Kyiv city military administrations, ” - said in the Ministry of Reintegration.

You can read the updated liststhereandthere.

The order shall enter into force on the day of its official publication.

Thus, the territories referred to in the list are divided into territories temporarily occupied by the Russian Federation and the territories under which (conducted) hostilities. In turn, the territories in which the fighting was conducted is divided in the territory of possible hostilities and the territory of active fighting. Also, the list of retrospectively includes the territories in which hostilities were conducted and which were temporarily occupied since February 24, 2022 and at which hostilities or temporary occupation ended.
