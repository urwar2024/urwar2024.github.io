Civilian Russians discuss the forced involvement of Russian citizens in voting to ensure a high level of voter appearance at polling stations.

“Well, because from Moscow it came to an order to vote in the hospital. And they took the attachment there. Jeanne organized cars, ” - Rospovyda Zhinka.

“They said that we all come, otherwise we will not come, organize a departure to us. So you see, even all conditions. I did not want to go, but they are forced, ” - Doda ven.
