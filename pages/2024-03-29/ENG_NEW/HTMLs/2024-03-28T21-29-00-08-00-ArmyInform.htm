The first burials at the National Military Memorial Cemetery can take place in late summer - early autumn this year.

Asreported March 28The Cabinet of Ministers Acting Acting Acting of Ukraine Alexander Pryhong during the live broadcasting of the Uniform News TV.

“We begin preparatory work first. After the preparatory work, we expect to go to the direct construction of the first stage of the National Military Memorial Cemetery, ”said Alexander Pryhun.

The construction of the first stage is divided into two stages, in particular the first part is planned to be put into operation by the end of August - early September, which will allow the burial of the fallen Ukrainian heroes.

Oleksandr Pazhun noted that in the first stage they organize the cemetery, the square before entering the complex, the area for the ceremony, as well as the first sector for burials.

It is planned to fully build the entire complex of the National Military Memorial Cemetery by 2027.

As a reminder, at the end of March 2024, the state institution NVMK, which is the customer of the construction of the National Military Memorial Cemetery, registered the right to permanent use of land plots for its construction.

Earlier, the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine approved the procedure for using the funds provided in the state budget for the creation of a national military-memorial cemetery.

The National Military Memorial Cemetery is a nationwide place of honoring and perpetuating the memory of the fallen defenders and defenders of Ukraine.

The memorial complex involves the presence of a military cemetery with all the necessary ritual structures for the organization of honorary burials of the dead, which defended the independence, sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine.

A museum complex will also be built here, which will perpetuate the memory of the dead and record their contribution to the protection of Ukrainian statehood for future generations.

Asreported the armyinform, the National Military Memorial Cemetery received 265 hectares near Kyiv for permanent use.
