The Center and Cyber Department of the SBU found a network of spreading tools for enemy misinformation in Tiktok. Such accounts should be blocked in order not to be a victim of misleading information.

About thisReportsCenter for combating misinformation.

The department has prepared a list of channels that are engaged in the spread of enemy propaganda. They have personal channels of Russian propagandists, such as Vladimir Solovyov, Margarita Simonyan, etc., Kremlin media TASS, Vesti, REN TV, etc. The list includes the Russian artist Shaman and blogger Anatoly Shariy.

If you have discovered TikTok channel spreading enemy misinformation that is not on the list,- Write to mail -cpd@rnbo.gov.ua
