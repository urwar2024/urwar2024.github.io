The first stage of food supplies from marked DOT suppliers is successfully completed. More than 300 trucks have already been sent to military units throughout Ukraine.

About thisinformsThe state operator of the rear.

In the future, deliveries will be made weekly in accordance with the applications of military units.

The quality control of the products will be carried out first by the DOT team at the warehouses of suppliers and then when receiving the goods by the military.

"For the state operator of the rear it remains fundamentally important to comply with high standards of food quality and systematic support of the Armed Forces," - said in the department.

As reportedArmyinform, the procurement agency of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine "State Tale Operator" (DOT) has concluded contracts for food supply for the Armed Forces for the second quarter.

Recallthat DOT has changed the requirements for three categories of products to provide the Armed Forces.

Earlier a state -owned rear operatorconcludedwater supply contracts for the Armed Forces.
