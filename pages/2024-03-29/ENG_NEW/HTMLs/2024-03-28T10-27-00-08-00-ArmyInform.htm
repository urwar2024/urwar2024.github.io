Sweden has allocated 120 million crowns ($ 11.3 million) to help the Gaza, Ukraine and Sudan sector through the UN Food Program.

About thisinformsSweden's Ministry of Assistance and Foreign Trade.

It is reported that support will be allocated immediately.120 million Swedish crownsThey will be distributed equally between the three regions.

According to her, these funds will help reduce hunger in countries and save people's lives.
