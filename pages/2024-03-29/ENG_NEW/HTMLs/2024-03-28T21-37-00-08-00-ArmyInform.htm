The indictment was sent to court with respect to the contractor upon seizure of more than 350 thousand hryvnias of budgetary funds in the conditions of martial law.

About thisShe reported March 28Kyiv Regional Prosecutor's Office.

The investigation found that the contractor overstated the volume of construction works and materials used in the project documentation during the shelter in the high-rise building.

The actually completed works did not correspond to the data stated in the documents.

Thus, he illegally misappropriated budget funds in the amount of more than 350 thousand hryvnias. UAH.

The actions of the accused caused losses to the Vyshhorod territorial community in the specified amount.

Якreported the armyinform, entrepreneurs will be tried in Bukovina, who have appropriated 500 thousand UAH for repair of a military town.
