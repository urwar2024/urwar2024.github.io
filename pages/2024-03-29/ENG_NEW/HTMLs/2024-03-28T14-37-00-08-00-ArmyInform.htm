Personnel servicemen, law enforcement officers, war veterans, combatants and other preferential categories can buy housing in Chernivtsi and region with additional benefits through PrivatBank.

This is stated in a message onSitethe bank.

PrivatBank and Chernivtsi Regional Military Administration have signed a bilateral cooperation agreement in terms of compensation for interest on the purchase of housing under the state program of the Eosel.

In 2024, 4 million hryvnias were allocated for additional compensation in the regional budget.

In accordance with the conditions of interest compensation program for mortgage loans of certain categories of citizens in Chernivtsi region, for military, law enforcement officers and their families, the interest rate of 3% will be fully compensated at the expense of the regional budget.

For war veterans and their families, combatants, persons with disabilities as a result of war, families of fallen war veterans and for the dead defenders and defenders of Ukraine, the interest rate was reduced to 4% per annum.

The Eoselle program is implemented by the Ministry of Economy of Ukraine together with the Ministry of Defense and Ukrfinzit.

As reported by the Armyinform, last week loans for the purchase of housing under the Esosel programgot211 Ukrainians for a total amount of UAH 345 million, of which 113 are servicemen and security forces.
