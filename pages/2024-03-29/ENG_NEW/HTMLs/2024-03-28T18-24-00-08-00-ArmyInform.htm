Volodymyr Zelensky held a meeting on March 28 with a delegation of the French National Assembly, headed by Yael Bron-Pow.

About thisreportedPresidential official page.

“They discussed cooperation in the defense sphere and the primary needs of our soldiers. Particular attention was paid to the latest proposals by President Emmanuel Macron to expand our country's support, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

According to him, Ukraine appreciates recent support for the French Parliament Agreement on Security Cooperation between Ukraine and France.

As ArmyInform reported, almost10,000 Ukrainian military was trained in France.
