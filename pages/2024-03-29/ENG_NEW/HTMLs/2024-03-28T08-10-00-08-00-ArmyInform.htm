Last day, on March 27, Russia's troops fired at 17 settlements in Kherson region - two people were killed, four more were injured.

About thisreportedHead of Kherson Ova Alexander Proudin.

So, according to him, under the shelling of the enemy were Dudchany, Novovorontsovka, Mikhailivka, Obradokamyanka, Gavrilovka, Merry, Golden Beam, Kachkarkarivka, Antonovka, Stanislav, Dniprovskoye, Amber, Bilozer, Giant, Kizomis.

"The Russian military kissed the residential quarters of the settlements of the region, resulting in damaged 2 high -rise buildings and 10 private homes," said Alexander Proudin.

Also, the head of the Kherson OVA emphasized, a traffic infrastructure, a critical infrastructure, a shopping center, an outbuilding and a car were recorded.

"Due to Russian aggression, 2 people were killed, 4 more were injured," he said.

As reportedArmyinform, two women were injured in Zaporozhye as a result of a night attack.
