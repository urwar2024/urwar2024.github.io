Ratel Deminer is a mine trall that finds and neutralizes anti -personnel mines. He has received BRV1 status, which means that the development was determined by the priority for the defense forces.

About new equipmentreported March 28Viceremier-Minister for Innovation, Development of Education, Science and Technology-Minister of Digital Transformation of Ukraine Mikhail Fedorov

The complex is equipped with a day vision camera, which helps to control it better. Ratel Deminer destroys PFM-1, PMN-4, PMN-3 and PMN-2 mines.

The developers have already tested the Ratel Deminer in real conditions — the complex was used for humanitarian demining from anti-personnel mines near Sviatohirsk, Donetsk region.

It works remotely, and therefore saves the lives of the military. Now the complex is refining and increasing capacity.

This is not the first development from this manufacturer to go to Brave1. Ground work Ratel S and Ratel M received codifications, they are actively used in troops.

Development of defense technologies, the emergence of new developments have become possible due to the cluster Brave1.

Mikhail Fedorov urged to join the cluster of all who have a cool development that will strengthen the military. Brave1 will help to implement, test it, codify by NATO standards, attract investments and get state contracts.

Asreported the armyinform, Drone army eliminated more than 370 units of Russian military equipment in a week.
