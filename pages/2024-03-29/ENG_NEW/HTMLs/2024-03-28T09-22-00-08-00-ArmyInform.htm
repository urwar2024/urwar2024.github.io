Colonel Natalia Humeniuk commented on the combat situation near the village of Krynkya of Kherson region, noting that Ukrainian fighters continue to expand the bridgehead there.

Natalia Humeniuk provided her comments duringdirect broadcastOn the morning of March 28, 2024 in the morning of "One News".

According to a spokeswoman for the Defense Forces of South of Ukraine, the enemy continues in the said direction the assault actions - three assaults were recorded over the last combat day.

"They tried to act again, as our fighters joke, because armored vehicles in them are still banned in this direction. They act purely infantry storms, again collecting groups to the department, 10-15 people, from different directions try to approach our positions, ”Natalia Humeniuk said.

She stressed that the Ukrainian fighters manage to reflect these assaults, and the enemy's losses are in this direction near the crusts from 30 to 40 invaders.

“And these are irreversible and sanitary losses. And in this regard, it is very difficult for them to form the next units that will take such assaults. They have a very large imbalance of internal moral and psychological and they have to carry out permanent rotations, replacing those units that will carry out the following assaults in order to connect the uninitiated in the overall situation, ”-said the spokeswoman for the Defense Forces of South of Ukraine.

On the Ukrainian side, according to her, positions hold and work on the expansion of the bridgehead.

As reported by the Armyinform, the total number of liquidated Russian invadersis approachingup to 440 thousand.
