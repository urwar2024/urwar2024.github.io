On the morning of March 28, Russian invaders struck a rocket strike on Odessa. According to the Air Force of the Armed Forces, a Russian rocket flew to Odessa.

About thisreportedOleg Kiper.

“In the morning, the enemy struck rocket strokes. Previously - without the victims. The information will be supplemented, ”he wrote.

He also noted that at night from TU-22M3 aircraft, which came to the starting lines above the Black Sea, the Russians directed the X-22 missiles to the south of the Odessa region, and then released from the SU-30 aircraft and released a radiance rocket in the same direction.

"The missiles have lost their combat capacity in the sea," he said.

Then, according to Oleg Kiper, at about three o'clock in the morning, the enemy attacked the Odessa with shock drones. Two cheweds destroyed the air defense forces. People were not injured.
