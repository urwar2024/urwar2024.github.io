The President had a telephone conversation with the Speaker of the House of Representatives Mike Johnson.

AboutIt became knownfrom the message on the official page of the head of state.

Volodymyr Zelenskyy thanked Mr. Johnson, both parties, the American people and President Joe Biden for vital support from Ukraine from the beginning of the Russian invasion.

The President also spoke about the situation on the battlefield, in particular about the rapid intensification of Russian air terror.

“In the last week, Ukrainian cities and communities were hit by 190 rockets, 140 Shahheda and 700 cabin. The greatest Ukrainian hydroelectric power station has been displayed, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy reminded.

The President stressed that in this situation, the rapid adoption of assistance for Ukraine is critical for Ukraine.

"We are aware that the House of Representatives has different positions on how to do it, but the main thing is that the issue of assistance to Ukraine remained a factor in the association," said Volodymyr Zelenskyy.

The parties also discussed the need to overlap the sources from which Russia finances its war and the use of frozen Russian assets in favor of Ukraine.

"We also count on Congress leadership," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.
