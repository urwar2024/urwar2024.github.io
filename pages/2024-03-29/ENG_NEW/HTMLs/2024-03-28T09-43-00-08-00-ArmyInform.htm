In the city of Bucha, competitions at the Cup of Ukraine on hand -to -hand combat were held, dedicated to the memory of the special forces of Ukraine's military intelligence in the battle.

About thisinformsMain Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

More than two hundred participants from 15 regions took part in the event.

The event was organized by the All -Ukrainian Federation of hand -to -hand combat with the support of the GUR of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine and the Ministry of Youth and Sports of Ukraine.

As reportedArmyinform, on March 23, 2024, the All -Ukrainian Sambo Tournament named after GRU of the GUR of Ukraine Nazar Borovitsky - Hero of Ukraine (posthumously) was held in Kyiv.
