In all directions of the front, the construction of defense borders and structures continues.

This was reported by the command of support forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its ownFacebook page.

Servicemen of engineering troops of support forces of the Armed Forces almost constantly dig trenches, trenches, anti -tank ditches, build armor and equip other fortifications.

"Constant and intensive combat missions allows you to strengthen the defense bonds of the Defense Forces of Ukraine faster," the command said.

Fortification structures will protect people, combat equipment, ammunition from the enemy's defeat, and will serve as a refuge from adverse weather conditions and a place of rest for personnel.

Special antitrone equipment is installed on the connections.

The main task of the support engineers of support forces is to properly protect our soldiers.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Zhytomyr region on the area of the Ukrainian-Belarusian borderConstruction continuesadditional fortifications.

Also, nearly 7,000 Dragon teeth are installed in Dnipropetrovsk. In addition,Construction continuesfortifications in the Odessa region.
