The Security Service's cyberfactors disrupted a new attempt to strike a rocket strike on military and critical infrastructure of the Dnieper.

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

It is noted that thanks to a multi -stage special operation directly on the hot in the regional center, the FSB agent was detained, which conducted a reconnaissance near the hospital of defense forces.

“The attacker wanted to establish an indicative number of Ukrainian defenders who were in hospital. He also followed medical transport, which he arrived in the territory of the medical institution, ” - said in the department.

The investigation found that the information obtained together with the coordinates of the object was tried to transfer to the FSB through anonymous chat in the messenger.

Subsequently, the invaders planned to use intelligence to prepare a missile strike on a military hospital in the Dnieper.

“Another potential goal of the enemy was a local thermal power plant, which provides electricity to a large part of the metropolis. At the instructions of the FSB, their agent filmed on his own TV lelephone, as reported by the Armyinform, the perimeter of the TPP and the surrounding area, ”the SBU noted.

According to the investigation, such information was needed by the Russians to find out about the technical condition of the object to "take into account" in the preparation of a new series of air attacks on the city.

“The staff of the SBU“ step -by -step ”documented not only the intelligence activity of the FSB agent, but also identified the identity of his Russian curator. At the same time, comprehensive measures were taken on the territory of the Dnieper to protect the personnel of the defense forces and additional protection of critical infrastructure, ”the message reads.

According to the investigation, the agent was a 22-year-old resident, who in February this year was remotely recruited by a staffing officer of the Russian special service.

The "aggressor" of the new moon has been hit as an active user of Russian telegrams to find fast earnings. For his cooperation with the invaders, the traitor received a monetary "reward" for his own bank details.

During the searches, a mobile phone was removed with evidence of his criminal activity, as well as a bank card with Russian money.

Currently, investigators of the Security Service have reported the detainee of the suspicion under Part 2 of Art. 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (State betrayal committed in the conditions of martial law).

The attacker is in custody. He is threatened with life imprisonment.

The special operation was carried out by the SBU staff in Kyiv and Kyiv region under the procedural leadership of the Kyiv City Prosecutor's Office.

Asreported the armyinform, reported suspicion to two Russian generals who bombed Borodyanka.
