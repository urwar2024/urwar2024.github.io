The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine proposed to begin work on the legislative definition of the concept of "cyberwar".

As reportsTelegram channelThe defense department was discussed during the discussion at the All-Ukrainian Scientific and Practical Conference "Actual Problems of Management of Information Security of the State", organized by the National Academy of Security of Ukraine.

“The Defense Ministry has initiated research work on defining the concept of cyberwar, which is carried out by the SBU Academy. I am convinced that these developments will allow us, together with other state bodies, with the legislative proposals at the end of 2024, ” - said Deputy Minister of Defense Kateryna Chernoroenko.

Since the beginning of a full -scale invasion of the Security Service of Ukraine, almost 10,000 cyberattacks have been neutralized.

The actions of the aggressor in cyberspace are systematic and directed not only to Ukraine but also to its allies. Therefore, following the definition of the concept of "cyberwar" at the national legislative level - the international recognition of cyberattacks as an act of war in the legal field.
