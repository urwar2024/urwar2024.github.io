About it on airArmy TVThe representative of the press service of the 4th brigade of the operational purpose of the National Guard of Ukraine "Rubiz" Soldier Christina Warrior.

"All this is carried out with the support of aviation, artillery, just an innumerable number of KABs during the day in this direction. Christina Warrior separately noted the work of our UAV operators, who work when the defenders' positions flew in fact the swarms of enemy drones.

“Because the hostile direction is switched off, so we can also fly more FPV in contrast to their positions. We already have a similar solution for every innovation in the enemy, in most cases it is even better, and we can counteract the enemy, ”she added.
