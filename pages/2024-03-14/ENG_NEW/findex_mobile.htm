Each action is opposed. In our case, we can say more precisely that the production of some types of weapons and military equipment becomes the driver of the creation of protection systems from them. This is the situation with weapons-opening No. 1 in the Russian-Ukrainian war-unmanned aircraft and counteraction products.

Their one, not too developed subspecies-UAV-Flowers, reporter Armyinfor, was told by a well-known researcher of military history, equipment and weapons, Doctor of History, Professor of the National Academy of Land Forces named after Hetman Petro Sahaidachny Andriy Kharuk.

-Indeed, the current Russian-Ukrainian war gives rich food for historical analogies with the wars of the past,-Professor Haruk began our conversation. - Well, look: the emergence of aviation revolutionized intelligence in the First World War - and in our war such a revolution caused the rapid progress of drones. The next step in the development of aviation in the First World War was the appearance of bombers - and now drones are mastering shock functions. Do you catch logic? That's right-the appearance of drones-fighter became only a matter of time, as well as the appearance of fighter aircraft in the First World War,-said the weapons researcher.

- Andriy Ivanovich, in this particular case, an American hunter for the drones of Coyote can be an interesting object for research.

-This product is one of the first in the world (if not the first at all) of industrial-producing drones from the American concern "Reiton". Its companies "Bae System" and "Sensintel Ink.", But now release and further development is the Riteon.

The first Coyote Party in the Block 1b version was purchased by the US Army in July 2018-as part of an urgent operational need caused by an increase in losses from hostile drones-Kamikadze (barrow ammunition) in the Middle East.

- Professor, what exactly did these UAV attract representatives of the Pentagon?

-In the basic version of Coyote Block 1 is a six-kilogram dron-Kamikadze with an electric motor and a fragmentary-fragmental combat part with a contactless blast. Its length is 0.9 m, and the wingspan is 1.5 m.

Functional purpose is defined as "the destruction of enemy drones beyond the effective radius of the HRB and low -caliber guns." "Coyote" is optimized for the destruction of UAV (by American classification) of the 1st, 2nd and 3rd categories-political mass, respectively, up to 9 kg, from 9 to 25 kg and up to 600 kg.

The Coyote Self-Self-Improvement System is based on radio frequency scanning, and the preliminary detection of targets is provided by the Kumrfs ground radar (Ku-Band Multi-Mission RF System), which has four electronic scanning antennas and 7 km.

-But the manufacturers of the municipal ammunition-Kamikadze was constantly upgraded the drone park. What did the engineers of the Riteon concern answer?

- Coyote evolved quickly. In May 2019, Block 2 appeared, which is characterized by a cruciform plumage and a radically altered power unit. Instead of an electric motor with a push screw, a jet engine and four starting accelerators were installed. Due to this, the maximum speed has almost tripled - from 130 to 370 km/h. The flight range reaches 10 km.

In the Block 2+ version, the speed is increased to 555 km/h, and the onboard equipment is introduced into the onboard equipment, which prevents accidental damage to your own drones.

- The second generation of the second generation was established by the third series of these drones. What is their feature?

- Block 3, which has developed since 2020, unlike the first two, it is multiple and modular. An alternative to the fragile-fugitive combat part is the combat part of the "necinotic" type.

Details on its principle have not yet been published. It can be assumed that such a combat part is a miniature station of the IP, which is able to interrupt the line of communication between the enemy drone (or swarm of drones) and the management station.

-Various wheel platforms are used to start these drones. Let us stop on their equipment.

-Coyote is one of the means of damage in the Anti-Nitrone systems of M-Lids (Mobile, Low, Slow, Unmanned Aircraft Integrated Defeat System) and FS-Lids (Fixed Site Lids). The M-Lids was initially tested on the FMTV truck chassis, which was installed by the Kumrfs radar and two four-term Coyote. However, the choice was made in favor of M-ATV cars.

The machine is installed remotely controlled module MOOG RLWP with 30 mm automatic Khm914E1, 7.62-mm M240 machine gun and a two-charged launch installation. The machine is equipped with a Kumrfs radar and an electronic optical touch unit S3 (Italian company Leonardo).

It complements it with a second M-ATV complex and a remote control module with a 12.7 mm machine gun M2NV. The FS-Lids stationary complex is a touch platform with Kumrfs-T radar (range increased to 15 km) and a fire platform (or several platforms).

-Which units of the US Army are oriented these drones and how did they show themselves in hostilities?

-In October 2022, Reitonion received a contract for equipping the first two divisions of the US Army (1st Cavalry and 82nd Airborne) radars by Kumrfs and Koyot drones. At the beginning of 2023, the Pentagon ordered an additional number of these intended tools for the central command (use in the Middle East).

Probably M-Lids and (or) FS-Lids were already used in Syria and Iraq, because on January 20, 2023, the Central Command reported the destruction Homs province).

On December 18, 2023, Pentagon announced the intention to order from Reiton's 6,000 Drones "Koyot" Block 2, 700-Block 3, 252 FIM modules FS-Lids, 25-M-Lids, as well as 151 Kumrfs radar (118 in 118 in stationary performance and 33 - in mobile). In case of conclusion of the contract of supply will be carried out in 2025-2029.

The US Navy is also interested in Kiota. And in December 2022, the FS-Lids entered the international weapons market-these systems ordered Qatar (10 systems and 200 koyot drones).

In general, Coyote, especially in the M-LIDS or FS-Lids complex, is an interesting means of combating the growing threat from the UAV. Undoubtedly, the forces of Ukraine's defense are in dire need of such complex systems.

Currently, one of the most common options for resolving a housing issue among servicemen is to obtain a preferential loan within the Eosel program.

Only in the past week wasIssued173 Credits. 90 of them received servicemen and other security forces. The Armyinform tells which of the servicemen can get a preferential loan, on what conditions and what to do.

Changes have made changes throughout its existence over the course of its existence. For the most part, the expansion of the circle of those who can receive preferential loans. Currently, the following options are available for military personnel (both current and former):

Mortgage under 3%

- Contract Service Soldiers

Mortgage under 7%

- participants of hostilities

- War veterans and their families

- families of dead (dead) veterans and defenders

- people who have a disability due to hostilities

General requirements for those who plan to get a preferential loan under the program:

  • citizenship of Ukraine;
  • age - from 18 to 70 years (it should be no more than 70 years at the time of repayment of the mortgage);
  • solvency (official employment and payment of taxes);
  • absence in sanctions;
  • do not have their own housing or it is located in the occupied territories;
  • have real estate less than 52.5 square meters. m + 21 square meters. m for each family member;
  • They do not participate in other state housing programs.

The loan period is up to 20 years. Initial contribution - from 20%.

Housing requirements:

It can be an apartment or house located in the territory of Ukraine (except for the occupied territories and places where active fighting is ongoing).

If the apartment in Kiev, or in the regional center, it should be in operation at the time of conclusion of the contract for no more than 10 years. This also applies to residential buildings.

Regulatory area for the apartment - no more than 52.5 square meters. m of total area and additional 21 square meters. m per family member.

For an apartment building, the normative area is greater - 62.5 square meters. m of total area and additional 21 square meters. m per family member.

The cost of housing should not exceedThe average cost of housing in the regions of Ukraine,determined by the Minregionand multiplied by certain coefficients:

  • 2,5- for Kiev, Dnipro, Lviv, Odessa and Kharkiv;
  • 2-for regional centers and settlements located around them in the 15-kilometer zone, as well as cities in which more than 300 thousand people live;
  • 1,75- for cities with a number of residents 100-300 thousand;
  • 1- for other settlements.

If the cost exceeds the program limits, then this difference can be covered by increasing the initial contribution. Before concluding an agreement, they must be evaluated.

Additional credit payments:

We need to be prepared for the fact that, in addition to the first contribution, you will still need to make additional payments:

  • Credit Commission;
  • compulsory state pension insurance fees;
  • real estate insurance (mortgage object);
  • service of the appraiser;
  • notary services;
  • Commission for non -cash transfer of credit funds from the client's account in favor of economic entities and individuals;
  • the commission for opening a current account, in particular for servicing the loan;
  • Administrative meeting for re -registration of ownership in the State Register of Property Rights.

It is difficult to say how much you will have to pay. It depends on the amount of the loan, as well as the rates for services in each specific bank.

How to decorate:

The first and main step is to apply for action. The following is to receive proposals from banks and to choose the one you plan to work with. Verification of data and documents. Searching for a home you want to buy. Concluding an agreement with a bank, as well as a contract of sale.

- You have a volunteer since 2014, you have visited all the front areas. How did the war change over these 10 years?

"Everyone knows that at first I helped a military hospital. Later, my brother was mobilized, and I began to help his brigade. That was the first time I went to the front and could not stop.

Speaking of volunteers who began to help since 2014 and have been in this area for 10 years, we have not left so much ...

Remembering the war when it just started and now, these are radically different things. At that time, there was no aviation, there was no drone war, there were safe zones where you could drive relatively calmly at night.

Of course, a curiosity happened once. During the night trip, my driver accidentally turned on the dimensions for a moment - and a hostile AGS began to work on us.

However, we could come to our defenders to the front edge and stay with them for several days, because volunteers should not only close physical needs, but also support for the military, be able to listen and pray.

Starting from the large-scale invasion, we can no longer drive in like this. Of course, during these two years, we went to Bakhmut, Ocheretyne, and were under fire in Chasiv Yar, but we are no longer allowed into the trenches.

— Comparing the needs of the military at the beginning of 2022 and now, how have they changed and what are the main needs of defenders now?

- When the "wide -scale" just started, I saw my entire entrance escaped. Then I started crying very bitterly, because I realized that our boys now shed their blood and give their lives under all this invasion and shelling of all weapons ...

Then I pulled myself together and began to help with all my might. At that time, our army was growing rapidly, and everyone lacked bulletproof vests, helmets, sleeping pads, equipment, uniforms, and combat boots.

Later, there were cars, thermal imagers, drones and walkie-talkies. We tried to help people who were in occupied Bucha and Irpin. One of our volunteers was killed, the other is still missing...

Currently, the Alla Martyniuk Charitable Foundation focuses on the purchase of mavics, walkie-talkies, FPV drones and cars. In 2022, we managed to help the Armed Forces of Ukraine in the amount of $7 million, but in 2023, people have already donated less, and this amount was almost $2 million. Also, in two years, we have purchased 400 pickup trucks, which are so necessary for our defenders on the front line.

- What directions does your charity fund cover? Maybe there will be new projects?

- In addition to volunteer activity, I take care of the children of Donbass. These are orphanages where there are special needs children. We have the rehabilitation of the military, their families and children affected by the war in the nearest plans.

At the moment, we have groups of defenders, they are going to Kyiv or to Zakarpattia. There, a rehabilitation therapist from the Institute of Orthopedics works with them, who provides massages and performs special physical exercises for the wounded, as well as a psychologist and psychotherapist work with the military.

We plan to open a large center on the basis of a state institution that will work on the camp principle. In one part will be engaged in military, and in the other - women and children.

I have experienced for myself what it is like when your loved one does not get in touch, you are constantly waiting for a message and cannot find a place for yourself.

We also started production of buggy ... They will have seats against infantry mines, armored glass and tureli, from which it will be possible to shoot.

It will have six sitting places, which is quite convenient for the evacuation of the wounded. The advantage will be that such buggies will be much cheaper and more powerful than those that are already on the market.

"The heart and soul do not change, so it is impossible to get used to the death of children, pregnant and young"

- What is the most scary for you in this Great War?

— News of the death of our young boys and the death of children and pregnant women. When there are rocket attacks or Shahed drones that hit houses, and then the whole world sees these terrible images, my soul screams frantically.

Before our defenders left Avdiivka, we went to the military from the 3rd separate assault brigade. All there are young boys, they are 18-20 years old. We laughed, hugged, joked.

They have not seen life yet, they just have just ahead, and then you will find out that they died in two days ... In 10 years, it may be worth getting used to, but the heart and soul do not change, and it is never impossible to get used to it.

"The wounded man himself withstood the assault of 20 ruscists so that his comrades withdrew, and he died..."

— What moments since the beginning of the large-scale invasion are etched in your memory for the rest of your life?

- One of the moments happened when battles in the city itself were already in Bakhmut. Then we went to our combat doctors to the stabbon. Of course, the headlights are switched off, no connection, and only the arrivals were heard.

When we came to the meeting place, I saw the lights from cigarettes, realizing that someone was smoking. I tell them whispers, "There is a Sergei here?" And how did they start shouting: "Martynuchka !!! You came! ”.

Such moments are actually worth their weight in gold there. Then we went to the "stabik" and saw all the horrors that were happening there: a lot of seriously wounded people who had just been brought back from the battle, someone needed amputation... Such moments always remain in your soul.

There was a story about one hero who had already been wounded, and in order for his comrades to get out of the position, which was invaded by 20 soldiers of enemy infantry, he stayed and repulsed the assault himself.

Many of the Russian military he "supersed", but died to save those who became his family in the battles ...

My friends also told me that one of the commanders went to the side of the enemy to pick up the body of the brother, because he promised his wife that he would return him.

This commander took off his armor, helmet, raised his hands up and just went after his fallen friend ... The invaders were so shocked by his act that they stopped fire.

He managed to get the body of his comrade, take it back to our side so that this soldier could be buried with dignity. He kept his word...

"Enemy drones fly like bees"

— What was your last trip to the East?

— We went to the village beyond Kupiansk, which is the outermost in front of the enemy. That trip was difficult, because the situation is extremely difficult now. The guys say that they desperately need electronic warfare equipment.

The occupiers now have just a lot of drones. The defenders said that they swoop in like bees and just buzz over you. There is nowhere to get away from them, and this is where electronic warfare is needed, which will plant enemy "birds".

In addition, the military says that the enemy has improved his drones-Kamikadze so that they already have thermal imaging and artificial intelligence.

That is, the pilot is not even needed to control this drone. Unfortunately, these are all new technologies, and we really need to counteract such tricks of the enemy.

As a result of the Russian rocket attack on Kryvyi Rih, which occurred on March 12, more than 400 apartments were damaged in 31 multi -storey buildings, two kindergartens, five schools, two vocational establishments, a shop and other city facilities.

About thisreportedChairman of the Defense Council of Kryvyi Rih Alexander Vilkul.

According to him, there were three hits, including a nine -storey building and a five -storey building.

As a result of a hostile blow5 people were killed. At once - two women and a man. Another woman died at night from the injuries. And in the afternoon, in the analysis of the rubble on one of the floors, another dead man was discovered.

In total, 50 residents of the city sought medical help, including 12 children. At the same time, the infant-a 2-month-old boy and a 11-month-old girl-were also injured.

There are now 10 adults in hospitals (6 of them are heavy, with one woman very difficult) and 7 children (1 of them are difficult). Doctors fight for their lives, all necessary help is provided. Three people are discharged, one child who was in serious condition has been stabilized and now in a state of moderate.

Work on eliminating the effects of blows is ongoing. 25 teams of public utilities and 22 units of communal equipment are involved.
