President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky thanked the Lithuanian colleague of Gitanas Nausened, who declared support for a discussion on the possible placement of foreign troops in Ukraine.

The head of statewroteIn social networks H.

“Dear, Gitanas Nauseda, thank you for explaining it clearly. Ukraine must win this war so that the rest of Europe can remain peaceful, ”the message reads.

Volodymyr Zelenskyy added that in order to win the war, Ukrainian soldiers must have all the necessary tools.

"We are sure that we can do it with friends such as Lithuania, France and all our partners," he said.

Hitanas Nauseda said on Wednesday that I congratulate the idea of sending missions to the Ukrainian territory, and believes that in Europe it is necessary to discuss it.

He also urged the event to stop drawing "red lines", as it only helps Putin, "who begins to think that we are predictable and that he can control and manipulate us."

French President Emmanuel Macron allowed the departure of Western troops to Ukraine last week, although he stressed that there were no consensus on the allies.

In France, it was later explained that there was no question of the participation of foreign military personnel in hostilities.

As reported by the Armyinform, March 13 Gitanas Naussed in ParisHe had a meetingwith Emmanuel Macron to discuss ways to accelerate support for Ukraine.
