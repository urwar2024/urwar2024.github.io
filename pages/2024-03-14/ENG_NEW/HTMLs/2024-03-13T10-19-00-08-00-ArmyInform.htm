However, the SBU staff documented the criminal acts of the malefactor, and then established her whereabouts and detained.

As the investigation established, a local resident was an enemy hostile, who, after seizing part of Kharkiv region, went to the territory of the region under Ukraine -controlled Ukraine.

During the search at the place of detention, the mobile phone was removed with evidence of criminal activity.

The malefactor is in custody. She faces up to 12 years in prison.
