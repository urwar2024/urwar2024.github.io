In the Rivne region is the construction of non -dubbed barriers and platoon support points in the Varas and Sarny districts.

About thisreportedHead of the Rivne Regional Military Administration Oleksandr Koval.

"In the north of the region we continue to equip defense borders," he said.

According to Oleksandr Koval, all defensive lines are carried out in close coordination with the military. So, in particular, builders carry out earthworks, arrange communication passages, and so on.

The works occur in accordance with the approved schedule.

The head of Rivne Ova emphasized that reliable fortification is now one of the priority areas of their work.
