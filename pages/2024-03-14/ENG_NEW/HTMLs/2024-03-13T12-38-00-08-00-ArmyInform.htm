The United States intends to make progress on the issue of providing Poland with a direct loan for weapons in the amount of $2 billion, as well as selling almost a hundred Apache combat helicopters.

About thisIt is saidon the White House website.

"In a meeting with Polish leaders, President Joe Biden shared that the United States will promote a new $2 billion foreign military financing direct loan to Poland to strengthen its security and support its defense modernization, using previously allocated funds from Congress," the statement said.

It is noted that this loan will allow Poland to purchase additional US defense equipment, maintain jobs in the United States and deepen the US and Poland in the security.

Joe Biden also added that the United States will offer to sell 96 AH-64 Apache helicopters to Poland.

"This is an important step to giving Poland's armed forces for self -defense, strengthening NATO's operational compatibility and further strengthening the US defense industry," he said.
