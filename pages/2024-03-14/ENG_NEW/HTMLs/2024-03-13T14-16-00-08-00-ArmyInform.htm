Russian troops reduced the activity of aviation in the Tavriysk direction. In particular, yesterday, the enemy caused 17 aircraft (least in the last ten days).

About thisreportedGeneral Alexander Tarnavsky, commander of the operational-strategic group of Tavriya troops.

According to him, our warriors continue to conduct a defense operation. The situation in this direction remains difficult but controlled.

“64 combat clashes took place over the past day. The enemy made 5 missile strokes, made 1215 shelling and 107 kamp-Kamikadze shots, ”the message reads.

As reported by ArmyInform, the total losses of the Russians over the past daymake up: in manpower — 387 people; in weapons and military equipment — 45 units, not including UAVs. In particular, these are 2 tanks, 10 armored fighting vehicles, 12 artillery systems, MLRS, 16 cars and 3 units of special equipment. 297 UAVs of various types were neutralized by electronic warfare or shot down.

Ukraine's defense forces also destroyed the composition of ammunition and 5 more important enemy objects.
