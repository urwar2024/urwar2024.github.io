President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky celebrated 118 defenders of Ukraine state awards, 22 of them posthumously.

Appropriatedecree№154/2024 was published on the website of the Head of State.

The President decided to award defenders for personal courage, revealed in the protection of state sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine, and for selfless performance of military duty.

The Head of State awarded the President of Ukraine's distinction "Cross of Merit", Order of Bohdan Khmelnitsky III degree, "for courage" of the 3rd degree, awarded medals "for military service of Ukraine", "Defender of the Fatherland", "For Savior Life".

As reported by the Armyinform, March 12 President of Ukraine Volodymyr ZelenskyyHe noted state awards425 defenders of Ukraine, 236 of them - posthumously.
