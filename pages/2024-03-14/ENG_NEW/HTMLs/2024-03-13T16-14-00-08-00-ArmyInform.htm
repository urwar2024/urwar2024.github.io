The Russian occupation troops of the Russian Federation on March 13 attacked the city of Vovchansk in Kharkiv region. The shelling damaged a two -storey building.

About thisinformsNational Police of Ukraine.

“Around 15:00 the police received a report on the shelling of the city of Vovchansk. According to preliminary data, the blow was applied by a managed airbot. A two -storey building was damaged. People may be under the rubble, ”the message reads.

An investigative team, criminalists and explosives left the scene.
