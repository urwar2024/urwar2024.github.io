Tomorrow in Kryvyi Rih there will be a day of mourning for the dead as a result of the Russian attack on the evening of March 12.

About thisreportedChairman of the Defense Council of Kryvyi Rih Alexander Vilkul.

“Tomorrow, March 14, the day of mourning will be announced in Kryvyi Rih for the dead as a result of an hostile terrorist attack in our city. Sympathy for the relatives and relatives of the dead, ”he wrote.

As reportedArmyinform,On March 12, an explosion was heard in Kryvyi Rih during the announced alarm. The rocket attack of the Russian Federation caught fire.

The number of victims of the Russian blow to Kryvyi Rihincreased to four people.43 people, including 12 children, were injured in varying severity.
