The local resident informed the enemy every week about the location of the Armed Forces firing positions.

About thisreported March 13Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, from May to July 2023, a resident of Pokrovskaya of Donetsk region transferred to the employee of one of the military units of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation the direction of movement of equipment and personnel of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

He did it in the forbidden social network "Classmates".

In order to assist the occupiers in the fighting against Ukrainian defenders, a man sent a "curator" with a detailed description of the places of artillery fire every week, the number of artillery fire at the units of the Armed Forces, the number and directions of movement and gas trucks.

In addition, in his reports, he noted the geolocation of road infrastructure and engineering facilities of the region, which are important for the country's defense capability. He also described in detail the area around rail and bridges.
