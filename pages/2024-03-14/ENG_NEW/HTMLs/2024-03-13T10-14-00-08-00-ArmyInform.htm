According to the plan for January-February 2024, the State Budget General Fund provides and financed the security and defense sector in the amount of UAH 307.1 billion, which is 100% of the plan.

About thisinformsNSDC of Ukraine.

In addition, in January-February, the Cabinet of Ministers additionally distinguished for urgent needs for the subjects of the Sector of Security and Defense of Ukraine from the reserve and special funds of the state budget overUAH 5 billion.

Also, in the first two months of this year, the payment of financial security, other social payments to the personnel of the security and defense sector of Ukraine was fully paid.

“As the analysis shows, the subjects of the Security and Defense Sector of Ukraine in some places in some places inefficient and irrationally use budgetary funds. Some trends in the use of financial resources that may complicate the provision of security and defense forces informed the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine to take appropriate measures, ”the department said.

It should be noted that in January-February bodies of the Sector of Security and Defense of Ukraine at the expense of the General Fund of the State Budget are financed in full.

"In the context of martial law and general mobilization of the functioning of the budget system, ensuring the needs of national security and defense of Ukraine, taking into account the peculiarities specified in paragraphs 22-231 of the Final and Transitional Provisions of the Budget Code of Ukraine (as amended),"-summed up in the NSA.

As reportedArmyinform, the government has allocated additional funding for the Armed Forces teams.
