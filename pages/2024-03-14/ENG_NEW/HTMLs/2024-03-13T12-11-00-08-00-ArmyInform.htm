On the night of March 13, Russia hit the city with a Grim-E1 rocket. The five -storey building was partially destroyed, under the rubble of which people were.

About thisinformsNational Police of Ukraine.

The attack on the residential sector happened around 23:00. Rescuers and police came to the rescue of people.

“The bodies of two inhabitants-a 68-year-old man and a 74-year-old woman-were removed from the rubble. Five people were injured: three men 19, 26, 44 and two women 46 and 77, ”the message reads.

In addition, four apartment buildings and nine civilian cars were damaged.

The National Police added that the investigative team, explosive equipment worked on the site all night and morning. Law enforcement officers collected evidence of a war crime and received appeals from the victims.

It is known that the Grim-E1 hybrid rocket was created on the basis of the X-38 Soviet missile. Its maximum lesion can be 120 km.
