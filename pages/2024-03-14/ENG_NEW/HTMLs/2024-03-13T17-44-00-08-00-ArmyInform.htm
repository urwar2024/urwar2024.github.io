The order approving the form of decisions that give permission for entry, stay, residence, work and access to the border strip came into force.

About thisShe reported March 13State Border Service of Ukraine.

Persons who are planning to be within the border strip turn torelevant SSSU units, and they make a decision on granting permission, refusal to grant a permit or invalidate the permit.

At the same time, the construction, cutting of trees, video and photography, tourist travel and other activities should also be carried out with the permission and informing of the border department.

Separately defined category of persons who do not need to receive such permits:

- persons who enter the border lane to cross the state border at checkpoints (control points) across the state border;

- drivers of trucks and persons who are accompanied by who enter the border strip for the purpose of multimodal transportation;

— persons whose movement begins and ends outside the border zone, who move by motor transport on public roads of state importance, by rail transport by railways, some of which pass through the areas where the border strip is established, without making stops, exiting at railway stations in the border zone.

Earlier, the Government established a special border regime and prohibited the free entry, stay, residence, movement of persons and the conduct of works not related to the defense or protection of the state border within land plots 30-50 meters wide along the line of the state border on land, along the bank of the Ukrainian part of the border river, lake or other body of water, and along the line of the state border of Ukraine with the Russian Federation and the Republic of Belarus — up to 2 kilometers wide, which are provided for permanent use to military units of the State Border Guard Service for the construction, arrangement and maintenance of engineering and technical and fortification structures, fences, border signs, border clearings, communications.
