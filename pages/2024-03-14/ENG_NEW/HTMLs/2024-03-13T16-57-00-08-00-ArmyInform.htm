President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky awarded the Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Defense of Estonia, Kusta Salma and Commander of the Defense Forces of Estonia General Martin Herem Order of Merit of II degree.

About thisIt is saidOn the Twitter Defense Office.

"President Zelensky awarded the Custi Salma and General Heme to the Order of Merit of the II degree for a significant personal contribution to strengthening cooperation, supporting Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity and promoting the development of this worldwide," the post reads.
