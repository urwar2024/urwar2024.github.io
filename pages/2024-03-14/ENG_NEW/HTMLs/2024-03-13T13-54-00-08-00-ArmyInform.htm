Andrii Yusov, a representative of the Defence Intelligence of Ukraine, commented on the recent statement of the Russian Volunteer Corps, Freedom of Russia and Siberian Battalion, calling it very significant.

Andriy Yusov provided his comment during live broadcasting at the only news on March 13, 2024.

“As soon as a joint statement of the RDC, the Legion of Freedom of Russia and the Siberian Battalion, calling for the inhabitants of Kurshchyna and Belgorod region to be evacuated from these territories. And, in fact, calls to the authorities of these regions to save the lives of their inhabitants. I think this is a very serious and significant statement, ”the GUR spokesman said.

He commented on the raid in the specified areas of Russia of RDC, Legion "Freedom of Russia" and "Siberian Battalion", whose fighters are fighting on the side of the Armed Forces of Ukraine against the Russian occupation army and Putin's regime.

“Of course, these events are of serious and military importance and political. I think that yesterday, thousands and millions of Ukrainians simply looked at the heroic actions of the RDC fighters, the Legion "Freedom of Russia", "Sibbat", because they show that the one who brings war to another's house will call it. And this is what Putin did and what the Russians felt for these days, ”Andrey Yusov said.

As reported by the Armyinform, on March 12, 2024, Andrei Yusov during a live broadcast in the Telernahon "One News", answering the question of the presenter, whether Gur knows what happened to Il-76, exactlystated, all military incidents that have become more frequent in Russia, including combat clashes with the participation of the Russian Volunteer Corps and Siberian Battalion units in Belgorod and Kursk regions, is a consequence of the aggressive actions of the Russian authorities against Ukraine.
