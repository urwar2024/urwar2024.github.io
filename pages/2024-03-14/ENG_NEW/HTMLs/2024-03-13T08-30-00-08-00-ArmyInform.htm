In the sky above the Baltic, the German military discovered the Russian IL-20 aircraft.

About thisReportsGerman Air Force on Twitter.

The military department emphasized that the Russian IL-20 flew with a radio off and without a flight plan.

“Today, Eurofaiters from the German Air Force have gone for another task. The IL-20 aircraft was identified without a flight plan and radio, ”the military said.

As reportedArmyinform, recently France raised the Mirage fighter to intercept the Russian military aircraft.
