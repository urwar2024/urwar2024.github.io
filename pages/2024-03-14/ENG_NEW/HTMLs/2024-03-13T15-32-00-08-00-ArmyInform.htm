Representatives of Ukraine and Finland held another round of negotiations on the conclusion of a bilateral security agreement.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

"Another round of negotiations with Finland on the conclusion of a bilateral security agreement was held to implement the G7 joint declaration on support of Ukraine," the statement reads.

During the negotiations, the parties continued to work on the project of the agreement, outlined its boundaries and key elements, and also agreed on the schedule of further negotiations.

The Ukrainian official delegation, according to the Decree of the President of Ukraine, is headed by the head of the office of the Head of State Andriy Yermak. It is noted that today the round of negotiations was held by the Deputy Head of Presidential Office Igor Zhovkva. The delegation includes representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Defense and the Ministry of Strategic Industry of Ukraine, etc.

The Finnish side was represented by a delegation of government officials headed by representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
