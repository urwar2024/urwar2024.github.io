Russian troops fired on the border community of Sumy region on Wednesday, resulting in a man and two women were injured.

About thisreportedThe Prosecutor General's Office.

According to the investigation, on March 13, at 2:00 pm, the enemy carried out a mortar shelling of the civilian infrastructure of the Velikopysariv community of Okhtyrsky district.

As a result of the attack of the invaders, a 54-year-old peaceful resident was killed, two women were injured.

Under the procedural guidance of the Okhtyr District Prosecutor's Office, a pre -trial investigation into the violation of the laws and customs of war, combined with intentional murder (Part 2 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine) was started.

Prosecutors in cooperation with other law enforcement officers record the consequences of shelling. In the future, the materials of criminal proceedings will be transferred to the UBSU in the Sumy region.
