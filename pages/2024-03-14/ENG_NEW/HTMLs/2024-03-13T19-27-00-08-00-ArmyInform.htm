On the seven hundred and forty -nine days of large -scale armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine there were 70 combat clashes. In total, the enemy struck 2 rocket strokes and 68 aviation aircraft strikes, and made 37 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces, reports the Armyinform.

In Volyn and Polissya directionsOperational situation without significant changes. No signs of formation of offensive groups were detected.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage and intelligence activities, fired settlements from the territory of the Russian Federation, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border of Ukraine. The enemy struck in areas of settlements Vilna, Velika Pisarivka, Popivka of Sumy region; Vovchansk Kharkiv region. About 30 settlements, among them Rozhkovichi, Golubivka, Mid-Buda, Krasnopillya, Zapsillya, Popivka of Sumy region; Graniv, Sagittarius, Borisovka of Kharkiv region.

In Kupyansk directionOur defenders repelled 4 enemy attacks in the areas of the settlements of Tabaivka, Kharkiv oblast, and Stelmakhivka, Luhansk oblast, where the enemy tried to dislodge units from their positions. It carried out an airstrike in the area of the settlement of Nadiya, Luhansk oblast. More than 10 settlements, including Synkivka, Ivanivka, and Berestove of the Kharkiv oblast, came under artillery and mortar fire.

In the estuary directionOur soldiers repelled 5 enemy attacks in the areas of Terny and Yampolivka settlements of the Donetsk oblast, where the enemy tried to break through the defenses of our troops. The enemy launched an airstrike in the area of Bilohorivka, Luhansk oblast. More than 10 settlements were affected by artillery and mortar shelling, including Bilohorivka, Luhansk oblast; Terny ̧ Spirne, Rozdolivka, Donetsk region.

In the Bakhmut direction, The defense forces reflects 3 enemy attacks in the areas of the Eastern ravine, Bogdanivka and Klyshchiyivka of Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to improve his tactical position. The enemy struck aviation in areas of settlements Predict, New York, Friendship of Donetsk region. Also under artillery and mortar fire were about 10 settlements, including Bogdanivka, the times of Yar, New York of Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders reflect 14 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Berdychi, Orlovka, Tonenke, Pervomaisk Donetsk region. The enemy struck in areas of settlements Alexandropil, Ceramics, Novobakhmutivka, Novoselivka First, Novoaleksandrivka of Donetsk region. About 20 settlements, among them Alexandropil, Ceramik, Novobakhmutivka, Semenivka, Donetsk region, have experienced artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the districts of Krasnogorivka, St. George, Novomikhailivka, Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops 19 times. The enemy also struck in areas of settlements Maksimilyanivka, Vodyane, Maximivka of Donetsk region. Under the artillery and mortar fire of the invaders were about 20 settlements, including Krasnogorivka, St. George, Paraskovivka, Village of Donetsk region.

In Orikhiv direction the enemy, with the support of aviation, 5 times attacked the positions of our defenders in the districts of Staromayorsky Donetsk region, western Verbov and robot Zaporizhzhya region. Also, the enemy struck in the areas of settlements of Neskachny Donetsk region and Mala Tokmachka of Zaporizhzhya region. About 20 settlements, including Poltava, Red, White, and the robots of the Zaporozhye region, have experienced artillery and mortar shelling.

In the area of ​​responsibility, "Odesa", in the Kherson direction, the enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. Thus, during the day the enemy performed 2 unsuccessful assault actions on the positions of our defenders. About 30 settlements, including Tyaginka, Ivanivka, Sadove, Mykolaivka, Kherson region, have experienced artillery and mortar shelling.
