Russian invaders continue to fire the border areas of Chernihiv and Sumy regions. In total, 91 shelling (435 explosions) from different types of weapons were charged during the day.

About thisinformsOperational Command "North".

In the Chernihiv region shellings were held by: Semeniv community (5 villages), Snovsk community (3 villages), Horodnyansk community (2 villages).

In the Sumy region, the city of Sumy was under fire. There were 1 explosion, probably Shahaned UAV. As a result of the shelling, 8 civilians were injured and information is specified. A five -storey apartment building was damaged.

Sumy district was also under enemy fire, in it Russia fired at 20 settlements. In the Okhtyrsky district, 9 villages shuddered from the explosions, and in the Shostka district for the last days of the Russian troops hit 8 settlements.

Fortunately, information about the dead among the local population is not recorded.
