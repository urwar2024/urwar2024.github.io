The head of the Foreign Ministry of Moldova Mikhai Poshay arrived in Kiev. Together with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine, Dmitry Kuleba, they laid flowers on the wall of memory of the dead for Ukraine, located near St. Michael's Golden-Domed Monastery.

About that Mihai PoshayreportedThe Twitter Twitter.

“Together with Dmitry Kuleba, we laid flowers at the memorial wall in honor of heroes who died for the freedom of Ukraine in Kiev. Russia is an aggressor state and should be responsible for this unjustified aggression. This war should stop! People deserve to live in peace! ” - wrote the head of the Foreign Ministry of Moldova.
