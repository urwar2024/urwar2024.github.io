The commander-in-chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, Colonel-General Alexander Sirsky visited two brigades on the front, where the situation is gradually complicated and there is a threat of promotion of hostile units in the depths of our combat orders.

This is stated inmessageIn telegrams.

The Commander -in -Chief of the Armed Forces noted that after a detailed analysis and evaluation of the situation, together with the commanders and officers of the headquarters, all necessary decisions were made to strengthen reserves, ammunition, the means of the ICP of these military units, which will continue to ensure the stability of our defense on this area of the front.

“In general, the operational situation on the eastern front remains difficult. The enemy continues to take offensive actions, focusing basic efforts in the areas of Tern, Ivanovske, Berdychi, Tonny, Verbovo and a robot, ”added Alexander Syrsky.

At the same time, he said, probably due to a high level of loss, the activity of the enemy in other sections of the front decreased significantly.

"This was due to a timely managerial decision to strengthen the defense of a given area of the front, timely replacement of parts that lost their combat capability, as well as due to the courage and stability of our soldiers," Alexander Sirsky explained.

The Commander -in -Chief of the Armed Forces added that in two weeks of rigid battles, the enemy suffered extremely high losses. However, all the settlements he tried to capture remained under our control.

Alexander Sirsky, together with the Minister of Defense Rustem Umerov, presented state and departmental awards to servicemen who were distinguished in battles with the Russian aggressor.

“At the same time, there are enough questions that need to be resolved on the spot, so we continue our work. We will win!" - said the Commander -in -Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, during the past day at the frontHappened91 combat collision. The most attacks of defense force were reflected in the Avdiiv and Novopavlovsky directions.
