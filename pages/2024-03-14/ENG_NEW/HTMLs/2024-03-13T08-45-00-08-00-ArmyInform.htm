President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky expressed his gratitude to the United States for the decision of a package of assistance for $ 300 million with urgently necessary weapons.

Such a statement is the head of statePublishedIn his Twitter.

"I thank US President Joseph Biden, his administration, congress and the American people for the 55th package of $ 300 million defense, which provides for the satisfaction of Ukraine's urgent needs for artillery, anti-tank weapons and air defense," the statement said.

He noted that 155 mm and 105 mm artillery shells, Stinger air defense systems, ammunition for HIMARS MLRS, anti-tank systems and other weapons are critically important on the battlefield and for the protection of peaceful cities and infrastructure.

"I appreciate the implementation of our agreements with President Biden. We are counting on the earliest possible decision of the Congress to approve additional assistance to Ukraine, which will bring our common victory closer," Volodymyr Zelenskyy wrote.

As reported by ArmyInform, the United StatesAnnouncedAbout the military aid package of Ukraine for $ 300 million.

According to US National Security Adviser Jake Sullivan, funds for security assistance were found thanks to savings in contracts already concluded by the Pentagon to replace weapons transferred to Ukraine.
