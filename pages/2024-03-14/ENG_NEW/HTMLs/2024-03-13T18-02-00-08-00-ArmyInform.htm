The Defense Agency of the Ministry of Defense and the Korean car manufacturer of KG Mobility Corp (Ssangyong) signed a memorandum of cooperation in the purchase of transport for the needs of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

About it reportsplaceDefense Office.

The agency has already developed proposals for changes to the legislation, which will allow the purchase of pickups and other necessary goods for the needs of the Armed Forces without payment of excise duties, duties and VAT.

The plans include signing several direct transactions with the largest pickup producers to diversify the risks and provide the best bids.

"To conduct effective procurement of pickup trucks, it is important to establish direct cooperation with manufacturers. After all, the absence of additional intermediaries when concluding a contract will allow us to receive the most favorable prices and provide the military with more transport," said Maryna Bezrukova, director of the Defense Procurement Agency.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Defense Procurement Agency of the Ministry of DefenseannouncedIn Prozorro, the selection of suppliers to purchase 154 Pick-UP cars.
