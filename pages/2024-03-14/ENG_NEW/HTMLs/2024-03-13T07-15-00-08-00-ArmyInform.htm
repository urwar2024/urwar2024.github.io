There were 91 fighting collisions during the last day. In total, the enemy caused 10 missile and 84 aircraft strikes, and made 123 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces.

In Kupyansk directionOur defenders reflected 4 enemy attacks in the areas of Sinkivka and Tabaivka of Kharkiv region.

In the estuary directionThe warriors reflected 7 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements of Bigorivka of Luhansk region and Terne of Donetsk region.

In the Bakhmut directionThe forces of defense repelled 7 enemy attacks in the areas of the Eastern ravine, Ivanivsky, Andreevka and Klyshchiyivka of Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 26 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Berdychi, Orlovka, Tonenke, Pervomaisk Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of Krasnogorivka, St. George, Novomikhailivka, Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried 26 times to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy attacked the positions of our defenders 12 times in the areas of Staromayorsky Donetsk region, to the west of Verbov and Robovy Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson directionThe enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. So, during the day, 3 unsuccessful assaults of our troops were unsuccessful.
