According to sources, the TV Army, the Security Service of Ukraine attacked the drones at once three oil refineries in Russia. These refineries were located in the cities of Ryazan, KSTOV, and Kirisha Leningrad region and were part of the top 5 largest factories in Russia.

The Freedom of Russia Legion called on residents of Russian Belgorod and Kursk to leave their cities, as military facilities will be attacked there.

Warriors of a separate center of special operations "West" per day destroyed 6 units of Russian equipment and 25 Russians with the help of FPV-oils.

The special forces have damaged and disabled two tanks, a heavy flame-paradise system of the Sun-1A Sunpaths and three combat armored vehicles. In addition, FVP-oules eliminated 13 and injured 12 Russian infantrymen.

The Defense Ministry tests "floating bulletproof vests". When in contact with water, the special module reacts, inflamed and the bulletproof vest can be used as a lifeguard.

The Security Service of Ukraine detained another collaborator who cooperated with the Russians during the occupation of Kherson.

These and other topics are discussed by our presenters - junior lieutenant Sergei Lipko and Lieutenant Colonel Sergei Misyura.
