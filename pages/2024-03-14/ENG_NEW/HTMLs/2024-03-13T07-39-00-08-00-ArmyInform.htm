The number of victims of yesterday's rocket attack of Russians on Kryvyi Rih of Dnipropetrovsk region increased, a 47-year-old woman died in the hospital.

About it reports inTelegramHead of the Defense Council of Kryvyi Rih Alexander Vilkul.

“Unfortunately, a woman of 47 years old has died as a result of a hostile rocket attack in the hospital. Injuries were not compatible with life. Sympathy for relatives and loved ones, ”he said.

Thus the number of victims of the Russian blow to Kryvyi Rihincreased to four people.

According to Oleksandr Vilkul, as of March 13, six "heavy", including two children, remain in the hospital on March 13.

AsreportedRescue operations are ongoing in the SES of Ukraine, at the places of destruction due to rocket attack.

“Two dwellings in different areas have been destroyed. There were fires in the homes, which were eliminated by SES employees, ”rescuers said.

The destruction of the roof, walls and balconies, the apartments are knocked out windows, a damaged car. Rescuers of the SES disassemble the gaps.

As reportedArmyinform,On March 12, an explosion was heard in Kryvyi Rih during the announced alarm. The rocket attack of the Russian Federation caught fire.
