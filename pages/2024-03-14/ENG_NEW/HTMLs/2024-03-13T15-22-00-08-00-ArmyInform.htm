The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine continues to test "floating bulletproof vests", which are specially developed for the Naval forces of the Armed Forces, pontons, forces of special operations, and the special forces of the airborne assault troops.

About thisIt is saidOn the Telegram-channel of the defense department.

Two samples of floating modules are currently being tested: a Ukrainian and a foreign manufacturer.

The Ministry of Defense says that the floating module is attached to the bulletproof vest and withstands the weight of not only the military but also the additional equipment of 27 kilograms.

"If such a bulletproof vest enters the water, then the air cushion automatically inflates and raises the fighter to the surface," - said Alexander Sharko, Chief Specialist of the Central Department of Development of Material Support of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.

The central management also conducts a questionnaire of fighters who tested floating bulletproof vests.

“We are very important to the fighters. After such tests, we get important information from testing groups. This allows you to improve products for the Ukrainian army to receive ammunition that creates an advantage over the enemy's army, ”Alexander Sharko added.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Ministry of Defense of Ukrainestartedtesting of special modular bulletproof vests for the needs of the Naval Forces and engineering troops of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
