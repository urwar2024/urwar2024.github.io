The National Assembly of France (the Lower House of Parliament) confirmed the support of Ukraine, voting for the bilateral agreement on security in the field of security between our countries.

About thisinformsVerkhovna Rada of Ukraine.

“I thank the deputies. Your devotion strengthens us and gives hope. Another testimony of adamant support for Ukraine France, ”said Ruslan Stefanchuk, Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine.

He also thanked the head of the National Assembly of France, Yael Bron-Pow, and the deputies and all the French people for the support of Ukraine.

As reportedArmyinform, Ukraine and France have signed an agreement on security cooperation.
