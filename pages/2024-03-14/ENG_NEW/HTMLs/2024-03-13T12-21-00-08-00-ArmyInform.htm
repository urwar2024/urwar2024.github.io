The SSU has detained another collaborator who cooperated with russia during the occupation of Kherson.

About thisInformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

As noted in the department, the defendant turned out to be a current deputy of the local regional council. After the capture of the regional center, she supported the Russian invaders and offered them her help in the war against Ukraine.

In the personal protection of Gaulayter, the balance of the collaborator was appointed head of the so -called health department in the local occupation administration of the Russian Federation.

"In this" position "she organized the conversion of captured hospitals into military hospitals for wounded racists. In addition, the malefactor was engaged in the supply of medicines for Russian troops and the occupation bodies of the aggressor country, ”the message reads.

The investigation found to be conspired, the collaborator chose a nickname that she used during the "design" to the pseudo -institution and in the subsequent subversive activity in favor of the Russian Federation.

After the release of Kherson, the involvement sewed in the city, hoping to avoid justice, positioning herself "outside politics."

After some time, the offender planned to flee to the temporarily occupied Crimea under a fictitious name. But the SSU promptly exposed the collaborator and detained her.

“Based on the evidence collected, the security service investigators informed her about suspicion under Part 5 of Art. 111-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (collaborative activity, voluntary occupation by a citizen of Ukraine of a position related to the implementation of organizational-administrative or administrative-economic functions in illegal authorities created in the temporarily occupied territory), ”the SBU told.

Currently, the deputy is in custody. She faces up to 10 years in prison with confiscation of property.

The complex measures were carried out by SBU staff in the Kherson region for procedural guidance of the regional prosecutor's office.
