Both are LNR participants and tortured civilians and prisoners of war in the captured Luhansk region.

About thisreported March 13Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, one of the suspects is Lugansk, an exhillionist who in 2015 joined the so -called "MGB LNR".

At the beginning of a full -scale Russian invasion, he, together with representatives of the occupying troops, arranged torture in the captured administrative building of the Swatt tax inspection. They mocked the local civilian population there.

The suspect was supposed to suppress the resistance movement and carry out "preventive measures" with pro-Ukrainian people. Among the victims—Former mayor, deputy of the district council and local resident.

The man, together with his accomplices, illegally imprisoned civilians in the city, illegally kept them and tortured. The detainees were beaten with an example of a machine gun and fists, poured parts of the body with boiling water, used electric current and threatened with physical violence. In this way, people were inclined to cooperate with MGB LNR.

Another suspect is a resident of Sorokin, a former penitentiary employee. In 2014, he became "Deputy Chief of the Suid Correctional Colony of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the LNR".

After February 24, 2022, he personally participated in the torture of Ukrainian prisoners of war, which were kept in an occupation institution. He also gave orders for ill -treatment and torture to his subordinates.

On his instructions, the military was subjected to physical and psychological torture. They tied wires to their ears and gave them electric shocks, systematically beat them with batons, and kept them without water.

The prisoners were forced to perform the anthem of the aggressor state and songs of Russian performers during "events" in support of the Russian Federation.

The catam has now been informed of suspicion of violating the laws and customs of war (Part 2 of Article 28 of Part 1 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).
