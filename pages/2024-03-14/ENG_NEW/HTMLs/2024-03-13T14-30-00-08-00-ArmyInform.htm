The prosecutor's office sent an indictment to the court against a former Ukrainian athlete who supports the enemy and raises funds for his army.

About thisinformsOffice of the Prosecutor General.

"The prosecutor's office of autonomy has sent an indictment to the court in relation to the former Ukrainian athlete from Crimea on the fact

As it is noted, the accused - a former Ukrainian athlete and multiple champion of Europe and the world from Sumo, which since 2016 began to perform as a part of the Russian national team, but has taken a break in a career after banning Russian athletes to participate in international competitions.

The investigation found that in order to support the army of the invaders, it organized and led a volunteer movement in Crimea. She has repeatedly made funds for the purchase of diesel generators, races, thermal imagers and other resources for Russian servicemen, which, in particular, conducted fighting against Ukraine in Bakhmut.

In order to influence the formation of the information space in the Crimea, being a public person, the accused repeatedly performed in the pro -Russian media, promoting propaganda narratives.

Special pre -trial investigation was carried out by the Security Service of Ukraine in Crimea.
