Permanent representatives of the European Union have supported an increase in 5 billion euros of the European Peace Fund, which is funded by the supply of weapons for Ukraine.

About thisreportedBelgian Presidency of the Council of the EU on the social network X.

"The EU ambassadors have reached a fundamental consent to reform the European Peace Fund to support Ukraine with a budget of 5 billion euros for 2024," the statement said.

It is emphasized that the European Union is still determined to provide long -term support to Ukraine and to ensure that it will receive the military equipment it needs for self -defense. "

EU Diplomacy Head Josep Borrel tooconfirmedAchievement at ambassadors.

“The Foundation will allow us to strengthen our military support for Ukraine by another 5 billion euros. The message is clear: we will support Ukraine with all possible means to win it, ”he wrote.

Since 2022, the European Union has provided military assistance to Ukraine through the European Peace Fund. In particular, it compensates for the costs of Member States for the weapons provided to Ukraine.
