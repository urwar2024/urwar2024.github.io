The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine has introduced payments for children of the dead Ukrainian volunteer defenders, who were given the status of a family member of the deceased defender or defender of Ukraine.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

"It is about the children of the dead (dead) defenders and defenders of Ukraine from among the persons who voluntarily participated in the implementation of the measures necessary for the defense of Ukraine, and did not part of the volunteer formations of territorial communities (DFTG)," the statement reads.

Therefore,From April 1Children of dead volunteers will receive targeted assistance in high sizes:

  • with2361 UAHto7800 UAHon one minor,
  • to6100 UAH— for each, if there are two or more children in the family.

In this way, the government has regulated the issues of payments of social assistance and the children of volunteers who, in accordance with the Law of Ukraine "On the status of war veterans, guarantees of their social protection" established the status of a member of the family of a fallen defender or defender of Ukraine.
