The representative of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Andriy Yusov responded to explosions and fires at a number of oil refineries in the Russian Federation, which have become more recent days.

Andriy Yusov gave his comments live on March 13, 2024 on March 13, 2024.

In particular, the presenter asked the GUR representative to comment on the attack on the Novosakhtyn Plant of Oil Products in Rostov Oblast.

The GUR spokesman noted that the work of this enterprise is now stopped.

“There are a number of events on the military objects of the aggressor state. Despite the fact that the oil factory in Novosakhtyn is allegedly, it is a military facility that is used to supply and ensure an occupation group in Ukraine. We do not comment on information in more detail. Everyone can watch, in particular, in the online mode that is going on there. The work of the enterprise is now stopped. And that means that there will be problems and disruptions of certain plans from the enemy and additional opportunities and time with our defenders, ”Andrei Yusov said.

In turn, the presenter formulated the following question, starting with the statement that "yesterday and today, both the Main Intelligence Directorate and the Security Service of Ukraine are actively working on Russian refineries."

“And what poured it in the Russian oil refining industry? Can we strike such a blow that it will be paralyzed? ” She asked.

"I want to note that neither the Main Intelligence Directorate nor the Security Service of Ukraine have officially confirmed it. But some sources say so. We will not confirm, but we will not deny either," Andrii Yusov replied.

He added that any war is security and supply.

“And when it comes to the refining industry of the aggressor, recent events, in particular, the ban on the export of processed petroleum products is also the result of such excesses that continue in Russia very actively. Because you will not fill the tanks with raw oil, ”he said.

The GUR representative also clarified that the refineries in Russia were probably struck by "unknown flying objects of Ukrainian production."

According to media reports, on the night of March 13, 2024, three oil refineries were affected by drones at once: in Ryazan, in Kstvo (Nizhny Novgorod region) and Kirishakh (Leningrad region).
