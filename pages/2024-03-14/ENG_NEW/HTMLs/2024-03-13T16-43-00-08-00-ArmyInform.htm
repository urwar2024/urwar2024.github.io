2.7 million hryvnias from the sale of "battle" and "reconnaissance", which was purchased by Ukrzaliznytsia passengers, went to drones for defense forces.

About thisreportedViceremier-Minister for Innovation, Development of Education, Science and Technology Mikhail Fedorov.

"They went by train and bought" combat "and" reconnaissance "tea? UAH 5.5 from each went to the Grimm FPV. During the whole time, passengers purchased 507 thousand glasses of tea, so they were fooled 2 788 588 hryvnias on drones, ”he said.

According to Mikhail Fedorov, some of the funds have already gone for the purchase of "Grimm Birds" of Ukrainian production. They make it possible to work effectively on the enemy at a safe distance. The first 62 Kamikadze drones have already been transferred up to 110 Mark Bezruchka OMB-she has held a defense in the Avdiivka area since March 2022.

"The rest of the money will be transferred to the second batch of drones when the limited collections of" combat "and" reconnaissance "tea" will be sold in the iron shops in Kiev and Lviv, "Viceremier-Minister added.

He also thanked all passengers who support the initiative and the military.

As reported by the Armyinform, in seven areaswill teach drones operators.
