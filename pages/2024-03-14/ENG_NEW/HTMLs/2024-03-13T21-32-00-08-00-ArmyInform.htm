As a result of the Russian rocket attack on Kryvyi Rih, which occurred on March 12, more than 400 apartments were damaged in 31 multi -storey buildings, two kindergartens, five schools, two vocational establishments, a shop and other city facilities.

About thisreportedChairman of the Defense Council of Kryvyi Rih Alexander Vilkul.

According to him, there were three hits, including a nine -storey building and a five -storey building.

As a result of a hostile blow5 people were killed. At once - two women and a man. Another woman died at night from the injuries. And in the afternoon, in the analysis of the rubble on one of the floors, another dead man was discovered.

In total, 50 residents of the city sought medical help, including 12 children. At the same time, the infant-a 2-month-old boy and a 11-month-old girl-were also injured.

There are now 10 adults in hospitals (6 of them are heavy, with one woman very difficult) and 7 children (1 of them are difficult). Doctors fight for their lives, all necessary help is provided. Three people are discharged, one child who was in serious condition has been stabilized and now in a state of moderate.

Work on eliminating the effects of blows is ongoing. 25 teams of public utilities and 22 units of communal equipment are involved.
