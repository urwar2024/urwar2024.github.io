The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov thanked the United States of America for a new military assistance package.

About this head of the defense departmentwroteon Facebook.

"The United States will provide Ukraine with a new military assistance package worth $ 300 million, which includes providing air defense, artillery and anti -tank equipment," he said.

Rustem Umerov thanked the American people and personally by US Secretary of Defense Lloyd Austin for Ukraine's adamant support.

We will remind that this is the first package of weapons from the United States in 2024 and there is a 55 tranche of the equipment of the Baiden administration, which will be provided to Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, PresidentthankedFor his new assistance from the United States and stressed that this provides for the needs of Ukraine in artillery, anti -tank weapons and air defense.
