On Wednesday afternoon, the enemy again fired a guided missile from a tactical aircraft from the Black Sea at the south of the region.

About thisreportedDefense forces of South of Ukraine.

On the rise to the coast, the rocket lost its combat capacity in the air. The debris fell in the open area, the destruction of infrastructure and the victims was not recorded.

The Defense Forces noted that the enemy continues to persistently reconnoiter and test the region's air defense system.

"The distribution of air defense staff is the help of the enemy in his treacherous terror," the message said.
