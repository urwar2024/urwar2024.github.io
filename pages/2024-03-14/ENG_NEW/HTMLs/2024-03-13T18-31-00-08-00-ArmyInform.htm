Four mobile dental offices will start working in different areas of the frontline cities in a few weeks.

About thisreportedCommand of the medical forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Dental offices work on the basis of cars or trailers, which will allow dentists to approach the combat units as close as possible.

Each such mobile clinic is equipped with modern equipment and supplies. They will be treated with high-level dentists.

One of the modern mobile dental clinics received from partner countries has been successfully working in the Kherson direction for more than half a year.

The dentist team, as part of a therapist and surgeon, during which time she treated about two thousand patients.

It will be possible to find out about the movement and schedule of mobile dental clinics in the medical units of military units.
