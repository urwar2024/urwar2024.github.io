The special unit of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine "Kabul 9" detects and destroys Russian invaders in the Zaporozhye region.

In addition, the special unit participated in the battle for Kiev in Gostomel and in the Brovar direction. Mortar calculations and sniper groups of the Ukrainian military intelligence unit also worked during the defense of Bakhmut.
