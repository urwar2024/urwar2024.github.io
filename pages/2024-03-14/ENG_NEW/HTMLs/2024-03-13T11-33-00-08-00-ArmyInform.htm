Lithuania President Hitanas Nausede in Paris met with French President Emmanuel Macron to discuss ways of accelerating support for Ukraine.

About this Lithuanian PresidentwroteIn his Twitter.

“Acute discussions with Emmanuel Macron today in Paris. We need to strengthen our defense and industrial base, increase production and provide Ukraine with all the necessary military support that would open the way to the victory of the Ukrainian state. The bloody Kremlin mode will not stop if we do not stop it, ”the post reads.

Hitanas Nausede stressed that European leadership in the mobilization of Ukraine's support is vital.

“Today we must be the leaders whose decisions are approaching the victory. We need to help Ukraine strengthen air defense, supply artillery and missiles, fulfill obligations to provide a million artillery ammunition, ”he added.

According to him, the EU should take an ambitious position on the safety of the NATO Eastern Flang.

"We need NATO allies to strengthen the position of restraint and defense at the forefront of the Alliance to counteract the greatest and most uniform threat of our security - Russia," President Lithuania said.
