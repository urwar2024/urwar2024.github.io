Medical examinations, laboratory and instrumental studies, referrals for examination, assessment of vaccine status - all this is carried out free of charge for persons passing the VLK. These medical services are paid from the state budget. The amount is 883 hryvnias for one medical examination. That is why it is not necessary to pay for the passage of VLK.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defense of Ukraine, reports the Armyinform.

Medical examination of persons is organized by territorial centers of staffing and social support in healthcare facilities. It is the National Health Service of Ukraine that concludes contracts under the package of medical services with POPs of communal ownership.

It is important that healthcare facilities are obliged to provide medical services such as: examination of the multidisciplinary team of specialist doctors of the VLK and conduct laboratory tests, in particular: general blood test, general urine test, blood group and rhesus.

Healthcare facilities on which the VLK operates are obliged to provide the following medical services: antigen to hepatitis B virus, microprecipitation reaction with cardiolipin antigen, whole blood glucose or serum and carry out instrumental studies.

Also, healthcare facilities on the basis of which the VLK operate are provided by the person, in the presence of testimony, to other structural units of POPs or other POPs to carry out the necessary consultations and examinations.

Medical examinations, laboratory and instrumental studies, referrals for examination, assessment of vaccine status - all this is carried out free of charge for persons passing the VLK. These medical services are paid from the state budget. The amount is 883 hryvnias for one medical examination.

And if you or your relatives are required to pay for the medical examination, you must immediately inform the National Health Service of Ukraine by calling the hotline 16-77.
