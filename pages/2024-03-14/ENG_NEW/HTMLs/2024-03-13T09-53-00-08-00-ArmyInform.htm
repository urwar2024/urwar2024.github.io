The US Department of Defense has provided a full list of weapons that will be included in a new military assistance package of $ 300 million through direct supplies from US army reserves.

About thisIt is saidOn the Pentagon site.

It is noted that this is the first package of weapons from the United States in 2024 and there is a 55 tranche of Baiden administration equipment, which will be provided to Ukraine.

"The Ministry of Defense can support this extraordinary package after determining the savings by previously allocated additional funding, which can be used to replace the shares of the Ministry of Defense," Pentagon noted.

In particular, the United States will transfer:

  • Rockets for MANPADS STINGER;
  • additional ammunition for HIMARS;
  • 155 mm artillery shells, including fragmentation and double-use shells with improved cassette ammunition;
  • ammunition for artillery 105 mm;
  • anti-tank AT-4 grenade launchers;
  • additional ammunition for small arms;
  • ammunition to eliminate obstacles;
  • spare parts, maintenance equipment and other auxiliary equipment.

“Assistance for Ukraine remains a reasonable investment in our national security. This restrains potential aggression in other parts of the world, while strengthening our defense-industrial base and creating highly skilled jobs for the American people, ”the US Defense Ministry said.

As reported by the Armyinform, PresidentthankedFor his new assistance from the United States and stressed that this provides for the needs of Ukraine in artillery, anti -tank weapons and air defense.
