Three residents of Zhytomyr region, 20, 20 and 21 years, were found in the mountains border guards of the Division of the Mukachevo Squad.

About thisinformsState Border Service of Ukraine.

As it is noted, the young men planned to illegally cross the Ukrainian-Romanian border. Having made a route that ran through the mountains, they went on a journey. According to the plan of violators to overcome the chosen path, they need to take 24 hours, however, in almost two days they went a distance of 20 km and 3 km to the state border were in the field of view of the border guards.

“The response group immediately advanced to the detention of identified persons who were tracked by a quadcopter. "Alpinists" had neither equipment for overcoming the highlands, nor food and water, "the SSSU told.

According to the available information, hungry, soaked and frozen, they were taken to the border unit, fed, drunk with hot tea and gave the opportunity to dry clothes and shoes.

“In relation to Zhytomyr residents, the border guards drawn up protocols on the administrative offense under Art. 204-1 of the Code of Administrative Offenses "Illegal Crossing or attempting to illegally cross the state border of Ukraine" The case is sent to court ",-the message reads.

As reported by the Armyinform, DBRsuedThe case is against the Zaporizhzhya Exvikocom.
