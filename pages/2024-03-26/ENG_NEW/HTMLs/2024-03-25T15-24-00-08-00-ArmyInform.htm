Employees of the State Bureau of Investigation have completed pre -trial investigation in the case of the former head of the Zaporizhzhya Regional Territorial Center of Acquisition and Social Support, which is accused of unjustified accumulation of increased military payments to subordinates.

This is stated inmessageDBR.

The indictment was sent to court.

"While in the post of head of Zaporizhzhya OTC and JV, the official, without legitimate grounds, sent his subordinates to one of the Armed Forces brigades allegedly to perform combat missions," the DBR notes.

As it is noted, the military was not appointed to positions in the unit, did not participate in the battles and actually unreasonably in the frontline.

“They were on vacation, lived on a free schedule and even had the opportunity to go home for a weekend. However, servicemen received increased payments and the ability to acquire the status of combatants, ”the department said.

As a result, according to the investigation, seven servicemen who did not participate in hostilities were accrued more than UAH 1 million of payments.

The former military is accused of exceeding the official powers committed in the conditions of martial law (Article 5 of Article 426-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine).

The sanction of the article provides for imprisonment for up to 12 years.

The court also arrested the property of the accused: an apartment in Zaporozhye and three land plots in Zaporizhzhya region.

Criminal proceedings against other participants in the scheme are ongoing.

As reported by the Armyinform, the courtarrestedthe former High Affairs of the Armed Forces on suspicion of illegal enrichment.
