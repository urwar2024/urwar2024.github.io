The prosecutor's office began criminal proceedings on violation of the laws and customs of war (Part 1 of Article 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine) in connection with the Russian rocket attack in Kiev on March 25, 2024.

This is stated inmessageOffice of the Prosecutor General of Ukraine.

According to the investigation, on March 25, 2024, at about 10:30 the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation in violation of the laws and customs of war made a rocket strike on the objects of civil infrastructure of Kyiv.

According to preliminary information,7 people were injured, including a 16-year-old girl.

According to preliminary data, as a result of rocket stroke:

  • In the Pechersk District, a hidden in a non -residential building was recorded,Under the rubble may be people, windows and damaged cars are knocked out in neighboring homes;
  • In the Solomenskiy district, a rocket was recorded in the building and the wreckage of a rocket was found in the courtyard;
  • The Darnytskyi district recorded a fall in the courtyard of a private home.

Rescuers and law enforcement officers work on the events.

Measures are underway to document military crimes committed by the Russian military against civilians.

The pre -trial investigation is carried out by investigators of the Security Service of Ukraine in Kyiv and Kyiv region.
