45 combat clashes were recorded during the day. In total, the enemy caused 4 missile and 56 aircraft blows, carried out 43 shelling from the rocket launchers of the volley fire on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Towards LymanOur warriors reflect 13 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements of Bigorivka of Luhansk region; Tern, Yampolivka, Razdolivka and north of the Merry Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionOur warriors reflect 2 attacks of the enemy in the area of the settlement of the Klischivka Donetsk region.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 3 enemy attacks in the areas of Berdychi and Pervomaiske settlements of the Donetsk oblast.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe defense forces continue to restrain the enemy in the area of the settlement of Novomykhailivka, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defenses of our troops 15 times.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy, with the support of aviation, attacked the positions of our defenders 3 times in the areas of Staromayorsky Donetsk region; Southwestern Belogorye and northwestern willow Zaporizhzhya region.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Armed Forces in the SouthDestroyed112 occupiers, 8 artillery systems and 2 air defense systems.
