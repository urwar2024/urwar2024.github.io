The internal audit service of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine conducted monitoring of the purchase in 2024 of electricity in the apartment and operational department of Mukachevo in Transcarpathia.

About thisinformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

As noted in the department, during the study, the Defense Ministry's auditors found an overestimation of the cost of electricity by the use of optional ratio.

"In connection with this management of the apartment and operational department of the city of Mukachevo, it was proposed to amend the current agreement, which provided budget savings in the amount of UAH 3 947 064,"-the message reads.

Similarly, due to the unreasonable increase in the price under additional contracts, the Ivano-Frankivsk apartment and operational part suffered unnecessary costs in the amount of 2 429 285.44 hryvnias for the purchase of natural gas in January-October 2018.

“Due to the implementation of the recommendations of internal auditors and the recently adopted court decision, the supplier was obliged to reimburse the said funds to the accounts of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine. Thanks to the internal audit, the measures have managed to prevent the inefficient spending of almost UAH 6.5 million of budgetary funds, ”the Defense Ministry stressed.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Ministry of Finance reported on the allocation for January-February235.1 billion hryvnias for defense.
