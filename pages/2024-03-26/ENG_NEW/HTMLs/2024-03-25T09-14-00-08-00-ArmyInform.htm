On the night of March 25, the Russians again attacked the southern regions of Ukraine with Shahd-type Drony-Kamikadze. The defenders of the sky within the Nikolaev and Odessa regions were knocked down by 8 out of 9 percussion UAVs.

About thisinformsCommand of the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

"Tonight, the defenders of the sky knocked down 8 out of 9 shaped UAVs, which the enemy attacked from Cape Chauda (Crimea)," the Armed Forces reported.

It is noted that mobile fire groups of Defense Forces of Ukraine were involved in the reflection of the air attack.

As reportedArmyinform,The Armed Forces destroyed 11 Russian tanks a day and laid more than 600 invaders.
