The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine, in cooperation with the Ministry of Health and with the support of the Ministry of Digital Policy of Ukraine, implements the practice of submitting a medical declaration through a single portal of state services "Action".

About thisinformsMinistry of Defense of Ukraine, reports the Armyinform.

From now on, military units whose servicemen are involved in hostilities or combat missions can use a convenient and simple mechanism for obtaining a medical practice permit.

“The system works, the first declarations have already been submitted through the Action portal. This greatly simplifies the process that has previously required the collection of a large package of documents and the expectation of a decision from the Ministry of Health, ”Natalia Kalmykova explained.

The Deputy Minister of Defense also stressed that this procedure now takes only a few minutes, which allows the military units to effectively and promptly receive the necessary permits to provide medical care.

“The submission of electronic declarations through the action portal is a continuation of state policy for digitalization and elimination of corruption risks, as it eliminates the need for human contact with officials. In addition, in case of change of the commander of the military unit, the declaration remains valid. I also emphasize that the declaration will act until the end of martial law and three months after its termination, ”she added.

Earlier the Armyinformreportedthat MOU has announced simplification for the Armed Forces in obtaining permits for medical practice.
