The Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces, Colonel-General Alexander Sirsky congratulated his brothers-SBU staff on his professional holiday.

Alexander Sirsky about itwroteIn the telegram.

“Today we are all the forces of Ukraine's defense. We, side by side, fight with the Russian occupier. The brothers from the Security Service of Ukraine - now in difficult directions destroy the enemy and protect our country, ”he said.

The Commander -in -Chief of the Armed Forces emphasized that today there is a common goal and a common goal in the forces of defense today - to liberate Ukraine and win.

“We selflessly fight for our freedom, for our future, for our children.

An indomitable spirit to you, to take on weapons.

We will definitely win! ” -summed up the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Colonel-General Alexander Sirsky.
