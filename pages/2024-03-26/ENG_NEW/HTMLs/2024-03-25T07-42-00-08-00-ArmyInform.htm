The Russian aircraft release in the direction of the Odessa region of the X-31P rocket from the Black Sea. It happened this night, March 25.

About thisreportedThe forces of the South Defense.

"Before the attack of drones from the tactical aviation aircraft from the Black Sea, trying to damage the work of air defense, Russian terrorists released an anti-radios in Odessa Radiolocating missile X-31P," the statement said.

According to the military, the rocket lost its combat capacity in the air. There was no information on destruction and victims.

Further, the enemy attacked several waves of drones of Shahed-131/136 from the Black Sea.

"4 shock UAVs were shot in Odesa region and Mykolaiv region," - said the defense of the South.

In the Nikolaev area and in Odesa region there were damage to the energy sites where fires broke out. People were not injured.

As reportedArmyinform,President Volodymyr Zelensky noted that last week Russia has used almost 190 missiles and almost 140 Shakhs in Ukraine.
