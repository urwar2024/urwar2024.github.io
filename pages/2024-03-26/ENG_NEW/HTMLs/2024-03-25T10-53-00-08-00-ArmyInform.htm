The spokesman of the Navy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the captain of the 3rd rank Dmitry Pletenchuk, said that the forces of Ukraine's defense have been able to cause serious harm to the functioning of the Black Sea Fleet of the Russian Federation in Crimea due to a blow to the node of communication in the temporarily occupied Russia of Sevastopol.

Dmitry Pletenchuk provided his comments duringlive etherThe only news of the "only news" in the morning of March 25, 2024.

“The situation for them can be even worse. Because the naval base is a structure that provides the fleet in general. Therefore, this may not only be a ligament. This may be the security, the current repair, and many other issues that the naval base resolves, ”the Navy spokesman said.

According to him, information on the said impact will still be updated.

"But as of now, we can note that the operation was successful, the blow was serious and the harm is significant," Dmitry Pletenchuk said.
