Last week, drone drones eliminated more than 370 units of Russian equipment and almost 200 invaders.

Such dataPublicatedViceremier-Minister for Innovation, Development of Education, Science and Technology-Minister of Digital Transformation of Ukraine Mikhail Fedorov, reports the Armyinform.

In particular, UAV units have struck 50 tanks, 78 BBM, 95 guns, 21 SAUs and almost 700 enemy support points last week.

Mikhail Fedorov also noted the round -the -clock work of drones on the front and stressed that these are losses that have video confirmation.

“He has repeatedly repeated that modern war is a war of technology. We work for the technology at the front, ”the minister summarized.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Armed Forces in the SouthDestroyed112 occupiers, 8 artistem and 2 SPR.
