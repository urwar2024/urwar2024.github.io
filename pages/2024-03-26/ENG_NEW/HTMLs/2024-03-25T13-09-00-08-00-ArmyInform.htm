Defense Procurement Agency and the Organization of Procurement and Logistics of the Danish Ministry of Defense (Dalo) signed a memorandum on mutual understanding and cooperation in defense procurement.

This is said inmessageMinistry of Defense of Ukraine.

“We are grateful to our partners from Denmark for their support for Ukraine. This event is another important step in deepening our cooperation in the supply of modern weapons and military equipment to the Armed Forces of Ukraine, ”said Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Dmitry Klimenkov.

The event was also attended by the Director of the Defense Procurement Agency Marina Bezrukov and Dalo Commander Lieutenant General Kim Yorgensen.

As reported by the Armyinform, in March 2024, the Defense AgencystartedThe process of creating a supervisory board.
