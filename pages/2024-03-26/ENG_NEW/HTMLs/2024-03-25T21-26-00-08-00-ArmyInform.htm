The other day, according to the operatives of a separate checkpoint "Kyiv", the border guards detained three citizens of Ukraine who tried to cross the state border illegally into one of the areas in Chernivtsi region.

About thisinformsState Border Guard Service of Ukraine.

As noted in the department, in addition to the violators themselves, law enforcement officers found two vehicles that were delivered to the border.

“Drivers of vehicles and another organizer of the crossing tried to run away immediately. So the border guards had to organize urgent investigative measures. The cars were discovered and stopped 20 km from the border, ”the message reads.

The SSSU noted that "enterprising" carriers received for their services at 5.5 thousand euros for each passenger.

“In the end, the citizens of 204-1 of the Code of Administrative Offenses were submitted to the citizens of Ukraine who tried to cross the border illegally, but the border guards sent a message about the detection of signs of an offense under Art. 332 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine, ”the department noted.
