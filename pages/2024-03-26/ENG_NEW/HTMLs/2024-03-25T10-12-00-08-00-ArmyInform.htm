Ukrainians will be able to apply to the International Register for damages caused by Russian aggression to the International Register.

About thisinformsThe press service of the Ministry of Justice of Ukraine.

“The first stage will begin in April this year - Ukrainians will be able to formulate applications for damaged or destroyed residential real estate if there is an act of inspection from local authorities. Over time, it is planned to allow the submission to the Register of losses even if the person has not contacted the local authorities, ”the message reads.

In addition, the list of categories of losses due to aggression of the Russian Federation will be expanded. The register will be able to apply for:

  • damage to life and health;
  • forced movement and forced resettlement;
  • crime of torture and sexual abuse;
  • other damage and damage caused.

In the future, legal entities and the state will be able to submit the statements about business and infrastructure.
