The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine, together with the Ministry of Health of Ukraine and the Ministry of Digital Transformation of Ukraine, has simplified the receipt of permits for the army - now military units can obtain permission for medical practice through the portal.

This is said inmessageMOU.

"Convenience and efficiency: the first declarations have already been submitted, and the procedure takes only a few minutes, which allows you to promptly receive the necessary permits to provide medical care in the army," the statement said.

According to MOU, the filing of electronic declarations deprives the need for human contact with officials, which excludes the corruption factor.

The declaration will be valid by the end of martial law + 3 months from the date of termination of martial law.
