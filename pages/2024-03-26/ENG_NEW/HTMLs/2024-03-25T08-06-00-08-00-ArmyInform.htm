As of the morning of March 25, there are no enemy ships in the waters in the Black and Azov seas.

About thisinformsNavy of the Armed Forces.

In the Mediterranean, Russia holds one enemy ship - the carpet carrier "Caliber", a total volley of up to 8 rockets.

During the day in the interests of the Russian Federation the passage of the Kerch Strait was made:

  • 14 vessels to the Azov Sea, 4 of them moved from the Bosphorus Strait;
  • To the Black Sea - 5 vessels, of which 1 continued movement in the direction of the Bosphorus.

"The Russian Federation continues to violate the International Convention on the Protection of Human Life at the Sea of 1974 (Solas), switching off automatic identification systems (AIS)," the military said.
