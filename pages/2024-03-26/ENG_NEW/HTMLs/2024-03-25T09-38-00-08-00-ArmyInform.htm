Major General Viktor Nikolyuk goes from the post of commander of the Command of the Land Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

He is about itreportedon Facebook page.

Major General Viktor Nikolyuk thanked everyone for their fruitful cooperation and expressed confidence in its continuation in the new conditions.

“New challenges, new trials, new victories. The present time requires every Ukrainian, and especially the military, to make a maximum and more effort to win the Russian enemy. And I plan to do all this by heading one of the connections and one of the directions of the front, ”he said.

Recall that Major General Viktor Nikolyuk was appointed commander of the Command of the Training of Land Forces in March 2023.
