The Commander of the Air Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Lieutenant General Mykola Oleshchuk reported that the Ukrainian defenders of the sky managed to destroy two hostile ballistic missiles, which in the morning of March 25, 2024 were flying to Kiev.

This is stated inmessageMykola Oleshchuk.

“At about 10:30 the enemy struck in Kiev with two ballistic missiles from temporarily occupied Crimea. The goals are destroyed, the type is set, ”the Air Force commander said.

He thanked the defenders of the sky for their combat work.

As reported by the Armyinform, on March 21, 2024 the Air Force of the Armed Forces alsoreportedabout the destruction of all the missiles that the Russians attacked Kiev.
