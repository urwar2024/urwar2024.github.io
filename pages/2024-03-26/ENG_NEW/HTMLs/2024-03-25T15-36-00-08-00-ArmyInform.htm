President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky commented on the successes of the Defense Forces of Ukraine and, in particular, the Security Service of Ukraine in the fight against Russian invaders in the Black Sea.

Volodymyr Zelenskyy noted in hisspeechMarch 25, 2024.

At the same time, he outlined the task for the Defense Forces of Ukraine on the East of the Black Sea water area, where, according to the President of Ukraine, the Russians should also lose the opportunity to operate.

"Russian warships are no longer a place in the western part of the Black Sea and near our Crimea, but let them lose the opportunity to operate in the East of the Black Sea water area," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.

According to him, now every occupier already clearly understands that nowhere on Ukrainian land they can not feel calm.

"But they must lose even more logistics, even more staffs, warehouses - even more material basis of this war," the Head of State emphasized.
