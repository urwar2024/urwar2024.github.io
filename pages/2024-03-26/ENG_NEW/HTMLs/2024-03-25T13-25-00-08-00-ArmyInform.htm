March 26 from 15:00 to 4:00 pm at 0 800 507 028 Soldiers of the Forces of the Armed Forces and their relatives can communicate with the Commander of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces, Major General Igor Plahuta.

About it asinformsThe Public Relations Service of the TRI Armed Forces Command, reports the Army.

“The command is trying to resolve every issue that military personnel address. It is very important for us to support every fighter and a Favorite, ”the message reads.

The TRU's hotline has been operating since the beginning of February 2022. During this time, more than one hundred thousand calls were accepted. To get advice, you can call daily from 8:00 to 20:00 at 0 800 507 028.
