President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky commented on a blow that Russian invaders struck in the morning of March 25, 2024 in Kiev with two ballistic missiles.

Vladimir Zelensky's statement, in particular,Postedin his telegram channel.

“Russian terrorists have struck ballistic missiles in the capital. Unfortunately, there are damage to homes in ordinary urban development. At this time, five victims are known. There is a parsing of rubble, ”he said.

According to him, the Ukrainian authorities are not tired of repeating that Ukraine needs more air defense systems.

“It is safety for our cities and human lives preserved. All of us in the world who respects and protects life, we need to stop this terror, ”the President of Ukraine said.

Volodymyr Zelensky thanked the rescuers of the State Emergency Service of Ukraine, police, communal services and all the services involved, which this morning eliminate the consequences of the Russian attack on Kiev.
