Today, March 25, strong explosions were heard in the capital.

As a result of the rocket attack of the Russians, a three -storey building of the gym was damaged inPechersk districtKiev. 2 persons were injured by whom doctors provide the necessary assistance. Search and rescue work is ongoing. Rescuers disassemble the rubble.

Also inHolosiivskyi, Dniprovsky and Solomensk districtsPre -falling of debris, without victims. Rescuers are currently surveying territories.

InDarnytskyi districtThere is damage to a private home.

InHolosiivskyi districtThe fragments of the rocket have been found in the forest belt.

Rescuers work on all locations, law enforcement officers carry out investigative actions.
