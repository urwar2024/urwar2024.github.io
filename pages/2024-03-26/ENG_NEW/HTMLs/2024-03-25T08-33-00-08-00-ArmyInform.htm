The General Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine revealed the details as a successful fire damage to the objects of the Black Sea Fleet of the State-aggressor of Russia, in particular the Great Airborne Ship of Yamal, took place in the temporarily occupied Crimea.

This is stated inmessageGur.

It is noted that the forces of Ukraine's defense attacked the 13th Ship repair plant of the Black Sea Fleet of the Russian Federation in Sevastopol, where, in particular, "Yamal" was rolled up.

According to GUR, the nature of the damage to the Yamal ship is critical.

"The hole in the upper deck has led the roll to the right side," the intelligence reports.

Russian invaders, GUR notes, have to pump water from the affected ship continuously.

Yamal participated in the annexation of Crimea, and from 2017 to 2023 was under repair.

"Other details about the loss of the aggressor as a result of the missile attack of the security and defense forces of Ukraine against the military objects of terrorist Russia are specified," Gur writes.

As reported by the Armyinform, on March 24, 2024 the General Staff of the Armed Forcesstatedthat the forces of Ukraine's defense successfully impressed the large landing ships of the Russian invaders "Yamal" and "Azov".
