Prime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal stated that Russian invaders hit Kiev on March 25, 2024 from the occupied Crimea with hypersonic missiles.

About it is in hismessage.

“Russia does not stop its rocket terror. In the morning, the enemy attacked Kiev with hypersonic missiles from the occupied Crimea, ”Denis Shmigal said.

According to him, the Ukrainian heroic forces of air defense reflected the attack.

“Unfortunately, the fall of the debris is victims, they are assisted. Rescue services work, ”the Prime Minister of Ukraine said.

The air defense systems, as Denis Shmigal emphasized, once again proved their effectiveness in the hands of Ukrainian soldiers.

"Ukraine needs more such weapons so that our children do not run to shelter from explosions," said the Prime Minister of Ukraine.
