Colonel Natalia Humeniuk spoke about the consequences of the strikes caused by the forces of Ukraine on hostile objects in temporarily occupied Crimea, for Russian invaders.

Natalia Humeniuk provided her comments duringlive etherThe only news of the "only news" in the morning of March 25, 2024.

In particular, she said, as a result of hitting the command post, the enemy lost his executives.

“It is a really powerful stroke on logistics, it is a powerful blow to the management of troops. Because again, both the team and the officer's staff are among the listed. Accordingly, there is a reorientation of troops, reorientation of forces and means, as specific executives are lost, ”Natalia Humeniuk said.

In addition, she said, a powerful moral and psychological blow to the enemy army was caused.

"When they feel that even in such deep rear there is a powerful work of defense forces and interference with the further development of combat events," the spokeswoman for the Defense Forces of South of Ukraine explained.

It predicted further strikes by Ukraine's defense forces on hostile objects in their rear.

“This situation will continue. The cotton season does not end in Crimea. We still have a lot of work and many appropriate objects, ”Natalia Humeniuk said.

She also commented on the clutch on the Russian large landing ships "Yamal" and "Azov".

"The fact that logistics, in particular, the fleet again turned out to be a blow-it can again put it on its ship and cattery warehouse and make it stay at the point of base as long as possible,"-said the spokeswoman for the Defense Forces of South of Ukraine.
