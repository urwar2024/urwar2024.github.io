The Supreme Representative of the European Union on Foreign Affairs and Security Policy of Josep Borrel has specified the amount of military assistance to Ukraine since the beginning of a large -scale invasion of the Russian Federation. According to him, assistance is 31 billion euros.

About thisstatedEU EU Supreme Representative and Security Policy Josep Borrel.

According to him, the European Peace Fund (EPF) was established in 2021 to support partners with military equipment, which was impossible at the expense of the EU budget.

"Its budget initially amounted to 5 billion euros, today the financial ceiling has increased to 17 billion euros," Josep Borrel said.

He emphasized that although the EPF was not created for this purpose, the Foundation became the basis of Ukraine's military support.

"Today, we used 6.1 billion euros with EPF to stimulate Ukraine's support from the EU Member States, and together with them the EU has put military equipment in Ukraine a total of 31 billion euros from the beginning of wars," Josep Borrel wrote.

According to him, thanks to these funds, the EU continued the military support of Ukraine.

Among other events, says Zzepe Borrel, 60,000 Ukrainian soldiers will be prepared by the summer.

“We have handed over 500,000 artillery shells to Ukraine, and by the end of the year there will be more than 1 million. In addition, the European Defense Industry also provides Ukraine with 400,000 shells under commercial contracts. The Czech initiative to buy ammunition outside the EU is a supplement to these efforts, ”he said.

However, the EU Supreme Representative emphasizes, the EU should increase both production capacity and financial resources aimed at supporting Ukraine.

As reportedArmyinform, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky called on EU leaders to help protect Ukrainian cities.
