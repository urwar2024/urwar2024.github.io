The representative of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Vadim Skibitsky stated that Ukrainian military intelligence officers are constantly monitoring the movement of ships of Russian invaders.

This is said inmessageGur.

"All regarding the movement of warships and boats of the Russian Federation is constantly monitored in order to detect, transmit information and destroy the enemy military facility ... These are small bricks that are laid in large strategic operations - the release of all our occupied territories," Vadim said. Skibitsky.

According to him, Ukraine, without a powerful fleet, forced the Black Sea Fleet of the Russian Federation to escape from the territory of Crimea.
