Over the past day, the enemy army made 258 beats on 9 settlements of Zaporizhzhya region. In particular, 230 artudaries were caused.

About thisinformsHead of Zaporizhzhya Ova Ivan Fedorov.

230 Artudari was inflicted on the territory of Gulyaipol, Robovy, Novoandriivka, Malynivka, Little Tokmachka, Levadny, Svutny, Verbov and Novodanylivka.

“6 aviation strikes were carried out by Russian servicemen on Malinovka, a robot and a small toct. 22 UAV attacked Gulyaipole, Levadne, robot, small Tokmachka, Malynivka, Novoandriivka, ”the post reads.

There were 5 reports of destruction of residential buildings and infrastructure.

"The civilians were not injured," Ivan Fedorov added.

As reportedArmyinform, the Russian aircraft released in the direction of the Odessa region a rocket X-31P from the waters of the Black Sea. It happened this night, March 25.
