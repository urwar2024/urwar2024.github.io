The personnel reserve of civil service employees for the de -industrial territories registered 2423 questionnaires of applicants, of which 2217 have already been enrolled.

About thisReportsMinistry of Reintegration of Temporarily Occupied Territories.

In particular, men submitted 1380 questionnaires (57%), women — 1043 questionnaires (43%). Candidates prefer future work in Crimea, Donetsk and Zaporizhia regions.

"Among the proposed areas of work, they often choose administrative management, state supervision and control, administration and provision of public services, prevention of corruption, personnel management," - the message reads.

Thus, 2217 citizens have already been enrolled in the recovery reserve, which is 91.5% of the total number of submitted questionnaires.

“If you want to join the renewal of the de -industrial areas and have the necessary education, go to the link, register in the system and fill in the questionnaire. The coordinators of the project are Ministry of Reintegration and NADS, ”the department said.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Ministry of Finance reported on the allocation for January-February235.1 billion hryvnias for defense.
