The Ministry of Finance of Ukraine has stated that security and defense remain an absolute priority and the largest item of state budget expenditures.

This is stated inmessageministry.

Thus, in January-February 2024, expenditures of the General Fund of the State Budget for the Security and Defense Sector amounted to UAH 235.1 billion, or 55.4% of the total amount of expenditures of the General Fund of the State Budget.

In February - UAH 159.8 billion.

The funds were directed at:

✔ Cash support for servicemen, persons of ordinary and command staff, police officers;

✔ purchase of military/special equipment, weapons, ammunition, defense products, personal protective equipment (helmets, bulletproof vests and other special equipment), fuel and lubricants, food;

✔ Medical care;

✔ Other expenses to ensure the activities of the relevant bodies and formations.

The government and defense expenditures are funded exclusively at the expense of its own tax revenues as well as military bonds.

“Therefore, it is extremely important to support the Armed Forces of Ukraine by paying taxes and investing in military bonds. After all, every hryvnia attracted is an investment in the defeat of Russia! ” - The message says.

As the Armyinform writes, a third of the State Budget of Ukrainegoeson the financial support of servicemen.
