Last week, Russian troops used more than a thousand rockets, aviation and drones-Kamikadze for massive combined blows in Ukraine.

According to him, from Monday to Sunday, Russian terrorists used:

  • Almost 190 rockets of different types,
  • Almost 140 Shahaneda.

He also gratefully expressed gratitude to the defenders of the sky who reflect enemy air attacks.

“All the soldiers of mobile fire groups, our aviation, airplane air defense, all the specialists of our HR. Thank you for every whipped rocket, for every shot -shaped shahad, "the head of state said.

At the same time, Volodymyr Zelenskyy emphasizes that the "air shield" of Ukraine needs significant strengthening.

“We are working on all partners whose decisions can change the nature of this war and protect our cities and villages from terror. But now at least some of the missiles and most of the Shahaneda are knocking down our warriors, ”the President said.
