The court elected the former Chief of the Central Food Department for the Command of the Logistics Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, which the State Bureau of Investigation exposed an illegal enrichment, a preventive measure in the form of detention with the right to make a bail of UAH 6 million.

This was announced on the broadcast of the "Unified News" by Tatiana Sapyan's communications advisor to communications,passesDBB press service.

According to her, the prosecution asked the court to appoint a pledge of UAH 60 million. However, the court appointed 6 million.

"The prosecution party asked the court to choose a pre -trial detention measure with the possibility of bail 60 million hryvnias, but the judge decided that it would be detention with the possibility of making a bail of UAH 6 million," she said.

As reported by the Armyinform, on March 21, 2024, DBR employees in cooperation with the Office of the Prosecutor General and NAPCExposedon the illegal enrichment of the former head of the Central Food Department of the Command of Logistics Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

According to the investigation, the official in 2022-2023 acquired unreasonable assets in the amount of almost UAH 58 million. In particular, it is a car, an apartment in Kiev, 53 land in Ukraine and other property.
