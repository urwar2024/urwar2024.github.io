The Minister of Defense of Ukraine Rustem Umerov, together with the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Colonel-General Alexander Syrsky met in Kiev with a delegation of the heads of committees of the European Parliament.

About thisInformsMinistry of Defence Ukraine.

Rustem Umerov emphasized the great importance of assistance from the European Parliament and the European Union to Ukraine to confront Russian aggression.

According to him, the intention to increase the amount of assistance is of great importance for both the military and civilians and Ukrainian infrastructure.

Rustem Umerov and Alexander Sirsky informed the European delegation about the current situation at the front.

The Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine said that the priority needs of the Security Forces are air defense systems, missiles for them, artillery systems and ammunition, as well as long-range firepower.

He noted the importance of continuing the preparation of the Ukrainian military within the EU mission.

Rustem Umerov also urged the EU to strengthen sanctions against those who try to bypass them, including supplies spare parts for the Russian military machine.

“We need to be the only one in Europe to fight against Russian aggression. We must be one strong fist before the threat of barbarians, ”said the Minister of Defense of Ukraine.

For his part, the Chairman of the Committee of Foreign Affairs of the European Parliament David McAlistir said that the visit of several heads of the Committees of the European Parliament to Ukraine is a signal of unwavering all -party support of Ukraine by the European Parliament in the fight against Russian aggression.

“We appreciate the courage of the Ukrainian military. We will continue to support you and demand that you give Ukraine what you really need, ”he said.
