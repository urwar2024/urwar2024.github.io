Over the past day, on March 24, Russian troops fired at the territory of 12 regions of Ukraine.

About thisinformsMilitary Media Center.

In total, 87 settlements and 69 infrastructure sites were attacked from different types of weapons - mortars, artillery, tanks, RSZV, SPR, Tactical Aviation.

As a result of hostile shelling, they are dead and injured among civilians, the number of victims is clarified.

As reportedArmyinform, in the Nikolaev area, more than ten people were injured due to enemy attacks.
