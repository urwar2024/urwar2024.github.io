Polish Prime Minister Donald Tusk, due to constant shelling of the Russian Federation of Ukraine, sharply addressed the speaker of the House of Representatives of the United States Mike Johnson.

He wrote about it inTwitter.

“Look at Odessa, Mr. Johnson! How many more arguments do you need to make a decision? ” - the Polish Prime Minister wrote to Mike Johnson.

Thus, Donald Tusk called speaker Mike Johnson finally deciding to fund assistance to Ukraine.

As reportedArmyinform,In a conversation with the US Secretary of State, Dmytro Kuleba emphasized the urgent continuation of Ukraine's support.
