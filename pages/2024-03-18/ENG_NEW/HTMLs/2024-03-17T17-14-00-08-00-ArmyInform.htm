The Russian army continues to cover the peaceful settlements of Kherson region with fire. Today, on March 17, the sight was the village of Lviv, a man was wounded.

About thisinformsKherson Ova.

Thus, as a result of the discharge of explosives from the drone, a resident of the village of Lviv, in Beryslav region, was injured.

“A 43-year-old man was taken to hospital. He was diagnosed with explosive trauma, fragile wounds, open leg fracture, ”the message reads.

The Kherson Ova noted that the doctors provided the victim with the necessary assistance.
