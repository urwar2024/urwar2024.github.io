Russia has provoked in Transnistria with an attack by a military unit Dron-Kamikadze.

About thisinformsCenter for combating misinformation.

The main logic of these actions, as explained in the center, is the information injection of the situation, since in Russia there is no corridor to Transnistria to carry out military provocations.

"It is for the information that the Russians try to provoke and manipulate in the information space, spreading fakes," - added at the Center for Combating Misinformation.

It should be reminded that some Russian publications reported that on March 17 in the unrecognized Transnistria, a fire occurred in the territory of the military unit as a result of Dron-Kamikadze attack.
