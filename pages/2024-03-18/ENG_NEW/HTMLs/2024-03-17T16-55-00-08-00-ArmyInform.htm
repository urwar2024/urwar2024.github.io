On March 17 in the village of Velyka Pisarivka, in Sumy region, rescuers joined the evacuation of a civilian population living in border settlements.

About thisinformsSES of Ukraine.

In total, rescuers have helped to evacuate 256 citizens - residents of the border. Among the evacuated 26 children.

After the alert, rescuers helped citizens together with personal things to place in evacuation transport.

The SESS staff also met people who arrived from the border to a safer place.

As reportedArmyinformIn Donetsk, nearly 800 people were evacuated from the front line per day, including 124 children.
