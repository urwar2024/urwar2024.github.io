Today, on March 17, at about 9:15, the Russian military struck a rocket strike on the building of the medical center of the village of Bugayivka of the Chuguev district: the consequences were eliminated by three departments of the SES.

About thisinformsSES of Ukraine.

“As a result of the shelling, there was a partial destruction and a fire in a single -storey building of a temporary dislocation of emergency medical aid. The fire area was about 100 square meters. Rescuers were eliminated, ”the department said.

Also, according to rescuers, the explosive wave damaged nearby residential and other buildings, emergency medical cars and private transport.

"Two doctors who were hospitalized at the Kharkiv hospital were injured," the SES of Ukraine noted.

As reportedArmyinform, in the village of Bugayivka, the Russians fired a temporary ambulance location.
