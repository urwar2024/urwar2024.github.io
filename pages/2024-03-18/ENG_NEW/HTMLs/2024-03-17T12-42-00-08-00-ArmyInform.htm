President Volodymyr Zelenskyy thanked the people of Denmark and personally Prime Minister Matte Frederixen for the support of Ukraine.

The head of statewroteIn Fabutz.

According to the President of Ukraine, Europe is able to be a force that does not allow chaos. Each of the security agreements proves it.

“I thank Denmark for solidarity with Ukraine. A security agreement between our countries gives new security prospects. Increasing pressure on Russia, defensive interaction with Denmark, supporting our movement in the European Union and NATO - all that strengthens us, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy summarizes.
