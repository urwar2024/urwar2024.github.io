Within a few weeks, four mobile dental offices will start operating in different areas of frontline cities.

About thisreportedOn Facebook, Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova.

According to her, dental offices work on the basis of cars or trailers, which will allow doctors to approach the combat units as close as possible.

“Each such mobile clinic is equipped with modern equipment and supplies. Our defenders will be treated by crews of high-level dentists, ”Natalia Kalmykova said.
