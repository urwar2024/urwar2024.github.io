In the morning of March 17, Russia sent drones-Kamikadze to Nikopol of Dnipropetrovsk region, and drones hit the gas station.

About thisinformsHead of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration Serhiy Lysak.

"In the morning, the Russians sent two kamikaze drones to Nikopol. They hit a gas station," he wrote.

According to the head of the Regional Military Administration, three men aged 32, 43 and 67 were injured. Doctors provided them with all the necessary assistance.

The enemy attack was damaged by gas station equipment and a truck.

As reportedArmyinform, over the past day, Russian troops shelled the territory of 10 regions of Ukraine, 129 settlements were subjected to enemy attacks.
