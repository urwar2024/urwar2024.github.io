The German Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated that Russian "elections" in the temporarily occupied territories of Ukraine is another violation of international law.

This is said inmessageOffice.

“Pseudo -elections in Russia are neither free nor honest, their result will not surprise anyone. Putin's rule is authoritarian, it relies on censorship, repression and violence. "Elections" in the occupied territories of Ukraine are invalid and regular violation of international law, " - the message reads.

As reportedАрміяInform, more than 50 states of the United Nations have joined a joint statement with the condemnation of the efforts of the Russian Federation to hold pseudo -election in the temporarily occupied territories of Ukraine.
