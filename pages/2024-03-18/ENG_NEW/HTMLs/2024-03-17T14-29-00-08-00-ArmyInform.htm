From March 11 to March 17, units for the demining of the State Special Transport Service found, seized and disposed of 2907 explosive items.

About thisinformsThe State Special Transport Service in its telegram channel, reports the army.

The territory of 1658.99 ha was exchanged (checked).

  • Kherson - 1567,50
  • Nikolaevskaya - 30,07
  • Kharkiv - 49.76
  • Chernihiv - 10.76

In total, from the beginning of large -scale aggression of the Russian Federation, units of the State Supreme Service were discovered, seized and disposed of 122652 explosive objects.

The territory of 70922.69 hectares was exchanged (checked).

  • Water area - 21.66 ha
  • Roads - 866.07 km
  • Railway tracks - 2389.66 km
  • Power lines - 435,87 km
  • Gazoons - 43.20 km
  • Residential territories - 281.46 ha

Earlier the Armyinformreportedthat the forces of defense in a week eliminated more than 6.6 thousand Russians, 59 tanks and 168 artisms.
