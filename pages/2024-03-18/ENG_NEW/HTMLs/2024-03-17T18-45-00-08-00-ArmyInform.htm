The number of victims of today's Russian rocket strike in Nikolaev has increased to six people. Previously, five wounded were known.

About thisinformsThe press service of the National Police of Ukraine.

“The enemy attacked the city with rockets. Because of the shelling, six local residents were injured, ”the statement said.

The police also noted that as of the home of housing as a result of hostile shelling, vehicles and infrastructure are also damaged.

"Police bypass the affected premises and fix the destruction," - added in law enforcement agencies.

On this fact of shelling, criminal proceedings were opened under Art. 438 "Violation of the laws and customs of war" of the Criminal Code of Ukraine.

As reportedArmyinform, Mykolaiv strike was caused by the Spander-M ballistic missile from the territory of Crimea.
