About 15 settlements of Kharkiv region have suffered artillery and mortar shelling: Sinkivka, Ivanivka, Lukianka, Berestovo.

About thisreportedThe head of Kharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov in his telegram-channel, reports the Armyinform.

In the village of Liptsi, Kharkiv district, a private home and a private home were damaged as a result of hostile aviation shelling. Without the victims.

Also in the village of Dovgenke Kupyansk district also there was a firing of the territory, without the victims.

Over the past day, the enemy did not take offensive actions in the Kupyansk direction.

During the day in Kharkiv region, groups of explosives conducted a demining of 20.28 hectares of territory, destroying 382 explosive objects.

Earlier the Armyinformreportedthat the enemy attacks the most in two directions.
