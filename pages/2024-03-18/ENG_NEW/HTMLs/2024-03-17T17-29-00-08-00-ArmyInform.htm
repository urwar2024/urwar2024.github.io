Today, March 17, employees of territorial centers of completion and social support are celebrated in Ukraine in Ukraine.

Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of UkrainegreetedAll those involved with a professional holiday and thanked for the important and responsible work - the organization of mobilization and assembly of troops.

“How Ukrainians from the first day of a large -scale invasion of Russian invaders united and repelled the aggressor, struck the whole world.

Everyone remembers kilometer queues near military enlistment offices, registration, distribution, transportation of military personnel and means to military units, ” - they recalled those events in military intelligence.

GUR emphasized that mobilization workers made every effort to arrange, complete and promptly deliver everyone and everything for the front.

"It was on their professionalism that the resources and strengths of Ukraine and the defense of Ukraine depended or enough to restrain the enemy invasion in all directions," - summarized in the Head of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.
