President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky told in daily video reporting about units that showed themselves the best on the battlefield.

About thisinformsOffice of the President of Ukraine.

“First of all - gratitude to our soldiers. It is time to celebrate the combat units that have shown themselves the best, ”the Head of State said.

Thus, in the Kupyansk direction, the President noted the 57th separate infantry brigade named after the Cossack chieftain Kostya Gordienko.

The Liman direction is the 1st brigade of the operational purpose of the National Guard named after Hetman Petro Doroshenko.

Pokrovsky direction-55th separate artillery brigade "Zaporizhzhya Sich".

The Orikhiv direction is the 118th separate mechanized brigade.

The Dnieper direction is the 35th Separate Marine Infantry Brigade named after Mikhail Ostrogradsky and the 123rd Separate Brigade of Territorial Defense.

“Thank you all, warriors, for your power, for the effective destruction of the occupier. I am proud of every warrior who gets the results for his unit, for his brothers, for the whole of Ukraine, ”Volodymyr Zelenskyy emphasized.

As reportedАрміяInform, The forces of defense in a week eliminated more than 6.6 thousand Russians, 59 tanks and 168 artsystems.
