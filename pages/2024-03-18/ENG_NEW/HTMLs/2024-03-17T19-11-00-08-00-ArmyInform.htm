During the day, 63 combat clashes were recorded. In total, the enemy launched 7 missile and 55 air strikes, carried out 62 MLRS attacks on the positions of our troops and populated areas.

About thisReportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Towards KupianskThe enemy carried out 2 unsuccessful storms of the positions of defense forces in the area of the settlement of Sinkivka of Kharkiv region. He struck a bundle in the area of Basovo, Kharkiv region. More than 10 settlements were found under artillery and mortar fire.

In the estuary directionOur soldiers repelled 1 attack of the enemy in the area of the settlement of Rozdolivka, Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to break through the defense of our troops. More than 20 settlements were affected by artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Bakhmut directionOur warriors reflected 7 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Ivanivske and Klyshchiyivka of Donetsk region. There were about 10 settlements under artillery and mortar fire.

In the Avdiivsky direction11 enemy attacks were repelled in the areas of Berdychi, Tonenke, Pervomaiske, Orlivka, Oleksandropil and Nevelske settlements of the Donetsk oblast. The enemy carried out airstrikes in the areas of Netaylove and Novoselivka Persha settlements. About 20 settlements were shelled with artillery and mortars.

Towards NovopavlivkaThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of Krasnogorivka, St. George, Novomykhailivka and Vicoda, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, has tried to break through the defense of our troops 25 times. The enemy also struck a Shakhtarsky area. About 20 settlements were found under artillery and mortar fire.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy attacked the positions of our defenders 5 times in the areas of the robot and northwestern Willow Zaporizhzhya region. He struck aviation blows in the area of the settlement of Staromayorsk Donetsk region. About 15 settlements have undergone artillery and mortar shelling.

In the Kherson directionDuring the day, the enemy carried out 3 attacks on the positions of our troops on the left bank of the Dnieper. More than 10 settlements were shelled with artillery and mortars.

As reportedArmyinform, 58 combat clashes took place during the last day.
