In the village of Bugayivka, Russians fired a temporary ambulance location.

About thisinformsThe head of Kharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov in his telegram-channel, reports the Armyinform.

The enemy shelling of the village of Bugayivka of the Vovchansky community happened today, at 9:15.

The 36-year-old paramedic and a 56-year-old ambulance driver were injured. They are currently assisted in hospital in Kharkiv. The one -storey building where medical workers, buildings nearby, as well as two ambulance cars were damaged.

Earlier the Armyinformreportedthat during the day the enemy fired at 15 settlements of Kharkiv region.
