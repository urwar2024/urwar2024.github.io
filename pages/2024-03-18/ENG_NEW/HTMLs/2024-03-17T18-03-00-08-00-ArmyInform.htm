A modern rehabilitation center will appear in Zaporozhye, which will be able to receive assistance to more than 2500 military and civilians a year.

AsreportedThe chairman of the Zaporizhzhya Ova Ivan Fedorov, this was a meeting with representatives of the Zaporizhzhya administration and the Minister of Health Viktor Lyashko.

The parties discussed common medicine tasks in the Zaporozhye region, in particular, it was agreed to take care of the creation of high -quality and transparent medicine for both residents and health workers.

“We will create a modern regional rehabilitation center in Zaporozhye. More than 2500 military and civilians a year will be able to receive assistance in it, ”Ivan Fedorov said.

He thanked the doctors of the region who did not go, but stayed in Zaporozhye, 45 km from the front line to save life to military and civilian.

As reportedArmyinform, Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Natalia Kalmykova announced that in a few weeks there will be mobile dental offices in frontline areas.
