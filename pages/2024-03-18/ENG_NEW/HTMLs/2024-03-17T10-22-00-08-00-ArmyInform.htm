The Central Election Commission of Ukraine also officially confirmed this information.

About thisinformsThe Ministry of Reintegration of Temporarily Occupied Territories, reports the Army.

In particular, the CEC Resolution of February 23, 2024 No. 10 states that:

Any attempts to organize the preparation and carrying out on the territory of a sovereign independent Ukraine by the authorities of the Russian Federation and their officials of pseudo-elections are illegitimate. And their results in the event of the votes of voters in the temporarily occupied territories of Ukraine will be void and give grounds for non -recognition of the president.

The CEC also calls for foreign states and international organizations not to participate in the observation of the occupant pseudo-election process on the Tot, not to recognize the powers of the President of the Russian Federation chosen in an illegitimate way, including his legal personality in international relations and to stop any cooperation with and its bodies, exclude Russia from all international organizations.
