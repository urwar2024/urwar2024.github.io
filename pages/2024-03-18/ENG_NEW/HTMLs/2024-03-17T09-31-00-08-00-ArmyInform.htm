The Head of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Kirill Budanov cited two reasons why there was no protest in Russia in connection with large -scale losses in the war against Ukraine.

He stated thatIn the direct Etheri of Telelorphone "One News".

«The first is moral, because Russia is a real country of slaves. And the second reason is what everyone earns. For most Russians there is no other work but to go into armed forces», - Kirill Budanov explained.

The head of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine also noted that Russia's attempts to expand the circle of allies in Africa, Asia and Latin America were not successful.

«The range of countries that cooperate with the Russians, in principle, is not particularly changed. The main, let's say, Russian military ally is North Korea that supplies huge volumes of weapons. For Russians, it is critical - it will be very difficult to fight without it. Critically difficult», - said Cyril Budanov.
