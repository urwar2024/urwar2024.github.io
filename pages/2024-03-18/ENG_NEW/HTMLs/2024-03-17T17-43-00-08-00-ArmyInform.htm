The enemy today hit Balism in Nikolaev, releasing two rockets from the temporarily occupied Crimea, probably "Iskander-M". The tactics of Russians are an identical blow to Odessa.

About thisreportThe forces of the South Defense.

“The enemy continues the terror of the South. Infantry insidiously hit the infrastructure object in Nikolaev, releasing two rockets from the temporarily occupied Crimea, probably Iskander-M. The tactics of action are identical to the previous blow to Odessa: hitting one missile, in a few minutes he repeated the blow to the same place, ”the message reads.

The respective emergency services work on the spot.

As reportedArmyinform, as a result of rocket attack in the area, residential buildings and civilian cars were damaged. At least 5 wounded local residents are already known.
