Over the last day, 52 combat clashes took place in the operating area of the operational-strategic group of Tavriya troops. The enemy was struck by 18 air strikes, 114 blows of Kamikadze drones, and made 1142 shelling.

About thisreportedCommander of the operational-strategic group of Tavriya troops Brigadny General Alexander Tarnavsky.

According to him, our troops are firmly in defense and continue to destroy Russian invaders.

In addition, the commander noted that the total losses of the enemy are:

  • in living power - 360 people killed and wounded;
  • in weapons and military equipment - 27 units (excluding UAVs)

"In particular, these are 2 tanks, 7 combat armored vehicles, 3 artistem, 1 air defense, 13 cars and 1 unit of special equipment," he said.

As it is noted, our units have neutralized or destroyed 305 UAVs of different types. Also, the forces of Ukraine destroyed 1 more composition of the PMM of the enemy.

Oleksandr Tarnavsky also thanked for the accuracy of our soldiers, in particular those who destroyed the Russian anti-aircraft missile system 9K330 "Thor-M-2" and an automated station of obstacles of the R-330G "resident".
