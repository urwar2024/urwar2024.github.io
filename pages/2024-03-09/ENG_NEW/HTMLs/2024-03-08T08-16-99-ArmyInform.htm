A man and a woman were killed in their own private home, which was destroyed after the Russians.

About thisreported March 8Ministry of Internal Affairs Igor Klimenko.

According to him, the invaders hit around 22:00 on March 7 at Kupyansk private homes. Rescuers found the bodies of two people during the analysis of the rubble: a 64-year-old woman and a 58-year-old man. Another resident was injured.

In Donetsk, the Russians fired at Mirnograd and Novogrodivka. At least 2 people were injured, 4 more people who could not get out of the house saved the SES unit.

Damaged residential apartment buildings, administrative buildings, cars.
