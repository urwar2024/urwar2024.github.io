Within the framework of the Czech Republic's initiative to provide the Armed Forces of Ukraine with artillery shells raised funds for 300,000 ammunition.

About thisreportedCzech Prime Minister Petr Fial on the social network Kh.

“We sincerely thank all the countries that have joined the Czech initiative for the purchase of ammunition for Ukraine. We managed to raise enough money to buy the first batch of 300,000 artillery shells. However, our goal is to deliver much more, ”the message reads.

He stressed that the work and assistance of Ukraine does not end there and the Czech Republic continues to look for partners to continue to support Ukraine in its brave fight against the Russian aggressor.

Earlier, the media reported that the Czech Republic has already raised funds for all 800,000 shells that were found for Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, Luxembourgjoinsto the Czech initiative to purchase ammunition in support of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In total, 18 countries have joined the procurement of artillery ammunition for Ukraine. It is planned to buy 500 thousand ammunition of 155 mm caliber and 300 thousand 122 mm projectile
