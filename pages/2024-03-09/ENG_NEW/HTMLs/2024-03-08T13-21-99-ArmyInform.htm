The British company Bae Systems is ready to produce L119 gaubs 105 mm in Ukraine.

The Minister of Strategic Industry of Ukraine Oleksandr Kamyshin has informed about it after meeting with the UK Minister of Defense Grant Shapps.

“We are waiting for a contract and funding. They appealed to the British Ministry of Defense for support in this matter, ”Alexander Kamyshin wrote.

The Minister also thanked Britain for increasing the help package from £ 200 million to £ 325 million.

"We propose to send these funds to financing purchases from Ukrainian drones," he added.

The meeting also discussed the cooperation with the British Think Tank in the field of defense - Royal United Service Institute and St Andrew's University.

"Thankful Britain for a clear position and all the support given to Ukraine," Alexander Kamyshin added.

Howitzer L119 is already familiar with the Ukrainian army. It was handed over to Ukraine by Western partners within the framework of military assistance.

L119 is a light British gaub of 105 mm caliber. It is designed to support infantry, paratroopers and storms. Howy can affect hostile infantry and equipment.
