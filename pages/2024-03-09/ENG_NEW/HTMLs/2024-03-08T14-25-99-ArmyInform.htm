The court in absentia recognized a serviceman of the 64th Separate Rifle Brigade of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation in violation of the laws and customs of war.

About thisreported March 8Office of the Prosecutor General.

The court proved that during the occupation of the city of Bucha in March 2022, the sergeant and other Russian servicemen invaded the territory of households of local residents.

Convinced in the pro -Ukrainian position of two men, the convicted person forced them to kneel, threatened with weapons and murder.

In the presence of one of the victims, he discussed the variant of his murder, suggesting it in the basement and blowing up a grenade there. He also purposefully shot in the direction of the man.

The trial was carried out according to a special procedure in absentia, that is, in the absence of a defendant whose location is unknown.

Asreported the armyinform, the military of the Russian Federation was informed of suspicion of abuse of civilians.
