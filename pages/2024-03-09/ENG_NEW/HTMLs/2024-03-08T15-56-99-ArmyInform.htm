The first 473 servicemen who mobilized the Armed Forces after December 1, 2023 received financial assistance from the capital to bank accounts.

About thisinformsKSCA.

The people of Kiev were reminded that payment of 30,000 hryvnias is one -time and aimed at additional financial support for recruits.

The KSCA was also reminded of how to get a payment:

“To receive payment, the mobilized must provide the following documents and JV documents:

  • Copies of Identification Code,
  • passports of a citizen of Ukraine or ID card,
  • certificate/statement from the bank about opening a social (salary) account, ”the message reads.
