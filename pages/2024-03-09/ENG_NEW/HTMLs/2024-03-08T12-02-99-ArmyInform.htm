A few queues of this machine gun are able to stop the assault of the enemy.

Brown, Faraik, Virus - machine gunners of a separate mechanized brigade named after Yakov Gandziuk. We offer acquaintance with the warriors in the photo report of the Armyinform.

Machine guns are four people: gunner, shooter, service number and driver.

“And this is one whole mechanism. Everyone work on azimuth. Machine gun is a mechanism, and our calculation is also a mechanism that works smoothly. Everyone knows their work, ”the military says concisely.

The machine guns with which this calculation works is improved, equipped with thermal imaging sights.

- When the return goes, there is a great vibration. To avoid damage to the lens, we use a thermal imaging sight with a monitor that sees targets up to 4 kilometers, - the military explains.

- How many ammunition do we work? The more - the better. There are too many ammunition for the machine gunner. The minimum amount per day ... I think three thousand will be enough.

Machine gunners say that it is more interesting to work on armor than by living power: “Living power is more invisible. But working on the armor increases adrenaline. "

Brown recalls one of the cases where the Russians wanted to make a breakthrough and hoped that the Armed Forces would not see it and will not react.

- They drove "Pasik". The distance is about 800 meters. One short worked. During the day, I got 1100 cartridges, - recalls the army.

The chain of combat memories immediately continue colleagues:

- Behch was burned recently, when an enemy landing was planted ...

- And he didn't go back ...

- And Behah did not go anywhere anymore ...

The military says that their "board" is very capricious to moisture, temperature differences, sand - in fact.

- The main thing is a well -groomed mechanism. Everything should be greased and no dirt. This weapon is our older sister. When you go to a position, you become one with it. You have to say with her: "Oh, well -groomed, you have worked well today, you need to relax" or "Well, what are you starting? Need to rest? Well, let's rest. " I do not know how, but it helps, - says "virus".

The military emphasizes that it is most effective to work with DSKS, two or three cartridges. The advantages of this: you can adjust yourself, hear the gunner, feel whether the barrel is overheated.

-One or two queues are enough for hostile infantry to move back when he hears such a "small",-the machine guns say with respect.

Machine gun is not easy, so physical training is also important for the machine gun.

- I always played sports. There is no difficulty for me. We train, squat, run. The "breathable" should be developed, because to go far, you need to carry a lot of BC, weapons. My brother and I carried a "board" to a position, these are two kilometers of distance. Plus 50 to itself with BC and plus "board" from above. The machine guns are heavy, it's cool, - says Farik.
