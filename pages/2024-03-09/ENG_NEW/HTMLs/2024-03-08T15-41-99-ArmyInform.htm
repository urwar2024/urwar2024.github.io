The spokesman for the Navy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the captain of the 3rd rank Dmitry Pletenchuk, told how NATO countries suffer from the aggressive actions of the Russian occupation army.

Dmitry Pletenchuk gave his comments during the live broadcasting of the "Uniform News" on March 8, 2024.

According to a spokesman for the Navy of the Armed Forces, Russian mines in the Black Sea are sailed to the shores of NATO member states.

“Unfortunately, all countries that have access to the Black Sea face this problem. All these are the consequences of activity and aggressive behavior of the Russian Federation in the Black Sea. So the current is arranged in the Black Sea that other countries still suffer. Including NATO members, it is Romania, Bulgaria, and the Turkish Republic. And even the Georgian shores reached these mines, ”Dmitry Pletenchuk said.

He noted that these countries have to take measures to guarantee security in their waters.

“Constantly faced with these challenges, they are forced to respond to them in their territorial waters, first and foremost, protecting the safety of civilian navigation. Therefore, these measures continued, continue and will continue, ”the Navy spokesman said.

He added that the military-naval forces have already destroyed more than 100 such mines that he was thrown into the coast.
