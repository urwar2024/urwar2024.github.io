President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky addressed the International Day of Struggle for Women's Rights with a greeting to Ukrainian women.

About thisIt is saidIn his Telegram channel.

“Ukrainian women treat, teach, volunteer, save, protect their families and our country heroically. Professional, selfless, decisive, with great inner strength. Our women love and educate, protect and take care. Everything you do for Ukraine inspires the world, ”the head of state emphasized.

He thanked the Ukrainians for the multiplication of our common force.
