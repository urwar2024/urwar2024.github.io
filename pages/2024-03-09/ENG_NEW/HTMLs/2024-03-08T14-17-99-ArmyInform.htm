The Board of Managing IAEA approved a project of the resolution on the urgent return of the Zaporozhye NPP to the full control of Ukraine.

About thisreportedMinistry of Energy of Ukraine.

It is a resolution developed by Ukraine on nuclear security, security and guarantees.

The document calls on Russia for urgent withdrawal of all unauthorized staff together with the military from the ZPP and to immediately return the station under the full control of the competent authorities of Ukraine in accordance with the license issued by the State Inspectorate of Nuclear Regulation of Ukraine. This is necessary to guarantee its safe and reliable operation.

The adopted document, in addition, expresses serious anxiety by the unstable state of nuclear safety and security for the ZPP, especially the lack of proper qualified personnel on the site, gaps in carrying out routine-warning works, the lack of reliable supply chains, vulnerable water supply. in the buffer zone between the internal and external perimeter of the installation.
