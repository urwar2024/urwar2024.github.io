President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky celebrated 256 defenders of Ukraine state awards, 186 of them - posthumously.

The corresponding decree№147/2024Posted on the website of the Head of State.

The President decided to award defenders for personal courage, revealed in the protection of state sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine, and for selfless performance of military duty.

The head of state assigned the Order of Bohdan Khmelnitsky II and III degrees, Danylo Halytsky, Princess Olga III degrees, "For Courage", I, II and III degrees, awarded the Medals for Military Service of Ukraine, "Vishnya" .

As reported by the Armyinform, PresidentassignedThe name of Hetman Ivan Vyhovsky's second corvette of the ADA of the Navy of the Armed Forces.
