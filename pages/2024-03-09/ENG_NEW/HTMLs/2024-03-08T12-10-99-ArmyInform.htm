In the early days of a large -scale invasion of soldiers of DShV Ruslan Avtovnonov put a children's drawing with a simple inscription under the bulletproof vest: "Thank you for protecting us!".

Since then, the charm painted by the girl Pauline from Lebedin, supported and warmed the fighter in the most difficult situations, and even, he says, he had twice saved his life.

The Armyinform Form, a paratrooper with a cinematographic call sign "Bamblebi", told his story.

Two small homeland in a single Ukraine

The war has radically changed Ruslan's life from the very beginning of Russian armed aggression against Ukraine. Donetsk resident, in 2014 he was forced to leave his home and move to Sumy.

Ruslan's mother is a native of Sumy region, from Bilopillya, so he seemed to move from one small homeland to another within a single Ukraine.

The arrangement in a new place took for a while, but the man did not leave the desire to repay the enemy for a forced expulsion. The best option, in his opinion, was the service in the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Instead of being a “boar” decided to become a soldier

The first attempts by Ruslan Avtonov to join the ranks of the Ukrainian army were unsuccessful, did not help even previous experience in the State Security Service of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.

- At that time, it was problematic for natives of Ukraine to get to the ranks of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, - Ruslan recalls. - In 2014, some forms and documents of the military commissariats and other state authorities of Ukraine from Donetsk and Luhansk regions came into the hands of the enemy. This fact further complicated the already critical situation in a country that was not preparing for war.

The hero of our story did not put his hands and returned to public service. He got a job as a kinologist to train service dogs. Responsibility and love for animals quickly allowed him to gain authority and respect, but from the proposed training for the role of "boar" - an experienced instructor who trains dogs to attack himself - the man refused. Military service was more important to him.

Become a military nearly prevented the knee injury

In 2019, Ruslan tried to join the ranks of the Armed Forces of Ukraine again - and again encountered an obstacle. This time he failed to undergo a medical commission.

The reason for the refusal of doctors was the knee injury - Ruslan suffered it during the reconstruction of historical fencing for the famous Old Fortress festival.

The refusal was extremely upset by Ruslan, because he deliberately sought and prepared for service in the army for a long time. This was noticed by the officers who offered him a test two-week period, which the man withdrew with dignity, and then joined the ranks of the Airborne troops of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Bumblby's call sign gave historical fencing

The original call sign Ruslan was presented with the same historical fencing, trauma during which he almost prevented him from becoming a military.

During the training, friends, evaluating the good character and sociable attitude of Ruslan, began to call him "Bamblebi" in honor of the famous popular film hero from the films of the franchise "Transformers".

According to the plot of the films, it was the best to get along with people, first of all with children, was a true friend and a fearless fighter. Such a nickname perfectly suited Ruslan, who gladly kept him as a call sign in military service.

Help the Wife helped

Before a large-scale invasion, Ruslan served in a separate airmobile brigade of the landing troops of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, participated in the operation of the Joint Forces in eastern Ukraine.

At the beginning of a large -scale invasion, he had to defend his family, where his family, friends and acquaintances were. The war was not something new and unfamiliar to Ruslan, but the horror of the first days struck even such an experienced warrior as Bamblebi.

- I was very worried about my wife and my daughter, constantly thinking about how to save them. I thank Svetlana, who firmly said that the best way to protect them is to fight the enemy with his brothers.

Conversation with her husband gave Svetlana a ray of hope

Ruslana Svetlana's wife, who stands nearby, also mentions the first days of large -scale aggression:

- It was a day -to -day day. Covered despair. It was here that the man called and said that his unit in Sumy: "We are here, in Sumy!". And then I called my mom, called her girlfriends and reported: “Ours here. The Armed Forces did not throw us, our military will protect us! ”. It was like a ray of hope that it is probably something good.

Later, Svetlana supported the residents of Sumy, assuring that the military they saw on the streets was a Ukrainian army that came to defend the city. "She assured people in line that everything would be fine, our boys will protect us," she recalls.

Latte, cappuccino and "our eyes"

The first days were remembered by the couple with a sense of extraordinary cohesion and mutual support between civilians and military. Ruslan gratefully remembers the Sumy Terroboron fighters who fought side by side with paratroopers, and civilians who helped everyone they could.

- One uncle brought us oil for armored vehicles, mechanics were delighted because they needed it very much. Other time, a guy on a car from whom they trade coffee on the street came to us, and he treated everyone for free to all who only who wanted - Latte, Capuchino.

Particularly valuable, remembers Bamblebi, became the help of a young man who traveled by the car and regularly called the military, reporting the situation.

- I called him "our eyes" and always immediately took the phone as soon as he called, - the paratrooper recalls.

Drawing a girl from a swan

After the first fighting, the paratroopers went to Lebedin, where Ruslan was lucky enough to get a guard who rescued him twice from his death during the war. It was a picture of a local girl named Pauline with a simple sincere inscription: "Thank you for protecting us!".

The drawing with the food was brought by the military mom of Polina Natalia, and Ruslan left him, hiding closer to his heart under the bulletproof vest. Since then, the military has not parted with his charm until he saved his life in Zaporozhye.

- I was injured in Zaporizhzhya, and when I was removed from my bulletproof vest, they accidentally lost the picture. It was very upset, so I decided to find anything to find his author to thank her and ask to draw a new one,-says Bamblebi.

The new picture saved Bumblby for the second time

During his stay in the hospital, the military persistently sought the contacts of Pauline, but did not help carefully viewing social networks. Find the girl helped the media public amount thatThe plot was blownAbout the meeting "Bamblebi" with the girl.

The military brought her as a gift of the big bear and the Ukrainian flag, and Pauline gave him a new drawing, very similar to the previous one.

"And he, for the second time, saved my life," Ruslan says. - This time it happened near Kupyansk, where we got under enemy artobstil and the wreckage sewed my backpack, in which the drawing lay. I was injured, but I was escaped again thanks to the picture, - the fighter is convinced.

Children's drawings are the strongest charm

Unfortunately, this second drawing could not be preserved, so Bamblebi is waiting for the third. He continues to communicate with Pauline on social networks, sometimes they talk to video. Now he is on treatment, after which he is going to visit again, where he is gladly waiting for him.

- When I was confused or frightened, my hands no longer wanted to hold a machine gun, I always got a drawing of Pauline. He reminded you of whom and for what we are fighting, whom we defend. We protect our future, our children, - Ruslan is firmly convinced.

He recalls that children's drawings retain a lot of his brothers. These charms help the military to stay in the worst minutes under enemy fire, save from despair and despair, inspire confidence in victory.

- After treatment and rehabilitation, I hope to continue the service, - Ruslan completes our conversation. - Maybe I can become an instructor. The feeling that I will no longer be able to imagine myself civil. If not, I think to go back to cinema - I love dogs and work with them.

In any case, I want to devote myself to Ukraine's protection.
