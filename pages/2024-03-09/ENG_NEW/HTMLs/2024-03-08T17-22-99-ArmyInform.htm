The court has sentenced the former head of the military unit in Crimea for desertion and treason.

About thisinformsOffice of the Prosecutor General.

The sentence of the court was imposed on imprisonment for a period of 15 years with confiscation of all proper property.

The court proved that in 2014, during the occupation of the Crimea territory, the former head of the communication department - the head of communication of the headquarters of the military unit, did not appear to be given to the service of military service in order to evade the duties of military service.

"Having betrayed the military oath, he refused to perform the responsibilities of military service in the Armed Forces of Ukraine and moved to the side of the enemy, which he committed desertion and treason," the message reads.

From 2014 to this day the convicted person undergoes military service in the military unit 12767 of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation.
