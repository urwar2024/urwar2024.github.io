President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky awarded the Corvette of the ADA of the Navy of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Hetman Ivan Vyhovsky.

The corresponding decree№150/2024Posted on the website of the Presidential Office of Ukraine.

It is noted that the name was assigned to restore the historical traditions of the national army, the perpetuation of the memory of the prominent Ukrainian military, political and statesman of the hetman of the Zaporozhye Army Ivan Vyhovsky.

"To assign the corvette of the class" ADA "of the Navy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine the name of Hetman Ivan Vyhovsky and to call this corvette-Corvette" Hetman Ivan Vyhovsky "," the document reads.

As reported by the Armyinform, in IstanbulhappenedThe bookmark ceremony of the second ship of the ADA for the Navy of the Armed Forces.

Also in 2022 President of Ukraine Volodymyr ZelenskyyassignedThe name "Hetman Ivan Mazepa" is the first corvette of the ADA class, which was built for the Navy of the Armed Forces.
