Canada will join the drone coalition for Ukraine. Latvia and the United Kingdom are headed.

About thisIt is saidin a message on the government website of Canada.

It is noted that Canada's Defense Minister Bill Blair, at a meeting with Latvian colleague Andris Prauds, praised Ukraine's military support by Latvia.

The ministers have confirmed that both Canada and Latvia will continue to provide Ukraine with comprehensive military assistance.

“In order to further develop the potential of unmanned aerial vehicles in Ukraine, Minister Blair has promised that Canada would join a coalition on unmanned aerial vehicles at the Ukrainian Defense Contact Group, headed by Latvia and the United Kingdom. As a coalition member, Canada will continue to look for ways to strengthen the potential of drones in Ukraine, ”the Canadian Government website reads.
