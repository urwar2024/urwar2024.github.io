Russian information and psychological operations have intensified, which speculate on the topic of allegedly "redemption" of Ukrainian defenders from Russian captivity for money or the introduction of a captive serviceman into invented "lists for exchange".

About thisreported March 8Coordinating headquarters on prisoners of war.

The headquarters are reminded that the attackers regularly try to mislead relatives and close defenders, fraudulently liable to them for the return of captives from hostile captivity.

Law enforcement officers track and retain criminals, but fraudsters continue to find new casualties among prisoners.

The coordination headquarters urges to be careful when communicating with anonymous persons, to trust only official information from authorized Ukrainian institutions and not to be exposed to hostile provocations.

If you or your acquaintances have got into a situation where unknown people require money for a relative of a relative from captivity, immediately contact the coordination headquarters, national police or a united center for search and release of prisoners:
