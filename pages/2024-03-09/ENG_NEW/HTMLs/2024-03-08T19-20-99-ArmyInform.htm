There were 74 fighting collisions during the day. In total, the enemy caused 7 missile and 67 aircraft blows, and made 79 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In Kupyansk directionThe enemy attacked the positions of our defenders 3 times in the areas of Sinkivka and Kislivka of Kharkiv region.

In the estuary directionOur warriors reflect 4 attacks of the enemy in the areas of settlements of Tern and the disputed Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Bakhmut directionDefense forces reflect 9 attacks of the enemy in the areas of settlements of Andreevka and Klischivka of Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to improve his tactical position.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders reflect 14 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements Berdychi, Orlovka, Tonenko, Pervomaisk, Nevelskoye Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the areas of Vage, Krasnogorivka, Novomikhailivka, Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops 18 times.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy attacked the positions of our defenders 6 times in the areas of the settlements of the robot and west of the Verbov Zaporizhzhya region.

In the Kherson directionThe enemy made artillery and mortar shelling in the districts of more than 20 settlements of Kherson and Mykolaiv regions, among them the city of Kherson, Tyaginka, Ivanivka, Krynka, Novotyaginka.

We will remind, on the Left Bank of the Dnieper the defenders destroyed4 guns, 3 mortars and RSSV "Grad" of the occupiers.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Tavriysk direction the Russians were left without2 tanks, 7 BBM and Thor-M-2 missile system.
