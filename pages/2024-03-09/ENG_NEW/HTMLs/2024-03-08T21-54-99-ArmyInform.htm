The destruction of Russian ships in the territorial waters of Ukraine, as well as aircraft that violate our airspace will continue.

About thisstatedNSDC Secretary Alexey Danilov on the air of Ukrainian radio.

“The task is quite simple: no iron under the Russian flag in the territory of the Black and Azov seas will exist. We will destroy everything that will move within our territorial waters. We will not allow them to be here, ”Alexei Danilov said

He added that Ukraine, together with partners, prepares unpleasant "surprises" for the Russian army, which can not be discussed in public.

“The second situation is something that is directly surprised, they will definitely be. They will be pleasant to our community and very unpleasant - for the Russian Fuhrer, which is still sitting in Moscow, ”the NSDC secretary added.

Alexei Danilov noted that the Russian "aircraft", which lasted in February and was quite active, was due to the strengthening of Ukrainian capabilities by Western partners.

“We cannot tell about certain things taking into account the agreement with our partners, but believe me, the planes will continue to fall and not with less intensity. They will all be "planted" in our land, because we do not give the land, but only bury it, ”he said.

As reported by the Armyinform, on March 2 the Air Force Command of the Armed ForcesconfirmedBelling of the Russian fighter-bomber Su-34. In total, 13 enemy aircraft were shot down during February.
