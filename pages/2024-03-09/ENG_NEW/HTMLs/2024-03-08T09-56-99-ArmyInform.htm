On March 8, Volodymyr Zelenskyy will meet with President Recep Taiip Erdogan.

About thisreportedPresident's office.

During the visit, it is planned to discuss the topics of peace formula, the organization of the Peace summit, the safety of navigation in the Black Sea, global food stability, as well as the release of Ukrainian prisoners and political prisoners held by the Russian state.

Particular attention during the visit will be drawn to the common projects in the defense industry.

Volodymyr Zelensky will visit the ship, which continues the construction of corvettes for the Ukrainian Navy, will meet with representatives of Turkish defense companies.

Earlier, Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Dmitry KlimenkovcalledTurkey's defense companies to intensify cooperation with Ukraine.
