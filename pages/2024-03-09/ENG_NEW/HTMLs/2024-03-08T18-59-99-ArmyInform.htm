The Save Ukraine team rescued and returned to Ukraine -controlled territory of ten more children who, after a large -scale invasion of Russia, were occupied.

About thisreportedThe founder and head of the Save Ukraine Charitable Organization Mykola Kuleba in Telegram.

"Children after a long separation have finally been able to hug their dear, and now all families were safe," his post reads.

In his post, he also spoke about several children and their personal stories.

“Every time we are asked for help, we understand all the risks and all dangers for children. There, under the rule of the occupier, our children are deprived of any rights and ordinary happy childhood. Therefore, there is no time to delay. We start acting immediately, ”Mykola Kuleba added.

Today it is already 261 children rescued from Russia and occupation by Save Ukraine.

As reported by the Armyinform, USAjoinedto the international coalition for the return of Ukrainian children.
