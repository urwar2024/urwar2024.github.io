Experts of the Ministry of Defense have collected another 55 tons of scrap of military origin in Kharkiv region.

About it reportsplaceDefense Office.

The remnants of Russian tanks and armored vehicles will be explored about metal quality and sent for processing.

The money received for sale will be transferred to the support of the defense forces.

The department noted that the trophy equipment, which could be put in line or used as a donor - was taken from the fields at once. Now in the liberated territories there is an iron that is suitable for museums or for disposal.

"The tasks of the Ministry of Defense - to obtain this technique as scrap metal," - said Oleg Koval, Head of the Department of Exclusion and Disposal of Excessive Military Property of the Ministry of Defense.
