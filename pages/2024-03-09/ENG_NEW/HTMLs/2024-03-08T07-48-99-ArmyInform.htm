In total, seven locals were injured.

About thisIn the morning of March 8 reportedHead of Kharkiv Regional State Administration Oleg Sinegubov.

Three of the victims were hospitalized, others provided medical care.

According to the investigation, on March 8 at about 00:05 the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation struck a rocket strike in the center of Chuguev.

Seven people were injured, among them a 16-year-old boy and a girl 3 years 8 months.

Nine -storey residential building, bus station, hotel, shops, cars were damaged.

According to preliminary information, the enemy hit the city with a rocket type C-300 from the territory of Belgorod region of the Russian Federation.

BeforeIt became knownthat during the day the enemy caused air strikes in 4 regions of Ukraine.
