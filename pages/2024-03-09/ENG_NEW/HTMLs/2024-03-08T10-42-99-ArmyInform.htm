Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine Lieutenant General Ivan Havrylyuk drove several hundred kilometers along the front line to see at the spot at what pace the defense borders are arranged.

About thisreported March 8Ministry of Defence Ukraine.

“Objectively, recently engineering work on the construction of defense lines has been rapid. For several weeks, this is facilitated by the weather. New forces and technical capabilities are involved. The government has allocated the necessary funds, ”Ivan Havrylyuk said.

According to him, the plan of fortification of defense borders, which defines the volume of work, terms of execution and responsible for each section, was drawn up and approved.

Earlier the issue of strengthening fortificationwas consideredAt the meeting of the Supreme Commander.
