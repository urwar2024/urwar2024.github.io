The European Union launched Ukraine Facility Program. In the next two months, Ukraine will be able to receive 6 billion euros.

About thisstatedPrime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal during a government meeting.

“Ukraine Facility is a four -year program for 50 billion euros. In the next 2 months, Ukraine will be able to receive 6 billion euros from this amount. To do this, at a government meeting approved the necessary documents. It is about projects of a memorandum and a loan agreement with the EU, ”Denis Shmigal said.

According to the Prime Minister, the adoption of these documents will allow up to € 4.5 billion in March and up to € 1.5 billion in April this year.

The head of government also said that Ukraine Facility provides € 38.2 billion in direct budget support, almost € 7 billion to attract investments in our economy, as well as 4.7 billion euros of technical and administrative support, including reforms on the EU.

“This year, we strive to get about EUR 16 billion from the European Union. We are grateful to our European friends for this solidarity and help, ”the Prime Minister of Ukraine emphasized.
