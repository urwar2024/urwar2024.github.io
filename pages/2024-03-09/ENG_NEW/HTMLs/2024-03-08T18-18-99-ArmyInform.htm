The prosecutor's office exposed a judge who spied on his colleagues and defense forces in favor of the aggressor state.

About thisinformsOffice of the Prosecutor General.

"Under the procedural leadership of the prosecutors of the Office of the Prosecutor General of the Judge of the Bryankiv District Court of Lugansk region, it was reported suspicion of treason (Part 2 of Article 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine)," - the message reads.

According to the investigation, the suspect collected and transmitted confidential information about Ukrainian judges and servicemen of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

As it is noted, since 2014, he has not been justified and is hiding in the territory of the aggressor state. As registered in various closed judges, at the task of the FSB of the Russian Federation, he collecting data, photos, contact numbers of telephone and posts of judges with an active pro -Ukrainian position.

“The suspect was transmitted during personal meetings and through messengers. For this, the invaders helped the judges decide personal business in the territory of the Russian Federation. Particularly interesting to the enemy was information about the activities of the Ukrainian soldiers, which are held captive by the Russian Federation, in order to put pressure on them during interrogations, ” - said in the department.

The investigation found that the judge transferred a representative of the aggressor state of a photograph of a serviceman who was captured by the Russian Federation for further recruitment of his son.

In addition, this judge is suspected of another criminal proceedings and was wanted.

The pre -trial investigation is carried out by investigators of the DBR for operational support of the SBU.
