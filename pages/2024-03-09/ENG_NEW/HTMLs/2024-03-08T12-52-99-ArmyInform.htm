Luxembourg joins the Czech initiative to purchase ammunition in support of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

About thisannouncedLuxembourg Defense Minister Yurino Bekes.

"This will be part of our joint efforts to provide Ukraine with the necessary opportunities in the fight against the Russian aggressor," the Minister of Defense said in the social network.

The day before, the Czech President Peter Pavel confirmed that the Allies of Ukraine had transferred the entire amount of funds needed to transfer 800,000 artillery shells to her.

According to him, 18 countries have joined the procurement of artillery ammunition for Ukraine.

Peter Paul predicts that shells have to come to Ukraine "in the coming weeks".

The required amount for purchase of 500 thousand ammunition of 155 mm caliber and 300 thousand 122 mm shells was collected in less than three weeks.

As reported by the Armyinform, March 7 to UkrainecameAnother party of 155 mm of artillery shells from Lithuania.
