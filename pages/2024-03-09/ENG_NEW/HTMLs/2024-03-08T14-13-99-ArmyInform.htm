Ukrainian special forces destroyed in the Zaporizhzhya region the Russian automated station of obstacles of the R-330 "Life".

The forces of special operations informed about it.

During the reconnaissance in the Zaporizhzhya direction, the operators of the intelligence BPAC of the 3rd separate Regiment of the SCO were discovered by the ASP R-330 "Life".

The SCO operators have accompanied an important goal for some time and provided information to the missile and artillery unit of defense forces for further damage.

Thanks to the coordinated work of the military, the next "life" and its crew did not survive under the Himars missile blow, given by the SCO operators.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the south of Ukraine the forces of defenseEliminatedAnother 67 Russian invaders and destroyed enemy equipment, in particular, the MSTA-B gun and the station "Field-21".
