The United States announced the accession to the international coalition for the return of Ukrainian children illegally deported by Russia.

This is stated in a message onSiteUS State Department.

The statement stressed that the United States joins the coalition to support the safe return of all Ukrainian children who have been illegally deported or forcibly displaced by Russia and to ensure that the perpetrators are held accountable.

The United States also confirmed their support for the text of the Joint Declaration on the creation of an international coalition for the return of Ukrainian children, previously published by Canada and Ukraine.

Earlier Ukraine and CanadaCreatedInternational Coalition on the return of Ukrainian children. She started her work in February this year.

It is noted that the coalition will focus on the following basic purposes: coordination of joint efforts, exchange of information, in particular at the level of intelligence, to improve understanding and respond to Russia Coordination of actions aimed at a strong joint advocacy and communication.

The total number of coalition member countries is now 29.
