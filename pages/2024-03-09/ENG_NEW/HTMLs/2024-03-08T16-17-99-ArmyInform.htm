We continue to beat the enemy in the Zaporozhye direction. The invaders suffer great losses in living strength and technology.

About thisreportedCommander of the operational-strategic group of Tavriya troops Brigadny General Alexander Tarnavsky.

“Often, due to the lack of armored vehicles, invaders use hand-made vehicles for assault-golf carays, ATVs, Bagi and more. Accordingly, the Russians increase losses because these "hubs" do not give any additional protection. We are already calling them "Hands of Meat Assault," he said.

In addition, the commander showed the work of an indomitable 53rd Mechanized Brigade named after Prince Vladimir Monomakh.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Tavriysk direction the Russians were left without2 tanks, 7 BBM and Thor-M-2 missile system.
