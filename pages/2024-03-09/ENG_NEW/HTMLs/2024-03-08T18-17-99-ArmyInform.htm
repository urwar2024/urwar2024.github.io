In order to ensure the Armed Forces, from April 1, the Tyle State Operator will enter into contracts with suppliers within the current legislation without the use of Prozorro's electronic system.

This is stated in a message onSiteMinistry of Defense of Ukraine.

Today, a state -owned enterprise conducts market consultations with potential suppliers.

“We expect contracts for the next week. The main task is to provide the Armed Forces of Ukraine with nutrition of proper quality within the set deadlines, ” - said in the“ state operator of the rear ”.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Kyiv District Administrative Court inrefused to satisfy the claimConcerning the appeal of the requirements set out in the Project of the Agreement for the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces.

Among other things, the plaintiff challenged the requirements for the obligation of suppliers to register tax invoices in a timely manner and to give the customer the opportunity to check the storage conditions of food supplied to servicemen.

These requirements were set by the "state operator of the rear" in order to involve in the supply of food capable, virtuous suppliers, which will provide the Armed Forces with quality products.
