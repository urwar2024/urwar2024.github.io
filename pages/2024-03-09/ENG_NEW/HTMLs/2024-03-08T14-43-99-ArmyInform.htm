In total, 26 people were injured during a Russian blow that occurred on March 7.

About thisreportedSumy Regional Military Administration.

Damaged premises of the school, central city hospital, regional center of emergency medical care, water utility. A total of 135 windows, 10 doors were knocked out.

The hospital damaged 28 windows and 2 doors. The windows are closed, the area is cleaned. The institution works, hospitalization is carried out.

In the Sumy Regional Center for Emergency Medical Aid and Disaster medicine, the administrative building was damaged, 36 window blocks, ceiling on 4 floors, 3 auditory windows on the roof, garage room, 12 window blocks, 6 emergency vehicles were damaged and damaged; Window blocks in outbuildings. A total of 59 window blocks were damaged.

21 windows and 2 front doors were damaged in the regional blood center.

In the municipal institution of the Sumy Regional Council, 4 windows, doors and roof are damaged.

The school damages 12 windows, 2 doors, the wall of the building.

Electricity, heat supply, central water supply and sewerage were restored. The establishment works in full -time mode.

16 pieces of equipment and 92 rescuers were involved in carrying out works to eliminate the consequences of rocket strike.
