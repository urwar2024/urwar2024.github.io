On the left bank of the Dnieper in the Kherson region, the forces of defense destroyed 62 invaders and a reactive system of Grad.

About thisreportDefense forces of South of Ukraine.

The command noted that the forces of defense continue to cause fire damage to the locations of the enemy, fire positions and rear.

In particular, the last day was confirmed that on the left bank the enemy has become less than 4 guns, 3 mortars, 14 units of automotive equipment, 2 antennas and 4 boats.

In addition, our warriors destroyed the enemy ammunition to the MSTA-B gun.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Tavriysk direction the Russians were left without2 tanks, 7 BBM and Thor-M-2 missile system.
