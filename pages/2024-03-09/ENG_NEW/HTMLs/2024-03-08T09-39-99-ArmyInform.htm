A meeting with the Government Delegation of the People's Republic, headed by Eurasia Lee Huey, was held at the Presidential Office of the President of Ukraine.

About thisreported March 8Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

The meeting discussed a security situation in our country and other topical issues.

In particular, the delegation from China was informed about the situation on the battlefield, the functioning of the "grain corridor", the process of release of prisoners of war, the protection of civilian hostages and the return of Russian children deported by Russia.

Representatives of the PRC were also demonstrated by the fragments of the downed rocket and other elements of the means of damage made in North Korea and transferred to the Russian aggressor for attacks against Ukraine.

Earlier Volodymyr ZelenskyycalledChina to participate in the Global Peace Summit.
