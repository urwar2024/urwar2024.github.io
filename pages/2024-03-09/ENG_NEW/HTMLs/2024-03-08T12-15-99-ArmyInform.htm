The employee of the State Emergency Service took a bribe for the registration of fictitious disability to a military person.

About thisreported March 8State Bureau of Investigation.

According to the investigation, the 33-year-old captain of the Civil Protection Service of one of the district departments of the SES asked to help his friend who is mobilized, to go to work in the Czech Republic. The rescuer said that he has the "needed connections" and will be able to help decorate the "employment" of the second disability group for departure.

He first estimated at $ 8000 and then raised the price to $ 16,000.

The transfer of funds took place in several stages. After receiving the next part of the amount - $ 3000 - the law enforcement officer was detained by DBR employees.

Earlier, the DBR in Volyn exposed a criminal group that crossed nearly 170 conscripts across the border.
