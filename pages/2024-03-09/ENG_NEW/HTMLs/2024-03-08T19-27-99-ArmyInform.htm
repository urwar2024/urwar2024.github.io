President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky began a meeting with Turkish colleague Recep Taiip Erdogan.

He is about itreportedIn the social network H.

“I am grateful to Turkey for its reliable support for Ukraine. Both states, the whole region and global food safety require the unconditional safety of navigation in the Black Sea. We can achieve this by working together, ”the message reads.

The President added that Ukraine is interested in strengthening bilateral cooperation and joint production with Turkish defense companies.

"We also need the help of Turkey in the release of our prisoners of war and all people, including the Crimean Tatars in Russian captivity," he added.
