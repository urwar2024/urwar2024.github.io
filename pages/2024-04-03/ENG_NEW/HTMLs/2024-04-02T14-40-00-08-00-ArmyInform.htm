New professional opportunities are opened for veterans and veterans - free exercises under the Certificate Program "Governance in martial law and post -war recovery".

AsinformsMinistry of Veterans, this project is embodied by NGO "School of Governance" and the Andrey Meleshevich Kyiv-Mohyla School.

The training will start on April 22, 2024, and the applications will continue until April 8 inclusive.

Goal- to promote professional development and improvement of qualifications of veterans and veterans who seek to associate their professional careers with the public service and local self -government. After training, participants will receive appropriate knowledge, skills and competences for successful enrollment in personnel reserves, passing competitions for positions, employment and further work in public authorities.

Veterans and veterans are invited that:

  • have no experience in the field of civil service and/or service in the MPS, but intend to employ in such positions in the near future;
  • They are in the state service or service at the MPS up to 3 years as of the date of competitive selection of students and intend to continue working in these positions in the near future.
