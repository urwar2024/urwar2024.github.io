From the temporarily occupied part of Kherson region to the territory controlled by Ukraine managed to return a 17-year-old boyfriend

About thisreportedHead of the Kherson Regional State Administration Alexander Proudin.

He noted that for two years a minor guy had to live in occupation and go to the Russian school.

“Most of all, the young man feared that after the so -called Junarmia, the Russians would make him fight against their homeland. He is now seventeen. All this time, the guy continued to hope for returning to Ukraine, ”the post reads.

The Save Ukraine volunteer organization helped to fulfill this desire. Now the guy with his family is in a safe place, under the supervision of doctors and psychologists.

In total, from the beginning of 2024, 48 children from temporarily occupied Kherson were returned.
