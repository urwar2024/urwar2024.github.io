Germany will provide Ukraine with 180,000 artillery shells as part of a contribution to the Czech Republic of Ammunition Purchase Plan for Ukraine worth 576 million euros.

About it in a commentReutersThe German Ministry of Defense spokesman.

Last month the German Minister of Defense Boris Pistorius announcedPackage of assistance to Ukrainefor the amount of 478 million euros, which did not take into account the support of the Czech initiative for ammunition.

"Thus, the total cost of both support programs is more than one billion euros," said a representative of the German Ministry.

The share of Germany will be 40% of the Czech initiative, added to the German Defense Ministry. In the Czech RepublicThey expectthat Ukraine will receive the first shells within the initiative in June.
