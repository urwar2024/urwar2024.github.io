Already this month, a new wave of inspections of military medical commissions will begin in the framework of digitalization and deburetration.

About thisstatedPrime Minister Denis Shmigal during a government meeting.

He noted that the government puts special emphasis on the medical needs of veterans. Therefore, the VLK checks will become regular, and "a new wave will begin in April."

“Another aspect is the maximum convenience of the HCP for the military.Here, in addition to digitalization and deburocratization, there are inspections of commissions by the profile ministry. More than 53 thousand medical and expert cases have already been tested. The violations provided by the recommendations will be recorded for each fact, ”the Government Head said.

As reportedArmyinform, 10 freelance military commissions at the Tax Code and JV were checked in Kyiv. During the audit, they found a number of issues that need to be worked out to improve the work of the VLK.
