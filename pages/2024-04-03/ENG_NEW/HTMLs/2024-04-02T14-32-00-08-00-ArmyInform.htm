Last week, 234 Ukrainians for a total amount of UAH 388 million received the loans for the purchase of housing under the Esosel program, of which 110 were servicemen and security forces.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

From the beginning of the Eosel Program, 8630 Ukrainian family has already borrowed loans for housing for UAH 13.3 billion.

Yes, last week under Esosel's program received:

  • 110 servicemen and security officers;
  • 16 teachers;
  • 23 doctors;
  • 10 scientists;
  • 61 family without their own home;
  • 4 VPO;
  • 10 veterans.

Recall that both representatives of individual professions and citizens who do not have housing or want to improve its conditions can use the available lending program.For the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces under contractThe preferential rate is 3% per annum. For other categories -participants of hostilities, veterans, IDPs, other citizens - the mortgage rate is 7%.
