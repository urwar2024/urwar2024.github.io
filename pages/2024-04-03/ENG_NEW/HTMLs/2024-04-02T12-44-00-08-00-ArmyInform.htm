The FSB agent - the son of the Chief of Security of Viktor Yanukovych, who smoked the filtration camp in the Zaporozhye region, will be tried.

About thisreportedNational Police of Ukraine.

The 28-year-old employee of the Russian FSB organized illegal detention of Ukrainians, conducting searches of their personal property and interrogations with the use of physical violence and imitation of execution.

The illegally detained were taken to the filtration camp on the territory of the temporary detention center No. 2 of the Kamyansko-Dniprovsky Police Department of the SUNP in Zaporizhzhia region.

According to preliminary estimates, there were up to hundreds of people. Those who have been able to escape are currently giving Ukrainian police screenings and talking about their terrible experience to the world press.

"He organized all the tortures," says a 51-year-old resident of Zaporizhzhia, who was lucky enough to get out of the filtration camp, in an interview with one of France's most famous editions "Le Figaro"-"I was beaten and choke. He imitated my execution and said that the Chechens would come and rape me ... ”.

During the breaks between torture, the occupier forced the woman to confess to Russian media chambers that she helped the Ukrainian army. This information was spread among local collaborators who killed the victim's dogs and threatened their lives and health.

In January 2024, the Curator of the Kamyansko-Dnipro FSB from the Russian FSB in January 2024, with the procedural guidance of the Prosecutor General's office, was declared suspected of violating the laws and customs of war committed by prior conspiracy (Part 2 of Article 28, Part 1 of Art. 438 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine), which provides up to 15 years in prison.

Currently, the National Police have submitted criminal proceedings with an indictment regarding the person involved in the court of Zaporozhye.

Investigators of the National Police have already installed 67 similar places of freedom for Ukrainians, 42 of whom are located in the Russian Federation.

All filtration actions in them are carried out according to the method developed by the FSB of the Russian Federation. It is aimed at separating patriotic Ukrainians and their subsequent moral breakdown.

Ukrainian law enforcement officers continue to collect the full base of evidence to persecute the Russian Federation for crimes of genocide of Ukrainians at national and international levels.
