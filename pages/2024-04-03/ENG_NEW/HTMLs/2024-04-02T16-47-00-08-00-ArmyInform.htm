A new service appeared in the action, which can apply to the International Register of Losses. It is only possible to apply for destroyed or damaged real estate.

About thisinformsMinister of digital transformation Mikhail Fedorov.

“We launch a new important service. Data from the International Register will be the basis for future reparations. The action becomes an important part of the transmission and display of damages from Russian aggression, ”he said.

To use the service, you need to update the application. In the first stage, the application can only be applied for destroyed or damaged real estate: private house, apartment, country or garden house or other living space.

In order to submit the application, you must be the owner of the property and have an act of examination from local authorities.

Next, you will be able to apply for forced movement, harm, health, torture and sexual abuse. The complaints will then be able to file business and the state.

As reportedArmyinform, President of Ukraine at a conference in Hague stressed that every potential aggressor should know what awaits him.
