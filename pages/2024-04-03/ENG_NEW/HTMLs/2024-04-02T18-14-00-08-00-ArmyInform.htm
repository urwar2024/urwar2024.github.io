Ilya spent more than 2 years in occupation, in separation with the most dear - his mother and grandfather. He is sixteen now. All this time, the guy continued to hope for returning to Ukraine.

About thisreportedIn the Ministry of Reintegration.

On the eve of a large -scale invasion, the guy, along with his grandmother, went to the Zaporozhye region to visit another grandmother. From there it was unable to leave for two years.

"The mother of a boy, a woman with disabilities, was not able to go her son," the Ministry of Reintegration said.

At the beginning of March 2024, Grandpa Elijah appealed to the Ministry of Reintegration to help take his grandson from the temporarily occupied part of the Zaporozhye region.

"Thanks to his grandfather's appeal, the experts of the project" Way Home "together with the team of the Ministry of Reintegration found a way to return the teenager home," - said in the department.

As reportedArmyinform, also from the temporarily occupied part of Kherson region to the territory controlled by Ukraine managed to return a 17-year-old boy.
