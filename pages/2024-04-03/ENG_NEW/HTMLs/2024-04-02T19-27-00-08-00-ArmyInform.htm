As a result of a hostile shelling of the village of Novoosinovo, Kupyansky district, one person was killed and one child was injured.

About thisinformsHead of Kharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov.

According to him, the occupiers fired at the settlement around 17:00.

“A 59-year-old civilian man was killed in place. An 11-year-old boy with injuries was hospitalized at a medical institution, ”Oleg Synigubov said.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Dnieperincreased the number of victims due to rocket strike: 5 children were injured.
