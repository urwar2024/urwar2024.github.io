From the morning, the enemy attacks Nikopol region with artillery and shock drones, there are significant destruction in the area.

About thisinformsHead of Dnipropetrovsk Ova Sergey Lisak.

“The enemy target the area in the morning. Twice hit Kamikadze drones and as much as artillery. Nikopol and the Chervonohrigor community got, ”he said.

According to the head of Ova, 19 private homes, 5 outbuildings, cars and 2 garages, greenhouse were damaged. The transmission lines and the gas pipeline were affected. Fortunately, here without the dead and wounded.

Sergey Lisak also noted that the accident happened today in Kryvorizhzh.

“Working in the garden, a 45-year-old man ran into an enemy projectile. As a result, he was injured. The victim was hospitalized, ”he added.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the Dnieperincreased the number of victims due to rocket strike: 5 children were injured.
