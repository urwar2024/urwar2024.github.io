Ukrainian defenders during the day, April 1, caused significant losses in the personnel and equipment in the Orikhiv and Kherson directions.

About thisreportThe forces of the South Defense.

“The forces of the South further inflict fire damage to the locations of the enemy, fire positions and rear. During the day, there were less than 126 invaders and 57 units of weapons and military equipment during the day in the Orikhiv and Kherson directions, ”the message reads.

In particular, it is noted that Ukrainian defenders have destroyed:

  • 2 tanks;
  • 22 units of autobroetens;
  • 22 artillery systems;
  • 2 UAV;
  • 1 radar station;
  • 1 complex of long visual observation;
  • 6 aerial stations;
  • 1 boat.

As reportedArmyinform, in the east, the invaders lost 10 tanks per day, 5 remedies and warehouses with ammunition.
