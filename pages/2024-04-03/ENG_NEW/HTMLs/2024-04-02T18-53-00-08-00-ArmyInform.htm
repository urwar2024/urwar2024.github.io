To date, 13 320 points of indomitability have been created in Ukraine - 110% of the planned number.

About thisinformsMinistry of Community Development, Territories and Infrastructure.

“According to local administrations, all of them (points of indomitable. - Ed.) Are provided with basic equipment for the provision of appropriate services to the population: fuel supply, generators, Starlin. There are already 12,674 points, and 646 are ready for opening, ”the message reads.

For the efficient use of resources and in connection with the onset of stable warm weather, some of the points of indomitability are transferred to the mode of "ready for opening", taking into account potential threats and limiting the supply of electricity, in the event of an emergency, they will be deployed within 2 hours.

At the same time, work is underway to display information about unbroken points in the mobile app.

As reportedArmyinformIn the action, a new service has emerged to apply to the International Damage Register.
