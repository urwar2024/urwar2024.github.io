The Security Service has assembled a evidence base on Sergei Boatikov-CEO of the Russian Project Bureau "Raduga".

About thisinformsSecurity Service of Ukraine.

As it is noted, this company specializes in the mass production of long -range missile weapons for Russian troops.

First of all, we are talking about winged rockets of type X-101, which Russians use to carry out air strikes on the civil infrastructure of Ukraine.

The investigation found that it was such a rocket that the occupiers kissed the apartment building in Uman on April 28, 2023.

The SBU told that then 23 locals were killed due to the air attack of the Russian Federation, including 6 children. During the fire, the entrance of 27 apartments was completely destroyed. For the uninterrupted supply of rocket weapons by the occupation group of Russia, the company headed by Bogatikov received the Kremlin's distinction "gratitude of the President of the Russian Federation".

“On the basis of the evidence collected, the investigators of the Security Service informed Sergey Boatikov of suspicion under several articles of the Criminal Code of Ukraine: Part 5 of Art. 27, Part 2 of Art. 28 and Part 2 of Art. 437 (aiding and abilities, committed by a group of persons), ”the message reads.

Comprehensive measures are currently underway to hold the attacker justice for crimes against our country.

The investigation was conducted under the procedural guidance of the Attorney General's office.

As reported by the Armyinform, the President of Ukraine stated that international efforts toprosecuting Russia for war crimesIn Ukraine, it is the line from which movement to a reliable peace can begin.
