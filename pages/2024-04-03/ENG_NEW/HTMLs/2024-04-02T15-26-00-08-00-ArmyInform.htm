Today, in Russia, the current stock of Russian rockets is almost 950 pieces, and in April this year, the invaders seek to produce about 40 missiles X-101.

About thisreportedRepresentative of the GUR of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Vadim Skibitsky.

“Now, according to our estimation, there are about 950 high-precision missiles of operational-strategic and strategic levels with a range of more than 350 kilometers. We see the tendency that they tend to try to keep their stocks at least 900 missiles, ”Vadim Skibitsky said.

He added that in April this year Russia seeks to produce about 40 missiles X-101.

“We predict that Russians will be able to carry out a few more massive attacks (2-3 are the most massive), and then they will be forced to take some pause for the accumulation of rockets and "Shahmed", "Vadim Skibitsky said.

However, the representative of GUR MOU emphasizes, some local shelling may continue.
