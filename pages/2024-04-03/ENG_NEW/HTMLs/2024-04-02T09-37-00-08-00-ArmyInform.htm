Russian invaders continue to fire the border areas of Chernihiv and Sumy regions.

About thisreportedOperational Command "North".

In total, 35 shelling (175 explosions) from different types of weapons were charged during the day.

In Chernihiv region, the enemy fired at the settlements of Semenovskaya and Snovsky communities, applying RSSV, barrel artillery and mortars.

In Sumy region, Okhtyrsky, Shostka and Sumy districts were fired. The enemy used mortars, artillery, drones and released many rockets from the helicopter.

Information on the destruction, dead or injured among the local population is specified.

As reported by the Armyinform, occupiers during the day353 beats were struck8 settlements of Zaporizhzhya region.
