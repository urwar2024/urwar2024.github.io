In the temporarily occupied territories, Russians issue collaborators to visit Dagestan and Ossetia as a reward for "service".

About thisinformsCenter for National Resistance.

"The enemy has created a travel agency in the temporarily occupied territories, which will be engaged in the organization of tours to Ossetia and Dagestan," the department said.

These are the vouchers of the collaborators who have been the most in front of the invaders. Issuance of vouchers is scheduled for May.

"Having destroyed the tourist cluster on seized lands, limiting the free movement of the local population, the enemy motivates traitors tours to other enslaved peoples," the National Resistance Center emphasized.
