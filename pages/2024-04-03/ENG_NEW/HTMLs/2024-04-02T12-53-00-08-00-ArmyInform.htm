The forces of special operations of the Armed Forces of Ukraine announce the recruitment for the qualification course. Applications for participation in selection are accepted by April 23, 2024. The start of the Q-course is scheduled for the beginning of June 2024.

About thisinformsCommand of Special Operations forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

It is noted that the requirements for candidates: age - up to 35 years (senior - according to the results of the interview). Only servicemen of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, who are in military service under contract or called for mobilization, are allowed before the selection.

The duration of the qualification course is 4 months.

You can apply here (page update to the original means that the application is submitted) ➡️ https://sof.mil.gov.ua/join

Use your chance to stand in the ranks of the SCO of Ukraine!
