The Russian military was sentenced to 15 years in prison for the shooting of a woman in Izium, which communicated in Ukrainian.

About thisinformsOffice of the Prosecutor General.

Prosecutors proved that on June 2, 2022, a local resident shot a local resident at that time Izium.

Condemned-Deputy Commander of the 3rd Tank Battalion for Weapons of the 6th Separate Tank Brigade (54096, 1st Panzer Army of the Western Military District of the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation).

The woman approached the headquarters of Russian servicemen, where there was Wi-Fi and communication. She wanted to call her brother living in the Ivano-Frankivsk region.

“A convicted person came out of the headquarters who heard Ukrainian from a woman. It was angered and angry. He approached her with a gun and shot in his chest. The victim died on the spot, ”the message reads.

The court found the Russian military guilty of violating the laws and customs of war combined with premeditated murder.

He was sentenced to 15 years in prison.
