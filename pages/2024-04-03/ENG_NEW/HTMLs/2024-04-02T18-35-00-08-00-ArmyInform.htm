President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky has signed a law that reduces the age of mobilization from 27 to 25 years.

About thistestifiesThe Card of the Law 9281 on amendments to the Project of the Law of Ukraine "On Military Duty and Military Service".

He was approved in May 2023, since then he has been signature of the President of Ukraine.

Today, the website of the Verkhovna Rada stated that the law was "returned with a signature from the President of Ukraine".

The project of the Law of Ukraine "On Military Duty and Military Service" was developed at the initiative of the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine in order to reduce the age of the age of citizens on military registration of conscripts from 27 to 25 years of age.

Earlier, Ukrainians aged 18-27 were subject to conscription. With the adoption of the law will be the opportunity to mobilize more young people to the Armed Forces.

We will remind that in June 2023 the project of the Law of Ukraine "On Military Duty and Military Service" was signed by the Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada Ruslan Stefanchuk.

As reportedArmyinform, in Ukraine, the demobilization of the ages called to the beginning of a large -scale invasion began.
