In Kharkiv, two recruitment centers for defense forces began to operate since April 2.

About thisreportedThe head of Kharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov.

“A person can come to the recruiting center, which is actually a civilian information and information office and get acquainted with a full list of vacancies. It is not only about the Armed Forces, but also the National Guard, the SPSU is a general system of defense forces, ”Oleg Synigubov said.

The vacancies are diverse because the army is now the largest employer in the country. It is a cook, a driver, a repairman, a financier, and a physician, and of course, there are those specialties that are engaged in the hottest battles.

The recruiter can consult, and then, if desired, to choose the position and the military unit where he sees himself. There is also a system of full training of future military.

Important: The recruiting center does not hand over the narratives and collect personal data without consent and need. Such an initiative is the continuation of the reform of the set of recruitment to the ranks of the army.

“Together with the Defense Ministry and the Communities, we will gradually work to promote recruiting centers, increase their number and coverage throughout the region. Contact detailed information at the free phone number: 0 800 300 441, ”added the head of Kharkiv Ova.

As reported by the Armyinform, March 22 in PoltavaOpenedArmed Forces recruitment center. Previously, such centers were opened in Lviv, Dnipro and Zaporozhye.
