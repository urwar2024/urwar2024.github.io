The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine agreed to allocate another UAH 5.6 billion for the construction of fortifications.

About thisreportedGovernment Head Denis Shmigal at a meeting on Tuesday.

The Prime Minister informed that UAH 1.1 billion from this amount will go for the construction of fortifications in Donetsk region, more than UAH 1 billion-for Zaporizhzhya region, UAH 1.5 billion for Sumy region.

In addition, Kherson region and UAH 300 million - Mykolaiv region will receive UAH 400 million for fortifications.

“Work goes in 24/7 mode. All this is needed to strengthen our defense and protect our warriors, ”Denis Shmigal added.

Earlier this year, UAH 20 billion has already been allocated for the construction of fortifications by the government.

As reported by the Armyinform, in Kyiv region alreadyestablishedAlmost 10,000 concrete pyramids have been equipped with an anti -tank ditch. Fortification structures are built in other regions. In Dnipropetrovsk alreadyThey dugMore than 42 km of anti -tank ditches.
