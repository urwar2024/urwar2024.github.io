Military Incentives and Instructors of the Canadian Armed Forces from the UNIFIER Operation at the UK military training grounds are trained in combat operations in the urbanized area of the Ukrainian Armed Forces.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

Classes are held within the framework of the multinational educational operation of the Interflex on basic general -military training of the personnel of the Armed Forces of Ukraine under the guidance of the United Kingdom.

Urban is a city building where the battle is worked out in the city: capture, cleaning and maintenance of objects in settlements and release from the enemy. These training sites are entirely reconstructed streets with residential quarters with houses with windows, entrances, stairs and attics, as well as mail buildings, bank, religious buildings.

“The Ukrainian military, who will liberate Ukrainian cities from the invaders, will have the basic hostilities among these activities. How to move in a group, where to expect danger and how to warn it, ”the message reads.

Gensabi noted that the new -breaking men who have not yet been on the battlefield are especially useful for such training. And it is for those who recently joined the Armed Forces of Ukraine, first of all, the training program is designed. After all, the multinational Interflex educational operation under the leadership of Great Britain is called to form basic skills in people who only occupied civilian professions yesterday.

“A group of Ukrainian soldiers, who are in video, train military from Canada. They teach Ukrainians the proper treatment of weapons, movement, observation and patrol, survival methods, first domestic assistance, ”the department said.

As they added to the General Staff, Canada and the United Kingdom continue to support the people of Ukraine and provide continuous support in response to long -term Russian aggression and occupation of Ukrainian territories.
