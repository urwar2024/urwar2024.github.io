The Russian military is constantly trying to break through the Ukrainian positions in the Avdeevsky direction, carry out assault actions and intelligence, but now have no success.

The officer of the 59 Separate Mechanized Brigade named after Jacob Gandziuk Major Sergey Teshotsky told about it on the air"The only news."

“Small assault infantry groups go almost continuously. This is accompanied by constant escorts of FPV and artillery shelling, ”said Sergei Teshotsky.

He also commented on the mechanized attack of the Russians, during which 36 tanks and 12 enemy BMPs tried to break through Ukrainian defense. Our defenders destroyed 12 tanks and 8 BMPs.

According to experts, this attack has become the largest since October last year and one of the largest large -scale invasion for over two years.

“All the heavy equipment that appears on the battlefield is the first target. All the forces are concentrated there. Drones rise, artillery works. Then the enemy panic, throws equipment and runs out of the battlefield. Further, the infantry is sole and the drones burn the equipment so that it cannot be restored, ”said Sergei Teshotsky.

According to him, there were cases where the Russians rose to a mental attack in two directions. In one place, equipment was used, for example, in the area of Pervomaisky. At the same time, a group of up to 30 people came to Nevel.

"They went full height, through the field, no matter what," the officer added.

As reported by the Armyinform, in the last days of March in Donetsk Russiansuffered an epic defeat,Trying to attack the positions of defense forces - the destruction of a large column of hostile equipment is confirmed by 25 separate airborne Sicheslav Brigade.

On Monday, April 1,25th OpDBRandDShV commandThey spread a video that recorded the destruction of a column of enemy equipment, which can count about ten pieces of equipment.
