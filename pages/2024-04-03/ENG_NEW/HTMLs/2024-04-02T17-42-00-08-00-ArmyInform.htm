From the NBU special account, since the beginning of the large -scale war, almost UAH 34.5 billion has been transferred to the needs of defense, in March the account has been supplemented by more than UAH 182 million.

About thisinformsNBU press service.

"In general, since the beginning of the war, the National Bank has transferred almost UAH 34.5 billion for defense for defense, in March 2024 - more than UAH 358.1 million," the statement reads.

As of April 1, 2024, the residue for a special account, which the National Bank opened to support the Defense Forces on February 24, 2022, amounts to almost UAH 1.5 billion.

The funds were transferred by both citizens and enterprises of Ukraine and the international community (including the United States, Great Britain, Germany, Sweden, Finland, Poland, Switzerland, Norway, Australia, France, Canada, Bulgaria, Hong Kong and many other countries).

As reportedArmyinform, another UAH 500 million was allocated for the construction of the rehabilitation building for the military in the Rivne region.
