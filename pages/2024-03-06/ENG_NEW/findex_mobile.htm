General AtomAutical Systems (GA-ASI), which was created for the US Air Force Second Sensing Station (OBSS), made its first flight.

As the War Zone's defensive portal informs (Twz), we are talking about a new stage of UAV tests with a set of advanced sensors, capable of acting in front of manned fighters and transmit them data on the location of the enemy, the Army Informs.

It is planned that using a classic configuration gap with standard sub -structures and systems, it will be possible to quickly improve the design of unmanned aerial vehicles - an extra -cabin combat station (OBWS) for battle or extra -storage station (OBSS), equipped with a radar complex, which is particularly needed .

General Atomics received an OBSS contract in October 2021 with Kratos. Subsequently, General Atomics was chosen to move to the next stage, which included the creation and flight tests of the device.

The first XQ-67A flight was based on General Atomics Grey Butte flight control. The purpose is both the overall design of the design and the special emphasis on OBSS. When the glider is recognized as ready, it will be installed sensors, autonomous avionics and other systems and a payload.

«The main goals—check the concept of open aviation system for hardware and software, as well as demonstrate a rapid market exit and low cost of development»,Said OBSS Trenton White.

The technical characteristics of the drone do not reveal. From his photos, experts concluded that this is the Middle Plan. In general, the new UAV is similar to the famous shock drone MQ-20 Avenger from General Atomics and unmanned aircraft MQ-25 Stingray from Boeing.

The XQ-67A received a three-fold chassis, the V-shaded plumage and a wing with a small angle of boom. The XQ-67A engine is located at the top of the fuselage. Also on the apparatus were noticed panels, which can be antennas of radar of the lateral inspection.
