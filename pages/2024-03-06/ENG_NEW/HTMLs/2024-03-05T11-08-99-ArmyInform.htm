Ukrainian defenders have destroyed 19 enemy guns on the left bank of the Dnieper during the last day, including Hyacinth-C.

About thisreportedDefense forces of South of Ukraine in Telegram-channel.

The forces of defense continue to cause fire damage to the locations of the enemy, firing positions and rear.

It is also reported that 75 invaders were eliminated, 3 mortars, a MUR-M CCTV complex, a video surveillance station were affected.

In addition, 6 complexes and stations of electronic wrestling, 4 ground control stations, 15 units of auto and armored vehicles were destroyed.

Two enemy armor and three observation points were destroyed.
