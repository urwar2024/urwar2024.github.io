The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine spoke about key changes with the adoption of the Law "On Amendments to Certain Laws of Ukraine on the Implementation of Standards of the Organization of the North Atlantic Treaty in the Field of Medical Safety and Defense Forces".

About thisIt is saidon the website of the defense department.

Yes, this law is given by the Ministry of Defense with new powers, in particular, to approve NATO standards for all security and defense forces:

  • logistical and technical equipment;
  • standards;
  • The volume of assistance at the pre -hospital stage.

New norms will also standardize providing assistance at the pre -hospital stage, which, in turn, will help to save the lives of Ukrainian defenders.

"The norms of the new law will help save thousands of lives of the Ukrainian military and create a truly effective health care system for servicemen," said Natalia Kalmykova, Deputy Minister of Defense of Ukraine.
