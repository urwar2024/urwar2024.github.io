In the Black Sea, as of Tuesday morning, ships of the Russian military fleet are absent.

About thisreportedNavy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In the Mediterranean, there are three enemy ships among two carbive carriers, a total volley of up to 12 missiles.

During the last day in the interests of the Russian Federation the Kerch Strait, 7 vessels passed to the Azov Sea, two of them moved from the Bosphorus Strait.

15 vessels were passed to the Black Sea, 4 of them continued the movement in the direction of the Bosphorus.

"The Russian Federation continues to violate the International Convention on the Protection of Human Life at the Sea of 1974 (SOLAS), turning off automatic identification systems (AIS)," the Navy emphasized.
