The suspicion of violating the laws and customs of war, combined with premeditated murder, was reported to two military personnel.

About thisreported March 5Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, in the evening of March 19, 2022, two commanders of self -propelled artillery guns, while on the territory of Bucha, noticed a local resident dressed in civilian clothes with a white bandage.

The man was a civilian and did not participate in armed conflict. He lacked any weapon, tools or means that could justify the use of violence to him.

The invaders opened fire from small firearms without warning about the use of weapons. He died on the spot from the gunshot wounds.
