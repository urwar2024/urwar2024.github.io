The employees of the Zaporizhzhya NPP, who did not sign fake contracts with the occupiers, blocked the passes for passage to the station.

About thisinformsNNEGC "Energoatom".

Energoatom's head Petro Kotin noted that since the beginning of February 2024 the occupation administration of Zaporizhzhya NPP has banned the Ukrainian staff with a passage to the site. By this time, 360 atomic people worked at the station, which remained true to Ukraine.

In addition, since the illegal seizure of the station, nearly 5,200 experts have left the temporarily occupied energy, having left the territory controlled by Ukraine and to the EU countries.

He added that the fate of 17 people is still unknown.

“The occupiers replace qualified Ukrainian staff with people from cities near Energodar, who have no idea of the operation of nuclear objects. This, in turn, leads to illiterate actions with the equipment of the station, which makes it constantly degraded, ”Petro Kotin said.
