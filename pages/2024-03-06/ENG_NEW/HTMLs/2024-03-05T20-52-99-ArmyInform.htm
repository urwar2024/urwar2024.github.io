Viceremier-Minister Irina Vereshchuk made a working visit to the city of Irpin and the village of Gorenko of Kyiv region, where the reconstruction of the houses destroyed as a result of hostilities is being reconstructed.

About thisinformsMinistry of Reintegration of Temporarily Occupied Territories of Ukraine.

As it is noted, one house in Irpen will be built from scratch. The other four, two in Irpen and two in Gorenka - they will be overhauled.

The project is implemented with the financial support of the German government with the assistance of the Ministry of Reintegration. Financial partner - German State Bank KFW. The project manager is an international migration organization (IOM).

Irina Vereshchuk, together with representatives of the Kiev OVA, local authorities, KFW and IOM, examined the damaged homes and talked with residents. At the same time, the details of construction and the schedule of its implementation were discussed.

According to the department, there was also a separate meeting between representatives of the Ministry of Reintegration, IOM and local authorities, which discussed specific steps for the implementation of each of the projects in the Kiev region. As well as problematic issues that need to be addressed before construction.

In particular, they focused on the issues of demining and preserving Mural Banksi on the wall of one of the houses in Gorenka.

“I thank everyone who works on the implementation of these projects. We have to give people a roof over their heads as soon as possible, ”Irina Vereshchuk emphasized.
