During the Dragon-24 exercises, the Polish Army and NATO units work through the Vistula River. More than 3.5 thousand servicemen and a thousand pieces of equipment were involved in the maneuver.

About thisIt is saidOn the site of the Ministry of Defense of Poland.

As the units of nine countries overcome the Vistula River will be observed by the Presidents of Poland Andrzej Duda and Lithuania Gitanas Nauseda, as well as the Polish Defense Minister Vladislav Kosinak-Kkysh.

“Such exercises are performed by NATO's restraint and are not directed against anyone. But the context of what is happening today in the world is obvious. Obviously, we must be ready here and now, to fulfill our obligations to our allies every day, ”said the head of the Ministry of Defense of Poland.

In total, about 20,000 soldiers and 3.5 thousand vehicles from 9 NATO countries are involved in Dragon-24. About 15,000 soldiers are involved in the Polish side. Dragon-24 is a check of the interaction and performance of combat missions on Earth, at sea, in the air and cyberspace.
