The occupiers force Ukrainians from temporarily occupied territories to participate in the Presidential Election through the compulsory "flashmob" when families should post photos from polling stations.

About thisinformsCenter for National Resistance.

"The enemy announced the flashmob" The whole family - for the polls ", within which participants should post photos of the family at" polling stations "on social networks," the statement reads.

It is noted that all employees of fake state institutions on a temporarily seized Luhansk region have already been informed of the mandatory participation in the flash mob.

"In this way, the Russians will control attendance and hope to create an imitation of masses in the fake elections, which are conducted for the purpose of propaganda," - said at the Center for National Resistance.

At the same time, resistance recommends that Ukrainians try to ignore the "elections" and avoid "polling stations".

As reported by the Armyinform, Russiansdrive awayPersonnel of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation for "elections" in occupation to increase "turnout".
