In Kramatorsk, Donetsk region, rescuers completed the parsing of rubble on the site of the destroyed shelling of the enterprise building on February 20. Under the rubble, they were looking for a man for almost two weeks, but did not find it.

About thisinformsSES of Ukraine.

“Rescuers dismantled 980 tons of destroyed structural elements of the building. The 100% blockage was disassembled, but the man could not be found. The work is completed. A man is considered missing, ”the department said.

Recall February 20happenedThe city's rocket firing, which was partially destroyed by a two -storey building in one of the enterprises. According to information, 1 person could be under the rubble. The search operation lasted for two weeks.
