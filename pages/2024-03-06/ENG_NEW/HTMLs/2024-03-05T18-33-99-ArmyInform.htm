The European Commission has confirmed that Ukraine will receive a negotiation frame for accession to the EU in March this year.

About thisinformsPrime Minister of Ukraine Denis Shmigal.

“Arrest, European integration. Its dynamics should remain as high as in the previous two years. The European Commission has confirmed that the EU negotiation frame will be ready in March, ”Denis Shmigal said.

According to him, this means that Ukraine and the European Commission can start negotiations quite quickly.

"For our part, we will do all the" homework "in a short time and count on the promptness of European partners in political decision making," the Prime Minister said.
