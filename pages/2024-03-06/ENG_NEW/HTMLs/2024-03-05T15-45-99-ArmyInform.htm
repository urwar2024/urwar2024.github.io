In Bukovina, the state registrar will be tried for losses for almost UAH 43,000,000, he illegally gave the land of the Ministry of Defense of the local self -government body.

About thisinformsSpecialized prosecutor's office in the field of defense of the Western region.

The investigation found that the official illegally alienated 12 hectares of land on which the plant was located. He re -registered the land owned by the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine, according to the local self -government body.

“The accused without the permission of the defense department was assigned to this territory the cadastral number and changed the actual owner. Thus, it caused losses to almost UAH 43 million, ”the message reads.

At present, involvement in the crime of others is being checked.

As reportedArmyinform, the court returned the state defensive lands along the border with Romania worth more than UAH 803 million.
