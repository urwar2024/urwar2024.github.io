Since mid -February, the State Border Guard Service has started using the Oberig Register to strengthen the opposition of illegal traveling abroad.

About thisreported March 5Ministry of Defence Ukraine.

Over the last three weeks, border guards have stopped 230 attempts to cross the border with the use of counterfeit documents - in particular, thanks to the Unified State Register of military personnel, conscripts and reservists.

The Ministry of Defense, together with the State Border Service, established an exchange between the State Border Guard Database and the Unified State Register of conscripts, conscripts and reservists. This allows you to expose counterfeit documents and promptly check information about the military.

“Digitalization of work with the data of the Tax Code and the JV is already inevitable. "Oberig" allows you to automate those processes that have previously dependent on the human factor, " - said Kateryna Chernnoroenko, Deputy Minister of Defense for Digitalization, Digital Transformations and Digital Development.

Earlier, in order to confirm the validity of the documents for traveling abroad, the border guards had to contact directly with representatives of the CCC and JV. Now you can check information online through the registry of a charm - in several clicks.
