The European Union discusses the use of frozen Russian assets in favor of military assistance to Ukraine, as well as to avoid the destruction of the country.

About thisstatedEU High Representative on Foreign Affairs and Security Policy Josep Borrel on Twitter.

According to him, frozen reservoirs can be used to avoid the destruction of Ukraine and to increase military potential.

“Our dedication to Ukraine's defense capability is adequate. The potential use of surpluses from frozen Russian assets for weapons and purchases of weapons for Ukraine can be implemented through the European Peace Fund, ”his post reads.

Josep Borrel added that to implement the relevant decision of the EU, "the agreement of the Member States is still needed, the single base of the agreement, the proposal of a common financial security policy."

"We are currently continuing to work initially to reach an agreement on this issue," he said.
