The Prosecutor General commented on the issuance of orders by the International Criminal Criminal Court for the arrest of Russian generals.

About thisreported March 5Office of the Prosecutor General.

"I sincerely congratulate the International Criminal Court of Orders for Lieutenant General of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation and the Admiral Navy of the Russian Federation, who are suspected of systematic committing attacks on Ukraine's energy infrastructure, which caused significant damage to civilian population," Andrei said.

He thanked the International Criminal Court, the Karim Khan Prosecutor and all Ukrainian prosecutors who contributed to this common result, helping to collect evidence.

"We continue to work together to hold all the perpetrators of international crimes, regardless of their titles and positions," Andrei Kostin said.
