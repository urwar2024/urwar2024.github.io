During the last day, there were 74 fighting collisions on the front. In total, the enemy caused 5 missile and 66 aviation strikes, and made 108 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisreportedThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on its Facebook page.

In the area of responsibility, the "KhortytsiaIn Kupyansk directionThe enemy made 7 attacks in the areas of Sinkivka and Tabaivka of Kharkiv region, trying to improve their tactical position.

In the estuary directionOur warriors reflected 4 enemy attacks in the areas of the settlements of Terny of Donetsk region and Bigorivka of the Luhansk region.

In the Bakhmut directionThe forces of defense reflected 6 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements of Andreevka, Ivanivske and Klyshchiyivka of Donetsk region.

In the area of responsibility, "Tavriyain the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders repelled 28 enemy attacks in the areas of settlements of Novoselivka, Berdychi, Tonynke, Pervomaisk, Nevelskoye Donetsk region.

In the Novopavlovsky directionDefense forces continue to restrain the enemy in the districts of Georgiivka, Novomikhailivka, Vodyany, Prychistivka and the Harvest Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to break through the defense of our troops 22 times.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy carried out 1 attack in the area of the settlement of the robot of Zaporizhzhya region.

In the area of ​​responsibility, "Odesa"in the Kherson directionDefense forces continue to keep positions. Despite significant losses, the enemy does not leave the attempt to knock out our units from the positions. So, during the last day, the enemy performed 2 unsuccessful assault actions.

In the area of responsibility, the "north"in Volyn and Polissya directionsThe operational situation remains without significant changes.

In the Siversky and Slobozhansky directionsThe enemy retains military presence in border areas, conducts sabotage activities in order to prevent the transfer of our troops to threatening directions, increases the density of mine-explosive barriers along the state border in Belgorod region.
