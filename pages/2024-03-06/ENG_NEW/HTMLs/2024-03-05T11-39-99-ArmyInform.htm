The convicted person voluntarily agreed to the proposal of representatives of the occupying troops and occupied the position of "Director of the Dimerian Combine of Municipal Enterprises".

About thisreported March 5Office of the Prosecutor General.

According to the investigation, during the occupation of the Vyshgorod district of Kyiv region, a local resident went to cooperate with the enemy. He was engaged in staffing of the enterprise, bowing people to work for the enemy. He participated in meetings under the chairmanship of the Russian military in the village council, carried out other organizational and administrative, administrative and economic functions and acceptance of community residents.

The court found the resident of the Kyiv region in collaborative activity and imposed a punishment: 7 years of imprisonment with confiscation of all property owned by him and a ban on 10 years to occupy any positions in state authorities and local self-government.
