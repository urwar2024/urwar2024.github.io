In court, the indictment on the head of the forbidden party "Ours", the exNDAP Yevgeny Murayev.

About thisShe reported on March 5SBU.

According to the investigation, Muraev involved the TV channel "Our" controlled to him to submit distorted information about the internal situation in our country.

Thus, he contributed to the aggressor in numerous attempts to destabilize the socio-political situation in Ukraine on the eve of a full-scale invasion of the Russian Federation.

In this regard, the NSDC sanctions were imposed against Murayev in early February 2022.

The examination by the Security Service confirmed the facts of anti -Ukrainian activity of the person involved.

On the basis of the evidence collected, the Exnarade is accused of two articles of the Criminal Code of Ukraine:

  • Part 1 of Art. 111 (treason);
  • Part 1 of Art. 161 (violation of equality of citizens depending on their racial, national, regional affiliation, religious beliefs, disability and other grounds).

The attacker faces up to 15 years in prison with confiscation of property.

The special service reminds that in the fall of 2022 the SBU staff held a series of searches at places of residence and in the offices of Murayev in Kiev.

During the investigative actions, cash in Russian rubles, weapons, and computer equipment and mobile phones with evidence of criminal acts were found at the addresses of the person.
