The Ministry of Finance of Ukraine at an auction for the placement of internal state loan bonds (T -bills) attracted almost UAH 7.6 billion in the state budget.

About thisreportedIn the Ministry of Finance.

"According to the results of the location of the internal state loan bonds on March 5, 2024, 7 593 598 145,93 UAH (at the NBU rate) was attracted to the state budget," - the message reads.

According to the agency, investors were offered the hryvnia bonds of the T -bills: UAH 4.32 billion at 16.8% with repayment in 1 year; UAH 522.5 million at 17.6% with repayment in 1.9 years; UAH 345.6 million at 18.5% with repayment in 3.4 years.

Also, the USD in US dollars was offered at 4.66% with maturity in 1 year.

As reported by the Armyinform, Ukraine's monthly need for external fundingreachesAbout $ 3 billion
