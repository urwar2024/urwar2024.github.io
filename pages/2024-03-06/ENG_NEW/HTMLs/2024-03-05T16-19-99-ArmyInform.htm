The Ministry of Defense of the Russian aggressor state cannot cope with the consequences of the Ukrainian cyberattack.

About thisreportedThe main intelligence department of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine.

“As of March 5, 2024, the official website of the enemy's power department is still lying. IP telephony, domains of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation, as well as servers, which provided electronic document flow of the enemy structure, are also blocked, ”GUR said.

BeforeIt became knownthat Ukrainian cyberfactors, as a result of DDOSAtaki, were able to access the servers of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation.
