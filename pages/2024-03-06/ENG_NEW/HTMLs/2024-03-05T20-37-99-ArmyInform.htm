The Ukrainian soldiers are trying to take up heights in the Tabaivka area, from where the Russians come. It is now the purpose of the Armed Forces in the battles for the settlement.

About thisreportedKharkiv Ova Oleg Synigubov on Telegram.

According to him, the quality of hostile assaults in the Limano-Kupyan direction is also different from how it was before.

“At first they stormed tanks, but Ukrainian defenders destroyed the equipment. The enemy began to storm the armored areas, but similarly without a result for him. Currently, they are already going to MT-LB-that is, on the so-called "motorways", on which the assault actions are not in principle. From this you can already draw some conclusions, ”the message reads.

As reported by the Armyinform, the army of the Russian Federation 56 timesShe tried to break throughUkrainian defense in Novopavlovsky and Avdiivskyi.
