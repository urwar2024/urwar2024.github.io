The Russians beat 17 settlements of the region, and air strikes were caused by artillery and mortars.

About thisreported March 5Oleg Sinegubov, chairman of the Kharkiv Regional State Administration.

According to him, at 00:25 in Vovchansk during a series of shelling 8 private homes, outbuildings, a car were damaged. Without the victims.

At 19:20 in Kupyansk, a garage in private homeownership was burning as a result of shelling.

At 18:00 the enemy fired at the village. Senkovo Kupyansky district. The 49-year-old driver of the Center for Emergency Medical Aid received injury and explosive injury.

At 16:00 in the village. The Moscow of Kupyansk district damaged a private house as a result of hostile shelling. There was no information on the victims.

At 12:30 the enemy fired at a mortar fire with. Basovo Bohodukhiv district. As a result of 12 strokes, farm buildings, administrative buildings and power grids of the local agricultural enterprise were significantly damaged. There are no victims.

At 11:00 the enemy fired at the village. Ivashki Bohodukhiv district. A two -storey apartment building was damaged. The shelling took place during unloading of humanitarian aid.

During the day, Ukrainian defenders repelled 7 enemy attacks in the Sinkivka and Tabayivka area.

During the day in Kharkiv region, groups of explosives conducted a demining of 10.45 hectares of territory, destroying 248 explosive objects. The demining is ongoing.
