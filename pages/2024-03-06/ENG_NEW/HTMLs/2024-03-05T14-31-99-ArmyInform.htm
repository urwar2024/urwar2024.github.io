The team of the International School Brooks Westshor (Canada) invited Ukrainian children whose parents were killed in defending Ukraine.

About thisinformsMinistry of Veterans.

“According to the concluded memorandumUkrainian Center for Educational Quality AssessmentStudents have been evaluated and conducted to select for the international program, ”the department said.

So to the International SchoolBrookes WestshoreSeven Ukrainian children who will continue their studies at school will go. The establishment is located in Victoria Kanad province British Columbia.

During his studies, the school will provide transfer, nutrition, accommodation of Ukrainian children.

"Brookes Westshore works on an international bachelor's degree system on an intensive curriculum that is aimed at training students for study at universities in the world," the Ministry of Veterans said.
