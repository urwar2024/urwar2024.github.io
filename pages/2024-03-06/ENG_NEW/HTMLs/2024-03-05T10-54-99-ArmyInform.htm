The commander of the platoon of one of the military units of the air defense of the Odessa region sold more than 20 tons of diesel fuel.

About thisreported March 5State Bureau of Investigation.

The serviceman was responsible for obtaining and issuing diesel fuel intended for the needs of the unit. But instead of the unit, diesel fuel was sold. The losses caused to the state exceeded UAH 1.4 million.

Currently, the man reimbursed the full amount in full. He will be sentenced to him by a court.

Earlier SBU and National Policewere presented with suspicionsAlmost 1.5 billion UAH on mortar shots are involved in theft.
