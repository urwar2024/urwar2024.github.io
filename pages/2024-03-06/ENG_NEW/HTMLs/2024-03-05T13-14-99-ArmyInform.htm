The situation in the area of responsibility of the operational-strategic group of troops (Osv) "Tavriya" remains "complex but controlled". Defense forces keep the defense, perform active actions in certain directions and destroy the invaders.

About this commander of the operational-strategic group of Tavriya troops Bradyna General Alexander TarnavskywroteIn the telegram.

According to him, last day the enemy struck 26 aircraft blows, 144 blows with drones-Kamikadze, and also made 1175 shelling. There were 51 fighting on the battlefield.

“The total losses of the Russians were: 417 people (killed and wounded), in arms and military equipment - 2 tanks, 9 combat armored vehicles, 9 artisms, 3 rockets of volley fire, air defense systems, aircraft, 14 cars and 2 units of special equipment. 329 UAVs of different types are neutralized by means of EWS, ”the message reads.

Ukraine's defense forces also destroyed the composition of ammunition and 5 other important enemy objects.

As reported by the Armyinform, the overall combat loss of the enemy over the past daymake up1070 occupiers, destroyed 28 BBM and 48 artisms.
