The General Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine published interception of the conversation of the commander of the Russian brigade after the destruction of the ship "Sergey Kotov".

The audio recording is presented on the Gur YouTube channel.

The commander of 184 brigades of the ship's protection ships (Novorossiysk), in whose subordination was the patrol ship of the Black Sea Fleet "Sergey Kotov", describes the details of the destruction of the vessel.

According to him, the Russian ship was attacked at 00:50 "with non -extinguished boats in the amount of 5 units."

“I don’t know how they fought with them for 40 minutes. Because in 40 minutes there was information that there were 5 backing in the Kotov area. There was a helicopter, the ship itself with the AK-176-300, ”Rospovіda, Rosiysky commander.

As reported by the Armyinform, GURPosted a videoDestruction in the temporarily occupied Crimea of the Russian patrol ship "Sergey Kotov".
