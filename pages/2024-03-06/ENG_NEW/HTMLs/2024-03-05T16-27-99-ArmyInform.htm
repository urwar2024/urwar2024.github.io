Foreign Minister of Ukraine Dmytro Kuleba has negotiated with his Paraguay colleague Ruben Ramirez Leskano. One of the topics was the future global peace summit in Switzerland.

About this the head of the Ukrainian Foreign MinistryreportedIn Twitter.

Dmytro KulebaHe thanked Paraguay for the consistent bilateral and international support of Ukraine, including the UN and the OAD.

"I informed Minister Ruben Ramirez Leskano about preparation for the first global peace summit in Switzerland and invited Paraguay to take part in it," said the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine.

Ukraine and Paraguay have also focused on strengthening bilateral cooperation and agreed on holding the second meeting of the Intergovernmental Commission this year.

As reportedArmyinform, Dmytro Kuleba discussed with Radoslav Sikorsky ways to increase weapons supplies to Ukraine.
