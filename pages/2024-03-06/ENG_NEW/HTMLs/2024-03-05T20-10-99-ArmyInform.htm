In the north of the Sumy region, the Russians used dangerous ammunition-ball fragmentary bombs SHAB-0,5.

About thisreportedPolice of Sumy region.

“Last night, the enemy remotely replaced the area in the territory of the Shostka district, namely near the village of Marchikhina Buda. The Russian aviation used the CAST Bombs of the RBC-500 during the application of air strikes, ”the message reads.

It should be noted that one such cassette contains 565 ball aircraft ABOAB-0,5 weighing 400 grams each and has a large area of damage.

"In appearance, this item is similar to a small ball of silver color with small protruding ribs on the body. We do not advise you to touch such finds to anyone, ”the police said.

According to Igor Chernyak, Head of the Denial Department of the SUNP in Sumy Oblast, employees of the police department of the Sumy Oblast, who clean the territory from these bombs, work at the scene.

As reportedArmyinform, in the 118th brigade recorded the use of ball-shaped balloons.
