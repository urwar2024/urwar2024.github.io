The Sergey Kotov ship, together with Moscow's missile cruiser, participated in an attack on Zminin Island in February 2022.

About thisreportedOn March 5, a representative of the Main Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Andriy Yusov.

"Now Sergey Kotov has joined the cruiser" Moscow "again," Andrei Yusov said.


He reported that among the crew of the ship were dead and wounded, but did not rule out that one of the Russian sailors could be saved. At least about a dozen ambulances were called ashore.

Andriy Yusov recalled that "Sergiy Kotov" had twice attacked Ukrainian drones, and the third attack became fatal for him: the ship was five direct trains.
