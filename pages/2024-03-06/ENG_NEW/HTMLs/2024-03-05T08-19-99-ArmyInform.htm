The Russian military kissed several settlements of Kherson region in residential quarters, in particular, 8 high -rise buildings and 18 private homes were damaged.

About thisreportedKherson Ova Chairman Alexander Proudin.

In Kherson, a school and a car were damaged as a result of shelling.

Last day, the enemy fired Anthonovka, Berislav, Golden Beam, Lviv, Tomin Balka, Tyaginka, Gavrilovka, Belozerka, Goncharne, Dniprovske, Zelenovka, Stepanivka, Sadovo, Dudchan, Kachkarivka, Priozerne, Anniwyshchev.

As a result of hostile attacks, the settlements of the region were hit into administrative buildings, a gas station, warehouses and the territory of the cemetery.

Due to Russian aggression, one person was injured.
