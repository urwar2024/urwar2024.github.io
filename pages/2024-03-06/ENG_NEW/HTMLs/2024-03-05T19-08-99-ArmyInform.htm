There were 76 fighting collisions during the day. In total, the enemy caused 2 missile and 67 aviation strikes, and made 95 shelling of rocket launchers on the positions of our troops and settlements.

About thisinformsThe General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

In the estuary directionOur warriors reflects 5 enemy attacks in the area of the settlement of Terny of Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, tried to break through the defense of our troops. About 15 settlements were injured from artillery and mortar shelling, including Bigorivka of the Luhansk region and Torke, Serebryanka of Donetsk region.

In the Bakhmut directionThe defense forces reflects 1 attack of the enemy in the area of the settlement of White Gora of Donetsk region, where the enemy tried to improve his tactical position.

In the Avdiivsky directionOur defenders reflect 22 enemy attacks in the areas of Berdichi settlements, Tonenko, Pervomaisk, Nevelskoye, Semenivka and Orlovka.

In the Novopavlovsky directionThe forces of defense continue to restrain the enemy in the districts of Georgiivka, Novomykhailivka, Prechistivka and Krasnogorivka, Donetsk region, where the enemy, with the support of aviation, 34 times tried to break through the defense of our troops.

In the Orikhiv directionThe enemy made 5 unsuccessful attacks in areas of settlements Gulyaypole, Malynivka and robot.

In the Dnieper (Kherson) directionThe enemy does not give up the intention to knock out our units from the bridgeheads on the Dnieper Left Bank. Despite the significant losses, it does not leave the attempt to knock out our units from the positions. Thus, during the day the enemy performed 2 unsuccessful assault actions.

As reported by the Armyinform, the situation remains difficult in the Taurian direction, butThe Armed Forces are steadily holding defense.
