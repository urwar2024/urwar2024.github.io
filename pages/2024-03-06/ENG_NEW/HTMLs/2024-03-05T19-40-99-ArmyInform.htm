President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky spoke about the number of Russian military aircraft shot down and our boys' capabilities about the Russian Navy.

The head of statesaidIn the evening address.

“I want to thank all our soldiers today, all units that restore safety and control in both the Ukrainian sky and in our Black Sea. Ukraine has proved what we are capable of, what our strength is capable of. The number of Russian military aircraft and our boys' capabilities about the Russian Navy says this. There will be no safe harbor in the Black Sea for Russian terrorists. And there will be no safe space for them in the sky - provided our Ukrainian strength is sufficient. This applies to both partners and our domestic production, ”he said.

As reportedArmyinform, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky called on the world community to respond to every terrorist act of Russia.
