The Ministry of Defense of Ukraine has published clarification on the actions of servicemen who want to challenge the results of the Military Medical Commission (VLK).

This is stated in a message onSiteOffice.

If in the decision of the VLC the severity of the injury (injury), in your opinion, it is indicated incorrectly - such a conclusion can be challenged by the algorithm:

  • Write a written appeal to the commander of a part to consider the issue of changing the severity of injury
  • The commander directs the fighter to the medical and expert commission (LCC)
  • LCC makes a decision

In case of loss of performance for more than 60 days-any injury is considered severe.

The severity of injuries does not belong to the powers of full -time and freelance VLK.

As reported by the Armyinform, the Ministry of HealthStarts checkingAll military medical commissions in Kiev.
