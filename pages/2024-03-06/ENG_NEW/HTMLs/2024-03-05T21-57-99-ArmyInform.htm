Today, early in the morning occupation security forces searched for 10 houses of Crimean Tatars in Bakhchisarai, Dzhanka and Dzhankoy district of occupied Crimea.

About thisinformsRepresentation of the President of Ukraine in the Autonomous Republic of Crimea.

The department noted that after the illegal searches were detained Ramzi Kurtnezirov, Nariman Ametov, Enver Khalilayev, Arsen Kashka, Ali Mamutov, Mustafayev's wizard, Mustafa Abduuramanov, Memet Lumanov, Azizz and Rustem Ottomanov.

According to preliminary information from lawyers, all Crimean Tatars illegally incriminate the organization of activity of a terrorist organization and participation in the activity of such an organization.

The Occupational "Court" chose a preventive measure to the Crimean Solidarity journalist Rustem Ottomanov and detained from Bakhchisarayai Lumanov, Azizu Azizov and Mustafa Abduuramanov. The Court has assigned them an illegal arrest by May 4, 2024, "the statement said.

The detainees of the court do not agree and will be challenged.

“The invaders keep our citizens on their feet from 4am and do not even eat them. They were sent to the occupation pre-trial detention center-2 of Simferopol, where they will be illegally maintained during the so-called "investigation", "they said.

The President's representation in the Autonomous Republic of Crimea strongly condemns the illegal persecution of Crimean Tatar activists and religious figures in occupied Crimea, who have been increased by increased pressure from the invaders within the last few weeks.
