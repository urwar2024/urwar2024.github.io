On the night of March 4 to March 5, the Special Forces of the GUR of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Group 13 attacked the patrol ship of the Black Sea Fleet of the Russian Federation.

The mission was cooperated with the Navy of the Armed Forces and with the support of the Ministry of Digital Transformation of Ukraine.

As a result of the stroke of the Magura V5, the Russian ship was damaged by feed, right and left sides. Fire lesions occurred in the territorial waters of Ukraine, near the Kerch Strait.

The cost of a sinking ship is about $ 65 million.

"Signy Kotov" is a patrol ship of the project 22160, which was descended on the water on January 29, 2021. In addition, there are three such vessels in the Russian Black Sea Fleet.

The crew is 28 people.

The ships of this project have declared the possibility of installing modular container weapons and equipment, in particular the crystal missiles "Caliber", but now there are no such systems in the weapons of the Fleet of the Russian Federation.

We will remind, on February 15, a large landing ship of the Black Sea Fleet of the Russian Federation "Caesar Kunikov"DestroyedFifth with sea drones during the transition from Novorossiysk to temporarily occupied Sevastopol.
