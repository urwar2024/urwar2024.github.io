Last week, 204 loans for a total amount of UAH 351 million were issued for the purchase of housing under the Esosel program, of which 115 to servicemen and security officers were issued.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

From the beginning of the program, loans received 7798 families worth more than UAH 11.9 billion.

Yes, Ukrainians received: last week under the Esosel program:

  • 115 servicemen and security officers;
  • 15 teachers;
  • 14 doctors;
  • 2 scientists;
  • 47 families without their own home;
  • 5 VPO;
  • 6 veterans.

Recall that both representatives of individual professions and citizens who do not have housing or want to improve its conditions can use the available lending program.For the Armed Forces of the Armed Forces under contract, teachers, physicians and scientists, preferential rate is 3% per annum. For other categories -participants of hostilities, veterans, IDPs, other citizens - the mortgage rate is 7%.

Esosel programShe began her work in October 2022. They implement the Ministry of Economy Program together with the Ministry of Defense and PJSC "Ukrfinzhitlo". The partner banks are Oschadbank, PrivatBank, Ukrgasbank, Globus Bank, Sky Bank, Ukreximbank and Sense Bank.

As reported by the Armyinform, GovernmentMade changesto the Eoseli -Power Mortgage Program. One of the changes applies to veterans, people who have disabilities as a result of war, their families, and families of the dead.
