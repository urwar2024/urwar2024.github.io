Ilva's European Commissioner Johansson stated in Brussels that the European Union does not see signs that weapons from Ukraine could fall into criminal groups in Europe

About the relevant statementreportedMarch 5 of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.

“The Ukrainian Ministry of Internal Affairs works very closely with us so that the weapon that is now in Ukraine is not available to the EU. We see nothing, any firearms that would fall from Ukraine into the hands of criminal groups, ”Ilva Johansson said after the meeting of the EU Council of Ministers on Justice and Internal Affairs.

She also expressed support for Ukraine.

“I think it is really unprecedented - what Ukrainians do. As they fight to protect their sovereignty, freedom and the country, ”Ilva Johansson said.
