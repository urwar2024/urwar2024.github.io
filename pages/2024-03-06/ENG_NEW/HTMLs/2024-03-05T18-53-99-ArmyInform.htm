Over the two years of the occupation of the Zaporizhzhya Nuclear Power Plant, more than 150 gross disorders of the station were recorded.

About thisreportedPrime Minister Denis Shmigal during a government meeting.

“Two years ago, armed Russian military, contrary to all the rules of international law, occupied the Zaporizhzhya Nuclear Station. An unprecedented case in history that has put the continent on the border of the catastrophe. Since then, more than 150 gross disorders of the station operation have been recorded, each of which can become fatal, ” -Denis Shmigal told.

He stressed that it is necessary to activate the most severe sanctions against the Russian Federation, to use all possible IAEA and UN tools, to return Ukrainian control over the station as soon as possible.

"We also have plans to complete the construction of the third and fourth blocks at Khmelnitsky NPP, the construction of blocks 5 and 6 by AR 1000, the creation of two interconckers to Europe to strengthen energy integration with the EU and new objects of high-power generation with a capacity of 2-3 GW",----Denis Shmigal reported.

As reported by the Armyinform, occupiersblockedRecords for employees of the ZPP who did not sign "contracts" with them.
