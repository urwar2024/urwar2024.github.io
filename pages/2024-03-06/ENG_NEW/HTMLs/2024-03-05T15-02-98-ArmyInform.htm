According to the results of the third in 2024, the wave of applications for veterans grants was identified 41 winners. They will receive from the state 19 million UAH for start or development of their business.

About thisinformsGovernment portal.

"41 veteran and other of their spouses were recognized as the winners of the third this year the wave of the veterans component of the Program" Own business. " The state invests 19 million UAH in starting or scaling their business. From the beginning of this direction of the program, a positive decision to grant a grant has already received 368 veterans and their families at a total of 160 million UAH. The most popular among veterans are grants of up to 500 thousand UAH - more than half of the recipients have invited exactly the following amounts when submitting a grant application, ” - said Deputy Minister of Economy of Ukraine Tatiana Berezhna.

In total, 368 veterans and members of the families of combat participants have become the winners from the start of the veteran component of their own business.

Among the 41 winners of the third in 2024 the wave of applications for grants 26 participants of hostilities, 14 members of veterans, 1 person with disabilities as a result of war. 3 winners received a grant up to 1 million UAH, 28 - up to 500 thousand UAH, 10 - up to 250 thousand UAH.

The department also noted that the largest grantore from Vinnytsia and Kyiv regions, as well as the cities of Kyiv.

According to the conditions of the program, participants of hostilities or persons with disabilities as a result of war, as well as the second of their spouses can receive the following grants:

  • up to UAH 250 thousand, up to UAH 500 thousand and up to UAH 1 million for a veteran, subject to the creation of one, two and four jobs respectively;
  • up to UAH 250 thousand and up to UAH 500 thousand for the second veteran spouse, subject to the creation of one and two jobs, respectively;

Upon receipt of a grant from UAH 500 thousand to UAH 1 million, the veteran must be registered as a FOP for at least 3 years. In addition, such a grant is provided with co -financing with the recipient in the ratio of 70/30, that is, the grant recipient should invest 30% of their own funds, 70% of the funds will be provided by the state in the form of a grant.

Grant applications are submitted through the portal with the business plan.
