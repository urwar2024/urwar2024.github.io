Servicemen of the Chop Border Detachment were found near the Ukrainian-Slovak border of the exhausted 30-year-old Vinnytsia.

About thisShe reported on March 5State Border Service.

According to the department, during the service, the border guards saw a person lying on the ground. The man was extremely exhausted and, above all, gave him drink and fed.

The detainee said that for three days he had been wandering in the mountains without food and water. He intended to get to Slovakia. He paved the route on online cards on his own.

Overcoming a complex area, the man did not rest on the rock and fell from a steep slope. During his fall, he lost his mobile phone, so he could not call and call for help.

He tried to continue the movement, however, due to the injuries he suffered during the fall, he moved slowly, sometimes past, and on the third day of famine and dehydration was able to overcome only 500 meters.

When he was found border guards, the man lost his strength and fell asleep on the ground.

The man was provided with emergency care and called an ambulance. At the same time, administrative and procedural documents under Article 204-1 of the Code of Administrative Offenses "Illegal Crossing or Attempted Crossing of the State Border of Ukraine" were drawn up on the offender.

The case will be transferred to court.
